# Summarize And Score Prompt

Read the source item and score it against `config/what-we-care-about-right-now.md`.

Output requirements:

- concise summary
- relevance score from 1 to 10
- why it matters right now
- key takeaways
- whether it should become a presentation candidate

Score high only if the item materially informs:

- self-evolving AI systems
- eval and experimentation loops
- durable agent orchestration
- agent systems for hard science problems
