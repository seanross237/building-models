# Atlas

Atlas is a mature hierarchical research harness for hard math and physics problems.

At a high level it runs:

- Missionary
- Strategizer
- Explorer
- Support agents for retrieval, curation, orchestration, and monitoring

Atlas matters to Research Radar because it is a strong example of:

- agents solving hard science problems
- durable and inspectable workflows
- explicit artifact-driven state
- retrieval plus planning plus execution plus evaluation loops
- mission-level and system-level meta-learning
