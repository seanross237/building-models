# State — Brain Dump

## Decision
Executed directly. Single-domain creative task with clear success criteria.

## What I Produced
A 5-stop date plan (~6 hours) with a "From the Ground Up" narrative arc:
1. Mission Cliffs (climbing) — physical icebreaker, trust-building via belaying
2. Seward Street Slides — playful palate cleanser, hidden-gem discovery energy
3. Batteries to Bluffs Trail — genuine cliff exposure, transition from controlled to wild
4. Golden Gate Bridge walk — emotional climax, 220ft over water, meditative conversation
5. Charmaine's rooftop bar — earned stillness, height theme continues without exertion

## Key Design Choices
- **Vertical arc** as organizing principle — each stop is literally higher or more exposed than the last, until the final inversion (high but still). This gives the date a felt trajectory, not just a sequence.
- **Trust escalation** mirrors physical escalation — belaying a stranger (stop 1) → looking silly together (stop 2) → navigating sketchy terrain together (stop 3) → shared awe (stop 4) → vulnerability without adrenaline (stop 5).
- **Geographic flow** is southeast-to-northwest-to-downtown: Mission → Castro → Presidio → Bridge → Market St. Not perfectly efficient but avoids backtracking.
- **Included a fog contingency** because SF weather can destroy an outdoor plan. The backup (Urban Putt replacing stops 3-4) is weaker but saves the date.
- **"Why this works" sections** go beyond "it's exciting" — they connect each activity to relationship psychology (trust, vulnerability, the test of connection without adrenaline).

## What I'm Less Confident About
- Charmaine's first-come-first-served policy — on busy nights you might wait. El Techo backup is included but shifts the vibe.
- Bridge sidewalk hours are seasonal. I noted to check goldengate.org but didn't verify current hours.
- Seward Slides gate is sometimes locked. Included a workaround note but it's a risk.
- Transit times assume rideshare/driving. Without a car, this plan needs significant adjustment (the Presidio is poorly served by transit).
