# B3. USACO 2024 February Gold / Bessla Motors

- Type: Coding
- Subtype: Binary anchor
- Source: https://usaco.org/index.php?cpid=1401&page=viewproblem2

## Task

Given a large weighted graph with charging stations, identify all destinations that are reachable from at least K distinct charging stations within the specified range constraint.

## Grading

## Why It Discriminates
