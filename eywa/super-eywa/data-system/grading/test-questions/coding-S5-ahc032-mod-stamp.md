# S5. AHC032 / Mod Stamp

- Type: Coding
- Subtype: Score-gradient task
- Source: https://atcoder.jp/contests/ahc032/tasks/ahc032_a?lang=en

## Task

Choose up to K = 81 placements of 3 x 3 stamps on a 9 x 9 board to maximize the sum of board entries modulo 998244353.

## Grading

Score is the sum of final cell values modulo 998244353.
Higher is better.

## Why It Discriminates

Very combinatorial and not easy to solve greedily.
Good for search, beam methods, refinement, and modular arithmetic awareness.
