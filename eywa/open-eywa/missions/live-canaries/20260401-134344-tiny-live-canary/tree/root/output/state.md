The task requires creating a three-part briefing. This naturally decomposes into three research steps and one synthesis step. Each research step can be performed independently, and the synthesis step depends on the completion of all research. This approach ensures that each part of the briefing is adequately researched before being combined into the final output. The plan is designed to allow for parallel execution of the research steps, which is efficient. The final synthesis step has a higher complexity and importance as it combines all the information into the final deliverable.
