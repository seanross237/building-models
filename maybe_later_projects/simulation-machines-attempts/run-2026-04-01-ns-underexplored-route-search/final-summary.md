# Final Summary

## Favored Route

The favored route is:

- a canonical exact helical-packet program sharpened by a homochiral bottleneck test.

In concrete terms:

1. Define admissible Tao-like packet families directly in exact helical Fourier variables, preserving the local five-part Tao-likeness screen.
2. Canonicalize them variationally rather than by ad hoc choice:
   - normalize target drive
   - minimize spectator leakage
   - simultaneously minimize a helicity sign-defect functional measuring departure from near-homochiral dominance
3. Prove either:
   - a positive lower bound on `leakage + sign-defect`, which would act as a theorem-facing firewall against Tao-like delayed-threshold circuitry in exact NS
   - or compactness of a minimizing sequence, yielding a canonical extremizer that can be analyzed instead of an arbitrary packet family
4. Use the sampled helical literature as the one-sided mechanism:
   - near-homochiral dynamics are associated with extra control and inverse-transfer tendencies
   - minority-helicity modes are what restore forward transfer
   - but those same minority-helicity modes also reintroduce the heterochiral couplings and leakage that near-isolated Tao-like circuitry tries to suppress
