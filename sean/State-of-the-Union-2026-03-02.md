# State of the Union — 2026-03-02

## North Star

Everything is far too complex to predict or control. The system is so vast that I can only see five feet ahead. My highest priority is developing a better ability to listen to God and be led by that higher place and power. Everything below flows from this.

## The Current Foundation: Serenade

Serenade is a functioning business that blesses people and generates revenue.

- **Revenue:** ~$few hundred/day profit
- **Reach:** ~300 people meaningfully/day, ~1,000 lightly touched/day
- **Maintenance:** ~1 hour/day
- **Current limitation:** People use it once and leave. It's transactional, not transformational yet.

## Making Serenade a True Force for Good

Before amplifying reach, the priority is making what exists deeply good — something that genuinely helps people, not just serves them once.

### Playlists (Encouraging People to Become Who They Want to Be)

Songs all about *them* — personalized encouragement that helps people grow into the person they want to be. This turns one-time buyers into people who return, but more importantly, it creates an ongoing positive force in their lives.

- **Effort:** ~1 month of focused work
- **Projected impact:** ~3,000 daily listeners, growing ~50%/month
- **Why it matters:** Ongoing, personal, deeply encouraging. Not a transaction — a relationship.

### Love Fund (Redistributing Profits to Fund Acts of Kindness)

A new form of social media where Serenade's profits fund gifts and acts of kindness for people in need, submitted by their community.

- **Status:** Attempted, hit a cold-start problem
- **What happened:** As a faceless company with no established philanthropic credibility, asking strangers to go through an involved flow (think of someone, submit them, describe their situation) was too big an ask. Even offering free money to give away wasn't enough — the ask was too out of left field.
- **Belief:** The model works once the flywheel is spinning. The challenge is ignition — needs a rethought approach to getting the engine started.
- **Why it matters:** Turns commerce into community. Creates a cycle of generosity that's bigger than any one product.

## Amplification Strategies (Use *After* the Foundation Is Strong)

These are means of getting a lot of eyeballs. They're not goals — they're fuel. The question is what to point them at.

### Viral Content
- Doing things for homeless people
- Freestyle rap content
- Automated systems for funny content
- Automated systems for philosophical/thought content

Any of these could generate significant attention that gets directed toward whatever matters most.

### Remix (Teachers, Pastors, Anyone With Content)

Takes existing content (a sermon, a lesson, a talk) and instantly turns it into songs. Strong viral potential with specific audiences.

- **Bottleneck:** Distribution. Ads could work but aren't ideal. The real need is an AI-powered email outreach system that can identify, contact, and hold automated conversations with teachers, pastors, and other potential users at scale.
- **Dependency:** Needs the AI email/conversation system built first.

## The Force Multiplier: AI Infrastructure

Rather than using AI as a one-off tool, build systems where AI agents:
- Chain together for comprehensive work
- Feedback loop to understand what worked and what didn't
- Build a growing knowledge base over time
- Verify their own work before finishing
- Get smarter and more capable with each iteration

This sits underneath everything. Getting this right makes execution faster and more effective across *all* of the above — playlists, Love Fund, Remix outreach, viral content, and anything not yet imagined.

- **Current state:** Lightly developed to support other work
- **Tension:** Investing here doesn't directly serve people today, but dramatically increases capacity to serve people going forward
- **Timing:** Starting to feel like the right time to focus heavily on this

## VR-Based Visual Development System

A VR environment for better visual development — instead of staring at flat code and flat UIs on a screen, step into a spatial workspace where you can see, manipulate, and navigate your projects in three dimensions.

- **Vision:** Use VR as a development tool — lay out UI components spatially, visualize data flows, see system architecture as a walkable space, arrange and compare mockups around you
- **Why it matters:** The flat screen is a bottleneck for visual thinking. VR removes the constraint of a 2D rectangle and lets you work the way your brain actually processes spatial information
- **Potential applications:**
  - Spatial UI prototyping — arrange components in 3D, walk around your layouts
  - Architecture visualization — see how services, edge functions, and data flow connect as a living diagram
  - Project management — boards and roadmaps as rooms you walk through
  - Code review — immersive diff views, side-by-side comparisons at scale
  - Design iteration — surround yourself with mockup variations, compare at a glance
- **Stage:** Early idea — exploring what's possible

## The Sequencing Question

The implicit logic:

1. **Listen** — Be led by God. Don't over-plan.
2. **Deepen the good** — Make Serenade genuinely transformational (playlists and/or Love Fund)
3. **Build the engine** — AI infrastructure that makes everything faster and smarter
4. **Amplify** — Pour fuel on the fire only when what you're sending people to is worth staying for

But this is held loosely. The whole point of #1 is that the right path may not be the logical one.
