# Comedy Lens

## What we want

We are looking for signals that can be turned into 30-second AI-generated comedy sketches. A signal scores high if it has a clear protagonist doing something absurd, a public figure caught in a contradiction, a corporate move that contradicts itself in real time, or a mundane situation that escalates into chaos. We want a clear visual hook a video model can render: a podium, an office, a kitchen, a parking lot — somewhere a sketch can physically happen.

The tone we are aiming for is dry, observational, and a little surreal — closer to Tim Robinson and Nathan Fielder than to topical late-night. Workplace energy, mockumentary deadpan, absurdist escalation. Bonus points if there's a single line of dialogue that lands the punchline.

## What kills sketchability

Stories that are too niche to read in three seconds, memes everyone has already done, anything legally dangerous, and tragedies without a clear comedy angle. We do not want mean-spirited political dunks or low-hanging-fruit jokes about death, illness, or violence. If the first instinct is "this is dark," skip it.
