# Comedy Lens (Phase 1 placeholder)

We are looking for signals that can be turned into 30-second AI-generated comedy sketches. A signal scores high if it has a clear protagonist doing something absurd, a public figure caught in a contradiction, or a mundane situation that escalates into chaos. The tone we want is dry, observational, and a little surreal — closer to Tim Robinson than to topical late-night.

This file is a placeholder for Phase 1. The real lens with scoring rubric, tone references, and counterexamples will land in Phase 2 along with the real analyzer.
