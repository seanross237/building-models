# Open Brush AI — Features to Build

## Core AI Painting (High Priority)

- **Text-to-painting** — describe a scene, AI paints it stroke by stroke
- **Style transfer** — "paint this in the style of Van Gogh / anime / pixel art"

## Voice Commands (High Priority)

- **"Add trees over there"** — point your controller + speak, AI fills it in
- **"Make it bigger / smaller / darker / wilder"** — modify existing strokes by voice
- **"Undo what you did"** — AI-specific undo separate from your own strokes
- **"Surprise me"** — AI riffs on what's already in the scene

## Animation & Motion (Nice to Have)

- **Animated brushstrokes** — AI creates strokes that move (particles, flowing water, flickering flames)
- **Playback paintings** — record the AI painting process as a timelapse you can replay in VR
- **Looping scenes** — AI creates ambient motion (swaying grass, floating clouds)
- **Choreographed sequences** — describe a scene that evolves over time

## Music & Audio Reactive (Nice to Have)

- **Paint to music** — AI listens to audio and paints reactive visuals in real-time
- **Beat-synced strokes** — brush size/color/speed tied to BPM and frequency
- **Serenade crossover** — play a Serenade song, AI paints a visual world for it
