# Scope: root

Everything an Israeli needs to know to leave Israel and establish a life in
another country. The full problem space — from the decision of whether to
leave, through exiting obligations in Israel, to landing and settling in a
destination.

## Audience

Israeli adults making the decision or preparing to leave. Some are firm on
a destination; others are still deciding. Some have EU ancestry eligibility;
others will need visas. Some are leaving alone; others with families.

## Depth / fanout

This is the root. Partition is pre-committed in `CHILDREN.md` — do NOT
repartition. Eleven domain branches plus a cross-cutting `_countries/`
folder. Each domain branch decides its own sub-structure up to the global
depth cap of 4 (i.e. three more levels below this one).

## Languages required

All three surfaces — English, Hebrew, destination-country local — apply
at every leaf. Hebrew is especially critical for the exit-from-Israel
domains (01), the emotional-and-identity domain (10), and the money/tax
domain (02) where Israeli-side rules dominate.
