# Scope: 01 — Exiting Israel

All obligations, formalities, and practical wind-downs on the **Israeli
side** of leaving. This is Israel-specific by nature — almost everything in
this branch is sourced from Israeli government websites and Israeli legal
or financial advisors. Hebrew sources will dominate.

## What belongs here

- **Bituach Leumi (National Insurance)** — status as a "toshav chutz"
  (resident abroad), contribution obligations while abroad, how to stop
  paying, what happens to eligibility on return, the 5-year rule, forms
  and deadlines.
- **Kupat Holim (health fund)** — suspending or terminating membership,
  what happens if you return, the difference between temporarily abroad
  and formally leaving.
- **Tax residency exit (ma'amad toshavut l'tzorech mas)** — the criteria
  used by Israel Tax Authority, when you stop being an Israeli tax
  resident, the "center of life" test, exit tax (mas yetzia) on unrealized
  gains, reporting obligations in the year of exit and years after,
  Form 5328 and related filings.
- **Miluim (reserve duty)** — what happens to your reserve obligation
  when you leave, permission to be absent, the difference between a short
  absence and formal departure, procedures for leaving while on a reserve
  list, what triggers recall.
- **Bank account closure or maintenance** — whether to keep Israeli bank
  accounts open, FATCA/CRS reporting, foreign-resident account status,
  closing procedures, unclaimed account rules.
- **Foreign currency and asset transfer rules** — historical context
  (Israel had strict currency controls that are now mostly lifted),
  current reporting thresholds, wire transfer documentation, taking
  large sums abroad.
- **Aliyah reversal and Misrad Haklita** — for those who made aliyah and
  are now leaving, the administrative wind-down with the Ministry of
  Aliyah and Integration, what benefits must be returned, and what
  status they retain.
- **Apartment / lease / property** — breaking a lease, selling property,
  renting it out while abroad, property tax (arnona) obligations,
  power of attorney setups.
- **Car / vehicle disposal** — selling a car, suspending registration,
  the licensing (Misrad HaRishui) side.
- **Mail forwarding, utilities, and subscriptions.**
- **Passport status** — keeping Israeli passport, dual citizenship
  considerations, renewing from abroad.

## What does NOT belong here

- What to do in the destination (those are 04_destination-landing,
  05_healthcare, etc.)
- Cross-border tax treatment AFTER exit → `02_money-and-tax`
- Emotional / identity side of leaving → `10_emotional-and-identity`

## Language note

Most sources here will be **Hebrew primary, English secondary**. The
gov.il sites have inconsistent English coverage. A leaf that only finds
English content has likely missed the authoritative source. Record the
Hebrew search queries attempted in `sources.md`.
