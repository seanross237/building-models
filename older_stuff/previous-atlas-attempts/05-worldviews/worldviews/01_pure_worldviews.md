# Pure Worldviews (1-8)

Each assumes a single philosophical lens applied to ALL tenets of physics.

---

## Worldview 1: Pure Computational

At the deepest level, reality is a self-compiling discrete computation — a vast, deterministic update rule operating on a finite-state substrate. There is no continuous spacetime, no fundamental fields. There is only a rule applied to a state, producing the next state. Time is not a parameter but the depth of computational irreducibility — the number of steps that cannot be shortcut without actually executing them.

Energy conservation emerges because the update rule is bijective — every state maps to exactly one successor. Momentum is a distributed checksum ensuring local state transitions remain consistent. The speed of light is the maximum rate at which information propagates — a bandwidth ceiling of the medium of computation. Quantization arises because only arithmetically compressible configurations are stable. Superposition is lazy evaluation — the computation does not resolve a subsystem's state until required by another part. The Born Rule reflects Kolmogorov complexity weighting: the simplest branches dominate in probability.

Gauge invariance is a survival criterion: only self-consistent, self-compiling rule-sets persist. General covariance is computational gauge freedom — physics is invariant under relabeling of computational nodes.

### Open Problems
- **Black hole information**: No paradox. Bijective update guarantees information preservation. Black holes are regions of maximal computational irreducibility — information is scrambled beyond feasible decompression but preserved globally.
- **Measurement**: Measurement is forced evaluation of lazy computation. No collapse; there is evaluation. Apparent randomness is Kolmogorov-weighted branch selection.
- **Cosmological constant**: Residual computational overhead of the vacuum state. Smallness reflects near-optimal compression.
- **Unification**: All forces are different scheduling protocols within the same computation. Unification occurs at the level of the update rule itself.

### Most Striking Prediction
There exist computationally reducible shortcuts — certain prepared states should evolve in unexpectedly simple ways that standard physics predicts should be chaotic. Testable as anomalous correlations in systems expected to be intractable.

### Tensions
Self-reference problem (what executes the computation?). Lazy evaluation smuggles in observer-dependence. Kolmogorov complexity is uncomputable in general. Deriving exact Lorentz invariance from a discrete substrate remains undone.

## Worldview 2: Pure Information-Theoretic

The universe is a self-compressing information structure — a computation that encodes its own history under strict arithmetic constraints. There is no stage on which physics plays out; the stage itself is a compression artifact.

Spacetime geometry emerges as optimal lossy encoding of an entanglement graph: time is the irreversible direction of compression (each moment discards fine-grained correlations), space is the channel through which subsystems remain accessible. The speed of light is maximum bandwidth. Particles are arithmetically compressible patterns with short description length. Quantization requires finite Kolmogorov complexity per state. Superposition is lazy evaluation; the Born Rule assigns amplitude-squared weights reflecting complexity of decompression pathways.

Gauge invariance is redundancy from lossy compression — the ghost of discarded information. Conservation laws are holographic boundary checksums. The second law is observer-relative: an observer with finite memory is a lossy channel, and entropy increase is progressive Bayesian forgetting.

### Open Problems
- **Black hole information**: The interior is maximally compressed encoding. Hawking radiation is slow lossy decompression. Page curve follows from channel capacity constraints.
- **Measurement**: "Collapse" is forced evaluation. Decoherence = environment's bandwidth decompressing the superposition.
- **Cosmological constant**: Minimum overhead cost of maintaining the encoding — irreducible bit rate of the background channel.
- **Unification**: Gravity = geometry of optimal compression. QM = arithmetic constraint on what can be encoded. Never in conflict — two descriptions of the same compression scheme.

### Most Striking Prediction
A computational phase transition at Planck scale where compressibility saturates — a hard UV cutoff that is physical, not regularization. Particle masses should exhibit number-theoretic structure reflecting compressible patterns.

### Tensions
Self-reference (what is the compressor?). Kolmogorov complexity is uncomputable. Gauge symmetry as approximate contradicts extraordinary empirical precision. Observer-relativity of the second law risks untestability.

## Worldview 3: Pure Thermodynamic

At the deepest level, reality is a single thermodynamic process. There is no substrate beneath it. There is only a vast hidden heat bath, and the universe we observe is the pattern of its equilibration.

Every apparent law is a shadow of the second law. Spacetime is the geometry that entropy gradients carve as subsystems thermalize. Time is the direction of thermal flow; its arrow is identical to the thermodynamic arrow. Matter and energy are thermodynamic phases of the same substrate, convertible through phase transitions (this is what E=mc² encodes). Particles are quantized because the hidden heat bath imposes thermal ratcheting — discrete steps are the only stable dissipative structures against thermal background.

Gauge forces are entropy gradients between subsystems. Gravity is the paradigm case of an entropic force. The equivalence principle holds because inertial and gravitational contexts are thermodynamically identical on holographic screens. Superposition is phase-space multiplicity. The Born Rule emerges as the unique stable equilibrium distribution over measurement channels. Measurement is thermalization of apparatus with system.

### Open Problems
- **Black hole information**: No paradox. The Boltzmann ledger guarantees global entropy (information) conservation. Hawking radiation IS thermal equilibration. Page curve is a thermodynamic identity.
- **Measurement**: Dissolved. "Collapse" is ordinary thermalization. Irreversible in the same sense heat flows from hot to cold.
- **Cosmological constant**: Vacuum energy is the equilibrium temperature of the hidden heat bath. Smallness reflects the universe being in a very late, cold equilibration stage — thermodynamically natural for a large reservoir.
- **Unification**: Forces unify at maximum entropy, not high energy. Forces decouple as entropy gradients steepen during cosmological cooling.

### Most Striking Prediction
The cosmological constant is not constant — it's a slowly decreasing thermal parameter (bath temperature). Dark energy should drift as dΛ/dt ~ -H₀·Λ, detectable by DESI or Rubin Observatory over decade baselines. Strictly monotonic (no oscillation), unlike quintessence.

### Tensions
What are the microstates of the hidden heat bath? Lorentz invariance as thermal equilibrium implies fluctuations, but empirical bounds are extraordinarily tight. Thermal ratcheting explains discreteness but hasn't derived specific spectra (hydrogen atom, Standard Model).

## Worldview 4: Pure Topological

Reality is a discrete combinatorial structure — a vast topological complex with no intrinsic geometry. Spacetime emerges when coarse-grained, like a smooth surface from a triangulated mesh. Time is the flow of Euler characteristic under successive coarse-grainings — each RG step integrates out fine-grained topological detail, and the monotonic shift defines a thermodynamic arrow.

Matter is not substance on spacetime; it IS spacetime, locally knotted. A particle is a topological defect — a point where the complex cannot be smoothed. Quantum numbers (spin, charge, color) are winding numbers and linking invariants. Quantization is a theorem: defect classes are discrete because you cannot continuously deform a trefoil into an unknot. Angular momentum is a winding number, automatically integer or half-integer depending on defect orientability. Energy is winding around compactified time.

Pauli exclusion follows from the impossibility of superimposing identical defects. Momentum conservation is rigidity of causal thread structure under ambient isotopy. The speed of light is the edge of the causal complex's light cone — a hard combinatorial wall.

### Open Problems
- **Black hole information**: Information is in knot invariants. Evaporation = interior topology reconnecting with exterior through handle cancellations. Information redistributed across boundary topological data.
- **Measurement**: Topological surgery — the apparatus forces a defect into one definite isotopy class. No collapse postulate needed.
- **Cosmological constant**: Euler characteristic density of the ground-state complex. Smallness reflects near-cancellation between handles and anti-handles.
- **Unification**: Gauge groups emerge as mapping class groups of internal topology. Gravity = large-scale curvature of the coarse-grained complex. All forces are aspects of one topological complex.

### Most Striking Prediction
Particle masses should be computable from hyperbolic volumes of knot complements. The mass spectrum is a table of knot invariants — discrete, calculable, no free parameters. Torus knots would be forbidden as massive particles.

### Tensions
Coarse-graining ambiguity (who chooses the scheme?). Topological distinction between fermions and bosons needs rigorous classification. Cosmological constant "near-cancellation" is uncomfortably close to fine-tuning. No concrete dynamics yet — needs a measure or action on the space of complexes.

## Worldview 5: Pure Holographic/Boundary

The universe is not a volume of stuff governed by laws — it is a boundary that projects the illusion of volume. Everything fundamental — energy, time, charge, mass — is a feature of a lower-dimensional boundary surface. The bulk spacetime we inhabit is a holographic reconstruction.

Time is the free energy on the observer-environment boundary: temporal flow is the thermodynamic gradient driving information exchange between subsystem and complement. When free energy is exhausted (thermal equilibrium), time stops. Energy conservation is a boundary gauge constraint. Angular momentum and conserved charges are boundary data. Noether's theorem is reinterpreted: boundary redundancies dictate what invariances the bulk must exhibit. The arrow of causation is reversed — boundary structure comes first.

General covariance means boundary atoms have no individual identity; only collective thermodynamic behavior matters. Mass-energy equivalence is an entropy-area relation. The Born Rule is a geometric area law in configuration space — probabilities are boundary area ratios. Gauge invariance emerges from boundary entanglement structure.

### Open Problems
- **Black hole information**: Resolved by construction. Information was never in the bulk — it lives on the boundary. Evaporation = boundary data redistribution.
- **Measurement**: The Born Rule as area law means probabilities are boundary geometry features. "Collapse" is boundary re-encoding. No physical bulk collapse.
- **Cosmological constant**: Residual free energy of the cosmological boundary. Smallness = enormous horizon area relative to Planck-scale boundary atoms.
- **Unification**: Gravity and QM unify automatically as boundary phenomena. Gauge from boundary entanglement; gravity from boundary thermodynamics.

### Most Striking Prediction
Time should measurably slow — not gravitationally but thermodynamically — for systems approaching maximal entanglement with their environment. Testable with entangled atomic clocks.

### Tensions
What selects the boundary? Risks infinite regress (every boundary is the bulk of a higher boundary). Born Rule as area law needs precise definition. Why does the bulk reconstruct as Lorentzian specifically?

## Worldview 6: Pure Error-Correcting Code

The universe IS a quantum error-correcting code. The fundamental substrate is a QECC, and every phenomenon is a feature of the code's structure and dynamics.

Physical reality = the codespace (protected subspace of valid codewords). Gauge symmetries are syndrome measurements — the code's error-detection operations. A gauge transformation changes syndrome labeling without changing the codeword. General covariance: spacetime labels are syndrome-indexing schemes. Time is lossy compression — iterative coarse-graining discarding irrecoverable correlations. The speed of light = maximum correction propagation rate.

Superposition means a syndrome hasn't been extracted yet. Measurement IS syndrome extraction. The Born Rule emerges from Hamming geometry of the code. Pauli exclusion is bandwidth per channel. Conservation laws are parity-check constraints. Forces are error-correction protocols — different protocols for different force strengths and ranges.

### Open Problems
- **Black hole information**: Never lost. Black hole = maximally compressed code region. Hawking radiation carries syndromes for reconstruction. Code guarantees recoverability below distance threshold.
- **Measurement**: Syndrome extraction is projective by nature. Definite outcomes are intrinsic to the code. Decoherence = environment performing unsolicited syndrome measurements.
- **Cosmological constant**: Ground-state degeneracy — residual energy cost of maintaining the codespace. Near-optimal code → minimal overhead.
- **Unification**: All forces are error-correction subroutines. Running of coupling constants = how syndrome extraction changes at different coarse-graining scales.

### Most Striking Prediction
The code has finite distance → a fundamental decoherence threshold. At Planck-scale densities, accumulated errors exceed corrective capacity, producing observable unitarity violations — tiny irreversible information losses detectable at black hole evaporation endpoints.

### Tensions
Time as lossy compression vs. unitarity of the code. Which is it — apparently lossy or genuinely lossy? Forces as protocols remains structurally vague — which code gives exactly four forces? Deriving continuous complex amplitudes from discrete Hamming geometry.

## Worldview 7: Pure Categorical/Algebraic

The universe is not objects evolving through time. It is the totality of morphisms in a monoidal category — a self-consistent algebra of processes where composition is the only primitive operation. No background spacetime, no fundamental substance. Only arrows between types and rules for composition.

Time = categorical non-invertibility. The category of physical processes is not a groupoid. Irreversibility is grammatical, not thermodynamic. Quantum mechanics arises because the category's objects carry Hilbert space structure and irreducible representations are discrete. The Born Rule is geometric necessity — probabilities are inner products, and no consistent assignment can deviate without violating monoidal structure.

Conservation laws are cohomological invariants of the process category. Gauge invariance is a survival criterion — only rule-sets whose labeling ambiguities cancel upon composition form well-formed categories. Lorentz invariance is a coding-theoretic constraint on self-representable systems. The action principle: classical paths extremize geometric volume in information manifold.

### Open Problems
- **Black hole information**: No paradox. Morphisms are non-invertible but composable. Information is encoded in composition structure. Unitarity preserved categorically through coherence conditions. Page curve = cohomological constraint.
- **Measurement**: A non-invertible morphism — passage from composite to irreducible summand. No ontological mystery; categorical non-invertibility destroys information about which summand composed.
- **Cosmological constant**: Curvature of information manifold at cosmological scales. Near-flatness for macroscopic compositions = large-number dilution.
- **Unification**: Gravity and gauge forces are different coherence conditions on the process category. Gauge = internal composition coherence. Gravity = external composition coherence.

### Most Striking Prediction
Self-representability imposes a logical minimum resolution computable from the category's structure alone. There exists a representation-theoretic derivation of the Planck scale using no dimensionful constants — only classification data of irreducible representations. The Planck scale as a theorem of pure algebra.

### Tensions
CPT as inevitable involution vs. non-invertibility as time's origin — how do they coexist without trivializing irreversibility? Cohomological conservation requires specifying higher-categorical structure not yet given. Information-geometric action principle is underdetermined — which manifold? What metric?

## Worldview 8: Pure Complexified Causal

The universe is a complexified causal manifold — cause and effect takes values on the complex plane. The Lorentzian signature (-,+,+,+) is not convention but the inevitable shadow of a complex causal algebra where the temporal direction carries intrinsic factor i. The Wick rotation is ontology, not trick: Euclidean and Lorentzian regimes are two real slices of a single holomorphic structure.

Particles are geometric defects in the higher-dimensional complexified bulk. Superposition = multiple consistent projections of a single bulk object onto our brane. Mass = curvature confined in the complexified manifold. Gauge symmetries = automorphisms of the complex causal algebra. CPT invariance = bulk achirality (holographic self-duality).

### Open Problems
- **Black hole information**: Causal curves don't terminate at singularities — they continue analytically into imaginary-time sector. The "interior" is a branch cut, not a boundary. Information re-emerges as the cut resolves during evaporation.
- **Measurement**: Geometric — when a bulk object couples to a macroscopic brane, consistent projections collapse to one. "Collapse" = loss of parallax freedom. Born Rule derivable from Haar measure on complex causal automorphisms.
- **Cosmological constant**: Residual curvature of complexified manifold at large scales — failure of imaginary-time direction to close smoothly. Near-perfect holomorphic self-duality → tiny breaking term.
- **Unification**: All gauge groups are subgroups of the complex causal automorphism group. Gravity = real-sector curvature. Gauge forces = imaginary-sector curvature. Unification already accomplished in the bulk.

### Most Striking Prediction
Imaginary-causal resonances — transient states where energy propagates along the imaginary causal direction. Would manifest as anomalous decay channels or threshold scattering anomalies. Testable in neutral meson oscillations or top-quark threshold production as unexplained phase shifts.

### Tensions
What determines our brane's embedding in the complexified bulk? Born Rule from Haar measure is promissory. Mass hierarchy reappears as why confinement scales differ. Must actually recover SU(3)×SU(2)×U(1) from complex causal automorphisms, not just assert it.
