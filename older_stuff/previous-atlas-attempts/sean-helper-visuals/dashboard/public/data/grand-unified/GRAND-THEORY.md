- **S-wave condensation preferred:** 75-80% confidence from three independent arguments (spin-1 stabilization, Meissner energy, kinetic cost)

- **Effective Lorentz invariance:** Tree-level Lorentz satisfied; d=3 LIV forbidden by fracton gauge symmetry; d=4 LIV mitigated by three-layer defense (80-85% confidence)
- **Oppenheim prediction:** σ_a = √(Gℏ/R³) — acceleration noise from gravitational decoherence. Falsifiable with near-future optomechanics (diamond microspheres at LISA Pathfinder sensitivity).
- **Emergent constants:** c² = ρ_s/χ (speed of light from superfluid stiffness), G_N ~ 1/(ρ_s v²) (Newton's constant from condensate parameters)

### Standard Model Gauge Forces from the Fracton Substrate

**The Question:** Can the photon, W/Z bosons, and gluons emerge as excitations of the same fracton dipole condensate that produces gravity?

**Chosen Approach: Multi-Species Fracton Compositeness (MSFC)**

Introduce N fracton species with internal quantum number a = 1,...,N. The rank-2 tensor field becomes A_{ij}^{ab}, carrying both spatial indices (i,j) and internal indices (a,b). Dipole condensation breaks both:
- Spatial dipole symmetry → spin-2 Goldstone bosons (gravitons) [established FDCG result]
- Internal symmetry → spin-1 Goldstone bosons (gauge bosons) [the new claim]

The gauge group of the Standard Model (SU(3)×SU(2)×U(1)) would be determined by the condensation pattern: which subgroup of the internal O(N) or U(N) symmetry remains unbroken.

**Critical Question:** Do the internal Goldstones have spin-1 (vector gauge bosons) or spin-0 (scalars)? The order parameter ⟨ψ^{ab}_i⟩ carries a spatial index i, which may entangle spatial and internal symmetry breaking — this is the mechanism that could produce spin-1 rather than spin-0.

**Key Concerns:**
1. Spin of Goldstones — must verify via explicit calculation
2. Gauge enhancement — must show rank-1 gauge symmetry emerges (analogous to FDCG's rank-2 gauge enhancement)
3. Chirality — SM is chiral; Goldstone mechanism is generically non-chiral
4. Species count — why N species? (ad hoc until derived)
5. Weinberg-Witten theorem — loophole via emergent Lorentz invariance needs verification

**Status:** Phase B — Investigation begins iteration 2

**Candidates evaluated and deferred:**
- Defect-Mediated Gauge Fields (plausible for U(1) but cannot produce non-abelian groups without additional structure)
- Kaluza-Klein Fractons (too close to published rank-2 Higgs mechanism literature, Bulmash-Barkeshli 2018)
- Subdimensional Gauge Hierarchy (conceptually interesting but mathematically underdeveloped, non-abelian fracton theories barely exist)
