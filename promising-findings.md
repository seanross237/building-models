## 1. What Is Time?

**Source:** Atlas — Nature of Time mission (Mar 2026, 18 explorations across 2 strategies)

**The theory:** Time is irreversible determination — the ongoing, physically constrained process by which the indeterminate becomes determinate, as encountered from within.

**Two-layer architecture:**

- **Ground floor (established physics, no metaphysical commitments):**
  - Irreversibility: the Landauer-Bennett-Bekenstein engine forces information erasure in any finite system — not statistically, structurally
  - One-dimensionality: the Margolus-Levitin bound constrains processing to one sequential thread
  - Open future: computational irreducibility + Wolpert's impossibility theorem — no embedded system can predict its own universe
  - Time dilation: less energy = slower processing rate (ML bound)
  - The static-vs-evolving debate is dissolved by quantum reference frame equivalence (both are perspectives)

- **Upper floor (one philosophical commitment: quantum possibilities are real):**
  - The "now" is the locus of active resolution — ontologically distinct from settled past and open future
  - The arrow is intrinsic: possibility-to-actuality is inherently one-way
  - Flow has a phenomenological structure: retention (trailing past) — impression (active present) — anticipation (open future)
  - Compatible with Heisenberg, Kastner, and collapse interpretations; incompatible with many-worlds

**Key discovery:** Two independent traditions (information-theoretic physics and process philosophy + causal set theory) converged on the same deep structure without being designed to agree. Point-by-point structural mapping: input state = inherited past, computation step = event of becoming, information erasure = possibilities extinguished, computational frontier = the present.

**Honest limits:** Hard problem of consciousness remains. No novel predictions. Past Hypothesis assumed. The identification claim (erasure = possibility-extinguishing) is proposed, not proved. Factorization problem (presupposes well-defined subsystems).

**Validation:** 5/5 tiers passed (internal consistency, logical rigor, explanatory power, boldness with honesty, completeness). Survived 6 adversarial attacks across 2 strategies.

**Honest limits:** This is an existing research program (Anselmi et al., 25+ papers since 2017), not invented by the system. The derivation paths and uniqueness framing are novel contributions. The fakeon is purely virtual and never directly observable.
