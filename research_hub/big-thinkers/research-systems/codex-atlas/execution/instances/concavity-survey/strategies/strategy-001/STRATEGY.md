# Strategy 001: Literature Survey

## Methodology

Survey the mathematical literature on eigenvalue concavity in three focused explorations:

1. **Classical results** — Davis-Lewis, Ando, matrix convexity theorems. What's known about λ_max as a convex/concave function?
2. **Riemannian/Lie group setting** — geodesic convexity on compact groups. Relevant results from optimization on manifolds.
3. **Synthesis** — given what we found, assess whether the lattice Yang-Mills Hessian eigenvalue problem is tractable.

## Budget

3 explorations. Stop early if the answer is clear.
