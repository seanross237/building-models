# Exploration 007 — Summary

## Goal
Construct the unified QG+F–AS framework as a coherent theory statement, with regime structure, bridge mechanisms, novel predictions, and open problems.

## What Was Done
Built a 415-line framework document treating QG+F and AS as perturbative/non-perturbative descriptions of a single UV-complete quantum gravity theory, using the QCD analogy as the structural backbone. Grounded every claim in the prosecution results (explorations 001–006) and library entries with specific citations, numbers, and mechanisms.

## Outcome: SUCCEEDED

The framework has five major components:

1. **Theory statement:** QG+F (perturbative, asymptotically free, fakeon prescription) and AS (non-perturbative, NGFP, FRG) describe the same four-derivative gravity action in complementary regimes, joined at the Planck scale — the gravitational analog of Λ_QCD.

2. **Regime structure:** Six-regime phase diagram (trans-Planckian / Planck-scale / sub-Planckian / inflationary / BH exterior / BH interior) with the Planck-scale transition identified via three independent mechanisms (coupling strength, ghost mass threshold, BH branch crossing).

3. **Five bridge mechanisms:** Ghost (fakeon ↔ confinement), Fixed Point (AF ↔ NGFP), Inflation (Starobinsky ↔ NGFP-driven), Black Hole (Schwarzschild ↔ Bonanno-Reuter), Analyticity (average continuation ↔ obstructed Wick rotation).

4. **Seven novel predictions** that emerge only from unification:
   - Fakeon average continuation resolves AS's Wick rotation problem
   - Ghost confinement scale dynamically equals M_P
   - BH evaporation undergoes perturbative→non-perturbative phase transition at M ~ M_P
   - Sharpened r prediction (NGFP determines the b parameter)
   - Full d_s(E) spectral dimension profile as consistency check
   - Six-derivative couplings from NGFP truncation hierarchy
   - Higgs mass as UV boundary consistency check

5. **Open problems:** Two critical unresolved calculations — (a) the AF→NGFP connecting RG trajectory (not yet computed), (b) spin-2 ghost confinement (proven only for scalars). Both are well-defined FRG calculations.

## Key Takeaway
The unified framework is constructible, internally coherent, and makes falsifiable predictions. Its central claim — that the fakeon prescription is the perturbative avatar of ghost confinement — is the most important conjecture, and the spin-2 ghost propagator in the C²-extended FRG is the single most important open calculation. If that calculation shows the ghost pole dissolving into complex pole towers (matching Draper et al.), the framework is strongly supported. If it shows a persistent real ghost pole, the framework is in serious trouble.
