# Exploration 006 Summary: Stress-Test the Meta-Interpretation

## Goal

Hit the synthesis ("Time is irreversible determination," scored 57/65) with five adversarial attacks targeting its deepest vulnerabilities: the status of "determination" as a concept, score inflation, the Past Hypothesis reframing, dependence on real potentialities, and circularity.

## Outcome: WOUNDED BUT SURVIVING

No attack is fatal. Two partially survived (Attacks 1 and 5). Three inflict wounds requiring repair (Attacks 2, 3, 4). The revised score is **52.5/65** — down 4.5 from the claimed 57/65, but still beating both parents (CI: 47.5, CP: 48.5).

## Key Findings

1. **Most damaging discovery: the QRF-process tension (Attack 5).** The synthesis simultaneously claims becoming is ontologically real (process ontology) AND that the timeless Wheeler-DeWitt description is fundamental (QRF dissolution). These pull in opposite directions. The synthesis must take a position — process-primary or QRF-primary — rather than holding both.

2. **The synthesis is architecturally "CI + ontological upgrade."** Every improvement over CI traces to one commitment: real potentialities. Remove that commitment and the synthesis collapses to CI. This isn't fatal but must be honestly presented. The synthesis is not a transcendent meta-interpretation — it is CI with an optional ontological layer.

3. **Score inflation confirmed.** The original 57/65 was ~4.5 points too high, driven by max-function scoring (always taking the better parent's strength) and undervaluing the philosophical costs of the potentiality commitment.

4. **The Past Hypothesis reframing is verbal, not explanatory.** "Minimal initial determination" = "low entropy" with different words. The synthesis's genuine contribution to directionality is the intrinsic possibility→actuality asymmetry, not the initial condition reframing.

## Key Takeaway

The synthesis survives but must be reframed more modestly. Six mandatory modifications identified. Most critical: resolve the QRF-process tension, reframe "determination" as a bridging concept rather than a discovered primitive, and present the score as conditional on the potentiality commitment (52.5 with, ~47.5 without).
