# Exploration 001 Summary: Map the Escape Routes from the No-Go Theorem

## Goal
Systematically classify all 5 "escape routes" from the no-go theorem (which selects QG+F as the unique Lorentz-invariant, diff-invariant, renormalizable theory with d_s = 2) as EMPTY, SINGLETON, or OPEN.

## What Was Tried
Investigated each of the 5 routes via web search of 2024-2025 literature, analysis of the no-go theorem's assumptions, and assessment of existing vs. novel theory space.

## Outcome: All 5 Routes Are OPEN, But With Very Different Promise

**Ranked from most to least promising:**

1. **Route 5 (Alternative Axioms): OPEN — MOST PROMISING.** Information-theoretic constructive axioms (positivity of relative entropy, maximal vacuum entanglement, quantum focusing condition, Bianconi's entropic action) provide genuinely novel foundations that have not been systematically explored as starting points for UV-complete quantum gravity. This is the only route that changes the starting point rather than modifying assumptions within the existing framework.

2. **Route 4 (Relax Locality): OPEN — HIGH POTENTIAL.** Lee-Wick quantum gravity with meromorphic propagators (complex conjugate poles) escapes the no-go theorem entirely. It's super-renormalizable, ghost-free in the Lee-Wick sense, and distinct from QG+F at loop level. Key uncertainty: higher-loop unitarity is debated.

3. **Route 2 (Asymptotic Safety): OPEN — MEDIUM, with SINGLETON risk.** Multiple fixed points exist (SWY 2022 found both asymptotically free and asymptotically safe FPs). Bimetric structure opens unexplored theory space. But there's real risk all variations are secretly QG+F in different clothing.

4. **Route 3 (Relax d_s = 2): OPEN — VAST but UNDERCONSTRAINED.** Relaxing to d_s ∈ [1.5, 2.5] opens an enormous landscape but loses the constructive power of the sharp axiom. Best used in combination with Route 5: use alternative axioms to construct a theory, then predict d_s as an output.

5. **Route 1 (Relax Lorentz Invariance): OPEN but NARROW.** Essentially HL gravity only. GRB 221009A bounds exceed E_Pl for linear LIV, making this route experimentally hostile.

## Key Takeaway

**The most promising path to a genuinely novel quantum gravity theory is Route 5 (alternative constructive axioms), possibly combined with Route 4 (Lee-Wick propagator structure).** Specifically: start from information-theoretic principles (entropic action, maximal entanglement, quantum focusing) and see what UV completion they require. If the resulting theory naturally produces meromorphic propagators, it would be a genuinely new approach distinct from all existing programs.

## Leads Worth Pursuing

1. **Entropic-action QG** (Bianconi 2025) — very new, makes predictions, needs scrutiny
2. **Lee-Wick quantum gravity** — established formalism, under-explored as a complete QG program
3. **UV extension of Jacobson's maximal entanglement hypothesis** — framework exists, UV exploration hasn't been done
4. **Theory construction from the Holographic Emergence Bound axioms** — the axioms exist, no one has built a UV theory from them
5. **Route 5 + Route 4 hybrid**: information-theoretic axioms that naturally produce Lee-Wick-type propagators — completely unexplored territory
