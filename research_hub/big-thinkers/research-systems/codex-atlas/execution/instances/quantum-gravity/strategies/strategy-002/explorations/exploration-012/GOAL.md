# Exploration 012: QG+F and the Standard Model — Does Quantum Gravity Constrain Particle Physics?

## Mission Context

The mission seeks a theory with "breakthrough insights — new explanations for observed phenomena, new mathematical connections." QG+F's most powerful unexplored direction may be its connection to particle physics. The Shaposhnikov-Wetterich Higgs mass prediction (m_H ≈ 126 GeV from AS boundary conditions) suggests QG+F may constrain the Standard Model. This exploration investigates how far this connection goes.

## Your Specific Goal

### Task 1: QG+F + Standard Model
- How does QG+F couple to Standard Model fields?
- The quadratic gravity action allows arbitrary matter couplings. Are any constrained by renormalizability?
- Does asymptotic freedom of f₂ and f₀ constrain the number of matter fields?
- The asymptotic freedom condition: what is the maximum number of spin-0, spin-1/2, and spin-1 fields compatible with asymptotic freedom?
- Does QG+F predict the SM gauge group SU(3)×SU(2)×U(1)?

### Task 2: The Higgs Mass Connection
- Shaposhnikov-Wetterich (2010): AS boundary conditions → m_H ≈ 126 GeV (predicted before discovery)
- How does this work? What is the exact mechanism?
- Does QG+F (the perturbative face of AS) reproduce this prediction?
- Is the prediction robust against changes in the UV completion?
- What other particle masses or couplings could be predicted?

### Task 3: Agravity (Salvio-Strumia)
- "Agravity" = quadratic gravity + matter: S = S_QG + S_SM, with the claim that ALL masses (including Planck mass) arise from dimensional transmutation
- This is essentially QG+F + SM. What does it predict?
- Does it explain the hierarchy problem (why M_W << M_P)?
- What about the cosmological constant in agravity?
- Current status of the agravity program?

### Task 4: Fakeon Phenomenology at Colliders
- The fakeon (massive spin-2) has specific scattering signatures
- What is the production cross section at LHC energies?
- The "fake doublet" — additional scalar phenomenology?
- Any signatures in processes already measured? Any null results that constrain the fakeon mass?

### Task 5: Novel Connections
- Does the fakeon prescription have implications for the strong CP problem?
- Does QG+F's asymptotic freedom have implications for grand unification?
- Can the R² inflaton (the scalaron) play a role in baryogenesis?
- Any connection to dark matter (from the scalaron or fakeon)?

## Success Criteria
- Clear assessment of what QG+F predicts/constrains about the SM
- Evaluation of Shaposhnikov-Wetterich and agravity connections
- Identification of any novel particle physics predictions
