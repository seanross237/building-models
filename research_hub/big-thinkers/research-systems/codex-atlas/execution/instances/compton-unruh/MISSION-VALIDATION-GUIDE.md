# Mission Validation Guide — Compton-Unruh Resonance

## What Success Looks Like

Either a derivation showing the Compton-Unruh resonance exists and produces specific, testable predictions for galaxy dynamics — or a rigorous demonstration that it cannot work, with the physical reason identified. Either way, the result must be quantitative and defensible.

## Validation Tiers

### Tier 1: Framework
- The QFT-in-curved-spacetime setup is correctly specified (field, background geometry, detector model)
- The Unruh effect is correctly derived in the relevant background (not just quoted)
- The Compton frequency is correctly identified in the accelerating frame
- Dimensional analysis of the resonance condition is verified
- The regime of interest (a ~ cH_0) is physically motivated and correctly characterized

### Tier 2: Calculation
- The key integral or equation governing the resonance is identified and written down explicitly
- At least a partial evaluation is performed — series expansion, numerical approximation, or limiting cases
- If the full calculation is intractable, the exact point of difficulty is identified with what would be needed to proceed
- Intermediate results are cross-checked against known results (flat spacetime Unruh effect, standard Compton scattering, etc.)

### Tier 3: Physical Predictions
- If a resonance exists, the modified inertia law is derived from the physics (not assumed or fitted)
- Predicted rotation curves are computed for at least 2 galaxies with known observational data
- Predictions compared quantitatively against observations, MOND, and standard dark matter models
- Fit quality is assessed with explicit numbers

### Tier 4: Distinctness and Novelty
- The mechanism is shown to be genuinely different from McCulloch's quantized inertia (different equations, different predictions in some regime) — or shown to be equivalent
- The mechanism is compared against MOND (different acceleration-dependence, different transition function, or equivalent)
- Prior art search is thorough — checked Unruh, Milgrom, McCulloch, Verlinde, and related modified-inertia literature
- At least one observable that distinguishes this from MOND and QI is identified (or equivalence is proven)
- The result would be defensible in front of an astrophysicist or QFT specialist

### Tier 5: Consistency
- The modification doesn't break known physics — solar system dynamics, gravitational lensing, CMB power spectrum, bullet cluster
- Energy-momentum conservation and general covariance are maintained
- The strongest objection is identified and addressed
- If the resonance doesn't work, the null result is clearly stated with the physical reason — this is itself a valuable, publishable finding if done rigorously

## What "Exhausted" Looks Like

A strategy is exhausted when:
- The key calculation is either complete or definitively blocked (with the obstruction characterized)
- The relationship to MOND and McCulloch QI is settled
- Consistency checks have either passed or identified a fatal flaw
