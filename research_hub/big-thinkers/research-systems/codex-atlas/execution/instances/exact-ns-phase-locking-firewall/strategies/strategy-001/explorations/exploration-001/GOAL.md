## Required Deliverables

Your `REPORT.md` and `REPORT-SUMMARY.md` must provide:

1. One preferred intrinsic phase/coherence observable and at most one backup.
2. An explicit formula for each surviving candidate, including:
   - the exact triad population it is measured on,
   - the exact phase variable or phase concentration it uses,
   - the normalization,
   - the symmetry / gauge / conjugation convention,
   - and the exact sense in which it measures phase rather than amplitude only.
3. One invariance ledger stating what is intrinsic and what is still
   choice-dependent.
4. One sentence explaining why the preferred object does not secretly
   reintroduce packet roles, desired-channel labels, or hand-picked target
   triads.
5. One preferred delayed-transfer companion event for theorem-target phrasing:
   - reuse the existing exact `E_flux` event if the observable can be paired to
     it honestly,
   - otherwise say sharply why the pairing fails.
6. One theorem target and one counterexample target stated in terms of the
   preferred observable and the preferred event.
7. One explicit gate verdict:
   - `pass`: proceed to the smallest honest exact-support audit,
   - `fail`: stop with `definition-level failure` or `model-level failure`.

- Do not reintroduce packet roles, desired Tao edges, or hidden target-channel
  labels under new names.
- Do not accept an observable whose value changes materially under harmless
  conjugation, representative, or shell-window choices.

  a non-canonical packet model, say so sharply and treat that as failure rather
  than fake progress.
