# Mission: Exact NS Phase-Locking Firewall

## The Question

There are two prize-winning outcomes for 3D Navier-Stokes:

1. prove global regularity for all smooth divergence-free data
2. produce a genuine finite-time blowup

The current Tao-facing map is now sharp in one important sense:

- exact singleton Tao-circuit objects are impossible,
- trigger-only narrowing collapses back to the same dead object,
- clustered packet models fail canonicity,
- anti-circuit hypergraph restatements still require non-intrinsic role annotation,
- hidden-precursor physical-space observability fails because the exact balance law has no backward-memory term,
- and the helical-sign packet follow-up shows that sharper packet functionals still do not canonize the object.

So the live requirement is now:

```text
find an exact-NS-specific, intrinsic, dynamical object class that does not rely
on packet annotation or desired-channel labeling.
```

This mission tests one such object:

```text
does any Tao-like delayed forward transfer in exact NS require a quantitatively
strong phase-locking / coherence pattern across exact interacting triads, and
if so is that pattern impossible, unstable, or externally detectable?
```

The target is not a full regularity proof. The target is a concrete firewall object or a clean negative result.

## Why This Direction Is Distinct

This is not:

- another De Giorgi / epsilon-regularity mission
- another pressure mission
- another packet canonization mission
- another role-labeled Tao hypergraph mission
- another physical-space precursor mission

This is a **phase / coherence mission**. The idea is that Tao's averaged cascade may effectively bypass exact phase coordination costs that real NS cannot avoid. Exact NS transfer is not just about amplitude support; it also depends on complex phase relations inside actual triad populations.

## What Success Looks Like

One of the following:

1. **Positive phase-locking firewall**
   A concrete statement of the form:
   - any Tao-like delayed forward transfer in exact NS forces a phase-coherence observable to stay above a threshold on a stated time window,
   - or sustained low coherence rules out the delayed transfer,
   - or the required coherence is dynamically incompatible with exact NS structure.

2. **Negative kill**
   A faithful exact-NS setup or tightly justified reduced model exhibits the targeted delayed forward transfer without the claimed strong phase-locking burden, killing the route.

3. **Definition-level failure**
   No intrinsic phase-coherence observable can be defined sharply enough without smuggling back packet roles or ad hoc desired channels.

4. **Model-level failure**
   The chosen phase object is definable but does not track delayed transfer in any faithful exact-NS test.

## What Failure Looks Like

Any of these is a valid negative result:

- "phase coherence" is left metaphorical rather than tied to one explicit quantity,
- the object secretly depends on packet labels or a chosen Tao-like role graph,
- the test reduces to generic turbulence folklore rather than an exact-NS statement,
- or the reduced model discards the very phase structure the mission is supposed to study.

## Established Results (Do Not Re-Derive)

- De Giorgi `beta = 4/3` is sharp within that framework
- epsilon-regularity family appears structurally capped
- `H^1` pressure route is dead at the `W^{1,3}` wall
- harmonic far-field pressure loophole is closed
- Tao's averaged-NS blowup is mechanistically a five-mode delayed-threshold circuit
- exact singleton Tao-circuit embedding is structurally impossible
- trigger-only narrowing does not produce a new faithful exact object
- clustered packet models fail canonicity
- anti-circuit hypergraph restatements still require non-intrinsic role annotation
- hidden-precursor observability failed despite finding a real exact-NS event object
- helical-sign packet refinement still failed at the canonicity gate

This mission starts after all that.

## What Must Be Avoided

- Do **not** reintroduce packet roles under the name of "coherent mode families" without saying so.
- Do **not** treat generic resonance or turbulence heuristics as theorem content.
- Do **not** allow more than one primary phase-coherence observable and at most one backup.
- Do **not** drift into purely spectral support bookkeeping with no phase variable left.
- Do **not** attempt a full regularity proof or a full blowup construction.

## Core Mission Structure

### Step 1: Define one intrinsic phase-coherence object

Choose one preferred exact-NS phase/coherence observable that is intrinsic enough to be theorem-facing.

Examples of acceptable types:

- a triad-phase alignment observable built directly from exact helical Fourier amplitudes,
- a scale-local phase concentration quantity on exact transfer populations,
- a coherence defect measuring cancellation versus constructive forward transfer on exact triads.

The observable must be:

- explicit,
- intrinsic to exact NS dynamics,
- not dependent on post hoc Tao-role labeling,
- and meaningful under the natural symmetries.

**Deliverable:** one preferred phase-coherence observable and at most one backup.

**Kill condition:** if the observable cannot be defined intrinsically without smuggling in packet or role annotation, stop with `definition-level failure`.

### Step 2: Define one delayed-transfer test

Fix one delayed forward-transfer event or target phenomenon that the phase object is supposed to control.

This can be:

- an intrinsic forward-transfer burst across scales,
- a sustained net transfer direction on an exact triad population,
- or another exact-NS transfer event that is faithful to the Tao mechanism without requiring role labels.

**Deliverable:** one preferred transfer event with exact-NS fidelity ledger.

### Step 3: Test whether delayed transfer forces coherence

Ask whether the preferred transfer event can occur while the phase-coherence observable stays small.

Acceptable outputs are:

- a lower bound on coherence during the transfer window,
- an incompatibility statement,
- a faithful counterexample,
- or a structural reason the phase object is not connected strongly enough to delayed transfer.

**Deliverable:** one theorem candidate or one counterexample candidate.

### Step 4: Adversarial screen

Stress test any positive result against:

- symmetry reductions that trivialize the phase observable,
- changes of Fourier gauge or representative choices,
- generic high-amplitude transfer without true phase locking,
- and any possibility that the same effect survives Tao-style averaging.

Stress test any negative result against:

- hidden packet dependence,
- phase observables that were too weak or too coarse,
- or reduced-model artifacts.

### Step 5: Final report

The mission must end in exactly one of:

1. **positive phase-locking firewall**
2. **counterexample: delayed transfer without strong phase locking**
3. **definition-level failure**
4. **model-level failure**

## Budget

4-6 explorations.

Expected shape:

- 1 exploration for intrinsic phase-object definition,
- 1 exploration for delayed-transfer test definition,
- 1-2 explorations for quantitative forcing or counterexample,
- 1 adversarial screen.
