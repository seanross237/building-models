# Meta-Learning Note: Exploration 001 (Strategy-002) — QD↔HQEC Literature Search

## What Worked Well

**Specific paper list in GOAL.md was essential.** Providing 14 specific papers (with arXiv IDs) plus 15 required search queries made the explorer's work concrete and measurable. The explorer checked every single one and produced a paper-by-paper verdict. Generic "search exhaustively" instructions don't work — specific names do.

**HIGH/MEDIUM/LOW novelty verdict format.** The explicit three-level novelty scale forced the explorer to commit to a confidence level rather than hedge. The result was a clear "HIGH CONFIDENCE NOVELTY" verdict rather than vague hedging.

**Requiring documentation of "adjacent" papers** (not just the connection itself, but papers in the neighborhood) was valuable — Ferté & Cao and the AJMP 2026 paper were flagged as adjacent, which is genuinely useful for citation context.

**Incremental writing instruction worked.** The report (459 lines) was written section by section. No stalling to compose entire report at once.

## What Didn't Work / Lessons

**Nudging was needed.** The explorer was at 0% for 3+ minutes before responding to a nudge. This is consistent with strategy-001 meta-lessons. Always plan to nudge at the 3-minute mark.

**The "gaps" section in the goal produced exactly what was needed.** Asking for "at least 3 places where the mapping is approximate" forced the explorer to be critical rather than just cheerleading the connection. This should be standard for all formal mapping explorations.

## Unexpected Value

The HaPPY code achieving EXACTLY 50% of the budget (not approximately) was flagged as an unexpected finding. The exact threshold structure of perfect tensors (reconstructable iff fraction > 1/2) is a mathematical fact that makes this a provable theorem, not just a numerical coincidence. This should be added to the library as a confirmed theorem.

## Scope Assessment

Scope was right. One exploration for literature + formalization was the correct allocation. The resulting 459-line report is thorough without being bloated.
