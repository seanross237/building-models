# The Causal-Processual Interpretation of Time: Full Construction

## Summary

Selected as the strongest challenger to the computational interpretation of time (see [time-interpretation-challenger-survey.md](time-interpretation-challenger-survey.md) for selection rationale). Synthesizes causal set theory (Sorkin/Maudlin), process metaphysics (Whitehead), thermodynamic irreversibility (Prigogine), temporal naturalism (Smolin), and temporal phenomenology (Husserl/Bergson) around a **single unifying primitive: actualization** — the irreversible creative transition from indeterminate possibility to determinate fact.

**Core claim:** Time is the ongoing process of actualization — the irreversible creative transition from indeterminate possibility to determinate fact. Each actualization event inherits its causal past, integrates this data through concrescence, produces a novel determination, and perishes into objectivity — becoming part of the settled past from which future events will actualize. The causal order of these events constitutes a directed, one-dimensional, relativistically-structured partial order. The experience of temporal flow IS this process as encountered from within. The "now" is not a slice but the locus of active becoming. The arrow is the direction from potential to actual. The future is genuinely open because actualization is creative. Time dilation is the variation in the density of actualization events along different worldlines.

## The Unifying Principle: Actualization

**Definition:** Actualization is the process by which an indeterminate possibility becomes a determinate fact. It is: (1) **irreversible** — once a potential is actualized, alternatives are extinguished; (2) **creative** — the outcome is not fully determined by antecedent conditions; (3) **relational** — it occurs in the context of a causal past; (4) **constitutive of time** — actualization IS what temporal passage consists of.

### How the four traditions map onto actualization

Each tradition describes a *different aspect* of the same primitive operation:

- **Causal Set Theory (Sorkin, Rideout):** Each birth event in sequential growth dynamics IS an actualization. Baron & Le Bihan (2025, *Foundations of Physics* 55:63) show CST involves relations of *actual causation* — elements bringing one another into existence through genuine productive causation, not mere causal connectibility.
- **Whiteheadian Process Metaphysics:** Concrescence IS actualization. Each actual occasion inherits (prehends) its causal past, integrates the data into a new unity guided by subjective aim, reaches satisfaction (full determinacy), and perishes into objectivity.
- **Prigoginian Thermodynamics:** The "physics of becoming" describes the irreversible emergence of novel structures. Poincaré resonances in nonintegrable systems create genuine singularities making dynamics inherently irreversible. Non-unitary transformations formalize the transition "from groups to semigroups, from trajectories to processes."
- **Smolin's Temporal Naturalism:** Even laws participate in actualization. "All that is real are causal processes that continually make definite facts out of previously indefinite possibilities." Cosmological natural selection: each universe-birth through a black hole is actualization at the largest scale.

### The quantum connection: Heisenberg's potentia

The actualization primitive receives independent support from quantum foundations. Heisenberg interpreted the quantum state via Aristotle's *potentia*: the wave function describes objective potentialities; measurement is the transition from potential to actual. Kastner & Kauffman (2017, arXiv:1709.03595) develop this explicitly as an "ontological duality of res extensa and res potentia." Quantum measurement IS actualization — the same kind of event as a CST birth, Whiteheadian concrescence, and Prigoginian bifurcation.

## Six-Feature Explanations

### 1. One-Dimensionality (Score: 4/5)

Arises from the seriality constraint on actualization: (a) **Topological** — in a Lorentzian manifold, timelike directions form a single connected component; in a causal set, every maximal chain is linearly ordered (one dimension) while antichains can be arbitrarily large (many dimensions). (b) **Processual** — each actualization must integrate ALL causal past into a single outcome; you cannot concresce "in two directions at once." (c) **Information-theoretic** — a fact must be a single consistent determination; multi-dimensional time would allow partial determinacy, undermining actuality itself.

**Physics anchor:** Malament's theorem (causal bijection → conformal isomorphism); one-dimensionality is inherent in what a causal partial order IS.

### 2. Irreversibility (Score: 5/5, upgraded from 4)

Actualization is inherently one-way: (a) **Ontological** — reversal would require "re-opening" a single actuality into many potentialities, but the information about which potentialities existed was genuinely extinguished. (b) **Causal-structural** — CST's antisymmetric partial order ≺ is not a convention; Baron & Le Bihan (2025) show elements bring one another into existence through actual causation, inherently asymmetric. (c) **Prigoginian** — Poincaré resonances prevent unitary transformations diagonalizing nonintegrable dynamics; evolution admits only semigroup (one-way), not group (reversible). (d) **Generalized second law** — each actualization increases the world's definiteness; thermodynamic entropy increase is a *consequence* of this deeper principle.

**Key advantage over CI:** No Past Hypothesis needed for direction. The LBB mechanism (CI) is a *consequence* of the fundamental irreversibility of actualization.

### 3. Directionality / Arrow (Score: 5/5 — Strongest Feature)

Triple-grounded, no Past Hypothesis needed:
- **Causal direction:** The partial order has built-in direction. If a ≺ b, then a's actuality contributed to b's actualization.
- **Processual direction:** Always from less-determined to more-determined. Past is settled (fully actual); future is potential (indeterminate).
- **Thermodynamic direction (derived):** Increasing actualization → increasing correlations → entropy increase. The thermodynamic arrow is derived from the processual arrow, reversing the standard explanatory order.

**No Past Hypothesis needed:** Microdynamics are NOT time-symmetric — actualization is inherently directional. The initial low-entropy state is a *consequence* of minimal initial actuality (few facts settled, few correlations), not an unexplained brute fact.

### 4. Open Future (Score: 4/5)

Three layers of openness: (a) **Processual creativity (Whitehead)** — causal past underdetermines outcome; subjective aim involves genuine decision, not randomness. (b) **Stochastic dynamics (CST)** — Rideout-Sorkin sequential growth involves probabilistic choice at each birth; genuinely indeterministic. (c) **Law evolution (Smolin)** — parameters themselves are products of actualization; future open at the deepest level. (d) **Quantum connection** — quantum indeterminacy is the natural expression of the open future at the microphysical level; Born rule gives probabilities but doesn't determine outcomes.

**Advantage over CI:** CI explains openness via negative results (computational irreducibility, Wolpert's theorem). CP provides a *positive* account: the future is open because actualization is creative. CI explains why we *can't predict*; CP explains why the future *isn't there to be predicted*.

### 5. Flow (Score: 3.5/5, post-attack revision from 4/5)

Flow IS actualization as encountered by an actualizing subject. **Post-attack three-level framework** (see [cp-adversarial-assessment.md](cp-adversarial-assessment.md)):

**Level 1 (minimal, no controversy):** Continuous succession of actualization events, each inheriting predecessors' achievements. Third-person structural fact — flow is that actualization keeps happening. At this level, CP's flow account is roughly equivalent to CI's.

**Level 2 (moderate, defensible — THE SWEET SPOT):** Complex actualization patterns in biological systems produce temporal experience with Husserlian retention-protention structure through functional emergence. CP explains WHY: the brain's temporal processing mirrors reality's actualization structure. Better than CI but faces a version of the hard problem (why does the right functional architecture produce experience?).

**Level 3 (maximal, controversial):** Full panexperientialism — experience constitutive of actualization. The concrescence-specious-present **identity claim** (concrescence and the specious present ARE the same thing) requires this level. Most elegant (dissolves hard problem) but most controversial.

**The Husserlian bridge (strongest at Level 3, suggestive at Level 2):**
- Prehension of causal past → Retention
- Process of integration (concrescence) → Primal impression
- Subjective aim toward satisfaction → Protention
- Perishing into objectivity → Fading of retention

**Bergson's durée** as the qualitative character of actualization: lived duration (not clock-time) is what concrescence feels like from inside. Science measures *temps*; underlying reality is *durée*.

**Self-defeating argument:** Denying flow requires temporal processing, which IS actualization, which IS flow.

**Post-attack note:** The E002 identity claim ("concrescence and the specious present ARE the same thing") implicitly depends on panexperientialism (Level 3). Without it, the bridge weakens to "analogous structure" — weaker but still better than CI's account. Flow score downgraded from 4/5 to 3.5/5 at Level 2.

### 6. Dilation (Score: 4/5, upgraded from 3)

Time dilation is variation in the *density of actualization events* along different worldlines.

**(a) CST foundation:** Proper time along a worldline ∝ length of longest chain (Myrheim conjecture, confirmed in flat spacetime sprinklings). The hauptvermutung: number of elements ∝ spacetime volume. Different worldlines between the same events traverse different numbers of elements — this IS time dilation.

**(b) Gravitational dilation:** Near a massive object, timelike worldlines have smaller proper time → fewer causal set elements → fewer actualization events → clock ticks slower because there is literally *less becoming*.

**(c) Velocity-dependent dilation:** Moving worldline traverses fewer elements between same spacelike surfaces (twin paradox geometry). Fewer actualization events = less elapsed time.

**(d) Ontological interpretation:** Dilation is *real variation in the rate of becoming*, not a mere coordinate effect. A being on a lower-proper-time worldline genuinely experiences fewer moments of becoming.

**Physics anchor:** Malament's theorem, hauptvermutung, chain length proportionality (confirmed in flat spacetime).

## The "Now" — Perspectival-Processual Account

**(a) Each actualization event IS a now.** The "now" is not a global feature of the universe but a feature of each actualization event individually. While an occasion is concrescing, it IS the present. After satisfaction and perishing into objectivity, it is past.

**(b) No global now, no problem.** Compatible with special relativity: the "now" is local and perspectival, not global and absolute. Each worldline has its own sequence of actualization events, its own series of "nows."

**(c) Why THIS now?** "Now" is not a property of a moment but a feature of a process. The question "why is this moment the present?" has the answer: because this is the actualization event I currently AM. Like asking "why am I this person?" — presupposes a view from outside the process.

**(d) Advantage over CI:** CI has indexical "present" (step currently executing) but in a block universe all steps exist equally. CP has ontological distinction: past = settled (fully actual), present = actively becoming, future = merely potential. This gives the "now" metaphysical reality CI lacks.

## Honest Weaknesses (Post-Attack Revision)

### 1. Mixed Physics Grounding — WOUNDED (Attack 2)
CP's physics content is thinner than its packaging suggests. **Physics backbone:** CST only (speculative, zero experimental confirmation, swerves severely constrained at k ≤ 10⁻⁵⁶ kg²m²s⁻³). **Philosophy:** Whitehead and Husserl provide load-bearing concepts but zero equations. **Contested physics:** Prigogine's fundamental irreversibility is a minority position ("the rest of the profession versus Prigogine" — Bricmont 1996, Drouet & Lippens 2025). CP should present itself as a **metaphysical interpretation with physics anchors**, not as a physics-grounded theory.

### 2. Panexperientialism Dependency — WOUNDED (Attack 4)
The concrescence-specious-present identity claim ("we ARE the process of actualization") requires panexperientialism (Level 3). Without it (Level 2, functional emergence), the bridge weakens from identity to analogy. Flow score downgraded 4→3.5. CP should present the three-level framework explicitly, claiming the identity as strongest version, not the only one.

### 3. Near-Total Structural Isomorphism with CI — PARTIALLY SURVIVED (Attack 1)
The CP-CI convergence is much deeper than E002 acknowledged. Every structural feature of CP maps precisely onto a CI feature. The "creativity vs. mechanism" framing is largely rhetorical — computational irreducibility captures most of CP's "creativity." **Precise forks that save CP from identity with CI:** (a) **Ontological status of potentia** (STRONG): CP says quantum potentialities are ontologically real before measurement; CI treats them as epistemic. (b) **Direction of explanation** (MODERATE): CP says time→computation; CI says computation→time. CP must own this convergence and lead with its precise forks, not claim global superiority.

### 4. Lack of Mathematical Formalization
No mathematical framework integrating processual becoming with causal set dynamics. Baron & Le Bihan (2025) provide a philosophical bridge (actual causation in CST ≈ Whiteheadian prehension) but not a mathematical one.

### 5. Block Universe Stalemate (Attack 3 — PARTIALLY SURVIVED)
CP has a genuinely strong response to Rietdijk-Putnam (becoming structured by causal order, not simultaneity — Lorentz-invariant). But the deeper question (is CST growth real becoming or mathematical construction of a static structure?) is a genuine stalemate that physics alone cannot resolve.
