# Adversarial Assessment of the Unified QG+F--AS Framework

## Summary

Systematic devil's advocate attack on the unified framework claiming QG+F and AS are the same UV-complete theory. Seven attacks deployed, rated by severity. 5-tier validation performed. **Overall verdict: VIABLE BUT UNSUBSTANTIATED.** Three SERIOUS attacks, four MODERATE attacks; 5-tier results: Novel MARGINAL, Consistent PASS, Explanatory MARGINAL, Predictive FAIL, Testable MARGINAL. The framework survives devil's advocacy but barely — and only because it makes no claims strong enough to be definitively refuted.

## Attack Summary

| # | Attack | Rating | Key Issue |
|---|--------|--------|-----------|
| 1 | Tautology | **MODERATE** | ~60% trivially true (same action → same results), ~40% genuine conjecture (ghost bridge, fixed point connection, analyticity resolution) |
| 2 | QCD analogy broken | **SERIOUS** | Load-bearing analogy fails at its most critical joints — confinement mechanism has no gravitational analog |
| 3 | INCONCLUSIVE results may be failures | **SERIOUS** | Central conjectures (connecting trajectory, spin-2 confinement) unresolved after a decade; mildly negative evidence |
| 4 | Unfalsifiable predictions | **SERIOUS** | No prediction that is simultaneously novel to the unified framework AND experimentally testable AND numerically specific |
| 5 | Shared features ≠ same theory | **MODERATE** | More parsimonious "compatible but separate" interpretation exists; unification is underdetermined |
| 6 | Circular average continuation | **MODERATE** | Circularity breakable in principle but untested; Lorentzian AS (D'Angelo et al. 2024) may make it unnecessary |
| 7 | No expert endorsement | **MODERATE** | Neither QG+F nor AS community endorses the specific unified synthesis |

## Attack 1: The Tautology Attack (MODERATE)

The framework is ~60% trivially true and ~40% genuine conjecture. The trivially true part: writing down the four-derivative action and studying it perturbatively (QG+F) vs. non-perturbatively (AS) will OF COURSE give "the same theory." Shared Starobinsky inflation and d_s → 2 follow from the same action, not from a deep insight.

The QCD analogy masks this: nobody says "perturbative QCD and lattice QCD are the same theory" as a discovery — they're both QCD. The real question is whether they *must* be, not whether they *could* be.

**Non-trivial content (the ~40%):** (1) The ghost mechanism — fakeon vs. confinement being the same process at different couplings, unproven. (2) The fixed point question — whether AF = NGFP or AF connects to NGFP, a real mathematical question. (3) The analyticity bridge — average continuation resolving AS's Wick rotation, a concrete testable claim.

## Attack 2: QCD Analogy Broken (SERIOUS)

The analogy fails on the specific points that matter most for the confinement claim:

**(a) No compact gauge group (HIGH).** QCD confinement tied to SU(3) center symmetry Z(3). Wilson loop area law depends on compactness. Gravity's diffeomorphism group is non-compact and infinite-dimensional. No gravitational Wilson loop with area law. No center symmetry. The entire mathematical machinery of confinement (center vortices, monopoles, dual superconductor) has no gravitational analog. **Removes the primary theoretical mechanism for confinement.**

**(b) No color charge analog (HIGH).** QCD confines colored objects via color quantum numbers; only color-singlet combinations appear as physical states. The spin-2 ghost carries no analogous quantum number. No selection rule separates "ghost-like" from "graviton-like" at the non-perturbative level. "Confining the ghost" has no rigorous meaning without a quantum number to enforce it.

**(c) Universal coupling (MODERATE).** In QCD, color-neutral objects decouple from the strong force. Gravity couples universally — no analog of "color-neutral." Transition between perturbative/non-perturbative regimes affects everything.

**(d) No lattice proof (HIGH).** After 10+ years of the analogy being proposed (Holdom-Ren 2015), zero non-perturbative evidence specific to the gravitational spin-2 ghost. CDT exists but doesn't include C² and doesn't address ghost confinement. Conformal mode problem makes naive lattice approaches ill-defined.

**(e) Conformal mode problem (MODERATE).** Euclidean gravitational action unbounded below. No QCD analog. Techniques that work in QCD face qualitatively new obstacles.

**(f) Ghost is UV-specific (LOW).** Ghost only exists in 4-derivative theory, not at low energies. Confinement must operate only at E ~ M_P, unlike QCD. (This is actually a feature, not a bug.)

**(g) Non-perturbative uncertainty (MODERATE).** Anselmi (JHEP 01, 2026, 104) acknowledged non-perturbative effects introduce "a new type of uncertainty." In QCD, the non-perturbative sector is precisely defined (lattice); in QG+F, it is acknowledged to be uncertain.

**Cumulative assessment:** The three highest-severity breakdowns (a, b, d) all concern the confinement mechanism itself. Without confinement established, the QCD parallel is decorative, not structural. The framework needs the analogy to be load-bearing but it is broken at its load-bearing joints.

## Attack 3: INCONCLUSIVE Results May Be Failures (SERIOUS)

**Fixed point trajectory:** SWY (2022) found two distinct fixed points but did NOT compute a connecting trajectory. SWY (2023) computed AF → IR flows but did not check whether these pass near the NGFP. The FRG community has been computing trajectories in higher-derivative gravity for over a decade — no connecting trajectory found. Plausible defense: full four-coupling phase space has NOT been exhaustively mapped, truncation limitations may explain absence. But if trajectory doesn't exist, the SWY version of the framework is dead; the Codello-Percacci version (one fixed point) survives but reduces to near-tautology.

**Spin-2 ghost confinement:** Becker et al. (2017) scalar ghost decoupling is 9 years old. Has NOT been extended to spin-2 in the intervening decade. Draper et al. (2020) complex pole tower is for a general truncation, not specifically the Stelle ghost. Why hasn't the obvious calculation been done? Either genuinely very hard (generous) or preliminary attempts didn't give the "right" answer (cynical but not unreasonable given publication bias).

After a decade, absence of results is mildly negative evidence. The framework's viability hinges entirely on these two unresolved calculations.

## Attack 4: Unfalsifiable Predictions (SERIOUS)

Testability assessment of the 7 novel predictions:

| # | Prediction | Testable? | When? | Novel to unified? |
|---|-----------|-----------|-------|-------------------|
| 1 | Average continuation resolves AS Wick rotation | Theoretical computation | Now | Yes, but untested |
| 2 | Ghost confinement scale = M_P | No (Planck-scale) | Never (foreseeable) | Yes |
| 3 | BH evaporation phase transition | Planck-mass BHs needed | PBH 2030s-40s (speculative) | Yes |
| 4 | Sharpened r prediction | Yes (CMB) | LiteBIRD ~2036-37 | **Weak**: without NGFP b computation, no sharper than QG+F alone |
| 5 | Spectral dimension profile matching | Requires lattice 4-deriv gravity | Doesn't exist | Theoretical consistency check |
| 6 | Six-derivative couplings from NGFP | Theoretical computation | Now | Theoretical consistency check |
| 7 | Higgs mass consistency | Already tested (retrodiction) | Already happened | Not a prediction |

**No prediction meets all three criteria:** novel to the unified framework, experimentally accessible before ~2040, and numerically specific. The framework inherits the "one-prediction problem" from standalone QG+F.

## Attack 5: Compatible-But-Separate Alternative (MODERATE)

A more parsimonious interpretation: QG+F and AS are two compatible but separate theories that start from the same action, apply different quantization procedures, get compatible results in overlap regions (because same action), and potentially diverge in non-overlap regions. Under this reading, all shared features are trivially explained. The "compatible-but-separate" and "unified" interpretations are empirically indistinguishable without the missing calculations (joint constraints require NGFP b computation, etc.).

## Attack 6: Circular Average Continuation (MODERATE)

The claim "average continuation resolves AS's Wick rotation problem" assumes what it's trying to prove: applying QG+F's quantization scheme to AS presupposes QG+F is correct for the non-perturbative theory — which is the question being answered. Circularity breakable if average continuation is tested independently (apply to AS calculation, check physical sensibility). This test has not been performed.

**Additional problem:** Lorentzian AS (D'Angelo et al. 2024) works directly in Lorentzian signature without any Wick rotation. If AS can solve its own problem without QG+F's help, the "novel prediction" dissolves.

## Attack 7: No Expert Endorsement (MODERATE)

**Anselmi (QG+F architect):** Has NOT endorsed QG+F = AS. Recent publications focus on asymptotically local QFT, classicized dynamics, causality abandonment — does NOT point to AS as non-perturbative completion. Actively avoids engaging with AS.

**Reuter/Wetterich (AS side):** Treat four-derivative gravity as a truncation within AS, not a separate theory to unify with. SWY (2022) explicitly frame AF as "a viable *alternative* to asymptotic safety" — not a complement. Platania-Wetterich fictitious ghosts (2020) suggests ghost is a truncation artifact, making fakeon prescription *unnecessary* rather than complementary.

**Salvio:** Calls QQG "a concrete realization of asymptotic safety" — but this is the Codello-Percacci one-fixed-point picture, closer to tautology (Attack 1) than to the SWY-based unification.

**Holdom-Ren:** Closest to the unified framework's vision (explicit QCD analogy, ghost confinement). But describe this as a "conjecture" and acknowledge lack of evidence. Inspirational, not confirmatory.

**No leading researcher in either program endorses the specific unified framework.** Novel frameworks are often initially unendorsed, but this should temper confidence.

| Consistent | **PASS** | No internal contradictions; unproven assumptions acknowledged |
| Explanatory | **MARGINAL** | BH evaporation phase transition is best genuinely novel explanation; rest contingent on unproven conjectures |
| Predictive | **FAIL** | No prediction both novel to framework AND experimentally accessible AND numerically specific |
| Testable | **MARGINAL** | CMB r is testable but doesn't discriminate framework from components |

## Three Calculations That Would Change Everything

1. **AF → NGFP trajectory** in full four-coupling FRG: Exists → Attack 3 downgrades. Doesn't exist in SWY picture → framework needs Codello-Percacci (near-tautology).
2. **Spin-2 ghost propagator** in C²-extended FRG: Ghost pole dissolves → Attacks 2, 3 downgrade. Ghost pole persists → framework effectively dead.
3. **NGFP b parameter** for inflation: Specific r distinguishable from standalone QG+F → Attack 4 downgrades. b ≈ 0 → prediction collapses to standalone Starobinsky.

## Overall Verdict: VIABLE BUT UNSUBSTANTIATED

The framework is internally consistent and intellectually interesting. Not killed by any single attack. But severely weakened by cumulative weight: the QCD analogy (structural backbone) is broken at its critical joint (confinement); two central conjectures are unsupported after a decade; no prediction distinguishes it from the simpler compatible-but-separate hypothesis. Its fate depends entirely on two unperformed calculations.

**Should be treated as a speculative hypothesis, not a framework.**
