# Adversarial Assessment of the Causal-Processual Interpretation

## Summary

Systematic adversarial attack on the Causal-Processual (CP) interpretation of time across four dimensions: (1) convergence with the Computational Interpretation (CI), (2) physics vacuum, (3) block universe response, (4) panexperientialism dependency. **Verdict: CP is "wounded but competitive"** — it is a genuine competitor to CI, not a clone, but its advantages are narrower and vulnerabilities greater than the E002 construction report acknowledged. Overall score: 26 → 25 (modest wound, not destruction).

## Attack Summary and Verdicts

| # | Attack | Verdict | Severity |
|---|--------|---------|----------|
| 1 | Convergence with CI | PARTIALLY SURVIVED | One clear fork (ontological potentia) saves CP from identity with CI, but structural isomorphism is real and troubling |
| 2 | Physics Vacuum | WOUNDED | CP's physics is thinner than presented: 1 unconfirmed framework + 1 contested position + 3 philosophical traditions |
| 3 | Block Universe | PARTIALLY SURVIVED | Causal-order response to Rietdijk-Putnam is strong; deeper stalemate (growth vs. construction) is inherent |
| 4 | Panexperientialism | WOUNDED | Full flow advantage requires panexperientialism; without it, advantage over CI is moderate, not decisive |

## Attack 1: Convergence with CI

### The Structural Isomorphism

CP and CI map onto each other with near-total precision:

| CP Concept | CI Concept | Structural Role |
|-----------|-----------|----------------|
| Potential → Actual | Input → Output | Irreversible state transition |
| Concrescence | Computation | Data synthesis into determinate result |
| Creativity (underdetermined outcome) | Computational irreducibility (unpredictable) | Open future / unpredictability |
| Causal past (prehended data) | Input state | What the process works with |
| Satisfaction (determinate outcome) | Halting state | Terminal condition |
| Irreversibility of actualization | Landauer's principle | One-way character |
| Serial actualization | Margolus-Levitin bound | One-dimensionality |
| Perishing into objectivity | Output → next input | Compositionality |

The "creativity vs. mechanism" framing from E002 is **largely rhetorical** — computational irreducibility captures most of what CP means by "creativity." Indeterministic computation (quantum computation, probabilistic Turing machines) absorbs the randomness that CP calls "creativity." The claim that CP's "genuine novelty" exceeds computational irreducibility is **unfalsifiable** — no experiment can distinguish a computationally irreducible deterministic process from a genuinely creative indeterministic one.

### Three Genuine Forks

Despite the isomorphism, three real (non-verbal) differences exist:

**Fork 1: Ontological status of potentiality — STRONG.** CP says quantum potentialities are ontologically real before measurement (Heisenberg's potentia). CI says they are epistemic or computational (paths not taken). This gives different answers to: "Before quantum measurement, are unobserved outcomes real?" Connects to real stakes in QM interpretation (objective collapse vs. computational/informational readings).

**Fork 2: Direction of explanation — MODERATE.** CP: time is fundamental, computation derivative. CI: computation is fundamental, time derivative. Test question: "Is radioactive decay a computation?" CI must say yes (Church-Turing-Deutsch thesis). CP says no — it's actualization without an algorithm. But the CI advocate has room to maneuver if "computation" is defined broadly enough.

**Fork 3: Nature of the subject — WEAK AS DIFFERENTIATOR.** CP: each actualization has internal character (Whitehead's subjective aim). CI: subjectivity emerges from computation. This is really the panexperientialism commitment (Attack 4). Differentiates only if panexperientialism is accepted.

### Verdict

CP and CI are NOT the same interpretation in two costumes — Fork 1 (potentia) is a clear, non-verbal divergence. But CP's claimed advantages over CI (deeper directionality, better flow) are partly artifacts of Aristotelian vocabulary, not genuine theoretical differences. CP must own this convergence honestly and identify precisely where the forks are.

## Attack 2: Physics Vacuum

### Component-by-Component Inventory

| Component | Type | Equations? | Predictions? | Experimental Status |
|-----------|------|-----------|-------------|-------------------|
| CST (Sorkin, Rideout) | Speculative physics | Yes | Swerves (severely constrained: k ≤ 10⁻⁵⁶ kg²m²s⁻³) | No confirmation |
| Whitehead | Philosophy | No | No | N/A |
| Prigogine (late work) | Contested physics | Yes (non-unitary Λ) | None unique | Rejected by majority |
| Husserl | Philosophy | No | No | N/A |
| Smolin (temporal naturalism) | Philosophical stance | No (borrows from others) | CNS (weakened) | No confirmation |

**CST is the ONLY real physics in CP.** If CST falls, CP loses its physics backbone. The swerves parameter k ≤ 10⁻⁵⁶ kg²m²s⁻³ from spontaneous heating bounds on hydrogen gas. Cosmic ray swerves ruled out as inconsistent with laboratory bounds.

**Prigogine's fundamental irreversibility is a minority position.** Bricmont (1996, "Science of Chaos or Chaos in Science?") and Drouet & Lippens (2025, "Prigogine's temporalization of physics"): the mathematical transformation theory "failed to gain widespread acceptance." The reception was essentially "the rest of the profession versus Prigogine."

**CP's central concepts (concrescence, actualization, prehension, flow) are purely philosophical** — from Whitehead and Husserl. These are load-bearing concepts with no mathematical structure and no empirical commitments.

**Comparison with CI:** CI is built on established physics — Margolus-Levitin bound (theorem from Schrödinger equation), Landauer's principle (experimentally confirmed), Bekenstein bound (derived from GR + thermodynamics). CP's physics is thinner.

### Legitimate Counterpoints

1. **"Established" ≠ "deeper."** CI's physics is established but doesn't push into new territory. CP gambles on deeper physics (CST describes fundamental spacetime structure). Risk vs. safety.
2. **"What is time?" is irreducibly philosophical.** CI ALSO makes philosophical claims (time IS computation — a metaphysical identification, not a theorem). CP is more explicit about its philosophy; CI hides its philosophy behind established physics.
3. **CP makes testable predictions; CI makes none.** Despite CI's stronger grounding, it makes zero novel predictions. CP through CST at least predicts discreteness, swerves (even if constrained), and through Smolin, CNS.

### Verdict

CP is **wounded**. Its packaging is misleading — presenting itself as drawing on "five traditions" makes it sound physics-rich, when it draws on one unconfirmed physics framework, one contested position, and three philosophical traditions. **Required repair:** rebrand as "metaphysical interpretation with physics anchors."

## Attack 3: Block Universe

### Rietdijk-Putnam Response — EXCELLENT

CP's strongest defense. The standard argument ("relativity refutes becoming") assumes becoming must track simultaneity surfaces. CP rejects this: becoming is structured by **causal order**, not simultaneity. The causal past/future distinction is Lorentz-invariant. For spacelike-separated events, there is simply no becoming-relation — becoming is local and relational, not global.

The CST framework makes this precise: element a has become relative to element b if a ≺ b — observer-independent. This is a genuine philosophical achievement showing the "relativity refutes becoming" argument is weaker than commonly supposed.

### Growth Dynamics vs. Mathematical Construction — STALEMATE

The deepest objection: CST's discrete general covariance means birth order is gauge (probability depends only on the partial order, not the labeling). Does this make growth unphysical?

**CP response:** Diffeomorphism invariance in GR means coordinates are gauge, but spacetime is real. Similarly, CST labeling is gauge, but the growth process IS the dynamics. Baron & Le Bihan (2025) strengthen this: CST elements bring one another into existence through **actual causation** (productive causation, not mere connectibility). The integers analogy fails: 3 does not causally produce 4, but in CST element a can causally produce element b.

**Block response:** Causal production relations are real but exist all at once. Production is a structural feature, not a temporal process.

**Assessment:** Genuine stalemate. The physics is consistent with both interpretations. CP cannot prove becoming is real; the block advocate cannot prove it isn't. No repair needed — the stalemate is inherent.

## Attack 4: Panexperientialism Dependency

### The Hidden Dependence

CP's concrescence-specious-present bridge ("we ARE the process of actualization; flow IS what actualization is like from inside") requires that actualization has an "inside" — that there is something it is like to undergo concrescence. This requires Whitehead's panexperientialism (every actual occasion has experience). Without it, the bridge collapses to a third-person structural analogy.

**The dialectical trap:** CP's claimed strongest advantage (flow) depends on its most controversial premise (panexperientialism). The weapon is also the wound.

### Three-Level Flow Framework (Repair)

CP can decouple flow from panexperientialism:

**Level 1 (minimal, no controversy):** Flow = ongoing structural succession of actualization events. Third-person, no experience required. At this level, CP's flow account is roughly equivalent to CI's.

**Level 2 (moderate, defensible):** Complex actualization patterns in biological systems produce temporal experience with Husserlian retention-protention structure through functional emergence. Better than CI (CP explains WHY the functional structure produces temporal experience: it mirrors reality's actualization structure) but faces a version of the hard problem.

**Level 3 (maximal, controversial):** Panexperientialism — experience constitutive of actualization. Most elegant (dissolves hard problem) but most controversial.

### Verdict

E002's identity claim ("concrescence and the specious present ARE the same thing") requires Level 3. Without it (Level 2), the claim weakens to "analogous structure." CP still has a better flow account than CI at Level 2, but the advantage is moderate, not decisive. **Flow score: 4/5 → 3.5/5.**

## Proposed Repairs (Summary)

1. **Own the convergence.** Identify the precise CP-CI fork (potentia ontology, explanation direction) rather than claiming global superiority.
2. **Transparent physics/philosophy ratio.** Present as "metaphysical interpretation with physics anchors," not as physics-rich theory.
3. **Three-level flow account.** Present flow as a spectrum of commitments (Level 1-3). Claim the concrescence=specious-present identity as strongest version, not the only version.
4. **Revised scores.** Accept the 26→25 downgrade.
5. **Strengthened post-attack formulation:** "Time is the ongoing process of actualization. This interpretation is grounded in CST (speculative but rigorous), enriched by Whiteheadian process metaphysics, supported by quantum measurement theory. It differs from CI in one precise respect: it treats quantum potentialities as ontologically real. Its strongest features are intrinsic directionality (no Past Hypothesis) and perspectival 'now.' Its flow account is stronger than CI's at Level 2 and substantially stronger at Level 3, though the latter requires panexperientialism."

## Honest Meta-Assessment

1. **CP and CI are closer than E002 reported.** The structural isomorphism is real. CP is CI's metaphysical cousin, diverging on potentiality ontology and explanation direction.
2. **CP's physics is thinner than E002 reported.** The interpretation is primarily philosophical with physics anchors.
3. **CP's flow advantage is real but conditional.** Full advantage requires panexperientialism. Without it, advantage is moderate.
4. **CP's directionality advantage is real and unconditional.** Survived all attacks. Intrinsic arrow requiring no Past Hypothesis. This should be CP's lead selling point.
5. **CP's "now" account is genuinely strong.** Perspectival-processual account (each actualization IS a now) survived implicit testing in Attack 3.

**Bottom line:** A tournament between CP and CI should be close, with outcome depending on how judges value physics grounding (CI advantage) vs. metaphysical depth (CP advantage).
