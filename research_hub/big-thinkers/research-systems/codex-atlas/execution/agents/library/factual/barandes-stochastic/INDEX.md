# Barandes Indivisible Stochastic Dynamics (ISP)

Physical content assessment of Barandes' indivisible stochastic processes (ISP) framework for quantum mechanics. **Level 2+ reformulation** (refined from Level 2 after E005 adversarial review): genuine conceptual/foundational value (complex numbers derivation, Tsirelson bound characterization, phase-freedom/realm-selection connection, measurement problem dissolution) but NO predictive or computational value. Feb 2026 paper cluster assessed (7 papers, CHSH/Tsirelson strongest). Tsirelson claim circularity **CONFIRMED** (E005: proof assumes unistochastic = Born rule QM; authors acknowledge). Amplituhedron comparison: Barandes significantly weaker (conceptual vs. operational value). **6 findings.** (UPDATED: barandes-stochastic E005 adversarial steelman.)

## Core Findings

- **chsh-tsirelson-causal-locality.md** -- Causal local ISP -> Tsirelson bound (2sqrt(2)). **Circularity CONFIRMED (E005):** proof assumes unistochastic dynamics = Born rule QM; paper itself acknowledges in Section 5. Prior art: Tsirelson 1980, Buhrman-Massar 2005 (same result in QM language). Causal locality vs. no-signaling distinction is GENUINE and CORRECT. **Open question:** do causally local non-unistochastic ISPs also obey Tsirelson? (Not yet answered.) arXiv:2512.18105.
- **steelman-and-level-3-paths.md** -- **(NEW, E005)** Four steelman arguments graded: (1) measurement problem dissolution (SUBSTANTIVE), (2) Lagrangian analogy (apt but incomplete), (3) open systems advantage (most plausible path to operational value), (4) causal locality as new principle (philosophically strongest). Three Level 2→3 transition conditions: non-unistochastic proof, stochastic variational principle, operational quantum channel result. None exist yet.
- **complex-numbers-from-indivisibility.md** -- Complex numbers are the minimal algebraic extension needed for quantum transitions as amplitude-squared for N>2 states. Pseudo-quaternion observation. arXiv:2602.01043. Prior art: Stueckelberg (1960), Renou et al. (2021) -- ISP angle is novel derivation path, not novel result.
- **physical-content-verdict.md** -- Three value proposition tests: (A) Selection principle NEGATIVE within QM, (B) Computational advantage NEGATIVE, (C) Structural insight POSITIVE (3 insights). Amplituhedron comparison: no EFT-hedron analog, no new inequalities. Overall: **Level 2+ reformulation** (updated E005). Level 2→3 transition conditions identified. Includes pilot-wave/HMM minor finding and "What does it buy me?" one-paragraph summary.
- **fundamental-gaps-phase-erasure.md** -- Four fundamental limitations from phase erasure: (1) entanglement completely obscured -- same Gamma for singlet and maximally mixed, (2) no continuous generators -- no analog of Hermitian H, (3) QFT/Standard Model out of reach -- finite N, no particle creation, no Lorentz symmetry, (4) superposition invisible -- encoded only as non-factorizability.
- **feb-2026-paper-cluster.md** -- Catalog and ranked assessment of 7 papers (Jul 2025 -- Feb 2026): CHSH/Tsirelson strongest, Deflationary Account moderate, Pilot-Wave/HMM minor, two critique papers (Weak Values, ABL Rule) add no positive content.
