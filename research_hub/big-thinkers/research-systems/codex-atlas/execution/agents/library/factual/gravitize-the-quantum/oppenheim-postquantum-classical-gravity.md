# Oppenheim's Postquantum Classical Gravity (2023-2025)

## Core Idea

Spacetime is fundamentally classical — definite geometry at all times, never in superposition. Quantum matter interacts with classical spacetime through a stochastic coupling that is necessarily irreversible. This is the most active "gravitize the quantum" program as of 2025.

## The Decoherence-Diffusion Trade-Off

The central physical mechanism:
1. A massive particle in superposition bends spacetime around where it is → spacetime "learns" the position
2. This learning constitutes a measurement → decoherence of the quantum state
3. But if spacetime is noisy/diffusive, it can't learn precisely → particle stays in superposition longer
4. **Fundamental trade-off:** D_class × τ_decoherence ≥ bound (set by coupling strength)

Intuitively: *a massive quantum particle bends spacetime, so spacetime "learns" where it is and causes collapse. But the more unpredictable spacetime is, the less it "knows" — the longer superposition survives.*

## Evasion of No-Go Theorems

Previous no-go theorems (Eppley-Hannah, Page-Geilker) showed classical gravity + quantum matter is inconsistent, assuming reversible coupling. Oppenheim evades these by making the coupling **irreversible** (non-unitary). The stochastic, irreversible coupling preserves both the Heisenberg uncertainty principle and no-signaling.

## Experimental Predictions

1. **Decoherence bound:** Massive quantum superpositions decohere at rate ≥ (coupling strength)²/D_spacetime
2. **Diffusion bound:** Precision gravitational field measurements should show random fluctuations of magnitude D_spacetime
3. **The squeeze:** Current interferometry (LIGO-class) constrains diffusion from above; atom interferometry constrains decoherence from below. Constraints are converging and could close the allowed parameter space
4. **Renormalizability:** Pure gravity sector is formally renormalizable (2024 result)

## Problem of Time

Since gravity is classical, the problem of time doesn't arise in its standard form. There's a definite classical spacetime with definite causal structure at all times. Time is classical GR time — unambiguous.
