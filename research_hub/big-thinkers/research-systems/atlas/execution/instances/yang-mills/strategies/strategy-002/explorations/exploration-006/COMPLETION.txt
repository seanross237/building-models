RESULTS SUMMARY
================================================================================

PRIORITY 1: 4D LATTICE MEASUREMENT ✅
--------------------------------------
Measured H_normalized = |HessS(v,v)| / (48β × |v|²) on 4⁴ lattice

| β    | max H_norm | slack_factor |
|------|------------|--------------|
| 0.02 | 0.00725    | 138×         |
| 0.1  | 0.00791    | 127×         |
| 0.5  | 0.02023    | 49×          |
| 1.0  | 0.03452    | 29×          |

Key finding: 4D is ~3× TIGHTER than 3D (E005 had slack = 45× at β=0.02)

PRIORITY 2: WORST-CASE CONFIGURATION SEARCH ✅
-----------------------------------------------
Tested three adversarial strategies at β=0.02:

| Strategy               | max H_norm | slack_factor |
|------------------------|------------|--------------|
| Aligned config         | 0.00480    | 208×         |
| Gradient ascent        | 0.00463    | 216×         |
| Eigenvalue search      | 0.00569    | 176×         |

Overall worst-case: H_norm = 0.00569 (slack = 176×)
No configuration approached saturation (would need H_norm ≈ 1)

PRIORITY 3: PHYSICAL INTERPRETATION ✅
---------------------------------------
See REPORT.md Section 3 for full analysis.

Key mechanism: Plaquette cancellation
- More dimensions → more plaquettes → better cancellation
- 4D bound factor (48β) increases by 1.5× over 3D (32β)
- But actual max H_norm DECREASES by 3×
- Conclusion: Cancellation wins, bound is improvable
