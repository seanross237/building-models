## The Six Mandatory Modifications

### 1. Resolve the QRF-Process Tension (CRITICAL)

The synthesis claims both that becoming is ontologically real AND that the timeless Wheeler-DeWitt description is fundamental (QRF dissolution). These conflict. Take the process-primary position: becoming/determination is ontologically real; the WDW equation captures constraints on the process, not its underlying timeless nature. The QRF program shows that "static" and "evolving" descriptions are mathematically equivalent perspectives — but the synthesis takes a metaphysical position that the evolving/processual description is ontologically primary.

### 2. Reframe "Determination" as a Bridging Concept

Stop claiming "determination" is a discovered primitive that CI and CP were partial glimpses of. Instead, present it as a proposed identification: "We propose that CI's information processing and CP's ontological becoming are two descriptions of the same process. We call this 'determination' — the generation of the determinate from the indeterminate. The evidence for this identification is the structural isomorphism documented in the tournament."

### 3. Drop the "Natural Initial Condition" Claim

The Past Hypothesis remains an initial condition, not a natural starting point. The synthesis's real contribution to directionality is the intrinsic direction of determination (possibility → actuality), not the reframing of the PH.

### 4. Add QM Compatibility Matrix

Front-and-center: Compatible (Heisenberg/potentia, Kastner transactional, objective collapse). Incompatible (Everettian, pilot wave). Unclear (QBism).

### 5. Present Score as Conditional

52.5/65 with real potentialities; ~47.5/65 without (= CI). The ontological upgrade is optional.

### 6. Acknowledge Architecture Honestly

"CI with an ontological upgrade." CI provides the ground floor (established physics); the potentiality commitment provides the upper floor (ontological depth). The upgrade is optional but produces genuine explanatory gains.
