# Strategy 004: Prediction Forge — Completing the Theory

## Objective

Take the Unified QG+F–AS Framework produced by Strategy-003 and close its one remaining gap: **Predictive FAIL.** The framework has passed Consistency, scored MARGINAL on Novelty and Explanatory, but has exactly ONE discriminating prediction (the ghost propagator computation) which hasn't been performed and produces ZERO near-term experimental discriminators. This strategy extracts every possible concrete prediction using physical reasoning, dimensional analysis, the QCD analogy, and existing published results — without requiring original computation.

The secondary objective is to produce the **definitive theory document** — the complete novel theory of quantum gravity incorporating the best results from all four strategies. This is the mission's final deliverable.

## Why Predictions Can Be Extracted Without Computation

The Unified Framework makes specific physical claims (ghost confinement at M_P, BH phase transition, sharpened r, spectral dimension profile). Each claim has CONSEQUENCES that can be derived by reasoning, not computation:

- **Dimensional analysis:** If ghost confinement occurs at scale M_P, what's the confinement energy? What's the cross-section for ghost production near threshold? What's the remnant mass?
- **QCD analogy:** QCD's deconfinement transition has specific signatures (latent heat, order parameter, critical exponents). The gravitational analog should have analogous signatures derivable by mapping.
- **Existing results as inputs:** Both QG+F and AS have extensive published calculations. The unified framework makes specific claims about how these relate — those claims have testable consequences using ALREADY COMPUTED numbers.
- **Consistency constraints:** If two existing predictions (from QG+F and AS separately) must agree under unification, that agreement constrains parameters that were previously free. These constraints ARE predictions.

## Methodology: Targeted Prediction Extraction + Final Synthesis

### Phase 1: Prediction Extraction (6-7 explorations)

Each exploration targets one domain and extracts every concrete prediction the unified framework makes in that domain. Each prediction must be classified as:

- **DISCRIMINATING:** True if unified, false if compatible-but-separate
- **CONSISTENCY CHECK:** True if unified, also true if compatible-but-separate
- **NOVEL:** Neither QG+F alone nor AS alone predicts this
- **INHERITED:** Predicted by one or both standalone theories

Only DISCRIMINATING and NOVEL predictions count toward closing the Predictive gap.

**Domain 1: Ghost Propagator Specification.**
Strategy-003's exploration 009 identified this as the single most discriminating prediction. This exploration should derive THE PREDICTED OUTCOME in detail using physical reasoning:
- Use the QCD analogy to predict the FORM of the ghost dissolution (complex pole tower? mass divergence? residue vanishing?)
- Use existing Draper et al. (2020) results for general gravity propagators to constrain what the spin-2 ghost propagator SHOULD look like
- Derive the critical scale at which ghost dissolution begins
- Write a precise computational specification: "compute X in truncation Y, and the unified framework predicts result Z"
- This turns the prediction from "the ghost should dissolve" (vague) to "the spin-2 ghost pole at p² = -m₂² should split into a conjugate pair at p² = -m₂² ± iΓ with Γ/m₂ = O(f₂²(M_P))" (specific)

**Domain 2: Black Hole Evaporation Phase Transition.**
The framework predicts a phase transition at M ~ M_P where the Schwarzschild description breaks down. Use the QCD deconfinement analogy:
- QCD deconfinement is a first-order transition (for 3 colors) with specific latent heat L ~ T_c⁴
- Map: gravitational "deconfinement" at M ~ M_P should have analogous features
- Predict: the Hawking temperature at which the transition occurs, the change in BH heat capacity, whether remnants form or evaporate completely
- Use Bonanno-Reuter BH thermodynamics to derive the specific transition mass M_crit in terms of M_P and g*

**Domain 3: Inflation — Sharpened r from NGFP Constraint.**
The unified framework claims b (the NGFP correction parameter) is determined, not free. Extract:
- Use published NGFP critical exponents (from multiple truncations) to compute b
- Derive the sharpened prediction for r and n_s
- Compare with the six-derivative QG+F extension (Anselmi's n_s correction)
- Determine: does the NGFP-determined b agree with the six-derivative correction? If yes, that's a strong consistency check.

**Domain 4: Spectral Dimension Profile d_s(E).**
Both QG+F and AS predict d_s → 2 in the UV and d_s → 4 in the IR. The unified framework should predict the FULL PROFILE. Extract:
- Use QG+F's known spectral dimension formula (from Modesto 2009, Calcagni 2013)
- Use AS's known spectral dimension formula (from Reuter & Saueressig 2012)
- Check: do they agree at intermediate scales? Where do they diverge?
- The profile shape IS a discriminating prediction — if unified, the profiles must match; if separate, they need not

**Domain 5: Matter Sector — Higgs Mass and SM Couplings.**
The Shaposhnikov-Wetterich (2010) prediction: AS + SM coupling → Higgs mass ≈ 126 GeV (before discovery). Under unification:
- Does the fakeon prescription change the UV matching conditions?
- Does the C² term contribute to the SM RG flow in a way that shifts the prediction?
- If the prediction is UNCHANGED, that's a consistency check. If it's SHIFTED, that's a novel prediction.

**Domain 6: Cosmological Constant.**
The hardest problem. Under unification:
- Does the NGFP predict a specific value or scaling for Λ?
- Does the fakeon prescription affect the vacuum energy calculation?
- Honestly assess: does the unified framework say ANYTHING new about Λ, or is it as silent as QG+F alone?

**Domain 7 (if budget allows): Gravitational Waves.**
- Does the unified framework predict specific corrections to GW propagation?
- Does the ghost confinement affect the gravitational wave dispersion relation?
- Are there gravitational wave signatures of the Planck-scale phase transition?

## Validation Criteria

### Minimum success (strategy worth having run)
- At least 3 concrete, NOVEL predictions extracted (predictions that neither standalone QG+F nor standalone AS makes)
- At least 1 DISCRIMINATING prediction that distinguishes the unified framework from compatible-but-separate
- The Predictive tier moves from FAIL to at least MARGINAL

### Good success
- 5+ novel predictions, 2+ discriminating predictions, at least 1 with a specific numerical value
- The final theory document passes Tiers 1-4 (even if Tier 5 is only marginal)
- A domain expert reading the document would say "this is a serious research proposal"

### Breakthrough
- All 5 tiers passed
- Multiple discriminating predictions with specific numbers
- The theory document constitutes a novel contribution to the QG literature

### Strategy exhaustion signals
- Prediction extraction produces only consistency checks, no discriminators
- The QCD analogy breaks down for every domain-specific prediction
- Dimensional analysis produces only order-of-magnitude estimates that don't discriminate
