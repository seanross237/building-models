# Exploration 005 Summary: Devil's Advocate — Attacking SCG

## Goal
Ruthlessly attack SCG to find fatal flaws, hidden contradictions, and unjustified assumptions.

## Outcome: SCG does NOT survive in its current form.

**Seven attacks launched. Severity ranking:**

### FATAL
1. **Lorentzian signature problem:** A positive-definite cost function (Axiom 3) structurally CANNOT produce Lorentzian spacetime (-,+,+,+). This is not a gap — it's a structural incompatibility. No emergent gravity program has solved this without putting causal structure in by hand.

### NEAR-FATAL
2. **QM emergence is reformulation, not derivation:** Barandes-Doukas lifting is a mathematical isomorphism (runs both ways). Born rule is definitional (constructed to match probabilities). Phases are undetermined by the stochastic process. Indivisibility may smuggle in QM. Key papers not yet in mainstream physics journals.
3. **Continuum limit unproven:** Generic cost functions don't approximate manifolds (Gromov-Hausdorff theory). No mechanism for 4D. The gap from finite metric space to smooth Riemannian manifold is enormous.
4. **Pedraza derivation gaps:** CV conjecture unproven. Only rigorously proven in 2D dilaton gravity, not 4D. Goes wrong direction (gravity → complexity, not reverse). Depends on AdS/CFT.

### SERIOUS
5. **No unique predictions:** Every prediction inherited from component programs (Oppenheim, Diósi-Penrose, CDT, etc.). Nothing empirically distinguishes SCG from its ingredients.
6. **Self-consistency unproven:** Fixed-point condition asserted by analogy, not demonstrated. May have no solution.
7. **ℏ = 2mσ² is renaming:** Algebraic rearrangement of Nelson's D = ℏ/2m. Replaces one unexplained constant with another.

## Key Takeaway
**SCG is a creative research program, not a theory.** The conceptual vision (one stochastic computation → both QM and gravity) is compelling. But: one fatal structural flaw (Lorentzian), several near-fatal derivation gaps, and no unique predictions. To become a genuine theory, SCG must: (1) solve Lorentzian signature, (2) prove continuum limit with dimension selection, (3) prove self-consistency fixed point, (4) make a unique prediction, (5) honestly reframe QM emergence as reformulation.

## Most Damaging Finding
The Lorentzian signature problem is the deepest issue. Positive-definite metrics give elliptic operators (no causal structure, no light cones). Physical spacetime requires hyperbolic operators. This requires fundamentally changing Axiom 3 or adding causal structure axioms — either way, the current formulation is broken.
