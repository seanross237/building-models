## Finding

Cosmological polytopes extend positive geometry to **cosmological correlators** (wavefunction coefficients) in flat FRW spacetimes. This is a genuine, working extension beyond N=4 SYM — not a toy model, computing physically meaningful quantities. The extension to de Sitter / inflation with gravitons remains open.

## The Construction (arXiv:1709.02813, Arkani-Hamed & Benincasa)

For a massless scalar in a flat FRW universe, wavefunction coefficients Ψₙ(k₁,...,kₙ) equal canonical forms of **cosmological polytopes**. For a graph G, the cosmological polytope is defined by:
- One simplex per vertex (energy variables for legs)
- Intersections corresponding to edge energies
- Canonical form = wavefunction coefficient

**Key structural connection**: The flat-space scattering amplitude appears as a **higher-codimension face** of the cosmological polytope, where the "total energy" E_tot → 0. This geometrically explains why flat-space limits of cosmological correlators give S-matrix elements — a structural insight not transparent in standard QFT in curved space. This is something the geometric picture reveals that standard methods cannot.

## Extensions (2019–2025)

**Massive scalars** (arXiv:1909.02517, 2019): Extends to massive scalars with light masses. Flat-space amplitude still appears as a face; Laurent expansion in total energy is geometrically controlled.

**Cosmohedra** (JHEP 11(2025)029): Introduces "cosmohedra" as the positive geometry underlying the cosmological wavefunction for **Tr(ϕ³) theory** (matrix scalar). Structure is a "Russian doll" of non-overlapping subpolygons, generalizing associahedra. Related to surfacehedra (from string theory).

**Loop level** (SciPost Physics 2025): Positive geometry techniques compute cosmological correlators at loop level.

## What Works

- Flat FRW spacetimes (massless or light scalars)
- All-order wavefunction coefficient structure
- Geometric explanation for flat-space/cosmological S-matrix connection

## What Breaks

- **De Sitter** (positive cosmological constant): Mode functions and time-ordering introduce complications that break the clean polytope picture
- **Interacting inflation**: Non-Gaussianities from slow-roll inflation partially map onto polytopes, but non-linearities and tensor modes (gravitational waves) complicate significantly
- **Massive spinning particles**: The total-energy pole structure breaks
- **Non-perturbative cosmological physics**: No framework for non-perturbative effects

## Assessment

Cosmological polytopes are a **genuine, working extension** of positive geometry beyond N=4 SYM. The extension to gravitational inflation (de Sitter + tensor modes) is the major open problem and would represent the most physically significant success.
