# Exploration 001 Goal - Raw Exact Closure Ledger For `F_SS(1/12)`

## Objective

Reconstruct the raw exact closure burden of the frozen witness `F_SS(1/12)` on
the same canonical one-bridge role-labeled helical packet ledger inherited
from Step 7.

This exploration must separate:

- content already frozen in the Step-7 witness sheet;
- mandatory conjugate completion already forced by packet semantics;
- same-scale companion, feedback, and cross-scale burden that exact NS forces
  once the desired five-channel core is active; and
- any point where the local record stops being explicit enough to enumerate a
  finite exact mode list on the same ledger.

## Success Criteria

- Produce one closure ledger that states, explicitly and with local citations:
  what modes/interactions are already frozen,
  what interaction classes are forced by exact closure,
  and whether the repository actually pins a finite exact support list for the
  witness.
- Distinguish carefully between exact classwise burden already earned on disk
  and any finer mode-by-mode closure data that the local record still lacks.
- If the local record does not support a finite exact mode list, say that
  plainly and identify the exact missing ingredients.

## Failure Criteria

- It treats the Step-4 screening ledger as if it were already a closed exact
  dynamical system without checking support-level closure.
- It invents a new wavevector family, new packet semantics, new spectator
  partition, or a new bookkeeping currency.
- It ignores the structural exact-NS warning that the desired five-channel
  circuit automatically drags mirror, same-scale, feedback, and cross-scale
  spectators with it.
