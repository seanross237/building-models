## Major Differences Between Chains

- Chain `01` is family-local and theorem-facing.
  It works inside the already carried positive family `F_SL(rho)` and asks for
  an exact repaired-template frontier, not a new closure object or a support
  search.
- Chain `02` is relation-facing.
  It studies the implication gap between the Step-6 promoted gates and honest
  exact closure on the frozen ledger.
- Chain `03` is support-level and obstruction-facing.
  It tries to turn the Step-8 witness-local failure into a theorem-shaped
  impossibility or forced-support lower bound.
- Chain `04` is constructive and adversarial.
  It looks for an explicit exact survivor outside the invalidated `F_SS`
  branch, not a proof first.
