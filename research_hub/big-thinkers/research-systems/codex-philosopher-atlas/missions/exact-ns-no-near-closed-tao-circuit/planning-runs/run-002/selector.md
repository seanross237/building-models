# Selector Verdict - exact-ns-no-near-closed-tao-circuit / run-002

## Selected Portfolio

### 1. Chain 03 - Joint Feasibility Frontier On The Canonical One-Bridge Class

Selected.

This is the strongest lead branch because it uses both promoted Step-6 objects
exactly as frozen and attacks the main unresolved strategic question:
whether the joint gate pair has an empty frontier, a narrow survivor region,
or a genuine separation inside the canonical one-bridge class.

It also has the best failure value in the set. Even if it does not produce a
frontier theorem or stronger survivor, it can still return a clean separation
memo or a proof that the next work must split back into narrower theorem and
construction branches.

### 2. Chain 02 - Classwise Leakage Sharpness And Friendly-Minimality Theorem

Selected.

This is the best obstruction-floor branch. It hardens the repaired leakage
sheet on its own exact currency, tests whether the carried friendly set really
supports the repaired vector, and can still yield a corrected admissibility
sheet or narrow hostile-overload memo if the current budget is not theorem
ready.

It complements Chain 03 rather than duplicating it. Chain 03 studies the
joint frontier, while Chain 02 isolates the leakage object and strengthens the
obstruction side even if the joint frontier remains unresolved.

### 3. Chain 04 - Moonshot Near-Circuit Promotion From `F_SS`

Selected.

This branch stays in the portfolio because it is the cleanest high-upside kill
route. It is the only candidate that can turn the best positive witness on
disk into an explicit exact near-circuit challenger rather than a theorem or
audit result.

Its floor is lower than Chains 03 and 02, but its failure mode is still
valuable: an honest constructive failure atlas naming timing failure,
spectator takeover, coefficient misordering, or forced packet growth would
meaningfully sharpen the mission.

## Why This Is The Strongest Three-Chain Portfolio

- It maximizes diversity across frontier mapping, obstruction theorem work,
  and constructive moonshot search.
- It preserves at least one high-upside path by keeping Chain 04 active.
- Every selected branch still returns useful output on failure:
  Chain 03 can produce a separation memo,
  Chain 02 can produce a corrected leakage sheet or honest hostile-comparison
  limit,
  and Chain 04 can produce a constructive failure atlas.
- It avoids the main redundancy in the set. Chain 01 is a good narrow theorem
  branch, but it is the easiest cut because it overlaps with Chain 03 on the
  friendly and template-facing side while offering a smaller strategic cone of
  outcomes.

## Why Chain 01 Was Not Selected

Chain 01 is not weak. It is the cleanest single-object proposition route in
the portfolio. But under a three-slot budget it loses on marginal portfolio
value:

- it is narrower than Chain 03,
- it overlaps with Chain 03 on the `F_SL` and `G_tmpl` side of the record,
- and its failure output is less strategically broad than Chain 04's
  constructive atlas or Chain 02's classwise leakage correction.

If Chain 03 later collapses to a family-local theorem program, Chain 01 is the
natural reserve branch to reactivate.
