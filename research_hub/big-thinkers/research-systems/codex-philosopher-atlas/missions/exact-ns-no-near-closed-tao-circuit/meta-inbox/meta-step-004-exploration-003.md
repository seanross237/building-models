# Meta Note - Step 4 Exploration 003

## What Worked

- Keeping two distinct friendly families exposed a useful split:
  the template-defect and leakage-budget notions survive both,
  while the itinerary only survives the most engineered family.
- The scale-separated family made the remaining ambiguity precise:
  the bottleneck is the exact event-time trace, not the leakage sheet.

## Reusable Lesson

- When a behavior-based candidate is weaker than the algebraic or leakage-based
  candidates, the right friendly stress test is often the one where leakage is
  already small.
  If ambiguity survives there, the problem is usually event-trace rigidity
  rather than raw isolation.

## Caution

- Do not upgrade a friendly exact packet family into theorem evidence unless it
  survives comparison against the hostile dossier on the same ledger.
  A construction-facing witness is still only class-local until that symmetric
  comparison is done.
