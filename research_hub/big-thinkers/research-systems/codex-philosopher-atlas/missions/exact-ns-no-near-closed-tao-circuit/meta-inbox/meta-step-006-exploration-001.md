# Meta Note - Step 6 Exploration 001

## What Worked

- Forcing the collapse question explicitly kept Step 6 from mistaking
  co-survival on one dossier for a proof that two gates are equivalent.
- Keeping the theorem-facing role-template burden and the obstruction-facing
  leakage burden separate made it possible to promote both repaired objects
  without inventing a new conjunction object.

## Reusable Lesson

- When two repaired survivors live on the same packet ledger, keep both unless
  the local record actually lands one exact implication or one exact
  counterexample that collapses them.

## Caution

- Do not upgrade shared friendly/hostile outcomes into a packaging collapse.
  Parallel pass/fail behavior on the current dossier is not yet a theorem that
  one invariant gate subsumes the other.
