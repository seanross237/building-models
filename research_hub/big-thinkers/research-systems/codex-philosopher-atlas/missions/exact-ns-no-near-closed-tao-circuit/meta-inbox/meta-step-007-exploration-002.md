# Meta Note - Step 7 Exploration 002

## What Worked

- Freezing the hard scorecard separately from the diagnostics kept Step 7 from
  quietly re-promoting itinerary timing after the route had already been
  discarded.
- Writing the no-repair rule and the no-overclaim rule together made the Step-2
  readiness verdict decisive rather than rhetorical.

## Reusable Lesson

- A witness-local bootstrap step is clean only when it ends with all three of
  these locked at once:
  one hard scorecard,
  one explicit diagnostics-only bucket,
  and one concrete distinction between closure-forced bookkeeping and
  illegitimate rescue.

## Caution

- Do not confuse a frozen static witness dossier with exact dynamic success.
  If later steps still need closure, finite-system proof, or dynamic gate
  evaluation, say that explicitly and forbid any claim that the branch has
  already shown near-circuit behavior.
