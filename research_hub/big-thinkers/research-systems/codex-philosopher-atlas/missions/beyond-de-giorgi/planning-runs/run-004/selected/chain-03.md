# Chain 03 - Strain-Pressure Eigenstructure And Harmonic Hessian Route

## Central Premise

The exact local tensor relations among strain `S`, pressure Hessian `\nabla^2 p`, and vorticity stretching may carry NS-specific rigidity that scalar norm estimates erase. The point is not to get a better pressure norm, but to see whether eigenvalue, eigenvector, sign, or local harmonic-Hessian information can control the full stretching mechanism.

## Verifiable Result Target

Either:

- one conditional tensor criterion, reduced eigenframe model, or falsifiable conjecture tied to a concrete stretching control target; or
- a bounded obstruction memo showing that tensor/eigenframe structure still does not operationally separate exact NS from Tao's averaged model.

## Why This Chain Is Meaningfully Different

This chain is tensorial rather than compactness-based or Lagrangian. It reuses pressure-side information, but not through the already-killed coefficient-shrinkage route.

## Ordered Steps

### Step 1 - Compile the exact tensor ledger

- Depends on: none.
- Action: assemble the exact identities linking `S`, `\omega`, and `\nabla^2 p`, including the local split between nearby and harmonic far-field pressure contributions.
- Action: separate exact NS identities from generic Calderon-Zygmund repackaging.
- Expected output: a formula sheet marking exact structure, nonlocal debt, and where harmonicity enters locally.
- Kill condition: if every candidate identity immediately collapses to generic singular-integral structure, stop and record the collapse.

### Step 2 - Choose a bounded observable family

- Depends on: Step 1.
- Action: shortlist at most three scale-credible tensor observables, for example eigenvalue-gap ratios, `\omega`-to-eigenframe alignment, pressure-Hessian/strain misalignment, or a harmonic-Hessian defect quantity.
- Action: state the Tao discriminator for each observable before any estimate optimism.
- Expected output: an observable table with scaling, intended leverage point, and exact NS-versus-averaged distinction.
- Kill condition: if no observable has both a concrete stretching target and a concrete Tao discriminator, close the branch negatively.

### Step 3 - Test control of the full stretching mechanism

- Depends on: Step 2.
- Action: fix one stretching representation and test whether the leading observable controls the full `S\omega \cdot \omega` term, not only a self-induced or local fragment.
- Action: charge all nonlocal pressure-Hessian debt honestly.
- Expected output: a lemma sheet or obstruction memo saying exactly which part of stretching is controlled and which part remains free.
- Kill condition: if the observable only repackages a known open condition or leaves the decisive nonlocal remainder untouched, stop.

### Step 4 - Convert any survivor into a proposition-scale target

- Depends on: Step 3.
- Action: if one observable survives, translate it into a conditional criterion, reduced eigenframe dynamics, or sharply testable conjecture.
- Action: name the first missing lemma and the intended next theorem target.
- Expected output: a draft proposition or a reduced-model memo with explicit assumptions.
- Kill condition: if the resulting statement is just a renamed version of a known criterion, downgrade to obstruction.

### Step 5 - Close with a Tao-sensitive verdict

- Depends on: Step 4, or directly on Step 3 if the chain dies there.
- Action: say explicitly whether the surviving mechanism uses exact tensor structure destroyed by averaging or only generic elliptic facts.
- Expected output: either a live tensor attack path or a sharp tensor-obstruction memo.
- Kill condition: if the same argument works unchanged in the averaged model, close the chain negatively.
