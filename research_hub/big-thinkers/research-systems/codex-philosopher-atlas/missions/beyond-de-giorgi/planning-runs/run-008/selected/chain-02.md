# Chain 02 - SQG-Blueprint Commutator And Scalarization Route

## Central Premise

The sharp De Giorgi barrier is probably not beaten by a better inequality in the old NS variables. The live question is whether one exact NS reformulation produces an SQG-like package in which the dangerous interaction is handled by a commutator, scalarization, or extension mechanism rather than by quadratic pressure bookkeeping.

## Verifiable Result Target

Either:

- one proposition-scale conditional route built around a specific reformulated unknown, one commutator gain, and one exact theorem endpoint; or
- one calibrated obstruction memo showing that every plausible reformulation collapses back to the dead LEI rewrite audit, generic Calderon-Zygmund structure, or Tao-compatible quadratic packaging.

## Why This Chain Is Meaningfully Different

This chain is reformulation-first and mechanism-specific. It is not a pressure branch, not a material-trajectory branch, and not another generic Tao-differential-diagnosis pass.

## Ordered Steps

### Step 1 - Freeze one target unknown and one endpoint before algebra begins

- Depends on: none.
- Action: choose one theorem-facing target quantity such as:
  a scalarized active variable,
  one extension variable,
  one projected component,
  or one compensated quantity built from `u`, `omega`, and pressure.
- Action: choose one endpoint:
  localized regularity,
  continuation criterion,
  or concentration exclusion.
- Action: forbid any target whose only role is to rewrite the old local-energy flux in prettier notation.
- Expected output: a setup memo naming the unknown, the endpoint, and the gain currency.
- Kill condition: if no exact target quantity can be frozen without immediately reproducing the old LEI rewrite setting, stop and write a reformulation obstruction.

### Step 2 - Derive the exact nonlinear package and isolate the commutator candidate

- Depends on: Step 1.
- Action: write the exact evolution for the chosen unknown and separate:
  linear drift pieces,
  commutator pieces,
  genuinely quadratic leftovers,
  and pressure-dependent remainders.
- Action: state which part is supposed to imitate the SQG mechanism and which part is the NS-specific debt.
- Expected output: a formula sheet with one promoted commutator candidate and one explicit remainder ledger.
- Kill condition: if the commutator candidate is only formal and the full equation remains dominated by untreated quadratic leftovers, close the branch negatively.

### Step 3 - Apply the Tao screen at the mechanism level

- Depends on: Step 2.
- Action: test whether the promoted commutator structure depends on exact NS algebra that Tao-style averaging would destroy, rather than on generic singular-integral facts.
- Action: give one exact pass example and one exact fail example inside the chosen package.
- Expected output: a Tao-comparator memo tied to the actual reformulated equation.
- Kill condition: if the commutator mechanism works unchanged in the averaged model or rests only on generic harmonic analysis, terminate with a Tao-insensitivity obstruction.

### Step 4 - Insert the mechanism into one endpoint-facing inequality

- Depends on: Step 3.
- Action: write one explicit inequality, contradiction line, or continuation implication where the commutator gain acts on the chosen endpoint burden.
- Action: include localization, pressure, and regularity debt immediately rather than as later cleanup.
- Expected output: an insertion memo with one benchmarked gain.
- Kill condition: if the route cannot produce one endpoint-facing line before hidden regularity upgrades appear, stop and write an endpoint-insertion obstruction.

### Step 5 - Audit the remaining gap for genuine narrowness

- Depends on: Step 4.
- Action: isolate the first missing lemma.
- Action: test whether it is narrower than arbitrary finite-energy regularity and specific to the reformulated package.
- Expected output: either a proposition-scale conditional route or a sharp obstruction memo.
- Kill condition: if the missing lemma is just "control the full NS nonlinearity in the new variables," downgrade immediately to obstruction.
