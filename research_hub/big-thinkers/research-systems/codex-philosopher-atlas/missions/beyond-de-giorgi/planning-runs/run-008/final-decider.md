It is now set up to fail usefully and early if the gain is only expectation-level, non-coercive, non-localizable, or solution-class fragile.

### 2. Better execution fit than the original selector ranking suggested

The selector was right that Chain 01 had the highest general floor, but the refined Chain 03 now has a tighter executable shape than before:

- it starts from one named blowup mechanism rather than a menu;
- it requires one observable to clear a coercivity or exclusion gate early;
- it forces solution-class and localization viability before broad endpoint narration;
- it demotes Tao-comparison to a secondary discriminator.

That sequence is well matched to this system because it produces fast kill points and clear memo-grade outputs rather than inviting long descriptive drift.

### 3. Failure is still highly reusable

Even if the stochastic route fails, the likely failure modes are informative in a way that should guide later mission work:

- expectation-only gain;
- non-coercive martingale structure;
- localization or stopping-time collapse;
- solution-class mismatch near the singular regime;
- prior-art redundancy;
- Tao-insensitive packaging.

Those are strong obstruction classes, not vague dead ends.
