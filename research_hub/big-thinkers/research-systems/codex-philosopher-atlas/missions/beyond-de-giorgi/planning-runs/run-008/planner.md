## Portfolio Summary

### Chain 01 - Exact-NS Property Atlas And Tao Differential Diagnosis

Central premise: the mission may first need a disciplined property map rather than another isolated attack. This chain compares exact NS against Tao's averaged model directly, ranks only the genuinely destroyed structures, and ties each survivor to one proof architecture or one obstruction output.

Why it belongs now: several attractive branches have died because they reached the endpoint screen too late. This chain front-loads the shared admission work and should prevent further budget from being spent on Tao-compatible or already-collapsed structure.

### Chain 02 - SQG-Blueprint Commutator And Scalarization Route

Central premise: the missing leverage may come from reformulating one NS interaction so that a commutator-type mechanism, not a better pressure estimate, handles the nonlinearity. The test is whether any exact NS variable package creates an SQG-like linear-drift-plus-commutator gain on a theorem-facing quantity.

Why it belongs now: the mission context explicitly says SQG won by changing the drift-coupling structure, not by beating Chebyshev. This is the cleanest route that targets that lesson directly.

### Chain 03 - Material Deformation And Stretching Route

Central premise: the decisive NS-specific structure may live in trajectory-level deformation and cumulative stretching rather than in Eulerian local estimates. A live route would use one material observable to reach a continuation criterion or a high-vorticity contradiction line.

Why it belongs now: this is transport-first without falling back into the already weak fixed-tube or fixed-Eulerian geometry package.

### Chain 04 - Stochastic Circulation And Martingale Transport Route

Central premise: viscosity changes Kelvin/Cauchy transport into stochastic transport rather than deleting it. If that adapted stochastic backbone is destroyed by Tao-style averaging, it may yield an NS-specific continuation or concentration-exclusion mechanism unavailable to deterministic harmonic-analysis methods.

Why it belongs now: this is the highest-novelty route that still stays close to exact NS structure rather than free-form geometry.

### Chain 05 - Critical Element With Pre-Admitted NS Firewall

Central premise: concentration-compactness is worth revisiting only as a second-stage architecture. The branch is live only if one exact NS-only firewall quantity survives Chains 01-04 and is definable on the compactness class before rigidity optimism begins.

Why it belongs now: profile decomposition remains an explicit mission lead, but prior runs showed that compactness-rigidity should not get a primary slot unless a real NS-specific lever is already in hand.

## Major Differences Between Chains

- Chain 01 is comparison-first and map-building. Its natural output is a ranked property atlas or a structured obstruction map.
- Chain 02 is mechanism-first and reformulation-driven. It hunts for an SQG-like commutator structure rather than a better estimate in the old variables.
- Chain 03 is deterministic and Lagrangian. It studies deformation and cumulative stretch along trajectories rather than stochastic transport or tensor repackaging.
- Chain 04 is stochastic and representation-based. It asks whether martingale or circulation observables carry theorem leverage that deterministic packages miss.
- Chain 05 is global and contradiction-based. It assumes blowup only after a concrete firewall quantity has already survived earlier screens.

## Suggested Priority

- Highest floor and best mission-wide payoff: Chain 01.
- Highest direct relevance to the SQG lesson: Chain 02.
- Best fresh deterministic attack path: Chain 03.
- Highest novelty ceiling with the most solution-class risk: Chain 04.
- Best second-stage architecture if an earlier chain produces a real lever: Chain 05.
