# Selector - beyond-de-giorgi / run-001

## Selected Portfolio

1. Chain 01 - Local Harmonic Pressure Localization
2. Chain 02 - SQG-Type Cancellation via Lamb Vector Reformulation
3. Chain 03 - Geometric Vorticity Alignment and Local Beltrami Tubes

## Why These Three Form the Strongest Portfolio

- Chain 01 is the highest-floor option in the run. It is the cleanest direct successor to the confirmed `vasseur-pressure` obstruction, and even a negative result would sharpen the map around the one surviving issue: whether local harmonicity of `p_far` gives any genuinely new leverage.
- Chain 02 preserves the highest-upside path. It is the most direct attempt to turn the SQG blueprint into an NS-specific mechanism by testing whether the exact nonlinear algebra hides a first-order cancellation that Tao's averaged model destroys.
- Chain 03 adds the strongest geometry-first route. It is genuinely different from Chains 01 and 02, it tests a property that averaging is unlikely to preserve intact, and its failure modes are still informative because they isolate whether local Beltrami structure is too fragile or too nonlocal to matter.
- As a set, these three cover the best analytic, algebraic, and geometric leads in the run. That gives wider structural coverage than any three-chain alternative while keeping a strong failure payload in every slot.

## Full Ranking

1. **Chain 01 - Local Harmonic Pressure Localization**
   Directly targets the only obstruction left standing after the prior pressure mission. It has the best floor, the shortest path to a reusable obstruction note, and no meaningful overlap with the algebraic or geometric candidates.

2. **Chain 02 - SQG-Type Cancellation via Lamb Vector Reformulation**
   This is the highest-upside chain and the one most tightly aligned with the mission's SQG blueprint. Even a fast failure would still answer a central question: whether NS admits any natural SQG-style first-order cancellation at all.

3. **Chain 03 - Geometric Vorticity Alignment and Local Beltrami Tubes**
   The best geometry-first candidate. It targets NS-specific vortex structure rather than norms or compactness machinery, and its negative outcomes remain useful because they cleanly separate local geometric depletion from global alignment fantasy.

4. **Chain 05 - Strain-Pressure Eigenstructure**
   Strong reserve candidate, but weaker portfolio fit than Chain 03. It is promising as a bridge chain, yet it overlaps part of Chain 03's `S omega`/alignment axis and part of Chain 01's pressure-structure axis, so it adds less marginal diversity to the selected set.

5. **Chain 04 - Critical Element / Concentration-Compactness**
   Highest proof ceiling, but the weakest floor for adversarial testing in this run. It is the most likely to die at the supercritical compactness gate before exposing a comparably concrete NS-specific structural property, so it is better kept as a later escalation path than as one of the first three tests.

## Non-Selected Notes

- Chain 05 is the next-best substitute if one wants a more tensorial third slot instead of the geometry route.
- Chain 04 should be revisited only after the structure-first routes are stress-tested, because it spends more budget on proof architecture than on isolating a specific NS-only mechanism.
