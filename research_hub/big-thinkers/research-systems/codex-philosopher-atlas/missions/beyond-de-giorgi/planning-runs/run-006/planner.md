Central premise: the exact tensor coupling among `S`, `omega`, and `nabla^2 p` may contain a coercive or cancellation mechanism that scalar norm methods erase, especially at the full-stretching level or in the harmonic moments that survived the old far-field audit.

Central premise: if Tao-style averaging destroys something decisive, the cleanest place to look is the exact Leray-projected triad geometry of the real NS symbol, not identity-level rewrites in physical space.

Why it belongs now: the physical-space exact-rewrite family is dead, but the frequency-space algebraic structure has not been audited at proposition scale.

### Chain 03 - Weak-Type Endpoint Architecture Audit Beyond Strong-Type De Giorgi

Central premise: the only admissible Lorentz/weak-type route is one that replaces the exhausted strong-type De Giorgi mechanism rather than tuning it. The question is whether weak-type structure supports a genuinely different endpoint criterion or concentration architecture.

Why it belongs now: the mission context names Lorentz-space structure as an untested lead, but the run must front-load guardrails so this does not collapse into forbidden De Giorgi retuning.

### Chain 04 - Lagrangian Tube-Family Geometry Reboot

Central premise: geometry may have failed only because the neutral Eulerian package was too weak to carry persistence. A live geometry branch now must openly switch to a tube-adapted or Lagrangian package and then pay the resulting normalization and nonlocal-strain debt honestly.

Why it belongs now: the prior geometry branch left one informative survivor, `direction coherence + tube persistence`, but it died on the neutral package rather than under every possible geometric packaging.

## Major Differences Between Chains

- Chain 01 is tensorial and local-to-nonlocal. It targets full stretching and pressure-Hessian structure directly.
- Chain 02 is spectral and algebraic. It works at the exact helical-triad level and lives or dies by summation and localization robustness.
- Chain 03 is endpoint-functional rather than structural in the geometric sense. It audits weak-type architecture while forbidding a return to the dead strong-type iteration.
- Chain 04 is geometric and transport-facing. It is the only chain that explicitly changes localization package and treats that change as part of the audited burden.
