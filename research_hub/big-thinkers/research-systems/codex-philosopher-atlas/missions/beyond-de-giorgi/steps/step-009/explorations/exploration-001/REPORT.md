## Findings

### 1. What Exact Endpoint Or Contradiction Line Is Actually Live

- [VERIFIED] The run-006 branch was selected only on the condition that Step 1
  freeze one exact theorem endpoint and contradiction line before any tensor
  language survives. `decision-014.md`, `CHAIN.md`, and
  `winning-chain.md` repeat this as the primary gate.
- [VERIFIED] The local record already contains one exact, inherited
  contradiction ledger: the suitable-weak / Leray-Hopf plus LEI floor with the
  CKN/Lin epsilon-regularity contradiction package as the theorem-level
  cash-out line. This is stated directly in
  `only-the-inherited-suitable-weak-leray-hopf-plus-lei-floor-has-a-locally-frozen-theorem-endpoint.md`
  and matches the Step-008 freeze result.
- [VERIFIED] The concrete live bad term inside that inherited ledger is the
  far-field pressure pairing
  `I_p^far = -∬ p_far div(v_k phi_k^2 e_hat)`,
  with operative estimate
  `I_p^far <= C_far 2^{12k/5} U_k^{6/5}` and bad coefficient
  `C_far ~ ||u||_{L^2}^2 / r_k^3`.
- [VERIFIED] `step-001/RESULTS.md` identifies the live obstruction as
  coefficient-side, not regularity-side. The only progress shape carried
  forward is a smaller effective coefficient for the full pairing, or exact
  cancellation of the affine-and-higher harmonic moments generating that same
  coefficient.
- [INFERRED] Therefore the only exact contradiction line seriously supported on
  disk is:
  improve the pressure-side contribution in the inherited LEI /
  epsilon-regularity contradiction ledger by shrinking the live far-field
  coefficient, or prove that no such shrinkage survives same-scale harmonic
  freedom.

  `full stretching control`,

  and `cancellation of the affine-and-higher harmonic moments`
  are introduced as Step-1 "frozen target quantities" before the run-006

- [VERIFIED] `judgments/chain-01.md` and `attacks/chain-01.md` both say this

- [INFERRED] The correct interpretation is:

- [INFERRED] Among them, `cancellation of affine-and-higher harmonic moments`

  already identifies those moments as the live source of the bad coefficient.
- [INFERRED] `pressure-Hessian/strain defect control` survives only as an
  admissible mechanism family if it yields estimate-level action on that same
  bad coefficient.
- [INFERRED] `full stretching control` is broadest and least frozen. On the
  present record it names a hoped-for bottleneck theme, not a theorem endpoint.

### 6. Rejections And Narrowing

- [VERIFIED] Reject `full stretching control` as a Step-1 endpoint freeze.
  It is too vague and remains theorem language without a theorem statement.
- [VERIFIED] Reject `pressure-Hessian/strain defect control` as an independent
  endpoint. It is only admissible as a mechanism family acting on the live
  far-field coefficient.
- [VERIFIED] Reject any endpoint setup that revives tensor packaging,
  observables, or Tao branding before freezing the contradiction line.
- [VERIFIED] Reject any claimed gain that only quotients by constants or
  controls generic harmonic regularity: affine and higher harmonic modes remain
  live, and generic harmonic estimates fail the Tao gate for this branch.

### 7. Does The Local Record Force A Return To The Inherited LEI / Epsilon-Regularity Architecture?

- [VERIFIED] Yes. The local record forces the branch back onto the inherited
  suitable-weak / Leray-Hopf plus LEI floor as the only theorem-attached setup
  frozen on disk.
- [VERIFIED] `ckn-1982-proof-architecture.md` and
  `lin-1998-proof-architecture.md` show the theorem-level endpoint package
  available on that floor:
  local-energy bookkeeping and epsilon-regularity exclusion.
- [VERIFIED] `step-001/RESULTS.md` and the far-field obstruction packet show
  the exact live pressure-side obstacle inside that architecture:
  the far-field pairing with fixed-energy-scale coefficient.
- [INFERRED] Run-006 does not replace that endpoint with a better-supported new
  theorem endpoint. It reopens the branch only to test whether a pressure-
  Hessian mechanism can act on that inherited live bad term strongly enough to
  matter.
- [INFERRED] So the exact Step-1 freeze should be:
  same inherited LEI / epsilon-regularity contradiction line, but with a
  sharpened estimate or defect identity on the live far-field pressure pairing.

## Conclusion

- [VERIFIED] The smallest serious theorem-endpoint menu is not three positive
  endpoints. It is one actual theorem endpoint plus a narrow mechanism submenu.
- [VERIFIED] The one actual theorem endpoint is the inherited LEI /
  CKN-Lin epsilon-regularity contradiction ledger, with the live bad term fixed
  as the far-field pressure pairing from `step-001`.
- [INFERRED] The only serious positive-looking subcandidate is not a separate
  endpoint but a mechanism claim:
  cancellation or quantitative reduction of the affine-and-higher harmonic
  moments, most plausibly through a pressure-Hessian defect identity, if and
  only if that shrinks the same live coefficient.
- [VERIFIED] Primary recommendation:
  freeze Step 1 on the inherited local-energy / epsilon-regularity
  contradiction line with one exact cash-out inequality on `I_p^far`.
  Reject `full stretching control` and free-standing `pressure-Hessian/strain
  defect control` as endpoint drift.
