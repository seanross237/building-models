**Mission:** Beyond De Giorgi — What Structural Property Could Break the NS
Regularity Barrier?

**Active chain:** `Calibrated Tao Differential Audit With Exact Theorem-Floor Freeze And Mandatory Evidence Gate`

**Why this step exists now:** `step-015` killed the previous geometry-first
branch at its theorem-floor gate, and planning run `run-011` responded by
selecting a stricter mission-wide audit chain rather than another single-family
mechanism bet. No executed step has weakened this new chain yet. The correct

contract, the theorem-facing frame, and the admission standard before building
candidate cards or spending budget on any one NS-specific feature.

Chronological `step-016` executes **logical Chain Step 1**, together with the
pre-activation scope lock that `CHAIN.md` requires before the chain is treated
as live.

## This Step Covers

This strategizer execution covers **Chain Step 1 only**:

- choose one exact Navier-Stokes formulation and one exact Tao averaged
  comparator;
- choose one comparison level only:
  `identity-level`, `coercivity-level`, `localization-level`,
  `propagation-level`, or `blowup-scenario-level`;
- state exactly what counts as `destroyed by averaging` at that chosen level;
- distinguish at least these statuses:
  `exact identity lost`,
  `formally preserved but no longer coercive`,
  `preserved globally but broken by localization`,
  `preserved statically but not dynamically propagated`,
  or `preserved`;
- freeze one intended theorem floor only:
  `suitable-weak / LEI` by default, or one named proxy class with explicit
  bridge debt;
- freeze one primary endpoint family only for the audit;
- freeze the four ledgers:
  `Tao distinctiveness`,
  `theorem-floor admissibility`,
  `endpoint contact`,
  and `bridge debt`;
- write one candidate that clearly passes the Tao filter and one that clearly
  fails; and
- decide whether the chain has earned entry into candidate-card construction.

Do **not** build the full candidate register yet. That belongs to Chain Step 2.
Do **not** rank mechanisms yet. Do **not** freeze one theorem-facing route per
candidate yet. Do **not** produce any same-currency artifact yet. Those belong
to later chain steps.

## Scope-Lock Discipline

This step must leave behind a single executable comparison frame rather than an
atmosphere of caution. Every promoted choice must be singular and explicit:

- one exact comparator pair only;
- one comparison level only;
- one theorem floor only;
- one primary endpoint family only; and
- one ledger structure only.

If the step needs multiple comparison levels, multiple theorem floors, or a
candidate-specific rescue story before it can define Tao distinctiveness, that
counts against the branch rather than for it.

   - state the exact Navier-Stokes formulation and exact Tao averaged comparator

   - freeze one intended theorem floor and explain why it is the correct floor
     now;
   - freeze one primary endpoint family for the audit and explain why it is the
     right endpoint family for the current branch;
   - freeze the four ledgers:
     `Tao distinctiveness`,
     `theorem-floor admissibility`,
     `endpoint contact`,
     and `bridge debt`.

4. **Pass/fail example packet**
   - give one candidate example that clearly passes the Tao filter at the chosen
     level;

5. **Step verdict**
   - classify the outcome as one of:
     - `scope lock passed, proceed to typed candidate cards`

   - if the step passes, freeze exactly the comparator pair, comparison level,
     theorem floor, endpoint family, ledger structure, and pass/fail examples

   - if the step fails, say plainly that the branch should not continue into
     candidate-card construction.

6. **Source map**
   - name the exact source files used for comparator choice, comparison-level

  candidate-by-candidate audit;

- the comparison level drifts between identity, localization, propagation, or
  scenario language depending on the candidate;

  actually use; or

  an executable comparison contract.

If a kill condition fires, count that as a successful bounded negative result
for this step and recommend chain invalidation before candidate-card work.
