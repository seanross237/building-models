# Exploration 003 Goal

## Objective

Choose one bookkeeping currency of gain, define the bounded exact-rewrite
family, and state the novelty / Step-2-readiness memo for `step-005`.

This exploration should run after `exploration-001` and `exploration-002`, or
at least inherit their most likely decisions as provisional inputs.

## Inputs To Inherit

Assume the step is converging toward:

- architecture:
  `local-energy flux/localization`
- one fixed localized bad term in that architecture
- one frozen cutoff / projection / commutator protocol
- one compatible weak-solution package

## Required Questions

Answer all of the following from local repository sources:

1. Which bookkeeping currency of gain is the most honest for this branch?
   Choose exactly one from:
   - derivative count,
   - integrability or exponent improvement,
   - coefficient shrinkage,
   - or a strictly weaker hypothesis in the same closure scheme.
2. What bounded family of at most `2-3` exact reformulations of the same
   quadratic nonlinearity should be frozen for the audit?
3. For each candidate, what is its intended leverage point on the fixed bad
   term, and what is the first obvious reason it may be cosmetic once
   localization/projection bookkeeping is paid?
4. Should the step admit one same-target hybrid route? Only include one if it
   genuinely still attacks the same bad term in the same architecture.
5. What is genuinely new here relative to:
   - the De Giorgi sharpness record,
   - the pressure-route negatives,
   - nearby prior art on the chosen architecture,
   - and the killed geometry branch?
6. Is the candidate family concrete enough for Step 2's Tao discriminator
   screen, or should the branch be invalidated now?

## Working Hypothesis To Test, Not Assume

The strategizer's provisional shortlist is:

- exact divergence / stress form of the quadratic interaction
- Lamb-vector / Helmholtz-projected form
- vorticity transport form

with **no** hybrid route unless the source record identifies one that still
targets the same localized bad term in the same architecture.

The provisional gain currency is:

- `coefficient shrinkage` or, if the sources force it more sharply,
  `closure improvement in the same localized balance`.

Confirm, refine, or reject this package.

## Success Criteria

- One gain currency is chosen and justified.
- The candidate family is bounded to `2-3` items and stated explicitly.
- Each item gets:
  - intended leverage point,
  - first obvious cosmetic-risk diagnosis,
  - and status as exact rewrite or hybrid route.
- The novelty claim is narrow and source-auditable.
- The memo gives a clear yes/no on Step-2 readiness.

## Failure Criteria

- The family cannot be bounded honestly.
- The supposed novelty collapses into already-settled negatives.
- No candidate family survives as a concrete Tao-screen input.

If failure occurs, say so directly and recommend early branch invalidation.
