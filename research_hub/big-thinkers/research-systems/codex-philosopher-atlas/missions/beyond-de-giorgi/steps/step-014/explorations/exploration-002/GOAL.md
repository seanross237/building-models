# Exploration 002 Goal

## Objective

For the active candidates selected in exploration 001, derive:
- the exact full evolution;
- the localized equation packet that actually interfaces with
  `I_flux[phi] = ∬ (|u|^2 + 2p) u · ∇phi`;
- the visible debt ledger.

## Required Output

Produce:
- `REPORT.md`
- `REPORT-SUMMARY.md`

For each active candidate, the report must:
- name the exact unknown or reformulated package;
- derive the full exact evolution in enough detail to expose the operative
  nonlinear structure;
- separate exact NS structure from generic projection / CZ / singular-integral
  packaging;
- derive the localized form actually relevant to the frozen LEI burden rather
  than a different bad term;
- record pressure re-entry, cutoff commutators, vector reconstruction debt,
  admissibility debt, and locality loss immediately;
- say whether the localized packet still acts on the full frozen burden or only
  on a surrogate object.

## Success Criteria

- Each active candidate gets one exact-equation sheet and one localized debt
  ledger.
- The report makes the re-entry mechanism explicit rather than hand-waving it.
- The fixed cutoff protocol is preserved throughout.

## Failure Criteria

- A candidate has no exact package precise enough to derive.
- The localized discussion drifts to stretching, geometry, or another branch's
  target instead of `I_flux[phi]`.
- Debt entries are postponed or omitted.
