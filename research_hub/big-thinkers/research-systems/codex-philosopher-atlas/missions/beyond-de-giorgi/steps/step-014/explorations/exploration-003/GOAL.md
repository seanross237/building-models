# Exploration 003 Goal

## Objective

For the active candidates from explorations 001 and 002, write:
- the endpoint-exchange memo;
- the nearest-prior-art packet;
- the Step-2 verdict recommendation.

## Required Output

Produce:
- `REPORT.md`
- `REPORT-SUMMARY.md`

For each active candidate, the report must:
- state how any native-variable gain would have to cash out into a smaller
  effective coefficient on the full localized LEI burden;
- identify the transfer lemma that would be required if the gain is not already
  in that currency;
- mark visible losses already present before promotion;
- name the nearest prior-art comparator on the repository record;
- state the exact shared structure and the exact claimed mathematical delta;
- say whether the candidate looks recycled, unresolved but not yet formally
  classified, or live enough for Step 3.

Then give one step-level recommendation choosing exactly one of:
- `candidate slate passed, proceed to anti-repackaging and prior-art gate`
- `recycled-reformulation obstruction, branch should stop before anti-repackaging gate`
- `exchange-failure obstruction, branch should stop before anti-repackaging gate`

## Success Criteria

- Every active candidate gets a concrete exchange memo and prior-art packet.
- The final verdict is tied to the frozen LEI burden currency.
- The report states plainly whether continuation into Step 3 is earned.

## Failure Criteria

- A candidate is kept alive without a named exchange path.
- Prior-art overlap is discussed only generically.
- The verdict outruns the actual equation and debt packets.
