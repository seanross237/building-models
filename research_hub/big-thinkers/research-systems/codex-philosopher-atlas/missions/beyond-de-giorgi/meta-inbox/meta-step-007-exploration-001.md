# Meta-Learning: Step 007, Exploration 001

- A host space does not earn Step-1 viability just because its norm appears as
  a scale-invariant quantity inside the suitable-weak partial-regularity
  architecture. Scaling relevance is not the same thing as a full
  extraction-package theorem packet.
- When the strongest local evidence for a critical space comes from inherited
  LEI architecture rather than a dedicated host-space local-theory / stability
  / extraction packet, that should count as a package obstruction, not as
  partial clearance.
- Endpoint-prior-art adjacency should be logged as an overlap burden.
  For `L^3`, strong proximity to endpoint regularity results raises the bar for
  admission rather than lowering it.
