# Exploration 002 Goal - Recursive Closure And Spillover Audit

## Objective

Run the recursive exact closure audit on each canonical first-budget seed from
Exploration 001.

The report must answer:

- what conjugate-completion companions are mandatory immediately;
- what forced helical sign sectors and exact projected triad interactions must
  be checked next;
- whether each seed reaches a genuine finite fixed point inside the current
  budget or spills beyond it; and
- what the decisive failure reason is for every dead seed.

## Success Criteria

- Every canonical seed ends with one sharp status:
  finite fixed point,
  budget spill,
  exactness failure,
  or representation dependence.
- The report identifies any closure-forced spectator or companion modes
  explicitly.
- The closure order is concrete enough for controller review.

## Failure Criteria

- The report only checks first-generation closure.
- Closure-forced spectator modes are omitted or pruned.
- A claimed survivor is only finite after suppressing forced sign sectors or
  mirror interactions.
