## What Worked

- Screening out repeated-wavevector and collinear "triads" at the seed stage
  prevented the step from treating non-seeds as if they were failed survivors.
- Keeping the sign-sheet quotient only as strong as the frozen Step-1
  equivalence rules preserved honesty without overcomplicating the first-budget
  catalog.

## Reusable Lesson

- When a support-search budget is "one exact triad orbit," first separate
  exact active triads from degenerate wavevector relations before doing any
  closure work; otherwise later negatives mix seed-definition failure with
  genuine closure failure.

## Caution

- Do not collapse mixed sign-sheet classes more aggressively than the frozen
  equivalence sheet actually allows; over-collapsing can hide real support
  distinctions.
