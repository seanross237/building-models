# Meta Note - Step 003 Exploration 001

## What Worked

- Reducing every connected two-triad shared-mode picture to the canonical form
  `{ (a,b,a+b), (a,c,a+c) }`
  exposed which drawings were genuinely new second-budget seeds and which only
  recycled the failed first-budget pseudo-support.
- Separating the generic five-orbit fan from the four-orbit
  mirror/parallelogram subfamily prevented cosmetic re-centered drawings from
  being counted as distinct seeds.

## Reusable Lesson

- In support-search steps above the first rung, classify by exact support
  identity after conjugation and triad relabeling before doing closure.
  Otherwise the step will confuse re-centered copies and first-budget
  decorations with new ladder-level seeds.

## Caution

- A two-triad picture that fits inside the old mirror-complete one-triad
  support is not a genuine second-rung seed even if it displays two distinct
  triad equations.
