# Fix A Provisional Stretching Representation Early In Geometry Screens

When screening geometry candidates, choose a provisional full-stretching
representation early instead of waiting until after broad candidate triage.

Exploration 002 showed that otherwise the required "what term becomes smaller?"
answer stays too schematic. Even a provisional representation makes it easier
to ask whether a candidate is claiming a smaller:

- local or self-induced stretching term
- exterior or nonlocal strain term
- localization or inter-scale interaction term
