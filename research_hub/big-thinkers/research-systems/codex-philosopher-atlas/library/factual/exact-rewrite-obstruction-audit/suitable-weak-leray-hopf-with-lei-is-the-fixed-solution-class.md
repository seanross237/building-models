# Suitable Weak Leray-Hopf With LEI Is The Fixed Solution Class

Status: `INFERRED`

For the fixed-protocol exact-rewrite audit, the operative solution class is
suitable weak solutions in the Leray-Hopf energy class, with the local energy
inequality available.

That is the correct compatibility floor because the chosen bad term

`I_flux[phi] = ∬ (|u|^2 + 2p) u . ∇phi`

lives inside the local-energy inequality package itself. Dropping to mere
distributional weak solutions would remove the architecture under audit, while
upgrading to mild or classical solutions would import regularity the branch
has not earned.

The inherited data that remains non-negotiable is:

- divergence-free velocity
- `u ∈ L^∞_t L^2_x ∩ L^2_t H^1_x`
- pressure in the suitable-weak-solution sense
- the local energy inequality
