# Exact Rewrite Obstruction Audit

Reusable findings for the fixed-protocol audit of exact Navier-Stokes
reformulations against the localized LEI cutoff-flux bundle.

## Entries

- `suitable-weak-leray-hopf-with-lei-is-the-fixed-solution-class.md`: the
  branch is locked to suitable weak solutions in the Leray-Hopf energy class
  with the local energy inequality available.
- `every-rewrite-must-stay-inside-the-same-localized-lei-package.md`: a
  candidate only counts if it remains meaningful in the same suitable-weak
  localized LEI setting before and after localization.
- `freeze-one-ckn-style-parabolic-cutoff-protocol-for-the-rewrite-audit.md`:
  all candidate rewrites inherit one CKN-style parabolic cylinder, one smooth
  cutoff, and one shared `I_flux[phi]` bookkeeping package.
- `the-exact-rewrite-endpoint-window-is-one-primary-exclusion-endpoint-plus-one-reserve-continuation-family.md`:
  the Step-013 endpoint window is one primary concentration-exclusion family on
  the inherited LEI / CKN ledger, with continuation language kept as a
  reserve-only fallback that must be earned on that same ledger.
- `anti-repackaging-for-reformulation-packets-uses-three-outcomes-on-the-full-iflux-bundle.md`:
  later reformulation packets are classified by their net effect on the full
  `I_flux[phi]` bundle after all debt is restored, with one middle
  `ambiguous redistribution` bucket kept only for exact unresolved transfer
  steps.
- `later-reformulation-packets-must-carry-a-nine-line-nearest-prior-art-audit.md`:
  later candidate packets must name one nearest comparator, one exact shared
  structure, one exact mathematical delta, the charged debts, and one
  same-currency cash-out or explicit transfer lemma on the frozen ledger.
- `projected-and-vorticity-rewrites-must-pay-localization-debt.md`: projected,
  Lamb-vector, and vorticity rewrites earn no credit unless their projection,
  Biot-Savart, and commutator debts are paid after localization.
- `divergence-stress-form-is-tao-insufficient-under-the-fixed-lei-ledger.md`:
  divergence/stress survives only as identity-level packaging, since
  integration by parts recreates the same stress-against-`grad phi` burden.
- `lamb-vector-projected-form-is-tao-insufficient-once-localization-debt-returns.md`:
  the projected Lamb-vector decomposition does not lower the fixed LEI
  coefficient once the exact localized pairing is written out: the Lamb-vector
  term vanishes against `u phi`, but `pi = p + |u|^2 / 2` restores the same
  cutoff-flux bundle and its pressure / projection debt. The Step-014
  comparator audit also shows no new theorem-facing delta or exchange path
  beyond the Step-006 packet.
- `vorticity-biot-savart-form-is-tao-insufficient-on-the-fixed-lei-ledger.md`:
  the vorticity/Biot-Savart representation earns no smaller coefficient on the
  fixed ledger because its native equation acts on a surrogate and only
  reaches the full `I_flux[phi]` burden after full velocity and pressure
  reconstruction, where the same nonlocal debt returns. The Step-014
  comparator audit also shows no new theorem-facing delta or exchange path
  beyond the Step-006 packet.
- `step-1-exact-rewrite-family-is-bounded-to-three-candidates.md`: the Step-1
  candidate table is limited to divergence/stress, Lamb-vector/projected, and
  vorticity/Biot-Savart rewrites, all judged on the same fixed ledger.
- `step-014-freezes-the-step-2-active-slate-to-lamb-vector-and-vorticity-with-divergence-as-reserve.md`:
  once the shortlist and burden ledger are frozen, the Step-2 active slate
  keeps Lamb-vector/projected and vorticity/Biot-Savart active, leaves
  divergence/stress as reserve-only comparator, and excludes branch-adjacent
  families such as pressure-Hessian from this audit.
- `step-014-active-exact-rewrite-slate-fails-first-at-endpoint-exchange.md`:
  once the full `I_flux[phi]` ledger is kept fixed, both active Step-2
  rewrites fail first on missing same-currency exchange, so prior-art overlap
  and anti-repackaging stay secondary to the Step-014 exchange-failure
  verdict.
- `same-target-hybrids-are-excluded-at-step-1.md`: Step 1 does not admit
  pressure-side or geometry-side hybrids before the pure rewrite family is
  screened.
- `the-branch-earned-novelty-is-a-bounded-auditable-rewrite-table.md`: the
  branch's earned novelty is procedural and bounded: one frozen architecture,
  protocol, and candidate table for later screening.
- `the-bounded-rewrite-table-is-ready-for-step-2-despite-a-negative-baseline.md`:
  the exact-rewrite branch is concrete enough for the Step-2 Tao screen even

- `step-013-scope-lock-passes-once-the-classifier-and-prior-art-audit-are-frozen.md`:

  because its endpoint window, burden ledger, anti-repackaging classifier, and
