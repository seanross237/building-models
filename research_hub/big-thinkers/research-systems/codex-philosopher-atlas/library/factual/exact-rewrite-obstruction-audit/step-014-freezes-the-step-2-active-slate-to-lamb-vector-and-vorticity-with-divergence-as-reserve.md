# Step-014 Freezes The Step-2 Active Slate To Lamb Vector And Vorticity With Divergence As Reserve

Status: `INFERRED` slate freeze with `VERIFIED` shortlist and ledger support

The Step-014 exact-rewrite candidate slate is not an open menu.

Once the repository record has already frozen the admissible exact-rewrite
family to `divergence / stress form`, `Lamb-vector / Helmholtz-projected
form`, and `vorticity transport / Biot-Savart form`, the Step-2 active slate
should keep active only the families that still name a nontrivial route back
to the same frozen localized LEI cutoff-flux bundle
`I_flux[phi] = ∬ (|u|^2 + 2p) u . grad phi`.

On that record:

- `Lamb-vector / Helmholtz-projected form` stays active because it is the
  sharpest exact algebraic delta on disk and still names a testable route back
  to the same `I_flux[phi]` burden, even though the later Tao screen already
  warns that localization, pressure, Calderon-Zygmund, and commutator debt
  likely erase the gain.
- `vorticity transport / Biot-Savart form` stays active because it is the
  only other repository-native rewrite with a concrete theorem-facing story on
  the same ledger: any gain would have to appear by rewriting velocity-side
  factors in the same localized flux bundle through `omega` and then repaying
  the full Biot-Savart and localization debt.
- `divergence / stress form` is reserve only because its most honest
  insertion point localizes straight back to the same stress-against-`grad
  phi` burden and therefore serves mainly as a control comparator rather than
  as one of the active audit slots.

Branch-adjacent families such as `strain / pressure-Hessian` are omitted from
this slate because they were not part of the frozen exact-rewrite shortlist.
Reintroducing them here would reopen a different branch architecture rather
than sharpen the bounded audit already on disk.
