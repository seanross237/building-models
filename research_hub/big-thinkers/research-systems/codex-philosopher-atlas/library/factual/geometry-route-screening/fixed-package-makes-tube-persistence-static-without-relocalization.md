# Fixed Package Makes Tube Persistence Static Without Relocalization

Status: `VERIFIED` with `INFERRED` consequence

Under the same Step-3 neutral Eulerian package, tube coherence or persistence
does not come with a repository-backed transport law for connected components,
tube axes, or "the same tube" across time. The only honest dynamic reading is
therefore indirect: intense superlevel geometry inside `E_r(t)` might remain
tube-like for part of the parabolic window.

That weak heuristic is not enough to keep the comparator dynamically live on
its own. In the fixed ball one still has to account for threshold crossings,
component births and deaths, mergers and splits, and leakage across
`partial B_r`. Once those burdens are named explicitly, the route collapses
toward descriptive tube language unless one installs tube-adapted relocalization
or stronger tracking by hand.

The reusable consequence is strict: under this package, needing tube-fitted
tracking is a negative result rather than a rescue, because the localization
would then be building in the very persistence that the audit was supposed to
test.
