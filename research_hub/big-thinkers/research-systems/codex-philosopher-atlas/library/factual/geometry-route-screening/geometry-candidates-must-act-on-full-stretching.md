# Geometry Candidates Must Act On Full Stretching

Status: `VERIFIED` with `INFERRED` geometry-screen specialization

For the geometry route, the live target is the full stretching mechanism
`S omega . omega`, not a local geometric proxy.

Exploration 002 sharpened the screen into a reusable branch rule:

- a candidate only survives if it points to a smaller contribution in one full
  stretching representation
- before Step 4 fixes that representation, the admissible targets can be named
  schematically as a smaller local or self-induced stretching piece, a smaller
  exterior or nonlocal strain piece, or a smaller localization or inter-scale
  interaction term
- claims that only improve local alignment language, concentration language, or
  the Lamb-vector side `u x omega` do not count as live progress

This is the geometry analogue of the earlier pressure-branch Tao gate: the
screen asks which exact full-stretching term becomes smaller, not which nearby
geometric picture becomes cleaner.
