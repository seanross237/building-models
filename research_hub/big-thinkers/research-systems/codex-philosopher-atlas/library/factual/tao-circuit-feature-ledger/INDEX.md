# Tao Circuit Feature Ledger

Reusable findings for freezing the load-bearing features of Tao-like
delayed-threshold circuits before exact-Navier-Stokes packet testing.

## Entries

- `ordered-delayed-threshold-stage-order-is-essential.md`: Tao-likeness
  requires the inherited delayed-abrupt transfer sequence, not just a sparse
  interaction graph on the same roles.
- `delayed-threshold-trigger-logic-is-load-bearing.md`: the circuit must keep
  the exponentially small trigger and delayed activation mechanism, rather than
  collapsing into an ordinary sparse cascade.
- `tao-circuit-role-hierarchy-is-essential.md`: the slow clock, tiny trigger,
  amplifier, rotor exchange, and next-shell transfer roles are distinct
  causal jobs, not optional labels.
- `amplitude-hierarchy-is-load-bearing-even-when-literal-coefficients-are-not.md`:
  Tao's literal formulas may vary, but the ordered size hierarchy they encode
  must survive.
- `admissible-leakage-must-be-declared-but-is-a-branch-level-policy-choice.md`:
  near-isolation is load-bearing, while the exact admissible-leakage rule must
  be declared at the branch level rather than inherited as a theorem fact.
- `summable-time-scale-separation-is-essential-to-tao-likeness.md`: the shell
  transfer times must shrink summably while still supporting repeated
  delayed-abrupt transfers.
- `the-tiny-trigger-role-is-dynamically-central.md`: the tiny trigger is
  energetically negligible before threshold but dynamically decisive.
- `literal-ode-coordinates-coefficient-formulas-and-shell-packaging-are-dispensable.md`:
  coordinate names, literal coefficient formulas, and shell-style packaging
  are secondary to preserving the role ledger.
- `tao-likeness-requires-a-five-part-canonicalization-screen.md`: later steps
  should use one explicit five-part screen to decide whether a packet family is
  honestly Tao-like.
- `destroyed-by-averaging-means-loss-under-averaged-circuit-freedoms-not-under-canonical-relabeling.md`:
  a feature is destroyed by averaging only if it survives canonicalization,
  depends on exact-NS packet constraints, and disappears specifically when
  Tao-style averaged freedoms replace those constraints.
