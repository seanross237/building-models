# Tao-Likeness Requires A Five-Part Canonicalization Screen

Status: `VERIFIED` core screen with `INFERRED` bookkeeping and leakage clauses

Later steps should treat a packet family as Tao-like only if all five of the
following survive canonicalization:

- identifiable carrier, delay-clock, tiny-trigger, transfer-conduit, and
  next-stage carrier roles;
- the inherited ordered delayed-threshold sequence rather than an arbitrary
  sparse network;
- a real dominant-channel hierarchy with weak clock pump, much smaller seed,
  larger post-threshold amplifier and rotor response, and delayed next-stage
  transfer;
- phase and helical-sign bookkeeping tight enough to prevent representation
  drift;
- and spectator couplings bounded by a declared admissible-leakage rule on a
  declared finite time window.

A family that is only sparse, multiscale, or triad-engineered but lacks this
package should not count as Tao-like.
