# Affine And Higher Harmonic Modes Survive As Moments

Status: `INFERRED`

With `F_k = v_k phi_k^2 e_hat` and `psi_k = div(F_k)`, an affine harmonic
function `h(x) = a + b . x` gives

`-\int h psi_k dx = b . \int F_k dx`.

No source-backed structural reason forces this vector moment to vanish.
Quadratic-and-higher harmonic pieces likewise reduce to moments of `F_k`
against polynomial gradients, and no cancellation mechanism in the inherited
source trail annihilates them wholesale.

Only the constant harmonic mode is automatically killed. Affine and higher
harmonic content remains live in the exact far-field pairing.
