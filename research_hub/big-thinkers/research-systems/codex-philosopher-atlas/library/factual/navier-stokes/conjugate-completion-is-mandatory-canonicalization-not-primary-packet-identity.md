# Conjugate Completion Is Mandatory Canonicalization Not Primary Packet Identity

Status: `VERIFIED` real-valuedness constraint with `INFERRED` identity policy

Real-valued velocity fields force conjugate completion in the canonical
representation of a packet, but that mirror completion should not be treated as
the packet's primary identity.

The primary object is the role-labeled helical packet on one chosen canonical
sheet. The conjugate mirror packet is mandatory bookkeeping that makes the
representation real-valued. This keeps two errors separate: treating conjugate
completion as optional, and treating it as if it were the whole support object.
