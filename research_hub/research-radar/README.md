# Research Radar

Research Radar is the home-base system for continuously collecting, analyzing, and surfacing material relevant to Eywa and adjacent agent research.

It is the source of truth for:

- Topics we care about
- What we care about right now
- Nightly collection pipelines
- Analysis and relevance scoring
- Presentation candidate queues
- The local copy of the Railway agent runner

Raw collection and analysis artifacts live under `research-radar/data/`.
Published decks live under `home-base/presentation_hub/`.
