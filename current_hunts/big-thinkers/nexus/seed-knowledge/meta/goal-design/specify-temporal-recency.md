## Lesson

When the goal involves literature search and you want recent results, specify an explicit time window (e.g., "focus on recent (2024-2026) developments"). This produces genuinely current results rather than the explorer defaulting to well-known older work.

## Evidence

- **riemann-hypothesis strategy-001 exploration 001** — Specifying "focus on recent (2024-2026) developments" got genuinely current results. Without this directive, explorers tend to report established results from the standard reference literature.

## When to apply

Any exploration that needs recent or cutting-edge results. Especially important for rapidly moving fields. Less critical when the goal is a comprehensive historical survey.
