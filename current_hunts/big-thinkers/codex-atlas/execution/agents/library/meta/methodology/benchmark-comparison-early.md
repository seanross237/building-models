## Lesson

Always compare a new theory or framework to the established benchmark early in the exploration cycle. This reveals the gap between "research program" and "proven theory" before too many explorations are invested in development.

## Evidence

- **strategy-001 exploration 008** — Compared SCG v2.0 to QG+F using a 5-tier validation framework. Made the gap between SCG (research program) and QG+F (proven theory) starkly clear. The framing "natural philosophy vs physics" was the most honest assessment. If done at exploration 004 or 005 instead of 008, it would have saved effort.

## When to apply

After the first construction exploration, before investing heavily in repairs and extensions. The benchmark comparison tells you whether you're building on sand or solid ground. Also useful as a reality check whenever a framework starts feeling compelling -- compare it to what already works.
