# Unitarity of Lee-Wick Quantum Gravity: Definitively Resolved — FAILS

## Summary

The unitarity of Lee-Wick QG with the CLOP prescription was the central open question for this approach. **It is now resolved by five independent lines of evidence: the CLOP prescription fails unitarity.** Crucially, the creator of LW QG (Modesto) co-authored the 2025 paper reaching this conclusion.

## Evidence 1: Direct Unitarity Violation — Kubo & Kugo (2023)

**Paper:** "Unitarity Violation in Field Theories of Lee-Wick's Complex Ghost" (arXiv:2308.09006, PTEP 2023)

- Complex ghosts ARE created in fourth-order derivative theories above a threshold energy
- Mechanism: radiative corrections give complex masses; "complex delta function" at vertices enforces complex energy conservation → ghost production
- Below threshold: unitary. **Above threshold: unitarity violated.**
- Devastating for LW QG because the violation occurs precisely at high energies where QG effects matter

## Evidence 2: Structural Problems — Anselmi (2022)

**Paper:** "Purely virtual particles versus Lee-Wick ghosts" (arXiv:2202.10483, Phys. Rev. D 105, 2022)

Three fatal structural problems with the LW prescription:
1. Removing LW ghosts from external states → non-Hermitian classical limit
2. Including all states → unbounded Hamiltonian or negative norms
3. LW ghosts are NOT purely virtual — they leave imaginary remnants

The proposed fix (superposition of fakeon + particle) breaks general covariance → **cannot be applied to gravity**.

## Evidence 3: Definitive Comparison — Anselmi, Briscese, Calcagni & Modesto (2025)

**Paper:** "Amplitude prescriptions in field theories with complex poles" (arXiv:2503.01841, JHEP05(2025)145)

**THE definitive paper** — co-authored by BOTH Anselmi (fakeon creator) AND Modesto (LW QG creator):
- Compares four inequivalent prescriptions for complex poles (see `prescription-comparison.md`)
- The LWN/CLOP prescription breaks Lorentz invariance at the quantum level
- **Verdict: Only the fakeon/AP prescription is physically viable**
- This paper effectively ends Lee-Wick QG as an independent research program

## Evidence 4: Bound State Impossibility — Oda (2026)

**Paper:** "Bound States in Lee's Complex Ghost Model" (arXiv:2602.05562, Feb 2026)

- Shows "bound states cannot be created from ghosts by contributions of a complex delta function"
- "Violation of the unitarity is also connected to the non-existence of bound states"
- Refutes the ghost confinement strategy proposed by Asorey et al. (2025)

## Evidence 5: Ghost Resonance Structure — Buoninfante (2025)

**Paper:** "Remarks on ghost resonances" (arXiv:2501.04097, JHEP 2025)

- Ghost propagator has complex conjugate poles in the **FIRST Riemann sheet**
- Ordinary unstable particles (resonances) have poles only in the **second Riemann sheet**
- This structural difference is fundamental: ghosts cannot be treated like ordinary unstable resonances
- Confirms the Lee-Wick argument that "ghosts are just unstable particles" is flawed

## The Ghost Confinement Strategy — Also Fails

Asorey, Krein, Pardina & Shapiro (arXiv:2408.16514, JHEP 2025) proposed that complex ghost pairs could form bound states confining unphysical excitations. However:
- The theory "does not admit a Källén-Lehmann representation and does not satisfy the positivity criterion"
- This is a confinement strategy, NOT unitarity restoration
- Oda (2026) explicitly showed bound states don't form from complex delta functions
- **Conclusion: the ghost confinement approach does not salvage LW QG**
