## Finding

No amplituhedron for gravity exists. Partial geometric descriptions are available at tree level (CHY pushforward, associahedral grid). After 5+ years, the "gravituhedron" program has not produced a geometric object. N=8 SUGRA's UV finiteness is necessary but not sufficient without Yangian symmetry.

## Three Fundamental Obstacles

1. **No Yangian symmetry**: Gravity lacks the dual superconformal structure that generates the Yangian in N=4 SYM
2. **Harder BCFW singularity structure**: Graviton amplitudes grow as z² at large z (for non-adjacent BCFW shifts), not z⁻¹ — fewer recursion relations work directly
3. **KLT double-copy lacks geometric meaning**: Gravity amplitudes are "squares" of YM amplitudes, but "squaring a geometry" has no clear geometric interpretation

## Trnka's Gravituhedron Program (arXiv:2012.15780, JHEP 2021)

Trnka defines **G-invariants** (gravity analogs of N=4 R-invariants) and uses them to write explicit formulas for NMHV gravity amplitudes up to **8 points**. Structural parallels between YM and gravity amplitude expressions suggest a geometric object might exist.

**What works**: Explicit NMHV gravity amplitude formulas up to 8-pt; factorization diagram methods from YM; structural parallels.

**What doesn't work**: No actual geometric object (the "gravituhedron") constructed; only NMHV, not all-multiplicity; dual superconformal symmetry is fundamentally absent (not a technical gap). The paper explicitly says "towards" the gravituhedron.

**Status (2025)**: After 5+ years, no polytope has been found. The gravituhedron remains an aspirational program.

## CHY Pushforward — Partial Geometric Description (arXiv:2206.14196, JHEP)

Lukowski, Moerman, Stalknecht: For theories captured by CHY, the canonical form of a positive geometry in moduli space **pushes forward** via scattering equations to the kinematic space canonical form. Gravity amplitudes are computable from a geometric object, but the geometry lives in **moduli space** and requires the non-trivial scattering equations map to reach kinematic space.

**Assessment**: Partial — a geometric description exists, but it is one step removed from a direct kinematic-space geometry.

## Associahedral Grid and Inverse KLT (arXiv:2508.20161, 2025)

Most recent promising development: the associahedral grid provides the **inverse KLT kernel** a geometric interpretation. The double-copy structure (gravity = YM²) has a geometric home for the first time. However, this does not give a "graviton amplituhedron" — it gives the KLT map a geometric home, not the graviton amplitudes themselves.

## N=8 SUGRA: UV Finite but No Amplituhedron

N=8 SUGRA (maximal SUSY gravity, 4D) is UV-finite through at least 6 loops. Color-kinematics duality applies; KLT = (N=4 SYM)² at the amplitude level. But there is still no amplituhedron.

**Key lesson**: UV finiteness is necessary but **not sufficient** for an amplituhedron. The lack of dual conformal symmetry (gravity backgrounds break it) is the remaining primary obstacle. You need both UV finiteness AND Yangian symmetry.
