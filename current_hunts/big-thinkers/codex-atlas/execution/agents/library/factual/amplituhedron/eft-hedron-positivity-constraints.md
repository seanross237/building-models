## Overview

The EFT-hedron (Arkani-Hamed, T.-C. Huang, Y.-T. Huang, JHEP 2021) translates the positive geometry philosophy into concrete, real-world constraints on EFT Wilson coefficients. This is the most practically impactful output of the amplituhedron program — it applies to real theories (QED, gravitational EFT, Standard Model EFT), not just N=4 SYM.

**Background** (Adams, Arkani-Hamed et al. 2006): Requiring forward-scattering positivity (causality + unitarity) constrains which low-energy EFTs admit UV completions. The EFT-hedron formalizes this as positive geometry: the space of UV-completable Wilson coefficients forms a "totally positive" geometric object.

## Specific Constraints

- **Amplitude growth bound**: Amplitude growth faster than E^6 in some regime → EFT CANNOT be UV-completed.
- **Photon-photon scattering**: Specific bounds on derivative expansion coefficients of the photon EFT (QED with higher-derivative operators).
- **Graviton-graviton scattering**: Specific bounds on gravitational EFT operator coefficients.
- **Upper bound on new physics mass**: The EFT-hedron gives an upper bound on the mass of the next lightest new state in any UV-completable EFT — this constrains how far a consistent EFT can extend.
- **Infinitely many constraints**: Both linear and nonlinear inequalities on Wilson coefficients, not just a single bound.

## Status

**ESTABLISHED, PRACTICALLY RELEVANT.** These bounds:
- Apply to real theories (QED, Standard Model EFT, gravitational EFT)
- Constrain which UV completions are possible
- Cannot be derived from standard Wilsonian EFT alone — require the positivity geometry framework
- Are derived from causality + unitarity = positive geometry, a direct descendant of the amplituhedron program

**Limitation**: The sharpest bounds rely on scalar forward-scattering positivity; non-scalar theories require more care. Some bounds are less sharp than initially claimed in certain kinematic limits. The EFT-hedron is not as complete or numerically precise as the amplituhedron itself.

## Distinction from the Amplituhedron Itself

The EFT-hedron is conceptually related to but technically distinct from the N=4 SYM amplituhedron:
- The amplituhedron computes exact amplitudes in a specific theory
- The EFT-hedron constrains the space of consistent EFTs using the same positivity principle

The EFT-hedron is the most important demonstration that the positive geometry program generates **genuinely new physics** beyond reformulation — these Wilson coefficient bounds were not known before the geometric perspective.
