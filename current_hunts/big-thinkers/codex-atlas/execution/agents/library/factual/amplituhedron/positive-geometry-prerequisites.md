## Finding

Four structural features of planar N=4 SYM make the amplituhedron possible. Understanding which are essential vs. merely convenient is critical for identifying where the framework can extend.

## Essential Features

**1. Yangian Symmetry**
The amplituhedron requires Yangian symmetry Y(psu(2,2|4)) — an infinite-dimensional algebra generated by combining ordinary superconformal symmetry (psu(2,2|4)) with dual superconformal symmetry. Dual superconformal symmetry acts on "dual coordinates" xᵢ defined via pᵢ = xᵢ − xᵢ₊₁; it is a non-local symmetry of scattering amplitudes with no analog in a standard Lagrangian. This Yangian appears to be unique to planar N=4 SYM among 4D gauge theories. It does NOT appear in N<4 SYM, pure Yang-Mills, or gravity.

**2. Planarity (Large-N)**
The amplituhedron is defined only for planar (large-N_c) N=4 SYM. The planar limit restricts to color-ordered amplitudes (single-trace operators) and enables the amplitude/Wilson loop duality: planar loop amplitudes = null polygonal Wilson loops in dual space. This duality is what makes dual conformal symmetry visible. Non-planar amplitudes have no known amplituhedron.

**3. UV Finiteness (Maximal Supersymmetry)**
Maximal SUSY (N=4) suppresses all UV divergences in the planar sector. UV divergences would appear as poles at infinity in the BCFW/Grassmannian framework, breaking the clean identification of amplitude = canonical form of positive geometry. In N<4, UV poles appear and deform the geometric measure. N=4 is special: the β-function vanishes to all orders, amplitudes are IR-divergent but UV-finite, and IR divergences exponentiate cleanly (BDS ansatz), allowing a clean geometric picture of the remainder.

## Convenient (Not Essential) Features

**Superspace formulation**: N=4 SYM admits a superamplitude A_n(Λ) packaging all helicity components. In momentum twistor space, this has positive-definite geometric properties. But the momentum amplituhedron reformulation (arXiv:1906.10700) shows spinor helicity variables work equally well — the twistor formulation is not essential, just elegant.

**Specific particle content**: Beyond the requirement of maximal SUSY, the precise matter content does not matter.

## Implication

Extensions to non-Yangian theories can at most achieve "scattering form" / partial positive geometry (Tier 2), not a full amplituhedron. The Yangian is the hard barrier to the full construction.
