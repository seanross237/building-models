## What Was Established

   The desired `a1^2 -> a2` and `a1^2 -> a3` channels force roles 2 and 3 onto the same conjugate wavevector pair, after which the amplifier `a2 a3 -> a3` cannot satisfy exact triad closure for any sign choice.

2. This is stronger than a leakage estimate.

   The cleanest obstruction is not "spectator couplings are too large." It is that the sharp singleton exact-support embedding of the Tao circuit is structurally impossible before any coefficient-ratio or leakage analysis.

3. The fallback packetized object is too non-canonical.

   Once packetization is allowed, the object depends on extra engineering choices:

   - packet support
   - projection/coordinatization
   - desired-channel bookkeeping
   - leakage scalarization
   - exact-network restoration conventions

   So the mission no longer has one precise object to prove or disprove.
