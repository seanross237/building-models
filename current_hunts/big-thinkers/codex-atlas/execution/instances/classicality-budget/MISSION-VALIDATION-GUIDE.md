# Mission Validation Guide — Classicality Budget

## What Success Looks Like

Either a rigorously derived new physical quantity (the classicality budget) that survives stress-testing and prior art search, or a clear demonstration that the quantity is already known / trivially follows from existing work / is wrong. The finding must be precise enough to present to a physicist working on quantum foundations.

## Validation Tiers

### Tier 1: Derivation
- The formula is derived from clearly stated axioms
- Every assumption is identified and justified
- The derivation is mathematically complete — no gaps or hand-waving at critical steps
- Dimensional analysis checks out

### Tier 2: Consistency
- The budget gives sensible results for at least 3 different physical systems
- Edge cases don't produce absurdities (negative values, infinities where there shouldn't be, violations of known physics)
- The result is consistent with the holographic principle, Bekenstein bound, and quantum Darwinism individually
- Limiting cases are checked — does it reduce to something known when parameters are taken to extremes?

### Tier 3: Novelty
- Prior art search is thorough — checked Zurek's quantum Darwinism papers, Bekenstein/Bousso entropy bound literature, Brandão et al. on redundancy, holographic bounds, decoherence literature
- The specific quantity (objectivity-richness trade-off from bounded information) has not been explicitly derived before
- If similar results exist, the difference is precisely stated
- If the result IS already known, this is stated clearly with citations

### Tier 4: Robustness
- The result survives substitution of alternative entropy bounds (Bousso covariant, Casini, etc.)
- The result survives alternative decoherence/redundancy models
- The strongest objection has been identified, stated fairly, and addressed
- Limitations are stated honestly — what the result does NOT show
- The result would be defensible in front of a quantum foundations researcher

### Tier 5: Implications
- If the budget is real, its physical meaning is clear and non-trivial
- At least one in-principle experimental or observational test is identified
- Connections to black hole information, holography, and quantum computing resource limits are explicit
- The result is presented clearly enough that a non-specialist physicist could understand the claim

## What "Exhausted" Looks Like

A strategy is exhausted when:
- The derivation is either complete and verified, or shown to have an unfixable flaw
- Prior art search has converged — additional searching is unlikely to turn up new relevant work
- Stress-testing has either confirmed robustness or identified a fatal sensitivity to assumptions
