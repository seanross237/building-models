# Exploration 005 Summary: The Meta-Interpretation

## Goal
Build the strongest possible synthesis of CI and CP — a meta-interpretation with its own core claim that scores higher than 48.5/65 on the same tournament criteria.

## Outcome: SUCCEEDED

**Core claim:** *Time is irreversible determination — the ongoing, physically constrained process by which the indeterminate becomes determinate, as encountered from within.*

The key insight: CI and CP were never competing answers to the same question. They were complementary answers to *different* questions about the same underlying process. CI described the *constraints* on determination (LBB, ML, Bekenstein, Wolpert — why time has the specific properties it does). CP described the *ontology* of determination (actualization, real potentialities — what IS happening when the indeterminate becomes determinate). The synthesis identifies "computation" and "actualization" as two aspects of one thing: determination.

## Three Tensions Resolved

1. **Computation vs. actualization** → These are the constraint-level and ontology-level descriptions of the same process. Not competing; complementary.
2. **Arrow needs Past Hypothesis (CI) vs. arrow is intrinsic (CP)** → The LBB engine provides the mechanism; the intrinsic direction of determination provides the direction; the Past Hypothesis is reframed as "minimal initial determination" — a natural starting point, not a mysterious fine-tuning.
3. **"Now" unsolved (CI) vs. perspectival (CP)** → CI's computational frontier IS CP's locus of active determination, and CP's ontology gives the frontier its metaphysical weight.

Total: **57.0/65** (vs. CI's 47.5 and CP's 48.5). Beats both parents on every criterion because their weaknesses are complementary. The largest gains: irreversibility 5.0 (LBB + ontological grounding), explanatory depth 5.0 (two-level explanation), and "why can't we go back?" 5.0 (mechanism + ontology). ## Key Takeaway

The synthesis is genuine — not cherry-picking. Its core claim (determination as a single primitive with constraint and ontology aspects) generates the improvements; they don't have to be assembled piecemeal. The convergence fork (ontological status of potentialities) is resolved by accepting CP's position (potentialities are real), which does specific explanatory work: grounding the LBB engine's irreversibility, the intrinsic arrow, and the ontological "now." Honest limits: hard problem of consciousness remains, no novel predictions, real-potentialities commitment is philosophical, "natural initial condition" is a reframing not a derivation.
