# Exploration 003 Summary: Adversarial Attack and Repair of CP Interpretation

## Goal
Subject the Causal-Processual (CP) interpretation to four adversarial attacks (convergence with CI, physics vacuum, block universe, panexperientialism), then repair the damage.

## What Was Tried
Each attack was stated at full adversarial strength, given CP's best possible response, and assigned a verdict. Specific repairs were proposed for each wound.

## Outcome: Two wounds, two partial survivals

| Attack | Verdict |
|--------|---------|
| Convergence with CI | PARTIALLY SURVIVED — CP and CI are structurally isomorphic but diverge on one clear fork: the ontological reality of potentialities (potentia). CP is not CI in costume, but it's much closer than E002 acknowledged. |
| Physics Vacuum | WOUNDED — CP's physics content is one unconfirmed framework (CST) + one contested position (Prigogine). Its core concepts are philosophical (Whitehead, Husserl). E002 oversold the physics grounding. |
| Block Universe | PARTIALLY SURVIVED — CP's response (becoming structured by causal order, not simultaneity) is technically strong against Rietdijk-Putnam. The deeper stalemate is inherent in the problem. |
| Panexperientialism | WOUNDED — CP's full flow advantage over CI requires panexperientialism. Without it, the flow advantage is moderate, not decisive. |

## Key Takeaway
**CP's directionality advantage (intrinsic arrow, no Past Hypothesis) is its strongest feature — it survived all attacks unscathed and should be the lead selling point.** The flow advantage, while real, was oversold in E002 — the full version depends on a controversial premise. CP is a genuine competitor to CI, not a clone, but the competitive gap is narrower than E002 suggested. Revised total score: 25/30 (down from 26/30).

## Proposed Repairs
1. **Own the convergence**: Identify the precise fork (potentiality ontology) rather than claiming global superiority
2. **Transparent physics/philosophy ratio**: Present as "metaphysical interpretation with physics anchors" not "physics-grounded theory"
3. **Three-level flow account**: Minimal (structural) → Moderate (functional emergence) → Maximal (panexperientialist), letting judges choose their comfort level
4. **Lead with directionality**: This is the unconditional advantage; flow is the conditional one

## Leads Worth Pursuing
- The potentiality fork could be developed further — connecting to QM interpretive debates (objective collapse vs. many-worlds) to sharpen the CP-vs-CI distinction
- The block universe stalemate might be breakable if CST's growth dynamics can be shown to be non-gauge in a stronger sense
