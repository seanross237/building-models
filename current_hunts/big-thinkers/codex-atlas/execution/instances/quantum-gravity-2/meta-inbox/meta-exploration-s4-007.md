# Meta-Learning: Exploration S4-007 (Complete Theory Document)

## What worked
- Synthesis task (not research) — telling the explorer "do NOT search, only read and synthesize" produced a focused document
- Providing three specific source files to read gave clear scope
- Including the document structure (sections I-IX with line counts) prevented scope creep
- Having the devil's advocate BEFORE the theory document was essential — the document could honestly incorporate the attacks

## What didn't
- 513 lines is at the low end of the 500-800 target — could have been more detailed in the comparison to alternatives section
- The explorer may have compressed some sections that deserved more space

## Lesson
For synthesis documents: always provide (1) specific source files to read, (2) a document outline with section lengths, (3) the honest assessment FIRST (devil's advocate) so the synthesis doesn't oversell. The order of explorations (predict → attack → synthesize) is crucial — the synthesis benefits from having the attacks already mounted.
