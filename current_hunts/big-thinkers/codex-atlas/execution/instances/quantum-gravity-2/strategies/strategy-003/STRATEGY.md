# Strategy 003: Conjecture-Prosecution — The QG+F / Asymptotic Safety Unification

## Objective

Test a precise conjecture: **QG+F and asymptotic safety (AS) are manifestations of the same UV-complete theory of quantum gravity**, with QG+F describing the perturbative sector and AS describing the non-perturbative sector. If the evidence supports this, construct the unified framework as the mission's novel theory. If the evidence falsifies it, the falsification itself reveals what the correct non-perturbative completion of QG+F looks like — which IS the novel theory.

Either outcome produces genuine novelty. The conjecture is sharp enough that every exploration produces a verdict (supported / falsified / inconclusive), not just a summary.

## Why This Direction

### What the prior strategies established:

**Strategy-001** (9 explorations): Built SCG v2.0 from scratch. Lesson: building a complete alternative to QG+F from scratch is too ambitious. Produces a research program, not a competitive theory.

**Strategy-002** (10 explorations): Mapped QG+F's explanatory gaps, focused on the fakeon. Key findings:
- The **Wheeler propagator bridge** (fakeon = Wheeler propagator at tree level; causal sets independently produce the Wheeler propagator) is genuinely novel and unpublished
- The **23-debt catalog** ranks QG+F's explanatory gaps, with the fakeon interpretation as #1
- The **classical → quantum gap** blocks ALL interpretive approaches to the fakeon — this direction is exhausted
- Three-way interpretive convergence on the fakeon provides conceptual depth but zero predictions
- **Exploration 010's survey** identified the QG+F ↔ AS connection as the highest feasibility/impact direction

### Why conjecture-prosecution is the right methodology now:

The first two strategies used open-ended methodologies: "explore then build" (Strategy-001) and "map gaps then fill" (Strategy-002). Both produced interesting observations but not theories. The problem: open-ended exploration produces research programs, not results.

This strategy inverts the approach: **start with a precise claim, then systematically test it.** Every exploration has a clear success/failure criterion. The methodology naturally produces either a supported conjecture (= novel theory) or a falsification with specific reasons (= knowledge that constrains what the novel theory MUST be).

### The conjecture's properties:

The QG+F–AS unification conjecture is:
- **Sharp:** QG+F and AS make different predictions (r < 0.005 vs r up to 0.01; Schwarzschild BH vs modified BH). If they're the same theory, these must be reconciled — either one side is wrong, or the predictions are actually compatible.
- **Tractable with literature evidence:** Extensive RG flow calculations exist for both QG+F and AS. The explorers can compare existing results without needing to do original calculations.
- **High-impact:** Nobody has proven or disproven this connection. The answer reshapes the field.
- **Novel regardless of outcome:** If true, the unified framework is a new theory. If false, the precise reason for failure identifies the correct non-perturbative completion (or proves QG+F is already complete).

## Methodology: Conjecture-Prosecution

### Phase 1: State the Conjecture and Its Implications (1 exploration)

**Exploration 1: Precise Conjecture Statement.**

State the QG+F–AS unification conjecture with mathematical precision:
- What exactly does "same theory" mean? (Same UV completion? Same critical surface? Same non-perturbative spectrum?)
- List 6-8 implications that MUST be true if the conjecture holds. Each implication must be independently testable with existing literature.
- List 3-4 implications that would FALSIFY the conjecture.
- For each implication, identify the specific papers and results that bear on it.

This is the roadmap for the entire strategy. Get it right.

### Phase 2: Prosecution — Test Implications (5-6 explorations)

Each exploration tests ONE implication. The output is a verdict: SUPPORTED / FALSIFIED / INCONCLUSIVE, with the specific evidence and reasoning.

**Candidate implications to test (the Strategizer selects the most decisive ones):**

1. **Fixed point compatibility:** Does the asymptotically free fixed point of quadratic gravity (QG+F's UV behavior) connect to the Reuter fixed point (AS's UV behavior) via a well-defined RG flow? Literature: Salvio & Strumia 2018, Falls et al. 2020, Knorr & Schiber 2021.

2. **Prediction reconciliation:** Can QG+F's prediction r < 0.005 (Starobinsky-like inflation) be reconciled with AS's r predictions? If the theories are the same, their inflationary predictions must agree in some limit.

3. **Ghost fate at strong coupling:** What happens to the fakeon/ghost at non-perturbative scales? Does AS's non-perturbative framework naturally produce ghost confinement or fakeon-like behavior? This is the KEY bridge question.

4. **Black hole compatibility:** QG+F predicts Schwarzschild BHs are the unique static solution. AS predicts modified Schwarzschild with running G(r). Can these be the same in different regimes?

5. **Spectral dimension:** Both QG+F and AS predict d_s → 2 in the UV. Do they agree on the PROFILE d_s(E) at all scales, or only at the endpoints?

6. **Cosmological constant:** Does the unified theory predict anything about Λ that neither theory predicts alone?

**Rules for each prosecution exploration:**
- Start by stating what evidence would SUPPORT and what would FALSIFY the implication
- Cite specific papers and results (with arXiv numbers when possible)
- Give a final verdict with confidence level (high/medium/low)
- If INCONCLUSIVE, state precisely what calculation or experiment would resolve it

### Phase 3: Verdict and Construction (2-3 explorations)

After the prosecution phase:

**If conjecture is supported (≥4 of 6 implications supported, 0 falsified):**
- Build the unified framework. State its axioms, derive its predictions, explain it in plain language.
- The novel theory = "QG+F and AS are the same theory, and here's the framework that unifies them"
- Emphasize what the UNIFIED theory predicts that neither QG+F alone nor AS alone predicts

**If conjecture is falsified (≥2 implications falsified):**
- The falsification IS the result. Write up precisely WHY they can't be unified.
- What does the failure tell us about the true non-perturbative completion of QG+F?
- Use the failure to construct a DIFFERENT novel theory — one that incorporates the incompatibility
- This is where genuinely new ideas emerge: if QG+F ≠ AS, then what IS QG+F non-perturbatively?

**If inconclusive (mixed or mostly inconclusive):**
- Synthesize what's known. State the open questions precisely.
- Identify the ONE calculation or experiment that would resolve the conjecture.
- Use the partial results to build whatever novel framework the evidence supports.

### Phase 4: Harden (1 exploration)

Devil's advocate + 5-tier validation + comparison to standalone QG+F and standalone AS.

## Validation Criteria

### Minimum success
- A clear verdict on the QG+F–AS conjecture (supported or falsified) with specific evidence
- At least 4 implications tested with high-confidence verdicts

### Good success
- A novel theoretical framework — either the unified QG+F–AS theory or a new theory inspired by the falsification
- Passes Tiers 1-3 (novel, consistent, explanatory)
- At least one prediction that differs from both standalone QG+F and standalone AS

### Breakthrough
- A novel theory that passes all 5 tiers
- Explains why QG+F and AS have the relationship they do (not just what the relationship is)
- Makes predictions testable before 2040

### Strategy exhaustion signals
- The conjecture turns out to be trivially true (already known and published)
- The conjecture turns out to be trivially false (obvious inconsistency identified in Exploration 1)
- The existing literature is too sparse to test any implications (no RG flow calculations exist for the relevant regime)

## Context from Prior Strategies

### Key results to incorporate:

1. **Wheeler propagator bridge** (Strategy-002): The fakeon = Wheeler propagator at tree level, and causal sets produce the Wheeler propagator. If QG+F = AS, does the causal set structure emerge naturally in the non-perturbative regime?

2. **Fakeon is inherently quantum** (Strategy-001 + 002): Can't be derived from classical structures. This means the non-perturbative completion must preserve the fakeon prescription — it can't "explain away" the fakeon by replacing it with something classical.

3. **Spectral dimension d_s=2 in UV** (Strategy-001, QG-1): Universal across QG approaches. Both QG+F and AS predict this. Probe whether they agree on the FULL profile d_s(E), not just the UV limit.

4. **SCG v2.0's failure mode** (Strategy-001): Building a complete theory from scratch without anchoring to existing math produces an uncomputable research program. This strategy avoids that by anchoring to BOTH QG+F and AS, which have extensive existing calculations.

5. **23-debt catalog** (Strategy-002): The top debts — fakeon interpretation, non-perturbative completion, parameter freedom, BH interior — are all directly relevant to the QG+F–AS question. If they're the same theory, the non-perturbative sector might resolve multiple debts simultaneously.

### Operational lessons (MUST FOLLOW):
- **One task per exploration.** This is non-negotiable. Zero timeouts when followed, repeated timeouts when violated.
- **Devil's advocate immediately after first construction.** Don't wait.
- **Each exploration gets a verdict** (SUPPORTED / FALSIFIED / INCONCLUSIVE), not just a summary.
- **Explorers stall on pure synthesis.** If a task requires synthesizing 5+ documents, the Strategizer should do it directly or break it into sub-tasks.
- **Line count targets** prevent scope creep. 200-400 lines for focused tasks, 400-600 for surveys.
- **"If this fails, explain WHY"** — include in every exploration goal.
