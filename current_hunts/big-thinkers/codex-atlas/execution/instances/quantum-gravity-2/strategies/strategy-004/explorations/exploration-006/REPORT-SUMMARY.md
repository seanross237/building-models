# Exploration 006 Summary: Devil's Advocate — Adversarial Assessment

## Goal
Mount six specific attacks against all predictions (D1–D3, P1, C1–C4) claimed by the Unified QG+F–AS Framework. Rate severity and determine survival.

## Outcome: SUCCEEDED — All Six Attacks Mounted

### Key Results

**Zero genuinely discriminating predictions survive adversarial scrutiny.**

- **D1 (ghost pole tower):** SERIOUS damage. Circular — assumes unification to predict unification. Downgraded from DISCRIMINATING to CONSISTENCY CHECK. AS generically produces complex poles without needing QG+F.
- **D2 (BH phase transition trigger):** FATAL damage. Forever inaccessible to observation. Framework's own numbers (M_rem > M_crit) may eliminate the trigger mechanism entirely. Classification: UNTESTABLE NARRATIVE.
- **D3 (bounded r):** SERIOUS damage — but the prediction itself survives. It is pure QG+F (Anselmi et al. 2020), not a unified prediction. Reclassified as INHERITED from QG+F.
- **P1 (δ₃ correct sign):** SERIOUS damage. Correct sign is necessary but insufficient. Magnitude is unknown and rough estimates give 8–22 orders of magnitude too small. The b parameter fiasco (E003: suppressed from ~10⁻² to ~10⁻¹⁴ by RG running) is a cautionary precedent. Remains a LEAD, not a prediction.
- **C1–C4 (consistency checks):** COSMETIC to MODERATE damage. All survive but add no discriminating power. C1 is solid physics. C2 is trivially universal. C3 is expected. C4 is inherited from AS.

### The Null Hypothesis Test
H₀ (compatible-but-separate) explains **every** claimed prediction equally well. No prediction requires unification. H₀ is simpler and more parsimonious. By Occam's razor, H₀ should be preferred until the unified framework produces something H₀ cannot match.

### Predictive Tier Rating
**Remains FAIL.** The framework makes zero predictions that its component theories don't already make individually. It is a well-structured research program that identifies important computations, but a research program is not a predictive framework.

### Key Takeaway
The unified QG+F–AS framework is a conjecture with aesthetic appeal, structural coherence, zero novel surviving predictions, and practical unfalsifiability. Three specific computations (C²-extended FRG propagator, g̃₃*→δ₃ mapping, AF→NGFP trajectory) could upgrade the assessment — but none have been started.
