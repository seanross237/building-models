## The Five Tiers

### Tier 1: Novelty
- Is this a restatement of existing theories? Or genuinely new?
- A domain expert would recognize this as a fresh contribution?

### Tier 2: Logical Consistency
- Internally consistent? No contradictions?
- Survives devil's advocate without collapsing?
- Assumptions stated explicitly?

### Tier 3: Explanatory Power
- Explains WHY gravity behaves the way it does — not just how?
- At least one clearer/more intuitive explanation than existing accounts?
- A non-physicist can understand the core idea?

### Tier 4: Compatibility with Reality
- No contradiction with established experiments?
- Addresses extreme scales (Planck, BH, Big Bang)?
- Makes at least one prediction that could in principle distinguish it?

### Tier 5: Depth
- Internal structure — not just a single idea but a framework?
- Handles edge cases rather than dodging them?
- Suggests new questions worth investigating?
