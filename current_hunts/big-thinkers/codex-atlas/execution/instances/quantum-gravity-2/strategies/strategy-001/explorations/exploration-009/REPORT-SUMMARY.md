# Exploration 009 Summary: SCG v2.0 — Definitive Synthesis

## Goal
Write the definitive synthesis of SCG v2.0 as a research program: clean theory statement, concrete open problems, and lessons learned from the full 9-exploration cycle.

## What Was Done
Synthesized all findings from explorations 001-008 into a three-part document (~300 lines):
- **Part 1:** Complete theory statement — core insight, five v2.0 axioms (with causal order), four derivation chains, architecture diagram, predictions table, version history, and honest assessment of what works and what doesn't.
- **Part 2:** Five precisely stated open problems with difficulty ratings, suggested approaches, dependency graph, success/failure criteria, and probability estimates.
- **Part 3:** Seven lessons about the quantum gravity landscape beyond QG+F.

## Outcome: COMPLETED

SCG v2.0 is a coherent research program built on five axioms (finite configuration space, indivisible stochastic dynamics, causal structure, cost optimization, irreducible noise) with four derivation chains (QM via Barandes, geometry via Malament+Pedraza, bridge via Jacobson, collapse via Diósi-Penrose). Its central innovation is that one cost function drives both QM and gravity emergence, with one noise parameter σ connecting ℏ and G.

However, it is NOT a predictive theory. Every derivation chain depends on an unproven step. QG+F wins 16/21 criteria in head-to-head comparison. The five open problems (continuum limit, QM emergence ambiguity, unique prediction, cost function selection, 4D) range from hard to very hard.

## Key Takeaway
**SCG v2.0 is an honestly characterized research program, not a theory.** The exploration cycle achieved what it set out to do: construct the most ambitious possible synthesis of emergent QM + emergent gravity, stress-test it to destruction, repair the fatal flaw, compare it honestly against the best existing approach, and distill the result into a clean statement with precisely identified open problems. The seven lessons (complexity > entanglement, gravitize-the-quantum is live, stochastic-quantum is ambiguous, fakeon can't be derived classically, causal structure is mandatory, architecture has limits, landscape beyond QG+F is real but immature) are valuable independent of SCG's fate.

2. **Oppenheim's decoherence-diffusion experiments** are the nearest-term test of the "gravitize the quantum" direction.
