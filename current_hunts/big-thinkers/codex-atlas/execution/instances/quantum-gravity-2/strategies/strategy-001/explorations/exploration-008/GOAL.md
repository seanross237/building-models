## The Two Theories

### SCG v2.0 (Stochastic Computational Gravity, causal order version)
- **Axioms:** Finite configuration space + indivisible stochastic process + causal partial order with directed cost and volume measure + optimization principle + irreducible noise
- **QM emergence:** Barandes-Doukas lifting (indivisible stochastic → Hilbert space). ℏ = 2mσ²
- **Geometry emergence:** Causal order + volume → Lorentzian manifold (Malament theorem). Cost optimization → Einstein equations (Pedraza)
- **Predictions:** No graviton, spacetime diffusion, decoherence-diffusion trade-off, complexity plateau, modified dispersion, higher-derivative coefficients
- **Known issues:** QM emergence is reformulation not derivation, continuum limit unproven, Pedraza only 2D, no unique predictions, self-consistency unproven

### QG+F (Quadratic Gravity with Fakeon Quantization)
- **Framework:** Standard QFT of the metric field with R² and C² terms. Ghost pole quantized as fakeon (purely virtual particle).
- **Status:** The unique perturbatively UV-complete QG within {Lorentz invariance, diff invariance, renormalizability, unitarity}
- **Key results:** Passes Newton's law, GW tests, has 2 free parameters beyond GR (spin-2 and spin-0 fakeon masses), predicts specific r bound for CMB, spectral dimension d_s = 2 → 4/3 (six-derivative version)
- **Known issues:** Only one near-term testable prediction (r from CMB). Fakeon interpretation is non-standard. n_s tension with CMB data (resolved by R³ correction).
