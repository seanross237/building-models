# Exploration 001: Map QG+F's Explanatory Debts

## Mission Context

We are in a quantum gravity research program. The previous strategy established that **quadratic gravity with fakeon quantization (QG+F)** is the unique perturbatively renormalizable, unitary, Lorentz-invariant quantum gravity in 4D. It passes all validation tiers and predicts the tensor-to-scalar ratio r ∈ [0.0004, 0.0045] (testable by LiteBIRD ~2037).

But QG+F has significant explanatory gaps — things it cannot do, doesn't address, or handles with zero physical intuition. **This exploration catalogs and ranks those gaps**, because the next phase of our strategy will build a focused novel theory around the most fertile gap.

## Your Specific Goal

Produce a **comprehensive, ranked catalog of QG+F's explanatory debts** — everything the theory cannot explain, does poorly, or leaves as "that's just how the math works."

### What to Catalog

For each debt, provide:
1. **The debt itself** — what QG+F cannot do or explain
2. **Severity** — how serious is this omission? (CRITICAL / MAJOR / MODERATE / MINOR)
3. **Specificity** — is this a QG+F-specific weakness, or does every QG approach share it?
4. **Alternative coverage** — does any other framework (emergent gravity, string theory, LQG, causal sets, GtQ programs) address this gap? If so, how well?
5. **Explanatory opportunity** — if a new theory filled this gap, how valuable would that be? Could it produce predictions? Would it deepen understanding?

### Known Debts (from our library — verify and expand)

The library identifies at least 12 debts:
1. **Fakeon's physical interpretation** — What IS a fakeon physically? Why does this specific quantization prescription work? The fakeon is purely virtual, contributes to loops but never appears. Three competing interpretations exist (fakeon, IHO/DQFT, ghost confinement), none fully satisfactory.
2. **Why 4D?** — Renormalizability requires exactly d=4, but this is exploited, not explained.
3. **Standard Model origin** — QG+F does NOT predict SU(3)×SU(2)×U(1). f₂ is AF for ANY matter content. No gauge group selection.
4. **Black hole interior/singularity** — Perturbative QG+F cannot resolve singularities. Fakeon selects Schwarzschild. All BH corrections suppressed by 10⁻⁷⁶.
5. **Cosmological constant (new CC problem)** — Unimodular QG+F solves old CC. New CC (why Λ ~ 10⁻¹²²) unsolved. Shared by ALL continuum approaches.
6. **Microcausality violation meaning** — The theory's most distinctive qualitative prediction is fundamentally unobservable. What does it MEAN physically?
7. **One-prediction theory** — QG+F has effectively only one testable prediction: r. All 19 non-CMB signatures undetectable. 15-order gap between probes and QG+F scales.
8. **Non-perturbative completion** — The QCD-gravity analogy is structurally motivated but dynamically unproven. Full non-perturbative structure unknown.
9. **QG+F ↔ AS relationship** — Are they truly the same theory? Perturbative QG+F predicts GR black holes; AS predicts modified BHs. Sharp disagreement.
10. **Information paradox** — Fakeon microcausality violation provides potential leakage channel, but no quantitative calculation exists.
11. **n_s tension** — Partially resolved by six-derivative R³ extension, but requires going beyond strict QG+F. Also DESI-driven, may resolve with better data.
12. **Spectral dimension never computed** — Anselmi's group has never actually computed d_s for their theory.

### What I Need You to ALSO Do

Beyond verifying/expanding the above:
- **Search the 2025-2026 literature** for any NEW critiques of QG+F, especially regarding the fakeon prescription, unitarity beyond perturbation theory, and Lorentz invariance.
- **Identify conceptual debts** — not just "QG+F can't compute X" but "QG+F gives zero physical insight into Y." Examples: Why does the world obey quantum mechanics? What is spacetime made of? Why is there something rather than nothing?
- **Look for "dark corners"** — things the QG+F community actively avoids discussing or acknowledges only in passing.
- **Compare against other frameworks' strengths:** What does string theory explain that QG+F can't? What does LQG explain? Causal sets? Emergent gravity?

### Critical Output

The catalog must end with a **TOP 5 RANKING** of debts ordered by "explanatory opportunity for a novel theory" — considering:
- How deep is the gap? (cosmological constant > graviton mass bound)
- Is it QG+F-specific? (specific weaknesses > universal hard problems)
- Could a theory that fills this gap make predictions? (predictive gaps > aesthetic gaps)
- Is there intellectual room for novelty? (gaps where no framework has a good answer > gaps where string theory already has an answer)

## Success Criteria

- ✅ At least 15 distinct explanatory debts identified and characterized
- ✅ Each debt rated on severity, specificity, alternative coverage, and opportunity
- ✅ Clear distinction between "hard for everyone" and "QG+F-specific"
- ✅ Top 5 ranking with justification
- ✅ At least 3 debts NOT in the initial list of 12 (genuinely new findings)

## Failure Criteria

- ❌ Catalog has fewer than 10 debts
- ❌ No ranking or ranking is unjustified
- ❌ Debts are described superficially without assessing alternative coverage
- ❌ Report is just a literature review of QG+F without identifying specific gaps
