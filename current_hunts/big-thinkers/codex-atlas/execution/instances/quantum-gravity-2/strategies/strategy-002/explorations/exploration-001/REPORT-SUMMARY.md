## TOP 5 RANKING (the key output)

1. **Fakeon interpretation + analyticity sacrifice (Debts 1, 17, 21)** — 100% QG+F-specific, deepest conceptual room, actively contested in 2025-2026 literature. The fakeon IS the theory and nobody knows what it is.

2. **Non-perturbative completion + spacetime microstructure (Debts 8, 13)** — Theory can't define itself beyond perturbation theory. Ghost confinement would predict new particles and explain the fakeon.

3. **One-prediction problem + parameter freedom (Debts 7, 20)** — Two free parameters, one testable prediction, 15-order gap to accessible scales. Any mechanism constraining parameters would transform the program.

4. **Black hole interior + information paradox (Debts 4, 10)** — QG+F selects Schwarzschild and goes silent. Microcausality leakage channel is unique but uncomputed.

5. **Gravitational thermodynamics + entanglement (Debts 23, 6)** — QG+F computes entropy corrections but explains nothing about WHY area = entropy. MVEH construction is incomplete (linearized only).

## Key Takeaway
**The fakeon interpretation cluster (#1) is the most fertile target for a novel theory.** It is completely QG+F-specific (no other approach shares this question), the 2025-2026 literature shows the space is actively contested (analyticity sacrifice, causality abandonment, competing IHO interpretation), and an answer could both deepen understanding and generate new predictions. The non-perturbative completion (#2) is the natural companion target. A theory that provides an information-theoretic or physical understanding of WHY the fakeon works AND what QG+F looks like non-perturbatively would be the highest-value contribution possible.
