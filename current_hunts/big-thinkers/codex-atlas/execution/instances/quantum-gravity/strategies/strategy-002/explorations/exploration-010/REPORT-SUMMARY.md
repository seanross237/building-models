## Outcome: SUCCEEDED — Complete assessment with clear verdicts

### The IHO Paper: Interesting but Not a Breakthrough
The paper proposes replacing the ghost with an "inverted harmonic oscillator" instability — a mode with hyperbolic evolution and spacelike momentum that never goes on-shell. Quantized via DQFT with geometric superselection sectors. Claims 50-650x Bayesian evidence for explaining CMB parity asymmetry. **Verdict: creative but not credible yet.** The sign flip is ad hoc (changes the theory), unitarity is argued not proven, the 650x Bayes factor depends on methodology choices, no independent verification exists, and no explicit numerical predictions for standard observables are given. The fakeon approach remains more rigorous and better established. The IHO/DQFT is a *competing* interpretation, not compatible with the fakeon.

### QG+F Predictions Are Tightly Defined but Nearly Indistinguishable from Starobinsky
Beyond n_s ≈ 0.964-0.967 and r ∈ [0.0004, 0.004], all other QG+F observables are indistinguishable from pure Starobinsky: alpha_s ≈ -6.5×10⁻⁴, beta_s ≈ 2.4×10⁻⁵, n_T = -r/8 (consistency relation holds), f_NL ~ 10⁻⁴, no isocurvature, no spectral features. The C² corrections enter at O(1/115) — too small to measure for anything except r. The six-derivative extension shifts n_s to ~0.974 and r to ~0.0045.

### r Is the Sole Realistic Discriminator Between QG+F and AS
- QG+F: r < 0.005 (all variants)
- AS: r up to ~0.01 (Bonanno-Platania, depending on AS parameter b)
- If r > 0.005: favors AS. If r < 0.003: favors QG+F.
- All other observables (alpha_s, n_T, f_NL) are too small to discriminate.

### CMB-S4 Cancellation Shifts the Timeline
CMB-S4 was cancelled in July 2025 (DOE+NSF withdrawal). The discrimination now rests on:
- **2027-2028:** BICEP Array (σ(r) ~ 0.003) — can detect r > 0.01 only
- **2030-2034:** Simons Observatory enhanced (σ(r) ~ 0.001) — earliest discrimination
- **2036-2037:** LiteBIRD first results (σ(r) < 0.001) — definitive discrimination

## Key Takeaway
**The tensor-to-scalar ratio r is the only realistic near-term test separating QG+F from AS, and we'll have a definitive answer by ~2036-2037 (LiteBIRD), with a possible earlier result by ~2030-2034 (SO enhanced).** The IHO ghost paper does not change this picture — it's an alternative ghost interpretation with CMB anomaly connections, but lacks the rigor and quantitative predictions to be taken as seriously as the fakeon approach. CMB-S4's cancellation is a genuine setback but not fatal.
