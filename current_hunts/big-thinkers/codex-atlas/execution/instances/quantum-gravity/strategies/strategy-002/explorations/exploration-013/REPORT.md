# Exploration 013: Can Dimensional Transmutation Predict Both M_P and Λ?

## Goal
Investigate whether a fully classically scale-invariant version of QG+F (agravity) can generate the cosmological constant Λ through dimensional transmutation, the same mechanism that generates M_P. Assess a novel "cascading dimensional transmutation" mechanism.

---

## Task 1: Classical Scale Invariance and Dimensional Transmutation in QG+F

### The Agravity Framework

Agravity ("adimensional gravity," Salvio & Strumia 2014, arXiv:1403.4226) is QG+F with classical scale invariance imposed: **no dimensionful parameters appear in the fundamental action.** The complete action is:

```
S = ∫d⁴x √(-g) [R²/(6f₀²) - W²/(2f₂²) + ℒ_matter^adim]
```

where W is the Weyl tensor, and f₀, f₂ are dimensionless couplings. There is NO Einstein-Hilbert term M_P²R and NO cosmological constant Λ₀ at the fundamental level — these must arise dynamically.

### Scalar Field Content

The theory requires at least one additional scalar field S (the "Higgs of gravity") beyond the Standard Model Higgs H. The scalar sector Lagrangian is:

```
ℒ_BSM^adim = |D_μS|² - λ_S|S|⁴ + λ_HS|S|²|H|² - ξ_S|S|²R
```

Key scalars:
1. **S** — the gravitational Higgs (singlet scalar, generates M_P via its VEV)
2. **H** — the Standard Model Higgs doublet (generates v = 246 GeV)
3. **Scalaron** — the spin-0 degree of freedom from R²/(6f₀²), with mass M₀ = f₀M̄_Pl/√2
4. **Conformal mode** — from the four-derivative graviton kinetic term, described by auxiliary scalars (Ω₊, Ω₋)

### How Dimensional Transmutation Generates M_P

The mechanism is a gravitational Coleman-Weinberg (CW) process. In a classically scale-invariant theory with gravity, the Einstein-frame potential takes the form:

```
V_E(S) = M̄_Pl⁴ · λ_S(s) / ξ_S²(s)
```

where s ≡ ⟨S⟩ and all couplings run with the RG scale μ̄ ≈ s.

The conditions for a consistent vacuum with dynamically generated Planck mass are:

```
λ_S(s) = 0           → vanishing cosmological constant at minimum
β_{λ_S}(s) = 0       → stationarity condition (minimum of V_E)
ξ_S(s)⟨S⟩² = M̄_Pl²  → observed Planck mass
```

Near the minimum, the running coupling takes the CW form:
```
λ_S(μ̄ ≈ s) ≈ (b/2) ln²(s/⟨s⟩)
```
where b = d(β_{λ_S})/d(ln μ) evaluated at the minimum.

**Critical difference from non-gravitational CW mechanism:** In standard CW (e.g., scalar QED), the potential is always negative at the minimum (V_min < 0). In agravity, V_E can vanish at the minimum when λ_S(s) = 0, because the gravitational coupling ξ_S enters the Einstein-frame potential differently. This is actually a necessary feature — it's what allows the theory to produce (near-)zero cosmological constant rather than O(M_Pl⁴).

### Mass Spectrum

The mass spectrum after symmetry breaking is:
- **M₂ = f₂M̄_Pl/√2** — massive spin-2 ghost (quantized as fakeon)
- **M₀ = f₀M̄_Pl/√2** — massive spin-0 scalaron
- **M_s ≈ 1.4 × 10¹³ GeV** — inflaton (the S field)
- **Massless graviton** — the usual spin-2 state

The hierarchy M_s ≪ M₂, M₀ ~ M_Pl is set by the coupling constants.

### Is the Hierarchy Natural?

Partially. Agravity addresses the Higgs mass hierarchy through "softened" naturalness: since the fundamental theory is classically scale invariant, quadratically divergent corrections to M_h vanish (no dimensionful parameters to source them). The remaining corrections are proportional to f₂⁴M_Pl² (from the graviton sector), so naturalness requires:

```
f₂ ≲ 10⁻⁸   (Higgs naturalness condition)
```

This trades the hierarchy problem for the question of why f₂ is small. The beta function for f₂ is:

```
(4π)²df₂²/d(ln μ̄) = -f₂⁴(199/15 + N_V/5 + N_f/20 + N_s/60) < 0
```

This is **asymptotically free** — f₂ is small at low energies because it's driven to zero by RG running. This is a genuine dynamical explanation, not a fine-tuning.

## Task 2: Can Λ Be Generated by Dimensional Transmutation?

### The Core Question

If M_P arises from the VEV of S through dimensional transmutation, the value of the cosmological constant is determined by V_eff at the minimum:

```
Λ_eff = V_E(⟨S⟩) = M̄_Pl⁴ · λ_S(⟨s⟩) / ξ_S²(⟨s⟩)
```

The CW condition λ_S(s) = 0 at the minimum means **Λ = 0 at tree level.** This is a feature — it means the CC is NOT of order M_Pl⁴.

### Why Λ = 0 Is Not Enough

The problem is radiative corrections. At one loop, even after imposing λ_S(⟨s⟩) = 0:

```
V_1-loop ≈ (1/64π²) Σ_i (-1)^{2s_i}(2s_i + 1) M_i⁴(⟨S⟩) [ln(M_i²/μ²) - c_i]
```

The dominant contributions come from the heaviest particles:
- Spin-2 ghost contribution: ~ M₂⁴ ~ f₂⁴M_Pl⁴
- Scalaron contribution: ~ M₀⁴ ~ f₀⁴M_Pl⁴
- SM particles: ~ v⁴ ~ (246 GeV)⁴ (negligible by comparison)

Even for small couplings, these corrections are enormous compared to the observed Λ ~ 10⁻¹²²M_Pl⁴.

### Salvio-Strumia's Own Assessment

The agravity paper explicitly confronts this. They state that even making M₀ as light as possible (M₀ ~ eV), **"the cosmological constant is at least 60 orders of magnitude larger than the observed value."** They acknowledge requiring "a huge fine-tuning without trying to explain the smallness of the cosmological constant."

### The Fundamental Obstacle

For dimensional transmutation to predict Λ ~ 10⁻¹²²M_Pl⁴, the CW potential would need:

```
λ_S(s_min) / ξ_S²(s_min) ~ 10⁻¹²²
```

This requires either:
- λ_S ~ 10⁻¹²² with ξ_S ~ O(1), which is extreme fine-tuning of a dimensionless coupling
- ξ_S ~ 10⁶¹ with λ_S ~ O(1), which makes the scalar non-minimally coupled to absurd degree

Neither is natural. And even if achieved at tree level, one-loop corrections ~ f₂⁴M_Pl⁴/(16π²) destroy the tuning.

### Why Scale Invariance Doesn't Help the CC

Kugo (arXiv:2004.01868) proved that classical scale invariance is **necessary but insufficient** for the CC:
- **Necessary**: Without it, the CC receives uncontrollable power-law divergent contributions
- **Insufficient**: Radiative corrections destabilize any flat direction. An additional symmetry or mechanism is needed to protect the CC.

Weinberg's no-go theorem has been generalized to show that no continuous symmetry in a gravitational theory can protect the CC — including scale invariance.

**Bottom line: Dimensional transmutation can set Λ = 0 at tree level (and that IS valuable — it solves the "old" CC problem), but it cannot explain why Λ ≈ 10⁻¹²²M_Pl⁴ rather than zero.**

## Task 3: The Agravity CC Problem

### What Salvio-Strumia Say

The agravity papers discuss the CC in three contexts:

**1. Original agravity (2014):** The CC can be tuned to zero at tree level by imposing λ_S(s) = 0. But radiative corrections destroy this. They acknowledge the problem without solving it.

**2. Agravity up to infinite energy (2018):** When f₀ → ∞ at high energies, the conformal mode strongly self-couples and decouples. In this limit, agravity → conformal gravity + two conformally coupled scalars. The CC problem remains: even with the conformal mode decoupled, Planckian corrections to the CC remain unnaturally large. An accidental supersymmetry (adding a Weyl fermion in the adjoint representation of the gauge group) can cancel the Planckian CC contribution, but only if strongly-coupled dynamics generates M_P. This is an ad hoc condition.

**3. Unimodular quadratic gravity (Salvio 2024, arXiv:2406.12958):** The most recent treatment. Salvio imposes the unimodular constraint (fixed spacetime volume element) on QG+F. Key results:
- **Old CC problem solved:** Vacuum energy no longer gravitates. The physical CC is an integration constant, completely independent of Λ₀ (the bare parameter). Particle mass contributions cannot affect it.
- **New CC problem NOT solved:** Unimodular gravity provides no mechanism for selecting the observed CC value.
- **Anthropic argument invoked:** Salvio proposes a "multiverse made by different eras in a single big bang" where the observed CC is anthropically selected. Observers exist when matter and dark energy densities become comparable.

### Does Classical Scale Invariance Help or Hurt?

**Helps with the old CC problem:**
- In ordinary QFT + gravity, vacuum energy ~ Σ m_i⁴ → huge CC.
- In classically scale-invariant theories, all masses arise dynamically, so the CC is calculable (not afflicted by unknown UV contributions). The CW mechanism gives V_min = 0 at tree level.
- Combined with unimodular gravity, the old CC problem is genuinely solved.

**Does NOT help with the new CC problem:**
- Scale invariance gives Λ = 0 or Λ ~ f₂⁴M_Pl⁴ — no mechanism for the intermediate observed value.
- Radiative stability remains a problem: one-loop corrections to the CC are ≫ observed value.

### Recent Work (2024-2026)

- **Salvio 2024 (PLB 855, 138920):** Unimodular QG+F, anthropic CC — the state of the art for agravity's CC treatment.
- **Maggiore & collaborators (2014-2018):** Proposed that a nonlocal term m²R□⁻²R emerges from dimensional transmutation of the R² coupling in the IR. The mass m ~ H₀ (Hubble scale) generates dark energy dynamically. This is the closest existing idea to "CC from dimensional transmutation," but:
  - It's nonlocal gravity, not QG+F
  - The mass m must be fit to data, not predicted from first principles
  - Recent work (2024) challenges whether nonlocal gravity can truly explain dark energy
- **No work specifically on predicting Λ from agravity's dimensional transmutation appears in the 2024-2026 literature.**

## Task 4: Novel Mechanism — Cascading Dimensional Transmutation

### The Idea

What if the enormous hierarchy M_Pl/Λ^{1/4} ~ 10³⁰ (or equivalently M_Pl⁴/Λ ~ 10¹²²) arises from **multiple sequential stages of dimensional transmutation**, each providing an exponential suppression?

In QCD, dimensional transmutation generates:
```
Λ_QCD = μ · exp(-1/(2b₀g²(μ)))
```

where b₀ is the one-loop beta function coefficient. For QCD with N_f = 6 flavors:
```
b₀ = (11N_c - 2N_f)/(16π²) = (33 - 12)/(16π²) = 21/(16π²) ≈ 0.133
```

At μ = M_Z ≈ 91 GeV with α_s = g²/(4π) ≈ 0.118:
```
Λ_QCD = M_Z · exp(-1/(2 × 0.133 × 4π × 0.118)) ≈ M_Z · exp(-12.6) ≈ 91 × 3.4 × 10⁻⁶ ≈ 0.3 GeV  ✓
```

The exponential suppression factor is exp(-c/g²) where c = 1/(2b₀ × 4π).

### Proposed Four-Stage Cascade

| Stage | Mechanism | Input Scale | Output Scale | Ratio |
|-------|-----------|-------------|-------------|-------|
| 1 | Gravitational CW (agravity) | UV (classically scale-free) | M_Pl ~ 10¹⁸ GeV | — |
| 2 | Gauge unification running | M_Pl | M_GUT ~ 10¹⁶ GeV | 10⁻² |
| 3 | EW symmetry breaking (CW) | M_GUT | v ~ 10² GeV | 10⁻¹⁴ |
| 4 | IR gravitational running | v | Λ^{1/4} ~ 10⁻³ eV | 10⁻¹⁴ |

Total hierarchy: 10⁻² × 10⁻¹⁴ × 10⁻¹⁴ = 10⁻³⁰, which gives M_Pl/Λ^{1/4} ~ 10³⁰. ✓

And for the CC itself: Λ ~ (Λ^{1/4})⁴ ~ (10⁻³ eV)⁴ ~ 10⁻¹²⁰ M_Pl⁴... close but not exact.

### Detailed Calculation: Does the Numerology Work?

Let me check each stage carefully.

**Stage 1: UV → M_Pl (Gravitational dimensional transmutation)**

In agravity, M_Pl arises from:
```
⟨S⟩ = μ_UV · exp(-c₁/λ_S(μ_UV))
M_Pl² = ξ_S · ⟨S⟩²
```

This generates one exponential suppression. With λ_S ~ O(0.01-0.1), the exponent is:
```
exp(-c₁/λ_S) ~ exp(-(16π²)/(few × λ_S))
```

For λ_S ~ 0.01: exp(-1580/few) → absurdly small. For λ_S ~ 0.1: exp(-158/few) → still huge suppression. This gives M_Pl ≪ μ_UV, but the UV scale is arbitrary in a classically scale-invariant theory. The RATIO is what matters.

**Stage 2: M_Pl → M_GUT (gauge coupling running)**

This is just logarithmic running, NOT dimensional transmutation. The gauge couplings unify at M_GUT ~ 10¹⁶ GeV due to their beta functions, but this ratio (10²) is NOT exponentially generated — it's a gentle log ratio. No exponential suppression here.

**Stage 3: M_GUT → v (Electroweak symmetry breaking)**

If the Higgs mass is generated by a CW mechanism (as in agravity), the hierarchy is:
```
v/M_GUT ~ exp(-c₃/λ_eff)
```

For v/M_GUT ~ 10⁻¹⁴, need c₃/λ_eff ≈ 32. With c₃ = 16π²/(few) ≈ 50-160 and λ_eff ~ 2-5, this is plausible.

**Stage 4: v → Λ^{1/4} (The crucial new stage)**

This requires Λ^{1/4}/v ~ 10⁻¹⁴, needing another exponential with c₄/g_eff² ≈ 32.

But what coupling runs to generate this? Candidates:
- **Gravitational coupling at low energies:** G_N ~ 1/M_Pl² doesn't run significantly below M_Pl.
- **f₀ coupling:** This GROWS toward the IR (not asymptotically free), so it would generate a LARGER scale, not smaller.
- **Some hidden-sector gauge coupling?** Possible but ad hoc.

**There is no known coupling in QG+F that produces a dimensional transmutation scale at ~10⁻³ eV.**

### Alternative: Product of Two Exponentials

What if only TWO stages of dimensional transmutation produce the full hierarchy?

```
M_Pl/Λ^{1/4} ~ exp(c₁/g₁²) × exp(c₂/g₂²) ~ 10³⁰
```

Need: c₁/g₁² + c₂/g₂² ≈ 69 (since ln(10³⁰) ≈ 69).

With c = 8π²/b₀ for each stage:

**Stage 1:** Gravitational sector. b₀^grav = 199/15 ≈ 13.3 for f₂. Then c₁ = 8π²/13.3 ≈ 5.9. With f₂ ~ 0.3: c₁/f₂² ≈ 66.

**Stage 2:** Some IR sector. Need c₂/g₂² ≈ 3, which is easy.

So: 66 + 3 = 69 → M_Pl/Λ^{1/4} ~ exp(69) ~ 10³⁰. ✓

**But this is cheating.** f₂ is already constrained by other physics. The value f₂ ~ 0.3 gives M₂ ~ 0.3 × M_Pl/√2 ~ 10¹⁷ GeV, which is reasonable. But we're just adjusting f₂ to get the CC — there's no prediction.

### The Actual Numerology: Zeldovich's Formula and Variants

There are well-known "coincidences" linking the CC to other scales:

**Zeldovich (1967):** Λ ~ G_N² m_π⁶ (where m_π is the pion mass)
```
G_N² m_π⁶ ~ (6.7×10⁻¹¹)² × (0.14×10⁹ eV)⁶ in appropriate units
~ (M_Pl)⁻⁴ × m_π⁶
= (m_π/M_Pl)⁴ × m_π²
~ (10⁻²⁰)⁴ × (0.14 GeV)²
~ 10⁻⁸⁰ × 10⁻²⁰ eV² → Not quite right
```

Actually, more carefully: ρ_Λ ~ G_N² m_p⁶ with m_p the proton mass gives:
```
ρ_Λ ~ (m_p/M_Pl)⁴ × m_p⁴ ~ (10⁻¹⁹)⁴ × (1 GeV)⁴ ~ 10⁻⁷⁶ GeV⁴
```
But observed: ρ_Λ ~ (2.3 × 10⁻³ eV)⁴ ~ (2.3 × 10⁻¹² GeV)⁴ ~ 2.8 × 10⁻⁴⁷ GeV⁴

Zeldovich's formula gives ~10⁻⁷⁶ GeV⁴, which is 10²⁹ too small. Not a good fit.

**The "seesaw" formula:**
```
Λ^{1/4} ~ Λ_QCD²/M_Pl ~ (0.2 GeV)²/(2.4 × 10¹⁸ GeV) ~ 1.7 × 10⁻²⁰ GeV ~ 1.7 × 10⁻¹¹ eV
```
This gives ~10⁻¹¹ eV, whereas the observed value is ~10⁻³ eV. Off by 10⁸. Not good.

**Another variant:**
```
Λ^{1/4} ~ v²/M_Pl ~ (246 GeV)²/(2.4 × 10¹⁸ GeV) ~ 2.5 × 10⁻¹⁴ GeV ~ 2.5 × 10⁻⁵ eV
```
Off by a factor of ~40. Better!

**The best known "coincidence":**
```
Λ^{1/4} ~ (H₀ × M_Pl)^{1/2} ~ (10⁻³³ eV × 10²⁸ eV)^{1/2} ~ (10⁻⁵)^{1/2} eV ~ 10⁻²·⁵ eV ≈ 3 × 10⁻³ eV  ✓✓
```
This works! But it's the coincidence problem restated: H₀ ~ Λ^{1/2}/M_Pl by definition in de Sitter space.

### Can Cascading Transmutation Be Made Physical?

The 4-stage cascade produces the right numerology IF each stage provides ~exp(-8) suppression:
```
(exp(-8))⁴ ~ e⁻³² ~ 10⁻¹⁴  per stage
Four stages: 10⁻⁵⁶ total for Λ^{1/4}/M_Pl... but need 10⁻³⁰
```

With (exp(-8))² per pair of exponentials:
```
Two stages: exp(-8) × exp(-8) = exp(-16) ~ 10⁻⁷ each
Two pairs: (10⁻⁷)² = 10⁻¹⁴ for one power, ×2 for all = 10⁻²⁸... close to 10⁻³⁰
```

But the fundamental issue is: **there is no dynamical mechanism in QG+F that generates a scale at ~10⁻³ eV.** The theory has no coupling that becomes strongly coupled in the far IR.

- f₂ is asymptotically free (weak in IR, strong in UV) → generates UV scale, not IR
- f₀ grows toward UV → Landau-pole-like behavior, not helpful for IR scale
- SM couplings: lowest scale is neutrino mass ~10⁻¹ eV, but this is a Yukawa coupling, not strong dynamics

**The cascading mechanism fails physically because QG+F has no IR-active gauge group that confines at meV scales.**

### What Would Be Needed

For cascading transmutation to work, one would need:
1. A hidden gauge sector with gauge group G_hidden
2. G_hidden coupling that is asymptotically free
3. G_hidden confinement scale Λ_hidden ~ 10⁻³ eV
4. Λ_hidden connected to vacuum energy (e.g., a condensate that gravitates)

This is essentially the proposal of Maggiore et al. (2015, arXiv:1506.06217), who suggested that the R² coupling's running generates a nonlocal mass scale m ~ H₀ through dimensional transmutation. But:
- The R² coupling is f₀, which is NOT asymptotically free in standard agravity
- They invoke nonlocal gravity, going beyond QG+F
- The mass m must still be fit to data

## Task 5: Assessment

### Can Λ Be Predicted Within Agravity/QG+F?

**No.** The assessment is clear and negative across all approaches:

1. **Tree-level CW:** Gives Λ = 0. Useful (solves old CC problem) but not predictive of the observed value.

2. **One-loop CW:** Gives Λ ~ f₂⁴M_Pl⁴/(16π²) ~ (10⁻⁸)⁴ × M_Pl⁴ / 160 ~ 10⁻³⁴ M_Pl⁴. This is 10⁸⁸ times too large.

3. **Unimodular QG+F:** Makes Λ an integration constant. The old CC problem is solved, but the value is anthropic.

4. **Dimensional transmutation of some IR coupling:** No such coupling exists in QG+F.

### Does the Cascading Mechanism Work Numerically?

**The numerology can be forced to work, but it's not predictive.** Here's the honest assessment:

- **Two-stage cascade:** If you assume two independent exponential suppressions exp(-c₁/g₁²) × exp(-c₂/g₂²) with suitably chosen c and g, you can always match 10⁻³⁰. But this has two free parameters (g₁, g₂) and one target — it's underdetermined.

- **Physically motivated cascade:** The only natural stages are:
  - Stage 1: Gravitational CW → M_Pl (works, exists in agravity)
  - Stage 2: QCD-like confinement → Λ_QCD ~ 0.2 GeV (exists in nature)
  - Stage 3: ??? → Λ^{1/4} ~ 10⁻³ eV (nothing in QG+F does this)

  Stages 1-2 are physical. Stage 3 requires new physics.

### Is This Promising or a Dead End?

**Mostly a dead end, with one constructive lesson.**

**Dead-end aspects:**
- No mechanism in QG+F generates a scale at ~meV
- The cascading idea is underconstrained (too many free parameters)
- Even if the numerology worked, it would be numerology, not a derivation
- Salvio himself has moved to anthropic arguments (unimodular + multiverse)

**The constructive lesson:**
The Maggiore-type idea — that the R² coupling undergoes dimensional transmutation in the deep IR to generate a nonlocal mass m ~ H₀ — is the most physically motivated version of "CC from dimensional transmutation." The key question is whether f₀'s RG flow can be modified (e.g., by non-perturbative effects, or by a different matter content) to become asymptotically free instead of growing. If so, the analogy to Λ_QCD becomes exact, and m would be **predicted** from the UV theory.

**What would make this genuinely novel vs. numerology:**
1. A UV-complete theory where the RG flow of f₀ (or a similar IR coupling) is calculated non-perturbatively
2. A proof that this flow generates a confinement-like scale at ~meV
3. A mechanism connecting this scale to vacuum energy
4. An independent prediction that distinguishes this from simply fitting Λ

None of these currently exist.

### Connection to Previous Explorations

Exploration 006 already established that:
- Everpresent-Λ cannot be imported into QG+F
- Unimodular QG+F solves the old CC problem
- No approach within QG+F predicts Λ ~ 10⁻¹²²

This exploration reinforces that conclusion and adds:
- Even the most creative mechanism (cascading transmutation) fails physically
- The best existing idea (Maggiore nonlocal gravity) goes beyond QG+F
- Agravity's CW mechanism gives Λ = 0 at tree level but cannot give Λ ~ 10⁻¹²²
