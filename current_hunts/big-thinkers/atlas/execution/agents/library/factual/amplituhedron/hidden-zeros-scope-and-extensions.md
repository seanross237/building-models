## What Is Open

1. **Two-loop and beyond**: Surface framework exists (arXiv:2412.21027), but zeros at higher
   loop orders are not established.
2. **Yang-Mills at 1-loop**: Explicitly future work in arXiv:2503.03805.
3. **Standard momentum-space loop zeros**: Whether surface-kinematics zeros have a momentum-space
   analog is unknown and "likely non-trivial."
4. **QCD, realistic theories**: No evidence for hidden zeros in theories with fermions or
   non-cubic interactions.
5. **Higher-multiplicity non-local uniqueness**: Only 4-point numerically verified.

## Massive Theories: Zeros Are Symmetry-Sensitive (arXiv:2601.16860)

**Critical limitation**: Hidden zeros are NOT universal — they depend critically on how mass enters
the theory (Carrillo González & Ward, Jan 2026):

| Theory | Mass mechanism | Zeros survive? |
|--------|---------------|----------------|
| Tr(φ³) with uniform mass | Explicit (but universal) | YES (after massive shift of planar variables) |
| NLSM with pion mass from SSB | Spontaneous symmetry breaking | YES |
| NLSM with explicit mass term | No symmetry protection | **NO — zeros DESTROYED** |
| Yang-Mills with spontaneous breaking | Symmetry-protected | YES |
| Yang-Mills with explicit mass (Proca) | No symmetry protection | **NO — zeros DESTROYED** |

**Interpretation**: Hidden zeros are a feature of symmetry-protected theories. They probe the
underlying symmetry structure. Zeros **discriminate between SSB mass generation and explicit
mass terms** — a surprising physical probe not accessible from standard Lagrangian considerations.

## Double-Copy Extension: Galileon Amplitudes (arXiv:2403.10594)

Hidden zeros in Yang-Mills and NLSM **transfer to Galileon amplitudes** via the KLT double copy
(Bartsch, Brown, Kampf, Oktem, Paranjape, Trnka; 2024). This extends zeros to a gravitational
scalar theory. Implication: hidden zeros have implications for gravity amplitude constraints via
the double-copy structure, though full graviton scattering in GR is not covered.

## Cosmological Wavefunction Extension (arXiv:2503.23579)

De, Paranjape, Pokraka, Spradlin, Volovich (2025): Hidden zeros extend to **scalar field theory
in de Sitter space**. Specific cosmological correlators have hidden vanishing properties not
visible in standard in-in perturbation theory. First cosmological context for hidden zeros.

## Higher-Derivative YM and GR Extension (arXiv:2510.11070)

Zhou (2025): Hidden zeros extend to **higher-derivative Yang-Mills and gravity operators** — F³
operators, R², R³ insertions in gluon/graviton amplitudes. Systematic cancellation of propagator
singularities proved. Extends the program to EFT corrections to gauge/gravity theories.

## Novel Predictions of the Program

1. **Kinematic zeros**: Specific configurations where NLSM and YM amplitudes must vanish. Verified
   numerically; not predicted by standard Feynman rules or known symmetries.

2. **Unity of colored theories**: A one-parameter family (δ-deformation) interpolates between
   scalars, pions, and gluons. At intermediate δ values, the amplitude is string-theoretic (no
   field-theory interpretation). The family is uniquely fixed by the zero-preserving requirement.

3. **Loop integrands fixed by zeros**: (Conjectural) — one-loop Tr(φ³) uniquely determined
   from zeros alone, without assuming locality/unitarity as inputs.

4. **Cosmological hidden zeros**: Cosmological correlators have analogous vanishing structure.

5. **Yang-Mills canonical 1-loop integrand**: arXiv:2408.11891 defines "the" canonical YM 1-loop
   integrand via surface kinematics — first integrand with well-defined single-loop cuts at all
   multiplicities.
