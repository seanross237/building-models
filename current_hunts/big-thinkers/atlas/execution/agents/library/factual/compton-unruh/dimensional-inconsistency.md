## Finding

The direct Compton-Unruh resonance hypothesis — that the Compton frequency of a particle matches the Unruh frequency at cosmological accelerations — is **dimensionally inconsistent by 43 orders of magnitude**.

## The Matching Condition

Three equivalent matching conditions (energy, wavelength, frequency) all give the same unique result:

    a* = 2*pi*m*c^3 / hbar

For a proton: a*(proton) = 2.685 x 10^33 m/s^2
For an electron: a*(electron) = 1.46 x 10^30 m/s^2

Target accelerations:
- cH_0 = 6.60 x 10^-10 m/s^2
- a_0 (MOND) = 1.2 x 10^-10 m/s^2

**The matching acceleration is 43 orders of magnitude larger than a_0.** (a*/a_0 = 2.24 x 10^43)

## Key Properties

1. **Mass-dependent**: a* scales linearly with m. Different particles have different "resonance" accelerations. No universal critical acceleration.
2. **No cosmological parameter**: The condition involves only local quantities (m, c, hbar). H_0 is absent.
3. **Critical mass for a_0 matching**: For a* = cH_0, one needs m_crit = 4.11 x 10^-70 kg = 2.30 x 10^-34 eV/c^2 — 42 orders of magnitude below the proton mass and 35 below neutrino masses.

## De Sitter Modification Cannot Rescue

The de Sitter modified temperature T_dS = (hbar/2*pi*c*k_B) * sqrt(a^2 + c^2*H^2) introduces a cosmological term, but:

    (2*pi*m_p*c^3/hbar)^2 ~ 7.2 x 10^66
    (cH_0)^2 ~ 4.4 x 10^-19

The cosmological correction is negligible by a factor of 10^-85.

## McCulloch's Quantised Inertia

McCulloch's QI introduces H_0 by postulating that Unruh radiation modes with wavelengths exceeding 2R_H = 2c/H_0 are suppressed. This is an **ad hoc boundary condition** imposed by hand, not derived from the QFT framework.

## Verdict

The Compton-Unruh resonance at a ~ cH_0 does not exist in standard QFT. The 43-order-of-magnitude discrepancy is not a "small correction" problem — it is a fundamental dimensional mismatch.
