# Experimental Signatures Beyond CMB: Comprehensive Catalog

Systematic assessment of all QG+F experimental signatures outside the CMB sector. **Bottom line: every non-CMB signature is undetectable with current or foreseeable technology.** The fundamental barrier is the ~15 orders of magnitude gap between the highest probed energies (~10⁴ GeV at LHC, ~10² Hz at LIGO) and QG+F mass scales (M₂, M₀ ~ 10¹³-10¹⁹ GeV). **QG+F is effectively a one-prediction theory: the tensor-to-scalar ratio r.**

## Gravitational Wave Signatures

### Scalar Mode (Spin-0 from R²)

The scalaron is a physical particle (unlike the spin-2 fakeon) sourcing scalar (breathing) GW polarization. But M₀ ~ 3×10¹³ GeV gives Compton wavelength λ₀ ~ 10⁻²⁷ m — far below any detection scale. **NOT DETECTABLE.**

### Modified Dispersion Relations

The massless graviton dispersion ω = |k|c is **unchanged** from GR. Massive modes have ω² = k² + M² with M near the Planck scale. Graviton-massive mode oscillation length L_osc ~ E/M² ~ 10⁻⁶⁰ m for LIGO frequencies. LVK tests (arXiv:2511.00497, 2025) parameterize E² = p²c² + A·p^α·c^α but cannot reach Planck-scale corrections. Suppression: (E/M_Planck)² ~ 10⁻⁸⁰ for LIGO. **NOT DETECTABLE.**

### Extra GW Polarizations

QG+F in principle has scalar breathing mode (from scalaron) and massive spin-2 modes (5 helicities). Under fakeon prescription, the spin-2 does NOT produce real GW polarization. The scalar mode requires M₀ light enough to be sourced astrophysically — at M₀ ~ 10¹³ GeV, it is not. Current networks (LIGO+Virgo+KAGRA) and future (ET, LISA) can distinguish polarization types, but no extra modes are present at observable frequencies. **NOT DETECTABLE.**

### BH Merger Ringdown and QNMs

**Most studied GW signature.** Massive spin-2 QNMs computed (arXiv:2412.15037, March 2025). Hairy BH solutions exist for 0.876 ≲ r_h·μ ≲ 1.143. Massive spin-2 carries more energy than spin-0 in GW emission.

**First LVK ringdown constraints on quadratic gravity (arXiv:2506.14695, June 2025):** Analyzed GW150914, GW190521, GW200129. 90% C.I. bounds: coupling length ℓ < 34-49 km. But these constrain M₂ > 5×10⁻¹² eV — **40 orders of magnitude weaker** than QG+F's expected M₂ ~ M_Planck.

Under fakeon prescription with M₂ ~ M_Planck, QNM corrections suppressed by exp(-M₂·r_h) ~ exp(-10⁵⁰) for astrophysical BHs. **NOT DETECTABLE.**

### Six-Derivative Extension GW Signatures

Lee-Wick complex-mass pairs (14 DOF total): additional QNM families, interference patterns. Same Planck-scale suppression applies. **NOT DETECTABLE.**

### Stochastic GW Background

QG+F-specific GW background from: (1) scalaron oscillation during reheating → f ~ 10⁸-10¹⁰ Hz; (2) possible ghost confinement transition at T ~ M_Planck → f ~ 10¹⁰ Hz; (3) CDT B-C phase transition → f ~ 10¹⁰ Hz. All at frequencies far above LISA (10⁻⁴-10⁻¹ Hz), LIGO (10-10³ Hz), and PTA (10⁻⁹-10⁻⁷ Hz). **NOT DETECTABLE.**

## Table-Top Experiments

### GQuEST — NOT RELEVANT to QG+F

GQuEST tests holographic "geontropic" spacetime fluctuations (Verlinde-Zurek pixellon model). QG+F is a perturbative QFT with no pixellon field; modifications are at ~10⁻³⁵ m, 17 orders below GQuEST sensitivity (~10⁻¹⁸ m). A null result is consistent with QG+F. A positive result would be evidence AGAINST QG+F.

### BMV Gravity-Induced Entanglement — NOT DISCRIMINATING

BMV tests whether gravity mediates quantum entanglement (binary yes/no). QG+F predicts YES, but so do ALL quantum gravity theories. At BMV energies (E ≪ M₂), only the massless graviton mediates — fakeon and scalaron don't contribute. Cannot distinguish QG+F from any other QG theory.

### Short-Range Gravity (Yukawa Bounds)

Stelle potential Yukawa ranges: 1/M₀ ~ 7×10⁻²⁸ m, 1/M₂ ~ 10⁻³⁵ m. Best experiments (Eöt-Wash): |α| ≤ 1 down to λ = 48 μm. Gap: **~25 orders of magnitude.** Current bounds constrain M₂ > 4×10⁻³ eV — 31 orders of magnitude too weak. **NOT CONSTRAINING.**

## Cosmological Signatures Beyond CMB

### 21-cm Cosmology

QG+F modifications to Friedmann/growth equations suppressed by (H/M)² ~ 10⁻¹¹⁰. The scalaron mass in QG+F (M₀ ~ 10¹³ GeV) is NOT the light scalaron of f(R) dark energy models. **NOT DETECTABLE.**

### Large-Scale Structure and Matter Power Spectrum

Scalaron fifth-force range ~ 10⁻²⁷ m — irrelevant for cosmology. Theory reduces to ΛCDM + GR at all cosmologically accessible scales. DESI and Euclid f(R) constraints are not relevant to QG+F. Gravitational lensing corrections: (E/M)² ~ 10⁻¹¹⁰. **NOT DETECTABLE.**

### Dark Matter Candidates

1. **Scalaron as DM:** Recent work (arXiv:2506.06436, June 2025) finds viable freeze-in DM mass range m_Φ ≲ 0.17 GeV. But QG+F scalaron mass M₀ ~ 10¹³ GeV is far above this window; decays rapidly. **NOT VIABLE** in standard QG+F.
2. **Planck-mass BH remnants (AS):** Non-perturbative prediction, not accessible to perturbative QG+F. No abundance prediction.
3. **Agravity DM (right-handed ν):** Lightest right-handed neutrino (M ~ 10⁸-10¹⁰ GeV) via freeze-in. Model-dependent (requires specific agravity model, not generic QG+F). Testable through leptogenesis and neutrino oscillation parameters.

## Microcausality Violation

**Does NOT propagate to observable scales.** Anselmi & Marino (CQG 2020) explicitly investigated amplification. Result:
- Violation confined to Δx ~ 1/M₂ (extends up to ~10⁻²⁹ m, six orders above Planck length, if M₂ as low as 10¹² GeV)
- Does NOT propagate along light cones
- Does NOT propagate via gravitational waves
- "The universe even conspires to make the effect disappear" — the Hubble expansion suppresses acausal effects
- No amplification mechanism exists

### The "Missing Resonance" Signature

The most distinctive QG+F prediction: at E ~ M₂ the cross section shows **no resonance peak** (smooth, with at most a gentle shoulder or dip). A physical particle would produce a Breit-Wigner bump; the fakeon produces its absence. In six-derivative extension: complex-mass poles produce interference ("pair of bumps"). **Requires collider energies ~10¹⁹ GeV** — a gap of 10¹⁵ from the LHC. UHECR reach ~10¹¹ GeV, still 10⁸ below M₂.

### Indirect Channels (All Null)

1. **Cosmological correlations:** Effects already captured in CMB predictions (n_s, r). No additional signature.
2. **BH information leakage:** Rate ~ exp(-M₂·r_h) ~ exp(-10⁵⁰) for astrophysical BHs.
3. **GW propagation:** Anselmi & Marino (2020) showed explicitly microcausality violation does NOT propagate via GWs.
4. **Arrow of time:** H₀ positivity connected to time direction in fakeon framework — philosophical, not testable.
