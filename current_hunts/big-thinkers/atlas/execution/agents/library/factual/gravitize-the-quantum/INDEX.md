# Gravitize the Quantum

Programs that reverse the usual "quantize gravity" arrow — taking gravity or spacetime as more fundamental and deriving quantum mechanics from it. Also includes programs that derive QM and gravity from a common deeper substratum.

See [landscape-overview.md](landscape-overview.md) for the meta-analysis of what programs actually achieve. 15 findings.
