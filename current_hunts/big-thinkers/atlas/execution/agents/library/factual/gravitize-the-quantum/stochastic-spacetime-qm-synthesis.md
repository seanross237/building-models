# Stochastic Spacetime → QM Synthesis

## The Pipeline

A novel synthesis combining the strongest elements of multiple "gravitize the quantum" programs into a unified framework. The individual elements are well-known; the specific pipeline and combination appear to be novel.

### Step 1: Spacetime Has Fundamental Stochastic Structure (Oppenheim + Hu-Verdaguer)
- Spacetime geometry fluctuates stochastically at a fundamental level
- This is NOT quantum gravity (metric is never in superposition) — it's *noisy classical gravity*
- Oppenheim: if gravity is classical, coupling to quantum-like matter must be stochastic
- Hu-Verdaguer: provides the mathematical template (Einstein-Langevin equation)

### Step 2: Stochastic Spacetime → QM (Nelson + Barandes + Doukas)
- Particles on stochastic spacetime experience effective Brownian motion (Nelson's stochastic mechanics gets a physical source)
- Diffusion coefficient: ℏ/2m ∝ √(G × noise amplitude) → physical derivation of ℏ from gravitational parameters
- Resulting stochastic dynamics is "indivisible" (Barandes) because spacetime stochastic structure creates non-Markovian correlations
- Doukas lifting procedure gives Hilbert space as natural mathematical framework

### Step 3: Collapse From Gravitational Self-Interaction (Diósi-Penrose + Adler)
- Massive systems: own gravitational field affects experienced noise (self-interaction)
- Creates instability in extended superpositions (Diósi-Penrose mechanism)
- Collapse timescale τ = ℏ/E_G emerges naturally
- The "thermodynamic bath" for Adler-type fluctuations is identified as the stochastic spacetime

### Step 4: Gravity as Mean-Field (Jacobson + Verlinde)
- Einstein equations are the equation of state of the stochastic spacetime (Jacobson's insight)
- Gravity describes average, large-scale behavior of stochastic spacetime (as pressure describes average molecular motion)
- Stochastic spacetime is the common source of both QM (via noise → diffusion → Hilbert space) and gravity (via averaging → Einstein equations)

## Concrete Predictions

1. **No graviton** — gravity is mean-field, not quantized. Graviton scattering amplitudes = zero
2. **Spacetime diffusion** — irreducible stochastic metric fluctuations, beyond any quantum source. Constrained by (but not yet ruled out by) GW observations. LISA could improve bounds
3. **Modified decoherence rates** — specific form determined by gravitational noise kernel. Atom interferometry approaching relevant regime
4. **ℏ-G relation** — if ℏ determined by G and noise amplitude, measuring all three independently tests the relation
5. **Departure from unitarity at Planck scale** — near Planck scale, stochastic substratum becomes directly relevant

## What the Synthesis Offers

1. Physical source for quantum noise (spacetime stochasticity)
2. Derivation of ℏ from gravitational parameters (in principle)
3. Natural resolution of the problem of time (time = stochastic process parameter)
4. Unified origin of QM and gravity from common substratum
5. Built-in objective collapse mechanism (Diósi-Penrose)
6. Consistency with known experimental constraints (inherits Oppenheim's framework)

## Critical Gaps

1. **No derivation of WHY spacetime is stochastic** — assumed, not derived
2. **No specific noise kernel** — would need a more fundamental theory
3. **Lifting is mathematically demonstrated but physically mysterious** — why should nature "use" the lifted description?
4. **ℏ ∝ f(G, ...) is schematic, not derived** — computing this would be a major achievement
5. **No Standard Model derivation** — no gauge structure, no matter content
6. **Diffeomorphism invariance puzzle** — how does a stochastic process on spacetime respect general covariance?
