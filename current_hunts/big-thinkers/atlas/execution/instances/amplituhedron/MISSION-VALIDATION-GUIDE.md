# Mission Validation Guide — Amplituhedron vs QFT

## What Success Looks Like

A concrete, novel finding about the relationship between geometric amplitude methods and standard QFT — either a case where they diverge, a proof of equivalence with precise scope, or a demonstration that the geometric framework reaches results QFT cannot. The finding must involve actual computation, not just philosophical argument.

## Validation Tiers

### Tier 1: Computation
- At least one scattering amplitude computed independently via amplituhedron and Feynman diagrams
- Results agree where they should (known cases in N=4 SYM)
- Computation is explicit and reproducible

### Tier 2: Extension
- Framework applied beyond textbook cases — higher loops, more external legs, or different theories
- At least one calculation where the amplituhedron approach reaches a result that Feynman diagrams make impractical
- The scope of the geometric approach is mapped — where does it apply, where does it break?

### Tier 3: Physical Content
- The question "is this just a better calculator?" is addressed with evidence, not philosophy
- If physically equivalent: the equivalence is characterized precisely (for which theories, in which regimes)
- If not equivalent: the discrepancy is identified concretely (specific amplitude, specific observable)
- The role of locality and unitarity is analyzed — do they emerge exactly, or approximately?

### Tier 4: Novelty
- Prior art search covers Arkani-Hamed, Trnka, Bai, He, Ferro, Lam, and related geometric amplitude literature
- The finding is new — not a restatement of known equivalences or known limitations
- If a discrepancy is found, it is cross-verified by independent calculation
- If no discrepancy exists, the proof of equivalence is tighter than what exists in the literature
- The strongest objection is addressed
- The result would be defensible in front of an amplitudes researcher

### Tier 5: Implications
- What the results mean for the ontology of spacetime and quantum fields — is spacetime emergent?
- Whether the geometric framework suggests new physics beyond the Standard Model
- Connections to other "spacetime is emergent" programs (holography, causal sets, etc.)
- Clear statement of what remains open vs. what has been settled

## What "Exhausted" Looks Like

A strategy is exhausted when:
- Computations in both frameworks have been compared as far as tools allow
- The equivalence/non-equivalence question has been answered for the accessible regime
- Extensions to realistic theories have been attempted and either succeeded or hit characterized obstacles
