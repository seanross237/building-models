# Strategy 002: Beyond the No-Go — Novel Theories from Relaxed Assumptions

## Objective

Strategy 001 proved that {d_s = 2, Lorentz invariance, diffeomorphism invariance, renormalizability} uniquely selects quadratic gravity + fakeon — an existing program. This strategy systematically explores what happens when each assumption is relaxed, modified, or replaced, to find genuinely novel theories that no existing program has developed. The aim is to discover a theory that either (a) produces new testable predictions distinct from quadratic gravity + fakeon, or (b) solves open problems that quadratic gravity + fakeon cannot (cosmological constant, n_s tension, Standard Model origin).

## Methodology

The Strategizer should follow a **systematic relaxation and reconstruction** protocol:

### Phase 1: Map the Escape Routes (explorations 1–3)

Strategy 001's no-go theorem constrains the landscape tightly. The first step is to map *exactly* which assumptions can be relaxed and what each relaxation opens up:

1. **Relax Lorentz invariance** — Horava-Lifshitz (z=3) achieves d_s = 2 without ghosts, but known versions face severe Lorentz violation bounds. Are there UNKNOWN Lorentz-violating theories in the landscape? What about theories with emergent Lorentz invariance (exact in IR, broken only at Planck scale)?

2. **Relax strict renormalizability** — What if we require only asymptotic safety (non-perturbative UV completion) rather than perturbative renormalizability? The Reuter fixed point exists in truncations with higher-order curvature invariants. Do any of these produce d_s = 2 with a different action than quadratic gravity?

3. **Relax d_s = 2** — What if spectral dimension universality is an artifact rather than a fundamental constraint? What theories produce d_s → 2 + ε (near but not exactly 2)? What if the "true" UV spectral dimension is irrational or scale-dependent in a non-trivial way?

4. **Relax locality** — Nonlocal theories (IDG) give d_s → 0, not d_s = 2. But what about *partially* nonlocal theories — local in some sectors, nonlocal in others? Or theories with scale-dependent locality?

5. **Replace spectral dimension with a different constructive constraint** — What if instead of d_s = 2, we start from the cosmological constant (Λ ≈ 10⁻¹²² M_P⁴) or the Bekenstein-Hawking entropy formula or the holographic entropy bound as constructive axioms?

For each escape route, determine: is the resulting theory space empty (no viable theories), singleton (already known), or open (genuinely unexplored territory)?

### Phase 2: Construct in the Open Spaces (explorations 4–14)

For each escape route that opens genuinely unexplored territory, construct explicit theories using the constraint-driven method from strategy 001 (but now with modified constraint sets):

**Construction protocol for each direction:**
1. Write down the modified constraint set explicitly
2. Determine the most general action/framework consistent with those constraints
3. Check structural sanity (Tier 1): correct DOF, unitarity, ghost freedom
4. Check known physics recovery (Tier 2): Newton's law, GR limit
5. If it passes Tiers 1-2, push to precision tests (Tier 3) and predictions (Tier 4)
6. Compare against quadratic gravity + fakeon: is the new theory distinguishable? Does it solve any problem that QG+F cannot?

**Key construction directions to prioritize:**
- **Theories that solve the n_s tension** — Strategy 001 found 2.3σ tension between QG+F's n_s ≈ 0.967 and ACT DR6's n_s = 0.974 ± 0.003. A theory that naturally produces n_s ≈ 0.974 while retaining QG+F's other successes would be a significant advance.
- **Theories that address the cosmological constant** — The "new CC problem" (why Λ ≈ 10⁻¹²² M_P⁴) is unsolved in QG+F. Any theory that constrains or predicts Λ would be a breakthrough.
- **Theories with richer prediction landscapes** — QG+F has essentially one near-term prediction (r). A theory with multiple independent testable predictions (r + something else) would be more valuable.
- **Non-perturbative structures** — Strategy 001 established the QCD analogy (QG+F = perturbative, AS = non-perturbative). What are the "confinement" and "hadron" analogues for gravity? Are there non-perturbative predictions invisible in the fakeon framework?

### Phase 3: Sharpen and Compare (explorations 15–20)

Take the most promising novel theory(ies) from Phase 2 and:

1. Run full validation (Tiers 1–4)
2. Compute explicit predictions and compare against QG+F point by point
3. Identify the sharpest discriminating observable — the measurement that would distinguish the new theory from QG+F
4. Assess the n_s tension: does the new theory resolve it?
5. If no novel theory beats QG+F overall, identify specific regimes or problems where it provides new insight

## Validation Criteria

**Minimum success:** At least one genuinely novel theory (not an existing program) passes Tier 2 and is distinguishable from QG+F.

**Good success:** A novel theory passes Tier 3 and either resolves the n_s tension, addresses the cosmological constant, or produces multiple testable predictions.

**Breakthrough:** A novel theory passes Tier 4 with predictions distinct from both GR and QG+F, especially if it explains the cosmological constant or connects to Standard Model structure.

The strategy is **exhausted** when:
- All escape routes from the no-go theorem have been explored and either lead to known theories or empty theory spaces
- Multiple construction attempts in the open spaces all fail Tier 1 or Tier 2
- The landscape beyond QG+F is convincingly mapped and contains no viable novel candidates

## Context

### What Strategy 001 Established

1. **The no-go theorem:** Ghost-free Lorentz-invariant theories cannot produce d_s = 2. This is the central constraint — it's what makes the problem interesting.

2. **Unique selection:** {d_s = 2, Lorentz invariance, diffeo invariance, renormalizability} → quadratic gravity + fakeon. Exactly one theory.

3. **The landscape has only two viable d_s = 2 theories** in the Lorentz-invariant sector: QG+F and Lee-Wick gravity (same action, different quantization).

4. **QG+F ↔ AS duality:** Quadratic gravity + fakeon is the perturbative face of asymptotic safety. They are the same theory in different regimes.

5. **Validation:** QG+F passes all 7 validation tests. Prediction: r ∈ [0.0004, 0.0035].

6. **Tensions:** n_s ≈ 0.967 vs ACT DR6 n_s = 0.974 ± 0.003 (2.3σ). The "new CC problem" is unsolved.

7. **Constraint types:** Constructive (spectral dimension selects action), selective (unitarity forces quantization), confirmatory (entanglement checks consistency).
