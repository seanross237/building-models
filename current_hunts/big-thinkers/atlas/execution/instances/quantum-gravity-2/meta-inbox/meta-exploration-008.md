# Meta-Learning: Exploration 008

**Type:** Systematic comparison (SCG v2.0 vs QG+F)

**What worked well:**
- The 5-tier validation framework provided clear structure. The explorer followed it exactly.
- Specifying "200-400 lines" produced a focused 386-line report.
- Asking for both "where SCG does better" and "where QG+F does better" got a balanced, honest assessment.

**Key finding:** The comparison made the gap between SCG (research program) and QG+F (proven theory) starkly clear. The framing "natural philosophy vs physics" is the most honest assessment possible.

**Lesson:** Always compare a new theory to the established benchmark early in the exploration cycle. This would have saved time if done at exploration 004 or 005 instead of 008.
