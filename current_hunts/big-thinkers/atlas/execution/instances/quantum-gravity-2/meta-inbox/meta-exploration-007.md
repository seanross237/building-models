# Meta-Learning: Exploration 007

**Type:** Theory repair (narrowly scoped)

**What worked well:**
- NARROW SCOPE was the key. Exploration 006 (same topic, broad scope) timed out. Exploration 007 (same topic, narrow scope) succeeded in under 10 minutes of active work.
- Specifying "keep report 200-400 lines" prevented the explorer from expanding into unnecessary detail.
- The 5-section structure (axioms, chain survival, what's fixed, what's broken, assessment) was perfect for a repair task.
- Asking for a scorecard (v1.0 severity vs v2.0 severity) produced the clearest possible output.

**Lesson for future strategizers:** For theory repair/revision tasks: (1) ONE repair per exploration, (2) specify a line count target, (3) use a structured format (what changed, what survived, what's fixed, what's broken, verdict), (4) include a scorecard. This is now the proven pattern for repair explorations.

The contrast between exploration 006 (timed out) and 007 (clean success) is the most important meta-learning from this strategy cycle.
