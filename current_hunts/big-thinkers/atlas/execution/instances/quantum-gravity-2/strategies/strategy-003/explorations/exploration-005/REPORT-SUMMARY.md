# Exploration 005 Summary: Black Hole Prediction Compatibility

## Goal
Determine whether QG+F's Schwarzschild BH prediction is genuinely incompatible with AS's modified BH (running G, singularity resolution, Planck remnants), or whether the two describe different regimes of the same theory.

## Approach
Analyzed QG+F's perturbative BH predictions (Lichnerowicz theorem → R=0 → Schwarzschild selected by fakeon, corrections O(l_P²/r_H²)), AS's non-perturbative predictions (Bonanno-Reuter metric, G(r)→0, de Sitter core, Planck remnants), and the bridge works (Holdom-Ren QCD analogy, Bonanno 2025 spontaneous ghostification, branch-crossing instability).

## Verdict: SUPPORTS

The predictions are compatible — they describe perturbative vs. non-perturbative regimes of the same physics. Five arguments:

1. **Anselmi admits non-perturbative blindness** (JHEP 2026) — QG+F's "BHs are Schwarzschild" means "to all perturbative orders," not "exactly."
2. **Both agree at large r** — AS's Bonanno-Reuter metric reduces to Schwarzschild + O(l_P²/r²) far from center, matching QG+F.
3. **Perturbative expansion breaks down where AS takes over** — at M ~ M_P, corrections become O(1), and only the full RG flow (AS) can predict what happens.
4. **Planck remnants fill a gap, not a contradiction** — QG+F makes no evaporation endpoint prediction; remnants are non-perturbative, beyond its reach.
5. **QCD analogy is precise** — Schwarzschild = "partonic" (perturbative) description, modified BH = "hadronic" (non-perturbative) description. Same theory, different regimes.

## Key Caveat
Compatibility depends on the ghost being successfully removed non-perturbatively. If the ghost survives at strong coupling, spontaneous ghostification (Bonanno 2025) predicts a naked singularity endpoint instead of a remnant — which WOULD break compatibility. Ghost fate remains the central bottleneck for the entire QG+F = AS conjecture.

## Key Takeaway
The BH sector does NOT falsify QG+F = AS. The apparent contradiction is an artifact of comparing a perturbative prediction with a non-perturbative one. Scorecard update: 0 FALSIFIED, 2 SUPPORTS (inflation, BHs), 2 INCONCLUSIVE (fixed points, ghost fate).
