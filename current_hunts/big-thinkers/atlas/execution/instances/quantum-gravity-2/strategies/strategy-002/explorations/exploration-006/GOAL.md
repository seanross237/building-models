# Exploration 006: Deep Build — Causal Fakeon Theory (CFT)

## Mission Context

Three concept sprints explored different approaches to the fakeon interpretation. The winner: **Causal Order → Fakeon** (Exploration 003), which discovered a concrete mathematical bridge:

1. The fakeon propagator = Wheeler propagator = (G_retarded + G_advanced)/2 at tree level
2. Belenchia, Benincasa & Liberati (2015) showed causal set dynamics produce the Wheeler propagator for unstable modes, automatically excluding them from asymptotic states
3. NOBODY has connected these findings to argue that causal order motivates the fakeon

The concept also has known weaknesses:
- Wheeler ≠ fakeon at loop level (Anselmi 2020)
- Classical→quantum gap (causal order is classical; fakeon is quantum)
- Circularity (using causal structure to constrain a mode that generates causal structure)
- SJ construction only done for scalars, not spin-2

This exploration builds the concept into a focused theory.

## Your Task

### 1. Axioms/Principles (be precise)

State the core principles of CFT. Suggested starting point (modify as needed):

**Axiom 1 (Causal Foundation):** Physical spacetime has a fundamental causal partial order ≺ that is prior to the metric. The partial order determines the conformal geometry via Malament's theorem. A volume measure determines the conformal factor (Sorkin's "order + number = geometry").

**Axiom 2 (Causal Propagation):** Every quantum field mode must have a propagator compatible with the causal order: G(x,y) must decompose into G_causal (support on causally related pairs) + G_acausal (symmetric). Only G_causal contributes to asymptotic states.

**Axiom 3 (Sign Rule):** Modes with positive residues at their mass poles have natural causal propagation (Feynman prescription = causal). Modes with negative residues have anti-causal propagation; the only ≺-compatible treatment is the Wheeler/fakeon prescription (average of retarded and advanced = purely virtual).

**Axiom 4 (Dynamical Consistency):** The causal order ≺ is dynamically generated by the theory itself (self-consistency). The metric fluctuations (including the fakeon mode) are perturbations of ≺. The fakeon is consistent because it contributes to ≺ only through virtual effects (loops), never as an on-shell disturbance.

### 2. Physical Consequences

Derive or argue for:
- **Recovery of QG+F:** Show that CFT reproduces QG+F at tree level (this should follow directly from the Wheeler = fakeon connection)
- **Newton's law and GR limit:** Does the theory reduce to GR at low energies?
- **Microcausality violation:** How does CFT explain/predict the Planck-scale microcausality violation? (Hypothesis: it reflects the discreteness of the causal order below the fakeon scale)
- **Spectral dimension:** What does CFT predict for d_s? (Belenchia et al. found d_s → 2 from causal set dynamics — does this connect to QG+F's d_s = 2?)
