## Part 1: The Convergence Observation

Three independent approaches were developed across Explorations 003–007 to interpret QG+F's fakeon prescription. Each starts from a different physical principle and arrives at the same conclusion: **the massive spin-2 ghost cannot be a real particle**.

| Approach | Starting Principle | Route to Exclusion |
|---|---|---|
| **Causal Order** (Exp. 003, 006) | Spacetime is fundamentally a causal partial order ≺ | The SJ vacuum construction breaks down for negative-spectral-weight modes; the only ≺-compatible propagator is the Wheeler propagator G_W = (G_R + G_A)/2, which equals G_fakeon at tree level |
| **Indivisibility** (Exp. 004) | Quantum processes exhibit stochastic indivisibility (Barandes-Doukas) | A mode with negative residue produces negative intermediate "transition probabilities"; the process cannot be factored through this mode → fakeon prescription forced |
| **Entanglement** (Exp. 005) | Gravity emerges from entanglement equilibrium (MVEH/Jacobson) | A Feynman ghost produces negative-norm states → pathological entanglement entropy (negative eigenvalues in reduced density matrix) → MVEH self-consistency requires fakeon |

**Is this convergence surprising or trivial?**

It is **partially trivial, partially substantive**. The trivial part: all three routes ultimately require a positive-definite physical Hilbert space — causal order demands it (SJ construction fails otherwise), indivisibility demands it (negative transition probabilities otherwise), entanglement demands it (pathological entropy otherwise). Since "positive-definite Hilbert space" IS unitarity, and unitarity requires the fakeon (Anselmi 2017), the convergence is guaranteed at the level of the final conclusion.

The substantive part: each approach arrives at the fakeon through a **different intermediate mechanism**, and these mechanisms are not logically equivalent:
- Causal order provides a *constructive* route: causal structure → SJ vacuum → Wheeler propagator → fakeon. This is the only approach that produces the specific propagator (Wheeler) from first principles rather than from elimination.
- Indivisibility provides a *process-level* explanation: the ghost cannot be a real intermediate state because the scattering process is fundamentally indivisible at that scale. This is the only approach that directly addresses the analyticity sacrifice.
- Entanglement provides a *self-consistency* argument: gravity-from-entanglement requires the fakeon to preserve the entanglement structure from which gravity emerges. This is the only approach that places the fakeon within the emergent gravity paradigm.

**Verdict:** The convergence is non-trivial in mechanism, even though it is guaranteed in conclusion. The three approaches form complementary perspectives on a single physical fact, not redundant restatements of the same argument.

## Part 2: Unique Contributions of Each Approach

### 2a. Causal Order → Fakeon (Explorations 003, 006, 007)

**What it uniquely explains:**

1. **Why the fakeon propagator specifically.** Unitarity alone doesn't select the fakeon — it eliminates the Feynman ghost but leaves open the Lee-Wick prescription (complex-conjugate poles), PT-symmetric quantization, or other ghost-removal schemes. The causal-order approach selects the Wheeler/fakeon propagator uniquely: it is the only propagator that (a) has no on-shell poles, (b) is real (no preferred time direction for the ghost), and (c) arises constructively from the SJ vacuum on a causal set (Belenchia et al. 2015).

2. **The Wheeler propagator bridge.** The tree-level mathematical identity G_fakeon = G_Wheeler = (G_R + G_A)/2 connects Anselmi's fakeon program to causal set QFT. This is the only concrete mathematical result across all three approaches — everything else is conceptual.

3. **Microcausality violation reinterpreted.** The microcausality violation at ~1/M₂ (which Anselmi treats as an acceptable cost or argues causality should be "abandoned") becomes a signature of discrete causal structure. Below the discreteness scale, events are neither causally related nor spacelike-separated but simply unresolved.

4. **Spectral dimension unification.** The d_s = 4 → 2 UV flow, present in both QG+F (from 1/p⁴ propagator) and causal set dynamics (Belenchia et al. 2016), is unified as having a common causal origin.

**Limitations (from devil's advocate, Exp. 007):** The SJ construction exists only for free scalar fields on fixed backgrounds — not spin-2, not interacting, not dynamical backgrounds. The loop-level extension is provably wrong if done naively (Anselmi 2020). The "structural prediction" that causal sets should produce quadratic gravity is currently unsupported — the Benincasa-Dowker action gives Einstein-Hilbert, not Stelle's action.

### 2b. Indivisibility → Fakeon (Exploration 004)

**What it uniquely explains:**

1. **The analyticity sacrifice.** This is the strongest unique contribution. Indivisible stochastic processes encode information in irreducible multi-time correlations — data that cannot be reconstructed from pairwise (two-time) information. In QFT, analyticity allows reconstructing amplitudes from their singularity structure (essentially two-point data via dispersion relations). The fakeon breaks analyticity because the gravitational process carries multi-time information that cannot be captured by analytic continuation. The analyticity sacrifice is not an unexplained cost but the **signature of indivisibility**.

2. **Connection to the measurement problem.** In the Barandes framework, divisibility corresponds to classical measurement compatibility. An indivisible process cannot be consistently measured at intermediate times. The fakeon mode is "unmeasurable at intermediate times" — it cannot appear as an asymptotic state. This connects the fakeon's non-observability to the same principle that makes quantum mechanics non-classical.

3. **Non-perturbative criterion (speculative).** If the fakeon arises from indivisibility, the non-perturbative completion of QG+F should be the most general indivisible stochastic process on the gravitational configuration space.

**Limitations:** The Barandes-Doukas framework has no QFT extension. The connection between stochastic indivisibility (time-domain property) and the fakeon prescription (momentum-domain pole treatment) is conceptual analogy, not mathematical derivation. The approach generates no new predictions or calculations. It is primarily an interpretive reframing of the unitarity argument (devil's advocate scored it as "barely passing the Aaronson test").

### 2c. Entanglement → Fakeon (Exploration 005)

**What it uniquely explains:**

1. **Self-consistency of emergent gravity.** This is the unique contribution: the fakeon is required not just for "consistency of the quantum theory" but for "gravity to exist as an emergent phenomenon from entanglement." MVEH (Jacobson 2015, Bueno et al. 2017) derives gravitational field equations from entanglement equilibrium. The ghost with Feynman prescription would make entanglement entropy pathological (negative eigenvalues in the reduced density matrix, as shown by Jatkar & Narayan 2017 for ghost-spin models), destroying the MVEH premises. The fakeon preserves the entanglement scaffold from which gravity itself emerges.

2. **Structural prediction: Wald entropy matching.** The Wald entropy corrections in QG+F should be exactly the corrections needed to maintain MVEH with the fakeon prescription. This is checkable by comparing Bueno et al.'s generalized MVEH with QG+F's Wald entropy functional.

3. **Research direction: nonlinear MVEH as discriminator.** The linearized MVEH cannot distinguish between Feynman ghost, Lee-Wick, and fakeon prescriptions (they agree at tree level). The nonlinear extension — if achieved — should uniquely select the fakeon. This is a prediction about the research program, not from the theory, but it's a well-posed one.

**Limitations:** The MVEH program is stuck at the linearization barrier for higher-derivative gravity (Bueno et al. 2017 explicitly state nonlinear equations cannot be derived from linearized equations applied to small balls). At linearized level, all ghost prescriptions are indistinguishable. The argument may reduce to "unitarity requires the fakeon" with entanglement decoration. The key technical claim (ghost entanglement is pathological in QFT, not just spin models) has not been rigorously established.

## Part 3: The Wheeler Propagator Bridge — Novelty Verification

The strongest concrete finding across all explorations is the **tree-level identity linking the fakeon propagator to the Wheeler propagator**, combined with the independent result that causal set dynamics produce the Wheeler propagator for unstable modes.

**The chain:**
1. G_fakeon(p) = P.V. 1/(p² − m²) = (G_R + G_A)/2 = G_Wheeler (exact mathematical identity)
2. Causal set d'Alembertian in 4D produces Wheeler propagator for unstable modes → these modes don't appear in asymptotic states (Belenchia, Benincasa & Liberati, JHEP 03(2015)036)
3. Nobody has connected (1) and (2) to argue that causal order motivates the fakeon

**Final literature search results (6 searches conducted):**

| Search | Result |
|---|---|
| Belenchia et al. + Anselmi fakeon | **No connection found.** The two programs cite zero papers in common. |
| Wheeler propagator + ghost treatment in higher-derivative gravity | **Only Anselmi's own 2020 paper** comparing fakeons to Feynman-Wheeler particles — concludes they differ at loop level. No connection to causal sets. |
| Sorkin-Johnston construction + ghost modes / negative spectral weight | **Nothing found.** The SJ construction has never been discussed in the context of ghost fields. |
| "Causal set" + "quadratic gravity" ghost propagator | **No intersection.** These research communities do not interact. |
| Fakeon + causal order/structure interpretation 2025-2026 | **Nothing found.** No recent papers interpret the fakeon through causal structure. |
| "Wheeler propagator" + "fakeon" / "purely virtual" | **Only Anselmi (2020)**, who explicitly compared fakeons to Wheeler particles but did not invoke causal sets. |

**Novelty verdict: CONFIRMED.** The observation that G_fakeon = G_Wheeler at tree level AND that causal set dynamics independently produce G_Wheeler for problematic modes constitutes a **genuinely novel bridge** between two disconnected research programs. No paper in the literature makes this connection.

**Important caveat:** Anselmi (2020) proved that this bridge **fails at loop level** — the Wheeler propagator naively used in loops violates renormalizability, unitarity, and stability. The fakeon's loop-level procedure (threshold-dropping) has no known causal-set derivation. The bridge is therefore a **tree-level observation**, not a theory.

## Part 4: Open Problems

Five precise open problems emerge from this work, ordered by tractability:

**1. The Loop-Level Completion Problem**
*Can the causal set formalism reproduce Anselmi's threshold-dropping procedure at loop level?*
This is the make-or-break problem for the causal-order interpretation. The naive approach (Wheeler propagator in all loops) provably fails. The correct approach requires an interacting SJ construction on causal sets that reproduces the spectral optical theorem. Current state: interacting scalar QFT on causal sets exists at tree level only (Albertini et al. 2024). Estimated difficulty: very high (decade-scale).

**2. The Spin-2 SJ Construction**
*Can the Sorkin-Johnston vacuum construction be extended from scalar to tensor (spin-2) fields on causal sets?*
The entire causal-order argument for the fakeon rests on a scalar analogy. The actual ghost in QG+F is spin-2. The SJ construction for spin-2 doesn't exist — it would require a vierbein formalism on causal sets (preliminary work by Dowker, Surya). Estimated difficulty: high.

**3. Whether Causal Set Actions Produce Quadratic Gravity**
*Does any causal set action have a continuum limit that includes the R² + C² terms of Stelle's quadratic gravity?*
The Benincasa-Dowker action recovers Einstein-Hilbert only. Eichhorn et al. (2023) found higher-order operators give R² − 2□R, not the needed R² + Weyl². If no causal set action produces quadratic gravity, the causal-order interpretation loses its foundational claim. This is a **falsifiable structural prediction** — currently unsupported. Estimated difficulty: moderate (well-defined calculation).

**4. Whether Entanglement Constraints Are Stronger Than Unitarity Alone**
*Does the nonlinear extension of MVEH uniquely select the fakeon prescription, or do all unitary prescriptions satisfy entanglement equilibrium?*
At linearized level, all ghost-removal prescriptions are indistinguishable. The entanglement approach gains independent content only if the nonlinear MVEH (beyond the linearization barrier of Bueno et al. 2017) imposes constraints beyond unitarity. This requires pushing the MVEH program past its current frontier. Estimated difficulty: very high.

**5. QFT Extension of Barandes-Doukas Indivisibility**
*Can the stochastic indivisibility framework be extended from non-relativistic QM to relativistic QFT, and if so, does it yield the fakeon prescription for negative-residue modes?*
Currently the framework works on finite configuration spaces without Lorentz invariance, renormalization, or path integrals. The connection to propagator pole prescriptions is conceptual, not mathematical. Estimated difficulty: very high (foundational gap).

## Part 5: Value to the QG+F Research Program

**The honest assessment:** Our work is **primarily interpretive, with one concrete mathematical observation**.

**What a QG+F researcher would gain from reading our findings:**

1. **The Wheeler propagator bridge (genuine value).** The tree-level identity G_fakeon = G_Wheeler, combined with the Belenchia et al. (2015) result that causal set dynamics produce G_Wheeler for unstable modes, is a **novel observation absent from the literature**. It connects two disconnected programs (causal sets and QG+F) through a concrete mathematical identity. A QG+F researcher would gain: awareness that the fakeon propagator emerges naturally from a discrete causal structure, suggesting the fakeon may have deeper geometric origins. This could motivate a short paper or letter.

2. **The analyticity sacrifice explanation (modest value).** The indivisibility interpretation (Exp. 004) provides the only physical explanation for WHY the S-matrix must sacrifice analyticity: the gravitational process carries irreducible multi-time correlations. A QG+F researcher would gain: a conceptual handle on an otherwise unexplained feature of the theory. This is philosophical value, not computational value.

3. **The entanglement self-consistency argument (framing value).** The MVEH-based argument (Exp. 005) places the fakeon within the emergent gravity paradigm: the fakeon isn't just "needed for unitarity" but "needed for gravity to exist as emergent from entanglement." A QG+F researcher working on connections to holography or entanglement gravity would gain: a bridge to their own research program.

**What our work does NOT provide:**
- No new predictions beyond QG+F
- No new calculations or mathematical tools
- No resolution of the loop-level problem
- No non-perturbative insight
- No help with QG+F's actual open problems (inflationary signatures, collider predictions, cosmological constant)

**Is this "philosophical decoration"?** Partially, yes. Two of the three approaches (indivisibility and entanglement) add interpretive framing without new mathematical content. They restate "unitarity requires the fakeon" in richer language. The causal-order approach comes closest to genuine derivation — it produces the specific propagator from causal structure — but fails at loop level.

**Final verdict:** Our work contributes a **landscape map** of the fakeon's possible physical interpretations, anchored by one genuinely novel mathematical observation (the Wheeler propagator bridge). It does not advance the computational frontier of QG+F. Its appropriate form is a review/perspective article connecting causal sets, stochastic indivisibility, and entanglement gravity to the fakeon prescription — not a research paper claiming new results. The Wheeler propagator bridge alone merits a focused short paper or letter.
