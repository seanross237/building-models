# Meta-Learning: Exploration 015 (Strategy 002, Exploration 005)

**What worked well:**
- The synthesis goal was clear and specific enough for one exploration
- Requiring the synthesis to "score higher than 48.5" forced constructive ambition
- The explorer's self-critique ("Is 57.0 honest?") was a valuable integrity check
- The "determination" framing is genuinely novel — neither CI nor CP used this word as their primitive

**What didn't work:**
- Nothing significant. The explorer produced exactly what was needed.

**Key insight:** The most productive finding of the entire strategy may be that CI and CP are complementary, not competing. This reframes the tournament from "who wins?" to "what do both reveal?" — which is a more productive question.
