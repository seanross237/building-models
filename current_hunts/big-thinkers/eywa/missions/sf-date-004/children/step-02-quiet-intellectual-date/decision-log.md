## 2026-03-31 Decision: execute-vs-plan

Type: execute-vs-plan
Options considered: execute, plan
Chosen: execute
Reasoning: This is a single-domain content creation task — designing a date itinerary for a specific personality type in a specific city. The goal is self-contained, success criteria are explicit (specific locations, timing, reasoning, narrative arc, 4-6 hours, step-by-step detail), and there's a clear path from start to finish. A plan would decompose into at most "research locations" and "write the itinerary," which is the over-splitting anti-pattern — just do it. No library knowledge is available to leverage. At depth 2, the guidance says lean toward planning, but only when the task has identifiable distinct sub-problems. This doesn't — it's a single creative synthesis task.
Confidence: high
What would change my mind: If the task required actual real-time verification of business hours/closures (which would need web research as a separate step), planning might help. But the task asks for a design detailed enough to follow, not a guaranteed-accurate reservation system. I'll note confidence levels on location details.
