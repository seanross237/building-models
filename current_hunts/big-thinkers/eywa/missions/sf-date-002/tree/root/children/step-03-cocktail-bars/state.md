# State — step-03-cocktail-bars

## What I decided and why
Executed directly rather than planning. This was a single-domain web research task with clear success criteria — no benefit from decomposition.

## What I considered and rejected
- **Planning into search → compile → rank steps:** Would have created trivially small children with no real independence. Over-splitting.
- **Including bars outside the "intimate" frame** (e.g., rooftop bars, wine bars, dive bars with charm): Goal specifically asked for cocktail bars with intimacy factor, so I stayed on scope.
- **Including Bourbon & Branch / Wilson & Wilson:** Classic speakeasy, but the strict 90-minute time limit and "no speaking loudly" rules make it feel more like a tourist experience than a natural date. Opted for Local Edition instead as the speakeasy representative.
- **Including The Snug or Casements:** Both came up in searches but had less distinctive profiles — good neighborhood bars without the standout features that justify a top-5 slot.

## Hunches
- Moongate Lounge and Valley Club are clearly a tier above for date-specific purposes. The rotating lunar menu at Moongate is a genuine differentiator — it gives couples a reason to return and creates natural conversation. Valley Club's "sexiest bar" framing from multiple publications is unusually consistent praise.
- Bar Iris might be underrated for dates — the walk-in-only policy removes friction, and the cocktail complexity is arguably #1 on the list. But the Russian Hill location is less atmospheric as a neighborhood than Chinatown or the Mission.
- Lone Palm's weekend crowding issue is a real concern. If this date is on a Friday/Saturday, it may not deliver on the intimacy promise.

## What surprised me
- Valley Club is very new (mid-2025) and already has strong consensus as best new bar. That's unusual — most bars take time to build reputation.
- Moongate's lunar-rotating menu is genuinely novel. I haven't seen that concept elsewhere.
- The price convergence at the top: Moongate, Valley Club, and Bar Iris are all in the same ~$18-22 range. The choice between them is really about atmosphere preference, not budget.

## Uncertainty
- Specific pricing for Moongate wasn't confirmed in exact dollar amounts — "adds up" suggests $18-22+ but could be higher.
- I didn't verify current hours or whether any of these have closed/changed since the articles were written. The data is from 2024-2026 sources, which should be current.
- Lone Palm's capacity and whether you can reliably get a table without a long wait on a given night.
