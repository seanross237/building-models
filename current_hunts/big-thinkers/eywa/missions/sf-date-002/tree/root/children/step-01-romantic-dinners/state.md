## Key Findings
1. **Nightbird** ranked #1 for romance — 10 tables, Michelin star, calm intimate atmosphere.
2. **Californios** ranked #2 — two Michelin stars, dramatic all-black room, $325/person Mexican tasting menu. Most prestigious but more "event" than "intimate."
3. **Empress by Boon** ranked #3 — best value ($118/person), stunning 6th-floor Chinatown views, but mixed reviews on consistency.
4. **Foreign Cinema** ranked #4 — most fun/unique (outdoor movie screenings), Mission District energy, but lively rather than quiet-romantic.
5. **Gary Danko** ranked #5 — classic institution, best service, but room has gotten louder and Wharf location lacks charm.

## Confidence
High. Multiple independent sources (Infatuation, Michelin Guide, OpenTable, Yelp, Timeout, food bloggers) converged on these five as the top romantic options. Pricing and accolades are well-documented. The ranking is my synthesis — reasonable people could reorder #3-5 based on what they value.
