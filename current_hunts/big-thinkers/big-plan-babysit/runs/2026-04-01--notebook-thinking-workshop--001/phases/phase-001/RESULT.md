# Result

## Audience

Internal product managers and designers responsible for notebook workflow decisions.

## Primary Use-Case

Use `Notebook Thinking` to turn scattered notes and observations into a shared decision memo that can guide the next workshop design step.

## Constraints

- the workshop must run in under `60 minutes`
- it must require only a notebook, a pen, and a facilitator
- it must produce a concrete written output by the end
- it must stay self-contained and avoid external dependencies

## Non-Goals

- not a general note-taking tutorial
- not a public-facing training or marketing event
- not a deep research-methods course
