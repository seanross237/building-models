# Plan V001

## Goal

Create a concise internal brief for a fictional workshop called `Notebook Thinking`.

## Phase 1

Lock audience, use-case, and constraints.

Acceptance criteria:

- one clear internal audience is named
- one primary use-case is named
- constraints and non-goals are explicit

## Phase 2

Define the core concept and promise.

Acceptance criteria:

- the workshop concept is clear and non-ambiguous
- the promise is one sentence
- the reason it matters internally is explicit

## Phase 3

Shape the smallest viable workshop format.

Acceptance criteria:

- a lightweight workshop spine exists
- timing, activities, and outputs are specified
- the format clearly follows from the audience and promise

## Phase 4

Draft the internal brief and run a plausibility check.

Acceptance criteria:

- the final brief includes audience, promise, workshop outline, and pitch
- the brief passes a self-check for clarity, feasibility, and internal fit
