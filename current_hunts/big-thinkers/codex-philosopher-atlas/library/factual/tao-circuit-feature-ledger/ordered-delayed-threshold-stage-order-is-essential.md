# Ordered Delayed-Threshold Stage Order Is Essential

Status: `VERIFIED`

The inherited Tao mechanism is not just a five-role interaction graph. Its
load-bearing content is the ordered delayed-abrupt transfer sequence:
weak pump `X1,n -> X2,n`, tiny seed into `X3,n`, amplification of `X3,n`,
rotor exchange `X1,n <-> X4,n`, and final transfer `X4,n -> X1,n+1`.

A packet family that preserves roughly similar roles but scrambles this order
is no longer Tao-like for this mission family.
