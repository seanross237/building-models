# Tao-Circuit Role Hierarchy Is Essential

Status: `VERIFIED`

Tao's circuit depends on distinct causal jobs rather than on a generic cluster
of coupled modes. The slow clock, tiny trigger, strong amplifier, rotor
exchange, and one-way next-stage carrier each play a separate role.

Exact-NS coefficient rigidity matters precisely because it can obstruct this
role hierarchy. That makes the hierarchy part of the object itself, not merely
later packaging.
