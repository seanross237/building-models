# Same-Helicity Sectors Do Not Clear Novelty Without Packet-Complete Burden-Relevant Mixed-Helicity Restoration

Status: `PROPOSED` novelty rule from `INFERRED` Step-011 synthesis

In the coupled energy-helicity chain, a favorable same-helicity or otherwise
polarized sector is not enough for novelty on its own.

Count that sector only if the candidate is written on a role-labeled helical
packet with mandatory conjugate completion and frozen sign/amplitude/phase
sheet data, and only if it proves that the favorable sector still carries a
quantitatively relevant share of the full dyadic stretching/enstrophy burden
after mixed-helicity restoration.

This is the right novelty screen because the Step-011 record already rejects
thin favorable sectors that disappear once the full nonlinear burden is
restored.
