# Support Means A Role-Labeled Helical Packet With Mandatory Conjugate Completion

Status: `VERIFIED` branch support with `INFERRED` Step-1 policy freeze

For this exact-support mission family, `support` should be frozen at packet
level rather than at single-mode level. The smallest honest object on this
record is a finite role-labeled helical packet together with the exact forced
conjugate completion needed to realize a real-valued velocity field.

A single Fourier mode is too small to carry the branch's pump/seed/amplifier
role bookkeeping honestly, while a bare conjugate pair is still only part of
the representation discipline rather than the full packet object. Later
candidates should therefore inherit packet-level helical support semantics
instead of renegotiating support object size ad hoc.
