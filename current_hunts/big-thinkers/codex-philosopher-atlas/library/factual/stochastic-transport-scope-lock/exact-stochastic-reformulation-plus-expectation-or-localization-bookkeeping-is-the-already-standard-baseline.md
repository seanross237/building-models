# Exact Stochastic Reformulation Plus Expectation Or Localization Bookkeeping Is The Already-Standard Baseline

Status: `VERIFIED` with `INFERRED` baseline summary

The repository's stochastic baseline is not a theorem-facing gain. It is the
availability of exact stochastic reformulation itself, possibly accompanied by
expectation-level, martingale, or localization bookkeeping.

The Step-012 sources say this several ways:
exact stochastic identities are common, exact reformulation by itself is
strategically insufficient, and a familiar failure pattern already exists in
which an elegant stochastic identity yields only expectation or localized
control and then fails to close at critical regularity.

So the already-standard benchmark on the local record is:
exact stochastic transport, circulation, or back-to-label reformulation,
possibly with conditional expectation or localized martingale bookkeeping, but
without one local or pathwise theorem-facing consequence that constrains the
named concentration or stretching burden.
