# Backward Stochastic Flow Back To Label Transport Is The Best Supported First Family

Status: `INFERRED` best-supported family with `VERIFIED` comparator support

For the Step-012 stochastic branch, the best-supported first family is
`backward stochastic flow / back-to-label transport`.

Why this family is the strongest honest first test:

- it is the only stochastic family on disk with an explicitly named prior-art
  neighborhood, namely `Constantin-Iyer`;
- it is also the family attached to the most concrete observable label in the
  planning archive;
- and nearby options such as stochastic Kelvin circulation or generic
  martingale language remain broader family labels rather than equally
  anchored comparator neighborhoods.

This is still only a provisional family freeze. The repository narrows toward
this family because it is the least rhetorical option on disk, not because it
has already earned a theorem-facing estimate.
