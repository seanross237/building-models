Status: `VERIFIED` with `INFERRED` consequence

only remaining family was the narrow pressure-Hessian / strain-defect line,
with harmonic-moment cancellation as its only serious subquestion.

Step-010 then screened the documented equivalent packagings of that family:

- the exact pressure relation `partial_i partial_j p = R_i R_j(u_i u_j)`;
- the stretching form `S omega . omega` together with the exact `S` /

- eigenvalue-gap or eigenframe-alignment packaging;

No packaging on the local record yields a packaging-independent,
estimate-level shrinkage of the same frozen full-pairing coefficient on
`I_p^far`.

So Step 10 admits no mechanism family for later tensor-package freeze. The
honest branch verdict is to stop at the repackaging gate rather than treating a
favored tensor decomposition as progress.
