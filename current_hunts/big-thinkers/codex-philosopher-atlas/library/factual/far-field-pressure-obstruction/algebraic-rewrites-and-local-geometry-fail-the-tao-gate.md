# Algebraic Rewrites And Local Geometry Fail The Tao Gate

Status: `VERIFIED` with `INFERRED` consequence

For this branch, two candidate routes fail the Tao gate:

- Exact algebraic rewrites of `u . grad u` do not act directly on the surviving
  affine-or-higher harmonic moments of `p_far`. Once the obstruction is encoded
  as the far-field pressure pairing, these rewrites remain identity-level
  repackagings unless they produce a new coefficient estimate.
- Local vorticity or strain geometry does not directly control the nonlocal
  exterior contribution that fixes `C_far`, so it cannot by itself neutralize
  the live far-field pairing.

The consequence is branch-specific: neither algebraic form nor local geometry
survives Step 1 as a live lever on the harmonic-tail pressure obstruction.
