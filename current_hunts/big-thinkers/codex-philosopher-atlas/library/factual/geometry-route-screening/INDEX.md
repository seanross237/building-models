# Geometry Route Screening

Reusable findings from the Step-1 benchmark and Tao-screen pass on the
post-pressure geometry branch.

## Entries

- `geometry-candidates-must-act-on-full-stretching.md`:
  the Tao screen only counts candidates that move a full
  `S omega . omega` contribution rather than a local geometric proxy.
- `direction-regularity-is-prior-art-not-a-standalone-novelty-claim.md`:
  direction-coherence criteria are benchmark material unless the branch adds a
  new NS-specific dynamic bridge to full stretching control; this entry now
  also freezes `Constantin-Fefferman / direction-regularity` as the primary
  comparator neighborhood.
- `criterion-input-is-the-frozen-gain-currency-on-the-criterion-first-geometry-branch.md`:
  once that comparator family is frozen, Step-015 progress must be measured in
  `criterion input` rather than in standalone proxy geometry language.
- `standalone-beltrami-alignment-collapses-to-a-fragility-screen.md`:
  local Beltrami language is not an operational route unless it controls full
  stretching rather than only local depletion or Lamb-vector structure.
- `tube-persistence-remains-live-only-as-a-dynamic-or-hybrid-route.md`:
  tube persistence survives the benchmark only as a dynamic ingredient, not as
  descriptive geometry.
- `direction-coherence-plus-tube-persistence-is-the-primary-step-2-survivor.md`:
  the only primary surviving ingredient family after the Tao screen is the
  transport-plus-coherence hybrid.
- `filament-or-tube-concentration-is-the-primary-step-3-scenario-for-the-live-hybrid.md`:
  Step 3 should test the live hybrid first in a filament/tube concentration
  setting because that is the repository's most direct match to the survivor.
- `sheet-or-pancake-concentration-is-a-step-3-comparator-not-a-co-primary.md`:
  sheet/pancake concentration remains useful only as a robustness comparator,
  not as the main operational home of the hybrid.
- `over-specific-tube-variants-are-too-weakly-supported-for-official-step-3-use.md`:
  thinner variants such as axial tube collapse are illustrative only and should
  not replace the broader scenario class yet.
- `step-3-should-start-with-a-neutral-eulerian-parabolic-localization-package.md`:
  Step 3 should begin from an Eulerian `B_r x [t_* - r^2, t_*]` package with
  `|omega| >= r^-2`, so tube persistence is tested rather than built into the
  coordinates.
- `the-primary-hybrid-is-dynamically-weak-under-the-fixed-eulerian-package.md`:
  under that fixed Eulerian package, the live hybrid remains conceptually
  relevant but lacks a concrete propagation or matching mechanism across the
  full diffusion-scale window.
- `direction-field-diffusion-burden-exceeds-the-inherited-step-3-package.md`:
  the direction half of the hybrid needs derivative-sensitive diffusion control
  beyond the inherited Step-3 threshold package, which is why dynamic
  coherence remains fragile.
- `fixed-package-makes-direction-coherence-informative-but-dynamically-weak.md`:
  under the fixed Step-3 package, standalone direction coherence stays
  stretching-facing but becomes only a weak comparator because localization
  persistence pulls in hidden `nabla xi` or threshold-boundary burdens.
- `fixed-package-makes-tube-persistence-static-without-relocalization.md`:
  under the same package, standalone tube persistence has no honest Eulerian
  transport law and collapses to descriptive geometry unless tube-adapted
  tracking is smuggled in.
- `neither-step-3-comparator-outperforms-the-direction-plus-tube-hybrid.md`:
  the fixed-package comparator audit sharpens why the hybrid still dominates
  its two standalone benchmarks.
- `all-audited-geometry-candidates-fail-before-step-4-representation-work.md`:
  once the fixed-package dynamic audits leave every candidate only dynamically
  weak or static-only, the geometry branch should stop before Step-4
  representation work.
- `the-live-geometry-claim-is-only-proxy-admissible-via-the-neutral-eulerian-intense-set-coherence-persistence-class.md`:
  Step-015 freezes the only remaining live geometry claim as proxy-based on a
  neutral-Eulerian intense-set coherence/persistence class; the corresponding
  theorem-facing sentence is only a provisional insertion line rather than an
  earned theorem statement.
- `the-geometry-branch-fails-at-the-theorem-floor-bridge-gate-before-package-selection.md`:
  because the proxy-to-theorem-floor bridge already looks theorem-scale, the
  branch should stop at theorem-floor obstruction before any further package
  choice.
