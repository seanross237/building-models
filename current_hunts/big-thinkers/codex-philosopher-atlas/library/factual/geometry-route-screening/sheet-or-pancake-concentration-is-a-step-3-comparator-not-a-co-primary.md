# Sheet Or Pancake Concentration Is A Step-3 Comparator, Not A Co-Primary

Status: `VERIFIED` with `INFERRED` consequence

`Sheet or pancake concentration` should be retained in Step 3 of the geometry
route, but only as an explicit comparator for the surviving hybrid

`direction coherence + tube persistence`

rather than as a co-primary scenario class.

The local record justifies that bounded role:

- the chain materials explicitly name sheet/pancake concentration as one of the
  concrete scenario classes that must be assessed;
- Step-3 reasoning says it is present mainly as a comparator demanded by chain
  discipline, not as the repository's favored home for the surviving hybrid;
- and the surviving Step-2 question is still specifically about persistence of
  direction coherence along a coherent tube family in a way that matters for
  full stretching.

So the comparator is useful for an honest reason: it tests whether the hybrid
really depends on tube-family persistence or whether it collapses into generic
direction regularity, generic concentration language, or other descriptive
geometry once it leaves the tube setting.

Its failure mode is correspondingly diagnostic. If the hybrid does not transfer
to the sheet/pancake comparator without degrading into prior-art direction
coherence or bare concentration language, the comparator exposes misfit rather
than supporting promotion to co-primary status.
