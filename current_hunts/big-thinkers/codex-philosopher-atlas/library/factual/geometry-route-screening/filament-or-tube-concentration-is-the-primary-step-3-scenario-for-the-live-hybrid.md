For Step 3 of the geometry route, the primary scenario class for the surviving
hybrid

`direction coherence + tube persistence`

between scenario class and survivor content:

- the chain materials require a concrete scenario class before observables are

- the Step-2 survivor was kept alive only as a persistence-heavy question about

should test whether direction coherence can persist along a concentrated
coherent tube family strongly enough to matter for full stretching, including
the exterior or inter-scale part, rather than merely preserve tube-shaped
imagery.

still collapse into descriptive tube language if only the core or self-induced
part improves while the exterior/inter-scale stretching contribution, or the
localization-evolution cost, stays uncontrolled.
