# Direction Coherence Plus Tube Persistence Is The Primary Step-2 Survivor

Status: `INFERRED`

After benchmarking prior-art overlap and applying the Tao screen, the only
primary surviving ingredient family for the geometry branch is the hybrid:

`direction coherence + tube persistence`

This hybrid survives because:

- direction coherence keeps the route pointed at stretching rather than at a
  purely descriptive geometry;
- tube persistence supplies the dynamic ingredient most plausibly destroyed by
  Tao-style averaging;
- and the combination is the only locally supported family that is both
  stretching-facing and not already collapsed into a prior-art criterion or a
  Beltrami fragility screen.

The consequence for later steps is narrow: Step 2 should fix scenario class and
localization around this hybrid first, while treating standalone direction
coherence and standalone tube language as comparators rather than primary
routes. The concrete test question is whether direction coherence can persist
along a coherent tube family strongly enough to make a full stretching
contribution, including the exterior or inter-scale part, smaller in one fixed
representation.
