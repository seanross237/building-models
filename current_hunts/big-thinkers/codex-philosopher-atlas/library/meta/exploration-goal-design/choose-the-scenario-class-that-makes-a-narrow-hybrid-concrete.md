# Choose The Scenario Class That Makes A Narrow Hybrid Concrete

When a branch already survives only as a narrow hybrid, choose the scenario
class that makes that exact hybrid operationally testable.

Do not promote alternative geometries into co-primary status just because they
are also named in the chain. Keep at most one explicit comparator when it helps
test whether the survivor is genuinely specific or merely a relabeling of
generic geometry language.

In Step 3 Exploration 001 this kept `filament or tube concentration` primary
for the hybrid `direction coherence + tube persistence` and preserved
`sheet/pancake concentration` only as a robustness check.
