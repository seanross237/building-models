# Exploration 002 Goal

## Objective

Screen the main equivalent tensor packagings for the surviving Step-10 family
menu and decide whether any candidate remains live against the frozen full
pairing `I_p^far`.

This is not Step 3 tensor-package freezing. The point is to test whether the
apparent leverage survives across equivalent representations, or collapses back
into known non-coercive pressure-Hessian, alignment, eigenframe, or reduced-
model language before the branch spends more effort on packaging choice.

## Required Questions

Answer all of the following from local repository materials:

1. For each serious family still live after overlap reduction, what are the
   main equivalent tensor packagings already documented on disk?
2. Which claims remain packaging-independent, and which are artifacts of one
   favored decomposition or wording?
3. Where does a candidate collapse into known non-coercive packaging:
   representation identity only,
   local geometry only,
   reduced-model / restricted-Euler flavor,
   conditional-regularity restatement,
   or another named failure mode?
4. After enforcing the frozen endpoint rule
   `smaller effective coefficient on the same full pairing or it does not count`,
   does any family still survive as a Step-10 admission candidate?
5. What is the most honest admission decision for the step:
   one admitted family with an explicit non-novelty risk,
   or no survivor because the route dies at the repackaging gate?

## Working Hypotheses To Test, Not Assume

- The only plausible survivor entering this screen is the narrow
  `pressure-Hessian / strain-defect` line, with `harmonic-moment cancellation`
  as its only non-cosmetic subquestion.
- Equivalent packagings likely wash out apparent novelty:
  the exact pressure relation, stretching language, eigenframe language, and
  restricted-Euler-style defect language are all vulnerable to becoming the
  same non-coercive family once measured against the frozen full-pairing
  coefficient.
- Unless a packaging-independent estimate-level gain on `I_p^far` is already
  visible in the record, Step 10 should end negatively at the repackaging
  gate.

Confirm, refine, or reject these hypotheses from source-backed evidence.

## Success Criteria

- Produce a compact packaging screen with columns:
  `family`, `main equivalent packagings`, `what survives across packagings`,
  `known non-coercive collapse`, `admit or reject`.
- Label major claims `[VERIFIED]`, `[INFERRED]`, or `[PROPOSED]`.
- End with one explicit admission decision only:
  one admitted family with a risk note,
  or `none survives`.
- If no family survives, say whether the first honest failure is
  `repackaging obstruction` or
  `known-family non-coercive against the frozen endpoint`.

## Failure Criteria

- The report chooses a favorite tensor package instead of screening packages.
- The report counts a cleaner representation as leverage without returning to a
  smaller coefficient on the same `I_p^far` pairing.
- The report lets a candidate survive by switching to a surrogate subpiece or
  by importing Step-3 observable selection too early.

If failure occurs, say so directly and recommend branch invalidation at the
repackaging gate.
