# Exploration 001 Goal

## Objective

Freeze the smallest serious theorem-endpoint menu for `step-009`.

This is not a tensor-observable shortlist and not a prior-art screen. The point
 is to decide, from the local repository, what exact theorem endpoint and
 contradiction line are actually live for the run-006 pressure-Hessian branch,
 and which candidates are only bottleneck themes or packaging variants.

## Required Questions

Answer all of the following from local repository materials:

1. What exact theorem endpoints or contradiction lines are still serious after
   `decision-013`, `decision-014`, and run `run-006`?
2. How should the branch's internal target menu
   `full stretching control`,
   `pressure-Hessian/strain defect control`,
   `cancellation of affine-and-higher harmonic moments`
   be interpreted:
   as genuine theorem endpoints,
   as distinct proof architectures,
   or as endpoint-adjacent bottleneck themes?
3. For each serious endpoint candidate, what would a positive deliverable
   literally look like:
   an inequality,
   a continuation criterion,
   or a defect identity with a named role in the endpoint?
4. Which candidate is best supported as the Step-1 freeze target, and which
   others should be rejected as endpoint drift, tensor packaging, or
   unsupported theorem language?
5. Does the local record force the endpoint back onto the inherited local-
   energy / epsilon-regularity contradiction architecture with a sharpened
   estimate on the live far-field pressure pairing, or is a different exact
   theorem endpoint genuinely better supported?

## Working Hypotheses To Test, Not Assume

- The three run-006 branch targets are not yet theorem endpoints; they are
  proof-architecture families until tied to one exact contradiction line.
- The strongest concrete endpoint on disk is still local-energy /
  epsilon-regularity style cash-out through the inherited bad-term ledger,
  especially the far-field pressure coefficient from `step-001`.
- A broad endpoint such as "full stretching control" is too vague to freeze at
  Step 1 unless it is translated into one exact inequality or criterion.
- Pressure-Hessian/strain defect language is only admissible if it acts on the
  same live endpoint quantity rather than on a repackaged surrogate.

Confirm, refine, or reject those hypotheses from source-backed evidence.

## Success Criteria

- Produce a compact endpoint menu with columns:
  `candidate`, `actual theorem role`, `literal cash-out`,
  `source support`, `survival or rejection note`.
- Label major claims `[VERIFIED]`, `[INFERRED]`, or `[PROPOSED]`.
- End with one primary endpoint recommendation only, or state plainly that no
  coherent endpoint setup exists.
- Keep the conclusion narrow enough that later work cannot silently redefine
  what counts as success.

## Failure Criteria

- The report treats bottleneck language as if it were itself a theorem
  endpoint.
- The report revives tensor packaging or observable talk before fixing the
  endpoint.
- The report invents a contradiction line not grounded in the local record.

If failure occurs, say so directly and recommend endpoint obstruction.
