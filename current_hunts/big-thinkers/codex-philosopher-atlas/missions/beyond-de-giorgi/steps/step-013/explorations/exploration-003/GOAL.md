# Exploration 003 Goal

## Objective

Freeze the anti-repackaging and prior-art gate for the active reformulation
audit, then decide whether the branch has earned continuation into Chain Step
2.

Tasks:

- define the exact three-way anti-repackaging classifier:
  `equivalent redistribution`,
  `ambiguous redistribution`,
  `potentially live mechanism change`;
- define the minimum evidence a later candidate packet must show to avoid
  immediate prior-art absorption;
- define the nearest-prior-art audit format naming the closest known
  reformulation and the exact claimed mathematical delta;
- decide whether the endpoint window, burden ledger, solution class, and
  localization regime are now concrete enough to proceed.

## Success Criteria

- later candidate packets can be screened quickly for prior-art absorption or
  identity-level redistribution;
- the step exits with one precise verdict:
  either `scope lock passed, proceed to candidate slate and localized equation audit`
  or `under-specified-ledger obstruction, branch should stop before candidate audit`.

## Failure Criteria

- the classifier cannot separate equivalent redistribution from a potentially
  live mechanism change;
- the prior-art audit remains too weak to expose absorbed reformulations;
- the final verdict outruns the precision of the frozen endpoint and ledger.
