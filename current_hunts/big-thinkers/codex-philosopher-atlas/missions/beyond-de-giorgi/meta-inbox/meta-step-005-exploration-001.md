# Meta-Learning: Step 005, Exploration 001

- The architecture choice only became honest once the prior negatives were
  treated as hard constraints rather than background color. In this mission,
  "best supported" and "least already closed" are both required.
- The most useful framing move was to distinguish:
  - already-killed De Giorgi pressure targets,
  - already-killed full-stretching geometry targets,
  - and the still-open local-energy cutoff-flux audit.
- For future strategizer goals in this mission family, it is worth naming the
  candidate architecture *and* the already-closed rival branches explicitly in
  the task. That reduces redundant exploration time.
