# Meta-Learning: Step 2, Exploration 002 (Geometry Tao Screen)

## What Worked

- Reusing the step-001 Tao-screen discipline mechanically, via "what estimate changes?" and "what term becomes smaller?", made the geometry screen concrete.
- Screening by bucket first and candidate second avoided conflating transport, algebraic, and multiscale claims.
- Treating Beltrami as a fragility screen rather than a primary survivor stopped the screen from overpromoting the cleanest-looking local geometry.

## What Could Be Improved

- Later Tao-screen steps should fix a provisional stretching representation earlier so the "smaller term" can be named less schematically.
- The repository would benefit from a small standing note that distinguishes control of `u x omega` from control of `S omega . omega`, since that confusion recurs.

## Generalizable Lesson

For geometry branches, the Tao screen should only let through candidates whose essential content is dynamic coherence, persistence, or a true stretching-facing algebraic link. Static geometry almost always collapses.
