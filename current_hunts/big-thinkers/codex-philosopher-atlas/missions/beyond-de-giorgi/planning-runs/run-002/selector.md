## Why These Three Make the Strongest Portfolio

The selected portfolio is Chain 01, Chain 02, and Chain 03.

Chain 01 stays first because it has the best expected value. It tests the cleanest remaining algebraic reserve path, front-loads the Tao screen, and still produces a sharp obstruction memo if every natural reformulation collapses back to the same estimate-level bottleneck.

Chain 02 stays second because it is the strongest remaining genuinely geometric branch and has a high-quality failure mode. Its structure is disciplined: it separates Beltrami alignment, direction coherence, and tube persistence, then forces an early confrontation with the full stretching term rather than allowing descriptive vortex language to substitute for control.

Chain 03 is selected third to preserve a true high-upside path and to maximize portfolio diversity. It is the only route that changes the game from local mechanism hunting to a global minimal-blowup/rigidity program. Even if it fails, the output is still useful because it should produce a compactness/rigidity obstruction map rather than a vague dead end.

## Why Chain 04 Was Not Selected

Chain 04 is serious and well-formed, but it is the least necessary addition to this three-chain portfolio. Relative to Chain 03, it adds less strategic diversity; relative to Chain 02, it partially overlaps the same broad NS-specific-structure territory through a different tensorial lens. Its likely failure output is still useful, but it is less portfolio-defining than the compactness-route obstruction map and less high-floor than Chains 01 and 02.

- Chain 01 supplies the highest-floor algebraic/estimate-first attack.
- Chain 02 supplies the highest-floor geometric/obstruction-first attack.
