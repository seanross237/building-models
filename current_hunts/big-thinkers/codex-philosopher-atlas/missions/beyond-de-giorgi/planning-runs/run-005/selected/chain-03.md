# Chain 03 - Tensor Stretching And Pressure-Hessian Route

## Central Premise

The exact coupling among strain `S`, vorticity `omega`, and the pressure Hessian `nabla^2 p` may contain NS-specific structure that scalar norm methods erase. This branch is live only if one tensor observable acts on the full stretching term `S omega . omega` and survives both nonlocal pressure debt and Tao-sensitivity screening.

## Verifiable Result Target

Either:

- one conditional tensor criterion, control lemma, or reduced model with a rigorous error bridge tied to the full stretching mechanism; or
- a calibrated obstruction memo showing that tensor structure remains informative but non-coercive.

## Why This Chain Is Meaningfully Different

This chain is tensorial and stretching-first. It does not assume blowup, does not work at triad-symbol level, and does not begin from coherent-structure geometry. It targets the exact local-to-nonlocal tensor mechanism directly.

## Ordered Steps

### Step 1 - Build an exact tensor ledger with frozen representations

- Depends on: none.
- Action: assemble the exact identities linking `S`, `omega`, and `nabla^2 p`, including the local-plus-harmonic pressure split.
- Action: fix one or two canonical stretching representations up front so later steps cannot switch coordinates opportunistically.
- Action: mark which terms are exact NS structure, which are generic Calderon-Zygmund packaging, and where nonlocal harmonic freedom remains.
- Expected output: a tensor ledger separating exact identities, representation choices, and nonlocal debt.
- Kill condition: if every candidate representation already collapses to generic singular-integral structure with no one-sided leverage target, stop and record a direct obstruction.

### Step 2 - Choose a bounded observable family under admissibility rules

- Depends on: Step 1.
- Action: shortlist at most three observables, for example eigenvalue-gap ratios, `omega`-to-eigenframe alignment, pressure-Hessian/strain misalignment, or a harmonic-Hessian defect quantity.
- Action: require each observable to have a concrete role in controlling full stretching, an explicit Tao discriminator, resistance to eigenvalue-degeneracy pathologies or a frame-invariant substitute, and dynamic meaning beyond static geometry.
- Action: reject observables that are only renamed versions of known alignment criteria or only self-induced/local fragments.
- Expected output: an observable audit table with scaling, Tao discriminator, degeneracy exposure, and intended leverage point.
- Kill condition: if no observable survives the admissibility screen, close the chain negatively.

### Step 3 - Stress-test Tao sensitivity and eigenframe stability before estimates

- Depends on: Step 2.
- Action: for each shortlisted observable, state exactly what should fail in Tao's averaged model: sign coupling, frame coherence, cancellation, or another named mechanism.
- Action: test whether the observable remains meaningful near repeated eigenvalues or can be reformulated invariantly.
- Action: remove any candidate whose Tao discriminator is post hoc or whose definition depends on unstable frame choices.
- Expected output: a Tao-and-degeneracy memo licensing or rejecting each observable before detailed control claims.
- Kill condition: if every survivor fails Tao sensitivity or frame stability, terminate with an obstruction memo.

### Step 4 - Test control of the full stretching mechanism

- Depends on: Step 3.
- Action: using the fixed representation from Step 1, test whether the lead observable controls the full `S omega . omega` term rather than a local, self-induced, or reduced-model fragment.
- Action: charge all nonlocal pressure-Hessian debt explicitly, including the harmonic far-field contribution and any freedom it retains.
- Action: classify outcomes as known open criterion, reduced-model-only control, faithful but nonclosing NS statement, or genuine new leverage.
- Expected output: a control-or-obstruction table saying exactly which bucket each candidate lands in and why.
- Kill condition: if every candidate lands outside genuine new leverage, end with a tensor obstruction memo.

### Step 5 - Promote only a non-circular survivor into proposition scale

- Depends on: Step 4.
- Action: if one candidate survives, translate it into a conditional criterion, control lemma, or reduced-model proposition with explicit assumptions and the first missing theorem.
- Action: benchmark the result against nearby literature so novelty and hardness are stated honestly.
- Expected output: one proposition-scale target or a calibrated negative diagnosis.
- Kill condition: if the bottleneck theorem is effectively the original regularity problem in disguise, downgrade the result back to obstruction.
