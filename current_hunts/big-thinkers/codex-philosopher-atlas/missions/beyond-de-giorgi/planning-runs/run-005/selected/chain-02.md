# Chain 02 - Helical-Triad Symbol Route

## Central Premise

If the true Navier-Stokes equations possess a structural property that Tao-style averaging destroys, the cleanest place to look is the exact Leray-projected quadratic symbol and its helical-triad interaction geometry. The branch is live only if one triad-level feature yields a concrete gain on one named bottleneck quantity after all summation and localization costs are charged.

## Verifiable Result Target

Either:

- one proposition-scale triad mechanism, with an exact interaction class, one bottleneck quantity, one measurable gain currency, and the first missing lemma; or
- a bounded obstruction memo showing that the helical-symbol structure becomes cosmetic after averaging, summation, or localization.

## Why This Chain Is Meaningfully Different

This chain is spectral and algebraic. It works at the level of exact Fourier interactions and Leray-symbol geometry rather than compactness, tensor criteria, or vortex-core geometry.

## Ordered Steps

### Step 1 - Fix the representation, comparison class, and gain currency

- Depends on: none.
- Action: choose one exact interaction representation: helical decomposition, triad symbol coefficients, and one admissible solution framework in which the quantities are defined.
- Action: choose one bottleneck target in advance, such as energy flux, enstrophy production, stretching transfer, or a rigidity quantity in a critical-element setting.
- Action: choose one gain currency in advance: sign, cancellation, coercivity, derivative count, or propagation-time improvement.
- Expected output: a representation memo fixing the symbolic language, the target bottleneck, and the gain currency.
- Kill condition: if no single representation and bottleneck can be fixed without switching architectures opportunistically, stop and write a setup obstruction.

### Step 2 - Build the exact triad-interaction ledger

- Depends on: Step 1.
- Action: decompose the NS nonlinearity into the chosen triad variables and record which sign, helicity, or resonance constraints are exact and which are only bookkeeping.
- Action: identify which parts depend on the exact Leray symbol and which parts a Tao-style averaged model could preserve at the same comparison level.
- Action: reject features that survive only as identity-level symmetries with no bottleneck relevance.
- Expected output: a triad ledger marking exact structure, Tao-sensitive structure, and generic quadratic leftovers.
- Kill condition: if every apparent special feature collapses to bookkeeping or to a property the averaged model can retain, end the chain negatively.

### Step 3 - Attach one surviving triad feature to one bottleneck theorem gap

- Depends on: Step 2.
- Action: choose at most one promoted triad mechanism and state exactly where it is supposed to act on the chosen bottleneck quantity.
- Action: specify whether the intended gain is local-in-frequency, sign-definite after summation, or conditional on one restricted triad family.
- Action: name the nearest prior-art family so the route does not quietly rediscover standard helical-decimation or shell-model observations.
- Expected output: an insertion memo from one triad feature to one theorem gap and one claimed gain.
- Kill condition: if the mechanism cannot be attached to a concrete theorem gap, demote it as descriptive and stop.

### Step 4 - Stress-test summation, localization, and robustness

- Depends on: Step 3.
- Action: charge the full cost of summing over triads, reintroducing physical-space localization if needed, and allowing non-favorable triads back into the picture.
- Action: test whether the claimed gain survives perturbation away from exactly favorable helicity or resonance classes.
- Action: classify failure as averaging-insensitive, summation collapse, localization collapse, or regime-only signal.
- Expected output: a bottleneck-interaction ledger saying whether the triad gain survives honest reconstruction of the full equation.
- Kill condition: if the gain disappears once full triad summation or localization is restored, terminate with an obstruction memo.

### Step 5 - Convert any survivor into proposition scale

- Depends on: Step 4.
- Action: if one mechanism survives, state a theorem-like conditional proposition naming the interaction class, the bottleneck quantity, the gain, and the first missing lemma.
- Action: if nothing survives, state the narrowest earned negative: exactly what the exact triad geometry buys and exactly where that benefit is lost.
- Expected output: either a proposition-scale helical route or a sharp symbol-level obstruction memo.
- Kill condition: if the final claim cannot be stated without vague references to "favorable triads" or "special cancellations," the chain has not earned promotion.
