# Chain 04 - Stochastic Circulation And Martingale Transport Route

## Central Premise

Viscosity does not erase transport structure completely; it replaces deterministic Kelvin/Cauchy laws with stochastic transport identities. This branch is live only if one stochastic circulation or martingale observable uses exact NS structure that Tao-style averaging destroys and converts it into a continuation or concentration-exclusion mechanism.

## Verifiable Result Target

Either:

- one conditional stochastic-transport criterion or contradiction line tied to a specific circulation, Cauchy, or martingale observable; or
- one obstruction memo showing that the stochastic representation is exact but non-coercive,
  incompatible with the needed solution class,
  or analytically no stronger than deterministic energy-plus-harmonic-analysis control.

## Why This Chain Is Meaningfully Different

This chain is viscous-Lagrangian and stochastic. It does not study deterministic deformation alone, and it does not try to win by changing variables in the Eulerian PDE.

## Ordered Steps

### Step 1 - Freeze one stochastic representation and one endpoint

- Depends on: none.
- Action: choose one exact representation family:
  stochastic Kelvin circulation,
  stochastic Cauchy formula,
  backward stochastic flow,
  or a martingale adapted to vorticity/stretching.
- Action: choose one endpoint:
  continuation criterion,
  local concentration exclusion,
  or a contradiction line for blowup concentration.
- Expected output: a setup memo naming the stochastic object, filtration level, solution framework, and endpoint.
- Kill condition: if the representation is only available far above the intended regularity class, stop and write a solution-class obstruction.

### Step 2 - Derive the exact stochastic identity and defect structure

- Depends on: Step 1.
- Action: write the exact martingale or stochastic transport identity, including drift, quadratic variation, localization terms, and any pressure dependence.
- Action: separate exact stochastic structure from merely formal probabilistic packaging.
- Expected output: a formula sheet with one promoted observable and one defect ledger.
- Kill condition: if the stochastic representation adds no inequality-level content beyond rewriting the deterministic equation, close the chain negatively.

### Step 3 - Apply the Tao screen to the adapted stochastic backbone

- Depends on: Step 2.
- Action: state exactly what Tao-style averaging is supposed to destroy:
  adapted composition along stochastic trajectories,
  exact circulation structure,
  or one martingale cancellation tied to the true NS nonlinearity.
- Action: reject candidates whose Tao distinction is only that they sound less harmonic-analytic.
- Expected output: a Tao-comparator memo attached to the actual stochastic observable.
- Kill condition: if the same stochastic argument would survive unchanged for an averaged quadratic model, terminate with a Tao-insensitivity obstruction.

### Step 4 - Write one endpoint-facing probabilistic inequality or contradiction

- Depends on: Step 3.
- Action: convert the stochastic observable into one explicit theorem-facing line:
  a martingale bound,
  a conditional continuation implication,
  or a concentration-exclusion estimate.
- Action: charge optional stopping, localization, and regularity debt immediately.
- Expected output: an insertion memo with one precise probabilistic burden reduction.
- Kill condition: if no endpoint-facing probabilistic statement can be written without importing a much stronger framework, stop and record an endpoint obstruction.

### Step 5 - State the narrowest earned claim

- Depends on: Step 4.
- Action: if positive, write one proposition-scale conditional statement with the stochastic observable, exact gain, and first missing lemma.
- Action: if negative, identify the first honest failure:
  representation-only,
  non-coercive martingale,
  solution-class mismatch,
  or Tao-compatible stochastic packaging.
- Expected output: either a stochastic-transport attack path or a calibrated obstruction memo.
- Kill condition: if the final claim reduces to "there is stochastic structure in NS," the branch has not earned promotion.
