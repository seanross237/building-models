# Chain 02 - Helical-Triad Symbol Depletion Route

## Central Premise

If the true Navier-Stokes equations possess a decisive structure that Tao-style averaging destroys, the sharpest candidate is the exact Leray-projected triad symbol and its helical interaction geometry. This route is live only if one triad-level feature yields a measurable gain on one frozen bottleneck quantity after honest shell summation and any required physical-space reconstruction.

## Verifiable Result Target

Either:

- one proposition-scale triad mechanism with an exact interaction class, one bottleneck quantity, one gain currency, and the first missing lemma; or
- a bounded obstruction memo showing that the exact triad geometry becomes cosmetic after summation, non-favorable triads, or localization are restored.

## Why This Chain Is Meaningfully Different

This chain is frequency-space and algebraic. It does not work through pressure-Hessian tensor observables, weak-type endpoint norms, or geometric localization packages.

## Ordered Steps

### Step 1 - Freeze the symbolic language, target bottleneck, and gain currency

- Depends on: none.
- Action: choose one exact representation in advance:
  helical decomposition,
  triad-symbol coefficients with Leray projection,
  or a closely equivalent Fourier interaction package.
- Action: choose one bottleneck quantity in advance, such as shell-to-shell energy flux, enstrophy production, or the stretching transfer in one dyadic interaction ledger.
- Action: choose one gain currency in advance:
  sign,
  cancellation after summation,
  coercive depletion,
  or a derivative-count improvement.
- Expected output: a representation memo fixing one symbolic package, one bottleneck quantity, and one gain currency.
- Kill condition: if the branch cannot freeze one target without drifting across architectures opportunistically, stop and record a setup obstruction.

### Step 2 - Build the exact triad ledger and the Tao comparator

- Depends on: Step 1.
- Action: decompose the NS nonlinearity into the chosen helical or triad variables and mark which sign, helicity, resonance, or angle restrictions are exact.
- Action: identify which features genuinely rely on the exact NS symbol and which ones a Tao-style averaged model could preserve at the same comparison level.
- Action: reject features that are bookkeeping symmetries with no frozen-bottleneck role.
- Expected output: a triad ledger separating exact structure, Tao-sensitive structure, and generic quadratic leftovers.
- Kill condition: if every attractive feature is Tao-robust or merely representational, terminate with a symbol-level obstruction.

### Step 3 - Attach one promoted triad feature to one theorem gap

- Depends on: Step 2.
- Action: choose at most one promoted mechanism, for example a helical-sign class, a restricted interaction geometry, or a depletion effect on a specific transfer channel.
- Action: state exactly where it is supposed to act on the frozen bottleneck quantity and whether the gain is local-in-frequency, shell-averaged, or conditional on one interaction family.
- Action: benchmark the idea against nearby prior-art families so the branch does not rediscover shell-model or helical-decimation rhetoric.
- Expected output: an insertion memo from one triad feature to one theorem gap and one claimed gain.
- Kill condition: if the mechanism cannot be attached to a concrete theorem gap, demote it as descriptive and stop.

### Step 4 - Stress-test shell summation, bad triads, and localization debt

- Depends on: Step 3.
- Action: restore the full triad sum and charge the effect of non-favorable helical classes, nonlocal triads, and any shell multiplicity.
- Action: if the mechanism needs a physical-space statement, charge the reconstruction and localization debt explicitly rather than importing a new architecture for free.
- Action: classify failure precisely as averaging-insensitive, shell-summation collapse, bad-triad contamination, or localization collapse.
- Expected output: a reconstruction ledger saying whether the claimed gain survives honest return to the full equation.
- Kill condition: if the gain disappears once full summation or localization is restored, stop with an obstruction memo.

### Step 5 - Promote only a proposition-scale survivor

- Depends on: Step 4.
- Action: if one mechanism survives, state a proposition-scale target naming the interaction class, bottleneck quantity, gain, and first missing lemma.
- Action: if nothing survives, state the narrowest earned negative:
  exact symbol geometry buys a local signal but not a full-shell gain,
  or it is erased by summation/localization.
- Expected output: either a proposition-scale triad route or a sharp symbol-level obstruction memo.
- Kill condition: if the final statement still relies on vague language such as "favorable triads" or "special cancellations" without a frozen interaction class, the chain has not earned promotion.
