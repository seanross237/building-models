# Chain 01 - Pressure-Hessian Tensor Route With Harmonic-Moment Accounting

## Central Premise

The exact tensor coupling among strain `S`, vorticity `omega`, and pressure Hessian `nabla^2 p` may contain an NS-specific mechanism that scalar norm methods erase. A live route must act on the full stretching quantity `S omega . omega` or on the affine-and-higher harmonic moments that survived the far-field audit, not on a local surrogate or a prettier pressure representation.

## Verifiable Result Target

Either:

- one proposition-scale tensor mechanism, criterion, or control lemma tied to the full stretching term or the surviving harmonic-moment forcing; or
- a calibrated obstruction memo showing that pressure-Hessian structure is informative but non-coercive once full nonlocal debt is charged.

## Why This Chain Is Meaningfully Different

This chain is tensorial and pressure-structural. It does not assume blowup, does not work through helical triads, does not rely on weak-type endpoint machinery, and does not begin from tube geometry.

## Ordered Steps

### Step 1 - Freeze one tensor representation and one bottleneck target

- Depends on: none.
- Action: choose one fixed representation package for the branch, for example the stretching equation with `S omega . omega` plus the exact relation between `S` and `nabla^2 p`, together with the local-plus-harmonic pressure split.
- Action: choose one frozen target quantity in advance:
  full stretching control,
  pressure-Hessian/strain defect control,
  or cancellation of the affine-and-higher harmonic moments isolated in the far-field audit.
- Action: choose one gain currency in advance:
  coercivity,
  sign,
  cancellation of a specific moment family,
  or a smaller nonlocal remainder.
- Expected output: a representation memo naming one tensor package, one bottleneck quantity, and one gain currency.
- Kill condition: if every reasonable representation collapses immediately to generic Calderon-Zygmund packaging with no target-specific leverage, stop and write a setup obstruction.

### Step 2 - Build a frame-invariant observable table before estimating anything

- Depends on: Step 1.
- Action: shortlist at most three observables that are meaningful on the chosen package, such as pressure-Hessian/strain misalignment, eigenvalue-gap or invariant combinations of `S`, `omega`-to-eigenframe alignment expressed in a degeneracy-robust way, or a restricted-Euler defect quantity with an explicit remainder.
- Action: require each candidate to be frame-invariant or explicitly robust near repeated eigenvalues.
- Action: record for each candidate the exact Tao discriminator, the intended leverage point on the frozen bottleneck, and the nearest prior-art overlap.
- Expected output: an observable audit table with definability, invariant formulation, Tao sensitivity, and intended leverage.
- Kill condition: if no observable survives definability, degeneracy robustness, and a non-cosmetic link to the frozen bottleneck, terminate with an observable obstruction.

### Step 3 - Charge the full nonlocal debt, including harmonic moments

- Depends on: Step 2.
- Action: test whether the lead observable controls the full `S omega . omega` term or the actual affine-and-higher harmonic moments from the old far-field branch, rather than only a local or self-induced part.
- Action: split local and harmonic pressure contributions explicitly and record exactly where exterior strain freedom or harmonic freedom remains.
- Action: reject any candidate that only closes on a reduced model, a self-induced core term, or a renormalized local piece while the live nonlocal remainder stays free.
- Expected output: a nonlocal-debt ledger marking local control, exterior freedom, harmonic-moment action, and any remaining uncancelled term.
- Kill condition: if every candidate loses at the exterior or harmonic-moment stage, stop and write a nonlocal-obstruction memo.

### Step 4 - Stress-test Tao sensitivity and packaging robustness

- Depends on: Step 3.
- Action: test whether the promoted tensor mechanism is tied to the exact NS pressure/stress structure or whether an averaged quadratic model could preserve it at the same level.
- Action: check equivalent tensor packagings so the gain is not an artifact of one favorable decomposition.
- Action: remove any mechanism that survives only as a representation convenience rather than an operative NS-specific feature.
- Expected output: a robustness memo with one promoted mechanism and one explicit Tao verdict.
- Kill condition: if the mechanism is packaging-dependent or Tao-compatible in the relevant sense, downgrade to obstruction.

### Step 5 - Promote only an earned survivor

- Depends on: Step 4.
- Action: if one mechanism survives, state a proposition-scale target naming the observable, the tensor package, the bottleneck term, the exact gain, and the first missing lemma.
- Action: if nothing survives, state the narrowest earned obstruction:
  no invariant observable,
  no full-stretching control,
  no harmonic-moment action,
  or no Tao-sensitive tensor lever.
- Expected output: either a proposition-scale tensor route or a precise obstruction memo.
- Kill condition: if the final claim requires hiding the whole regularity problem inside one unlabeled tensor estimate, narrow it back to obstruction.
