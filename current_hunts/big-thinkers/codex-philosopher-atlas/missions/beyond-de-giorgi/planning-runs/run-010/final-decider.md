- **Highest presentable-result odds.** Its refined judgment gives it the best floor at `0.78`, and that floor is credible because the chain now produces a bounded obstruction memo, a hybrid handoff, or a live geometry packet without needing a heroic positive outcome.

The selector originally ranked the adapted geometric route first, and the refined judgment strengthens rather than weakens that choice.
