# Chain 03 - Pressure-Tensor Moment Route

## Central Premise

The harmonic-tail pressure-localization branch is dead, but it died leaving one narrow survivor: the exact pressure-Hessian and strain structure may still act on the remote-shell moments that generate the affine-or-higher harmonic tail, or on the full stretching mechanism, in a way generic harmonic regularity and Calderon-Zygmund bounds do not detect.

## Verifiable Result Target

Either:

- a conditional cancellation, rigidity statement, or reduced criterion phrased in pressure-Hessian, strain, or remote-shell moment variables; or
- a bounded negative result showing that exact tensor structure still does not shrink the decisive remote moments or control the full stretching term.

## Why This Chain Is Meaningfully Different

This chain is pressure-adjacent and tensorial. It does not reopen generic harmonic localization, and it is not just another general nonlinear rewrite. Its core objects are `nabla^2 p`, `S`, remote-shell moments, and their exact coupling.

## Ordered Steps

### Step 1 - Fix one exact tensor target and front-load the Tao screen

- Depends on: none.
- Action: choose one fixed target: either the remote-shell moment family behind the surviving far-field obstruction, or one fixed representation of full stretching `S omega . omega`.
- Action: state in advance what a genuine gain would mean for that target.
- Action: separate exact tensor content from facts that are only generic CZ or harmonic-analysis repackaging.
- Expected output: a target memo naming the tensor quantity, the precise gain criterion, and the candidate Tao discriminator.
- Kill condition: if the chain cannot name a tensor effect sharper than generic elliptic repackaging, stop immediately.

### Step 2 - Compile the exact pressure-strain identity package

- Depends on: Step 1.
- Action: assemble the exact relations linking `partial_i partial_j p`, `S`, `omega`, and the nonlinearity, including any moment formulas relevant to remote-shell forcing.
- Action: separate identities that are exact NS structure from those that survive unchanged in the averaged setting.
- Expected output: an identity sheet marking exact formulas, nonlocal terms, and the first possible rigidity handles.
- Kill condition: if every candidate handle collapses to a generic singular-integral restatement, terminate with an obstruction memo.

### Step 3 - Stress-test candidate tensor mechanisms against the actual adversary

- Depends on: Step 2.
- Action: test whether each candidate controls the remote-shell moments or the full stretching expression, not merely a local-looking or already-killed piece.
- Action: use the remote-shell perspective from `step-001` and treat nonlocal contribution as the main adversary.
- Expected output: a lemma sheet or obstruction table stating exactly which tensor terms are controlled and which decisive remainder remains free.
- Kill condition: if every candidate improves only a surrogate while leaving the actual remote moment or full stretching burden unchanged, stop and write the negative result.

### Step 4 - Run robustness and averaging checks

- Depends on: Step 3.
- Action: test whether any surviving tensor mechanism depends on exact NS structure in a way the averaged model should destroy.
- Action: record whether the mechanism is stable across reasonable reformulations of the same tensor target.
- Expected output: a robustness memo classifying the survivor as NS-specific, Tao-blocked, or representation-fragile.
- Kill condition: if the candidate survives unchanged in the averaged model or only exists in one contrived representation, close the chain negatively.

### Step 5 - Convert the survivor into a criterion or obstruction memo

- Depends on: Step 4, or directly on Step 3 if the chain dies there.
- Action: turn the best surviving tensor statement into a conditional criterion, reduced model, or narrowly earned negative claim.
- Expected output: either a pressure-tensor attack path with a named next theorem target, or a polished obstruction memo showing why exact tensor structure still fails to separate NS operationally from averaged NS.
- Kill condition: if the final claim outruns the exact tensor family and target actually audited, narrow it until it is fully earned.
