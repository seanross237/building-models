# Chain 02 - Calibrated Triad/Helical Audit

## Central Premise

Exact Navier-Stokes helical and triadic interaction geometry is worth pursuing only if it yields an operational discriminator that the averaged model does not retain after summation, localization, and basic dynamic testing. If that stronger standard fails, the chain should end in a calibrated obstruction memo rather than in more spectral folklore.

## Verifiable Result Target

Produce one of the following:

- an NS-only sign, forbidden-transfer rule, or coercive cancellation that survives the chosen summation and localization protocol;
- a conditional scenario exclusion or conditional estimate based on a hybrid mechanism combining triad geometry with localization or concentration structure;
- or a calibrated negative result showing that the most plausible helical and triadic discriminators do not survive the operational bridge to regularity-relevant control.

## Why This Chain Is Meaningfully Different

This remains the Fourier-interaction route, but it is no longer allowed to win on coefficient asymmetry alone. It must compare exact NS structure against the averaged model at a fixed level of description and then test whether the difference survives:

- summation over admissible configurations,
- localization or packetization losses,
- and relevance along a blowup-facing scenario.

## Ordered Steps

### Step 1 - Benchmark prior art and fix the comparison protocol

- Depends on: none.
- Action: benchmark the candidate space against known helical and triadic literature, including helicity-selection effects, Waleffe-style taxonomy, and known spectral depletion heuristics.
- Action: specify the exact level of comparison to the averaged model: pointwise symbol, triad family, orientation-averaged statistic, or localized transfer form.
- Action: define what would count as an operational difference rather than mere nonidentity of coefficients.
- Expected output: a benchmark memo listing what is already known, what is genuinely untested, and what comparison notion will be used throughout the chain.
- Kill condition: if no coherent comparison protocol can be fixed, or if every candidate discriminator is already known to be descriptive-only at that comparison level, stop and write the obstruction memo.

### Step 2 - Fix one blowup-facing scenario, localization regime, and target observable

- Depends on: Step 1.
- Action: choose one concrete adverse scenario, such as a comparable-scale cascade packet or a nonlocal feeding mechanism into a critical quantity.
- Action: decide whether the mechanism is supposed to come from one triad family or from a controlled cloud of nearby configurations.
- Action: fix the localization regime in advance: frequency band, spatial or packet localization, time window, and the regularity-facing quantity to be affected.
- Action: define what would count as a usable gain only after these choices are fixed.
- Expected output: a scenario sheet naming the scenario class, localization protocol, target quantity, and success criterion.
- Kill condition: if the target quantity still reads as generic spectral bookkeeping once the scenario and localization regime are fixed, stop.

### Step 3 - Derive exact NS-versus-averaged discriminators at the chosen level

- Depends on: Step 2.
- Action: compute the exact NS interaction coefficients and the averaged-model counterpart at the fixed level of comparison.
- Action: test a bounded shortlist of candidate discriminators, including pure helical or triad mechanisms and hybrid mechanisms that already encode localization sensitivity.
- Action: for each candidate, ask whether it yields a sign, monotonicity, forbidden transfer, or quantitatively stronger cancellation after summing the admissible configurations relevant to the chosen scenario.
- Expected output: a discriminator table stating for each candidate whether the difference is pointwise only, survives summation, or already looks operationally promising.
- Kill condition: if every difference is only coefficient-level nonidentity with no summed consequence, close the chain as Tao-blocked at this resolution.

### Step 4 - Run the localization and summation stress test early

- Depends on: Step 3.
- Action: translate each surviving candidate into the chosen localized or packetized setting before investing in further symbolic development.
- Action: record phase loss, commutator costs, overlap losses, and any ambiguity created by the localization protocol.
- Action: separate three outcomes:
  - survives as a usable localized estimate,
  - survives only as a descriptive spectral identity,
  - or collapses under localization.
- Expected output: a feasibility memo showing exactly which candidate mechanisms survive contact with localization and why.
- Kill condition: if a candidate lives only in global Fourier space and dies under reasonable localization choices, classify it as descriptive and stop pursuing it as a positive route.

### Step 5 - Test dynamic persistence in the chosen scenario

- Depends on: Step 4.
- Action: ask whether the surviving discriminator or hybrid observable can remain relevant along a near-singular evolution in the chosen scenario class.
- Action: record any transport structure, diffusion loss, time-averaging requirement, or conditional persistence assumption needed to keep the mechanism alive.
- Action: reject mechanisms that depend on one-time configuration geometry with no plausible persistence story.
- Expected output: a dynamic plausibility note labeling each surviving candidate as persistent, conditionally persistent, or transient-only.
- Kill condition: if no surviving candidate has even a conditional persistence story tied to the chosen scenario, stop and write the obstruction memo.

### Step 6 - Close with either a narrow positive route or a calibrated obstruction

- Depends on: Step 5.
- Action: promote a candidate only if it survives all four filters:
  - coherent NS-versus-averaged comparison,
  - summed triad-family consequence,
  - localization robustness,
  - and dynamic plausibility.
- Action: if promotion fails, write the negative result at the right scope: not merely that one pretty coefficient identity failed, but that a specific class of helical or triad strategies fails under the audited protocol.
- Expected output: either a mission-ready conditional proposition or a calibrated obstruction memo about Fourier-structure routes beyond Tao.
- Kill condition: if the final claim extends beyond the audited comparison level, scenario class, or localization regime, narrow it until it is fully earned.
