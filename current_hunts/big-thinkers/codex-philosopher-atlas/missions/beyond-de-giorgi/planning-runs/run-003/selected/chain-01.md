# Chain 01 - Exact-Reformulation Bottleneck Audit

## Central Premise

If true Navier-Stokes has a beyond-De-Giorgi escape in the spirit of SQG, it has to show up as an estimate-level gain on one named localized interaction, not as a prettier identity. This chain tests a short list of exact reformulations of the NS nonlinearity against one fixed bottleneck term.

## Verifiable Result Target

Either:

- one concrete reformulation that improves the operative localized interaction in a way that clears the Tao screen; or
- a bounded negative result showing that the main natural reformulations collapse back to the same estimate-level obstruction except in regimes better treated as geometry or tensor branches.

## Why This Chain Is Meaningfully Different

This chain is the only portfolio member centered on local estimate-level gains from exact nonlinear rewrites. It is not a helical-triad audit, not a pressure-tensor route, not a global compactness route, and not a geometry-package experiment.

## Ordered Steps

### Step 1 - Fix the exact bottleneck term and the admissible notion of gain

- Depends on: none.
- Action: choose one localized or truncated interaction that would actually need to improve for mission-relevant progress.
- Action: define in advance what counts as a real gain: derivative reduction, improved integrability, or a closure improvement strong enough to change the local balance.
- Action: front-load the Tao screen by naming which exact NS feature each candidate reformulation would use.
- Expected output: a screening memo naming the bad term, the allowed meaning of "gain," and a shortlist of at most three reformulations.
- Kill condition: if no candidate is both estimate-relevant and plausibly nontrivial under the Tao filter, stop immediately and write the obstruction memo.

### Step 2 - Test the shortlisted reformulations at the estimate level

- Depends on: Step 1.
- Action: derive the actual localized estimate produced by each surviving reformulation.
- Action: test natural candidates such as Lamb-vector or projected form, vorticity transport form, and one additional tightly justified rewrite acting on the same bottleneck.
- Action: reject identity-level simplification as progress unless the localized estimate itself improves.
- Expected output: an estimate table stating, for each candidate, the rewritten bad term, where the localization cost lands, and whether any gain is real or cosmetic.
- Kill condition: if every candidate returns the same quadratic or Calderon-Zygmund structure at the estimate level, terminate with a negative result and do not proceed.

### Step 3 - Run contamination screens before promoting any gain

- Depends on: Step 2.
- Action: test whether an apparent gain is really pressure-localization nostalgia, near-Beltrami geometry, or another effect better treated in a different branch.
- Action: require the candidate to survive modest perturbation away from any special alignment regime.
- Expected output: a robustness memo classifying each surviving signal as genuinely algebraic, geometry-dependent, pressure-tensor dependent, or illusory.
- Kill condition: if every apparent gain is actually a geometry-only or tensor-only effect, hand the signal off to the appropriate branch and close this chain negatively.

### Step 4 - Convert any surviving gain into a concrete non-De-Giorgi attack path

- Depends on: Step 3.
- Action: translate the surviving estimate into a concrete proposition, closure criterion, or next theorem target.
- Action: require the proposed route to improve the operative mechanism itself rather than re-enter the exhausted De Giorgi architecture under new names.
- Expected output: a draft proposition with explicit hypotheses, the improved estimate, and the first missing lemma.
- Kill condition: if the gain does not materially change the closure mechanism, narrow the output to a bounded negative claim and stop.

### Step 5 - Close with the strongest calibrated claim

- Depends on: Step 4, or directly on Step 2 or Step 3 if the chain dies earlier.
- Action: state the narrowest conclusion supported by the tested reformulations and the Tao screen.
- Expected output: either a concrete follow-on target defined by a specific improved estimate, or a polished negative memo that natural exact reformulations do not produce an SQG-like gain on the named NS bottleneck.
- Kill condition: if the final claim is broader than the reformulation family actually tested, narrow it until it is fully earned.
