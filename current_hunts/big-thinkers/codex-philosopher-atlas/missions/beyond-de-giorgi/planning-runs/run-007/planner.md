## Portfolio Summary

### Chain 01 - Exact-NS Versus Averaged-NS Structural Property Atlas

Central premise: the mission may be closer to a publishable property map than to a single proof attack. The first admissible branch is therefore a direct structural comparison that ranks only those exact NS properties that are both truly destroyed by averaging and operational inside a named proof endpoint.

Why it belongs now: prior runs have already eliminated several attractive but Tao-compatible packages. A fresh wide funnel should start by building the updated map explicitly rather than letting each branch rediscover the same exclusions.

### Chain 02 - Material Stretching And Deformation-Tensor Route

Central premise: the exact NS transport/stretching law may be more usable in material variables than in Eulerian estimate form. A live route would act through cumulative deformation, singular-value growth, or strain amplification along trajectories, not through local coefficient improvement.

Why it belongs now: this is transport-first without reverting to the dead neutral tube package. It asks whether the missing structure is the exact material amplification law itself.

### Chain 03 - Joint Energy-Helicity Flux Constraint Route

Central premise: the real NS nonlinearity moves both energy and helicity, and the joint transfer geometry may be stronger than the energy-only or triad-only pictures already audited. A live mechanism would have to survive full mixed-helicity restoration and yield a theorem-relevant depletion or no-bad-cascade statement.

Why it belongs now: it is the cleanest untouched invariant route that is neither the dead LEI rewrite audit nor the old symbol-first triad ledger.

### Chain 04 - Stochastic Circulation And Martingale Transport Route

Central premise: viscosity does not erase transport structure completely; it converts Kelvin/Cauchy-style identities into stochastic transport statements. If Tao-style averaging destroys the adapted stochastic backbone, this could be an NS-specific route unavailable to purely harmonic-analysis methods.

Why it belongs now: earlier Lagrangian and geometry branches were broad or tube-focused. This chain isolates the genuinely viscous transport representation instead of treating it as one item in a mixed bucket.

## Major Differences Between Chains

- Chain 01 is comparison-first and architecture-ranking. It outputs a structural property atlas rather than a direct proof attempt.
- Chain 02 is deterministic and material. It studies deformation and cumulative stretch along trajectories rather than region geometry or tensor eigenframes.
- Chain 03 is invariant and cascade-facing. It tests the coupled energy-helicity budget rather than symbol-level triad exactness alone.
- Chain 04 is stochastic and representation-based. It asks whether martingale transport identities create proof leverage unavailable to deterministic Eulerian packages.
