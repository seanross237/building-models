## Canonical Packet-Language Memo

- `[INFERRED]` **Packet object.**
  The branch will count a packet object as a finite role-labeled helical packet,
  not as a single bare Fourier mode and not as a bare conjugate pair.
- `[INFERRED]` **Support semantics.**
  `support` means the role-labeled helical packet together with the exact
  forced conjugate completion required to realize a real-valued velocity field.
- `[INFERRED]` **Conjugate completion.**
  Real-valuedness forces conjugate completion at the canonical representation
  level, but conjugate completion is not the primary notion of packet identity.
  Packet identity lives at the role-labeled helical-packet level.
- `[VERIFIED]` **Why not single-mode semantics.**
  The local record explicitly says exact NS bookkeeping depends on more than a
  support graph: conjugate mirrors, full `p+q=k` closure, helical signs, and
  exact projection all matter; sources:
  `atlas-anatomy-exploration-002-REPORT.md`, `judgments/chain-01.md`,
  `planner.md`.
- `[INFERRED]` **Canonicalization policy.**
  Later robustness audits must fix in advance:
  one conjugate-representative convention,
  one helical basis convention,
  one role-label ordering,
  one amplitude normalization anchor,
  and one phase anchor or equivalent canonical gauge.
- `[INFERRED]` **Cosmetic representation changes.**
  Once that policy is fixed, reordering labels or displaying the mandatory
  conjugate packet explicitly versus implicitly is cosmetic.
- `[VERIFIED]` **Substantive modeling changes.**
  Packet regrouping, switching support semantics, retuning time windows or
  thresholds, changing helical sign sheets, or changing phase bookkeeping are
  substantive modeling choices, not cosmetic canonicalization; sources:
  `attacks/chain-01.md`, `judgments/chain-01.md`, `judgments/chain-03.md`.

## Tao-Likeness Discriminator

Later steps should count a packet family as Tao-like only if all of the
following survive after canonicalization:

1. `[VERIFIED]` The family contains identifiable carrier, delay-clock,
   tiny-trigger, transfer-conduit, and next-stage carrier roles.
2. `[VERIFIED]` Those roles interact in the ordered delayed-threshold sequence
   inherited from Tao's mechanism, rather than merely in an arbitrary sparse
   triad network.
3. `[INFERRED]` The dominant channels realize a genuine hierarchy:
   weak clock pump,
   much smaller seed,
   stronger post-threshold amplifier/rotor action,
   and delayed next-stage transfer.
4. `[INFERRED]` Phase data and helical sign bookkeeping are declared tightly
   enough that the claimed hierarchy is not a representation artifact.
5. `[INFERRED]` Spectator interactions stay within one predeclared admissible
   leakage or behavioral screen on one predeclared finite time window.

Negative discriminator:

- `[INFERRED]` A family that is only sparse, multiscale, mirror-complete, or
  triad-engineered, but has no delayed-threshold trigger logic or no honest
  near-isolation screen, does **not** count as Tao-like for this branch.
