# Gravitational Bound States: Graviballs, Condensates, and the N-Portrait

## Graviballs

Guiot, Borquez, Deur, and Werner (JHEP 11 (2020) 159) showed that two gravitons can form a bound state ("graviball") through gravitational self-interaction:
- Two gravitons CAN be bound by gravitational interaction
- Related to the "gravitational geon" (Brill & Hartle 1964)
- Calculations use QFT techniques applied to low-energy quantum gravity
- Numerical solutions give access to space-time dynamics

**These are NOT non-perturbative confinement states** -- graviballs are semiclassical objects formed by N gravitons bound by collective gravitational interaction.

## Dvali-Gomez N-Portrait of Black Holes

Black holes as Bose-Einstein condensates of N weakly-interacting soft gravitons:
- Wavelength: sqrt(N) x l_P
- Coupling: 1/N
- Mass: M_BH = sqrt(N) x M_P
- A black hole of mass M contains N ~ (M/M_P)^2 gravitons
- Hawking radiation = quantum depletion of the condensate
- Bekenstein entropy = exponentially growing number of quantum states with N
- Black holes are the "critical point of a quantum phase transition"

## Dark Matter Candidates

### Graviballs as Dark Matter
Guiot et al. propose graviballs as DM candidates with associated gravitational lensing effects. Masses and production mechanisms need further study.

### Planck Remnants as Dark Matter
If AS predicts stable Planck-mass remnants from primordial BH evaporation (~10^{-5} g), these could be dark matter. See `asymptotic-safety/black-holes-and-singularity-resolution.md`.

### Dark Glueball Scenario
Dark sectors with purely gravitational couplings to the Standard Model are unavoidably populated by graviton exchange, producing dark glueballs as viable DM candidates.

## Gravitational Vacuum Condensate (Hamber)

Hamber's Regge-Wheeler lattice path integral formulation reveals a nontrivial gravitational vacuum condensate -- a nonperturbative scale xi analogous to Lambda_QCD:
- Critical exponent nu ~ 1/3 (conjectured universal)
- Running of Newton's constant at large distances: G(r) ~ G_0[1 + c(r/xi)^{1/nu} + ...]
- Correlation length xi is related to the observed cosmological constant: xi ~ 1/sqrt(Lambda)
- Predicts "significant deviations from classical gravity on distance scales approaching the effective infrared cutoff set by the observed cosmological constant"

**This is inherently non-perturbative** -- requires summing over all gravitational field configurations. Perturbative QG+F cannot reproduce it.

## Lattice Gravity Evidence

### CDT
Provides non-perturbative evidence for emergence of 4D de Sitter-like spacetime (Phase C) and spectral dimension running, but has NOT demonstrated:
- Specific graviton bound state formation
- Ghost confinement
- A "glueball" spectrum of gravitational composites

### Hamber's Regge Calculus
- Gravitational Wilson loop computed on the lattice
- Critical exponent nu ~ 1/3
- Gravitational vacuum condensate identified
- Running Newton constant consistent with gravitational anti-screening
- But: no explicit bound state formation observed

### Gap in Evidence
Neither CDT nor Regge calculus has demonstrated bound state formation analogous to glueballs in lattice QCD. This remains one of the most important open questions in non-perturbative quantum gravity.
