# Stochastic Gravity (Hu-Verdaguer Framework)

## Core Idea

Go beyond semiclassical gravity (G_μν = 8πG⟨T_μν⟩) by including quantum stress-energy fluctuations as stochastic noise in Einstein's equations.

## The Einstein-Langevin Equation

G_μν = 8πG(⟨T_μν⟩ + ξ_μν)

where ξ_μν is stochastic noise from quantum fluctuations, characterized by the noise kernel (the two-point function of stress-energy fluctuations).

## Does It "Gravitize the Quantum"?

**No.** It takes QM as given and studies how quantum noise affects the gravitational field. The arrow goes QM → stochastic gravity, the opposite of "gravitize the quantum."

## Significance for the GtQ Program

The mathematical structure — gravitational noise producing quantum-like behavior — is suggestive if the arrow is **reversed**. If spacetime has fundamental stochastic structure (not sourced by quantum matter but intrinsic), the Einstein-Langevin equation provides the template for how that noise propagates to matter, potentially producing quantum behavior. This reversal is a key ingredient in synthesis attempts.
