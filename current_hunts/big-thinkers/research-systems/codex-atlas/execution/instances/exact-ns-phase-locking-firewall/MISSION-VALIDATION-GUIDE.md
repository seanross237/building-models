# Mission Validation Guide — Exact NS Phase-Locking Firewall

## Tier 1: Object Precision

- The phase-coherence observable is explicit and intrinsic.
- It does not require post hoc Tao-role labeling or packet annotation.
- The delayed-transfer test is defined concretely and remains faithful to exact NS structure.

## Tier 2: Exact-NS Fidelity

- The main test object preserves the exact phase structure it claims to analyze.
- Any reduced model states explicitly which phase-sensitive features of exact NS it preserves and which it discards.
- The mission does not silently collapse into amplitude-only bookkeeping.

## Tier 3: Quantitative Content

- The mission produces either a lower bound, incompatibility statement, explicit counterexample, or structural negative.
- "Transfer should require coherence" is not accepted without a named measurable witness.
- Any positive firewall states where and when the coherence burden is forced.

## Tier 4: Prior-Work Discipline

- Closed estimate-level, packet-canonicity, and hidden-precursor routes are not reopened in disguise.
- The mission uses the existing Tao mechanism reconstruction rather than redoing it from scratch.
- Any appeal to resonance or homochirality heuristics is converted into an exact statement or explicitly rejected.

## Tier 5: Outcome Sharpness

- The final result ends in one of the mission's named outcomes.
- Every substantial claim is tagged `[VERIFIED]`, `[COMPUTED]`, `[CHECKED]`, or `[CONJECTURED]`.
- The next-step implication is unambiguous: pursue, kill, or reformulate.
