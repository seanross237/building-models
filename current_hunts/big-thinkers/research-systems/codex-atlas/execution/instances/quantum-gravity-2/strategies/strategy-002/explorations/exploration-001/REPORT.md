# Exploration 001: Comprehensive Catalog of QG+F's Explanatory Debts

## Goal
Produce a comprehensive, ranked catalog of everything QG+F (quadratic gravity with fakeon quantization) cannot explain, does poorly, or leaves as "that's just how the math works." Each debt rated on severity, specificity, alternative coverage, and explanatory opportunity. Must include a TOP 5 ranking ordered by fertility for novel theory construction.

## Methodology
1. Verify and expand the 12 known debts from our library
2. Search 2025-2026 literature for new critiques
3. Identify conceptual/philosophical debts beyond computational gaps
4. Compare against other frameworks' strengths
5. Rank by explanatory opportunity

## CATALOG OF EXPLANATORY DEBTS

### Debt 1: The Fakeon's Physical Interpretation
**What QG+F cannot do:** Explain what a fakeon IS physically. The massive spin-2 ghost is quantized via a modified Feynman prescription that removes it from the physical spectrum — it appears in loops but never as an asymptotic state. Three competing interpretations exist:
- **Fakeon (Anselmi):** Purely virtual particle — a new quantization prescription alongside Feynman and Lee-Wick. Perturbative unitarity proven to all loop orders. But "purely virtual" is a mathematical description, not a physical explanation.
- **IHO/DQFT (Sravan Kumar, March 2026):** Reinterprets the ghost as an inverted harmonic oscillator instability with spacelike momentum support. Novel but ad hoc (requires flipping the C² sign) and unverified.
- **Ghost confinement (Holdom-Ren):** Non-perturbative analogy with QCD — the ghost is confined like quarks. The most physically intuitive picture but dynamically unproven.
- **Mass gap (arXiv:2501.16445):** Dyson-Schwinger approach shows ghost dynamically suppressed.

**Severity:** CRITICAL — The fakeon IS QG+F's defining innovation. If you can't say what it is physically, you can't say what the theory is about.

**Specificity:** QG+F-SPECIFIC. No other QG approach has this problem because no other approach has fakeons.

**Alternative coverage:** Lee-Wick theories tried treating ghosts as unstable particles — proven to fail unitarity (Kubo-Kugo 2023). The IHO interpretation is the closest to a physical picture but remains unverified. Ghost confinement (QCD analogy) would give a physical story if proven, but requires non-perturbative control nobody has.

**Explanatory opportunity:** VERY HIGH. A theory that explains WHY the fakeon prescription works — what physical principle it reflects — would deepen QG+F from a recipe into a worldview. Could connect to information-theoretic or causal principles. The modular flow unitarity argument (MVEH → modular unitarity → fakeon) is the closest existing lead.

### Debt 2: Why 4D?
**What QG+F cannot do:** Explain why spacetime is 4-dimensional. QG+F's renormalizability requires exactly d=4 (quadratic curvature couplings are dimensionless only in 4D). This is exploited as a constraint, not explained as a consequence.

**Severity:** MAJOR — This is a deep question about the structure of reality.

**Specificity:** PARTIALLY SHARED. String theory requires d=10 (or 11 for M-theory) and needs compactification. LQG works in any d but doesn't predict d=4. CDT's phase structure depends on d. Causal sets don't predict d. However, QG+F has a SPECIAL version of this problem: d=4 is uniquely required for the theory's consistency, making the question sharper.

**Alternative coverage:** String theory "explains" d=10 from Weyl anomaly cancellation but doesn't explain compactification to 4. CDT shows 4D de Sitter-like phase in simulations but this may be input-dependent. The anthropic landscape approach punts to observer selection. Causal set theory has the Kleitman-Rothschild result suggesting d=2 for generic causets — the opposite problem.

**Explanatory opportunity:** MODERATE. Deep question but extremely hard. Any answer likely requires going beyond QG+F entirely. A theory that derived d=4 from first principles would be revolutionary, but the difficulty is proportional to the value.

### Debt 3: Standard Model Origin
**What QG+F cannot do:** Predict or explain the Standard Model gauge group SU(3)×SU(2)×U(1), its particle content, or the values of its ~19 free parameters. QG+F is asymptotically free in f₂ for ANY matter content — the Weyl-squared coupling doesn't care what particles exist. The agravity program (Salvio-Strumia) is classically scale-invariant QG+F that generates M_P via Coleman-Weinberg, but it requires SM content as input. TAF (total asymptotic freedom) constraints limit but don't uniquely select the gauge group.

**Severity:** MAJOR — A complete theory of nature must eventually explain the Standard Model.

**Specificity:** SHARED. This is a weakness of virtually every QG approach except possibly string theory (landscape/swampland). LQG, CDT, causal sets all take SM as given. Even string theory doesn't uniquely predict the SM.

**Alternative coverage:** String theory's landscape gives ~10^{500} vacua — the SM is "in there somewhere" but not uniquely selected. The swampland program constrains what's consistent but doesn't predict. Emergent gravity approaches ignore the SM entirely. The real comparator is the SM itself — its structure remains unexplained by any approach.

**Explanatory opportunity:** LOW for our purposes. This is a universal hard problem, not a QG+F-specific gap. Building a novel theory here would mean building a GUT or TOE, which is outside our scope. However, if a QG+F-extending theory naturally produced gauge group constraints, that would be extremely valuable.

### Debt 4: Black Hole Interior and Singularity Resolution
**What QG+F cannot do:** Resolve the black hole singularity. The theory is perturbative — it computes corrections as expansions around classical solutions. The Lichnerowicz theorem (R=0 for static vacuum BHs) means all quadratic corrections vanish for Schwarzschild. The fakeon prescription selects Schwarzschild over non-Schwarzschild branches. All BH corrections are suppressed by O(l_P²/r_H²) ~ 10⁻⁷⁶ for solar mass BHs. The theory has NOTHING to say about what happens at r=0 or inside the horizon in a fundamentally new way.

Bonanno et al. (May 2025) propose "spontaneous ghostification" — evaporating BHs transition to stable naked singularities — but this treats the ghost as physical, contradicting the fakeon prescription.

**Severity:** MAJOR — Singularity resolution is a primary motivation for quantum gravity.

**Specificity:** PARTIALLY QG+F-SPECIFIC. The perturbative nature is the core issue — other perturbative approaches (string perturbation theory around a BH background) share this. But LQG, CDT, causal sets, and asymptotic safety all have non-perturbative tools that can address the singularity. AS predicts modified BHs with Planck cores. LQG's loop effects bounce/resolve the singularity. QG+F specifically selects Schwarzschild and then can't go further.

**Alternative coverage:** LQG: singularity resolved via quantum bounce (Modesto, Ashtekar-Bojowald). AS: Planck-scale BH remnants, modified Schwarzschild with quantum core. CDT: singularity softened by discrete structure. String theory: fuzzball paradigm replaces horizon with stringy structure. All have at least a story; QG+F has silence.

**Explanatory opportunity:** HIGH. Black hole interiors are where quantum gravity MUST say something new. QG+F's silence here is a genuine gap. But the non-perturbative tools needed are precisely what QG+F lacks.

### Debt 5: The Cosmological Constant (New CC Problem)
**What QG+F cannot do:** Explain why Λ ~ 10⁻¹²² in Planck units. The unimodular extension of QG+F solves the "old" CC problem (vacuum energy doesn't gravitate, Λ is an integration constant). But one-loop corrections still give Λ ~ f₂⁴M_Pl⁴/(16π²) ~ 10⁻³⁴ M_Pl⁴, which is ~10⁸⁸ times too large. Cascading dimensional transmutation fails — QG+F has no IR-active coupling confining at meV scales. Salvio invokes anthropic reasoning. Kugo (2020) proved classical scale invariance is necessary but insufficient. Weinberg's no-go: no continuous symmetry protects the CC in a gravitational theory.

**Severity:** CRITICAL — The worst quantitative disagreement in all of physics.

**Specificity:** SHARED BY ALL CONTINUUM APPROACHES. String theory uses the landscape (10^{500} vacua). LQG has no mechanism. CDT has no mechanism. Causal set theory uniquely predicted a small fluctuating Λ before its discovery (Sorkin 1991), but this is an order-of-magnitude argument, not a derivation.

**Alternative coverage:** Causal sets: stochastic Λ from Poisson fluctuations in spacetime atoms (Λ ~ 1/√N where N = spacetime volume in Planck units). Best existing approach — predicted accelerating expansion before 1998. Running vacuum model: Λ(H) removes quartic divergences, but doesn't predict the value. Spacetime foam: exponential suppression via Planck-scale inhomogeneity (Nesterov 2024).

**Explanatory opportunity:** LOW-MODERATE for our purposes. The CC problem is universal — solving it would win a Nobel Prize but isn't a fertile gap for a *focused* novel theory. Unless we target the QG+F-specific version: why does unimodular QG+F's one-loop correction fail? This is narrower and more tractable.

### Debt 6: Microcausality Violation — What Does It Mean?
**What QG+F cannot do:** Give a physical interpretation to its own most distinctive qualitative prediction. The fakeon prescription violates microcausality at Δt ~ 1/M₂ ~ 10⁻⁴³ s. This means that fields don't commute at spacelike separation at the Planck scale. Anselmi & Marino (2020) proved this violation does NOT propagate — it's confined to Planck distances. But the theory cannot say what this violation MEANS physically. Is it a smearing of spacetime? A fundamental acausality? A breakdown of the very concept of locality?

The violation is fundamentally unobservable — it extends to ~10⁻²⁹ m but doesn't propagate and leaves no macroscopic signature.

**Severity:** MODERATE — The prediction exists but its interpretation and observability are both problematic.

**Specificity:** QG+F-SPECIFIC. This is unique to the fakeon approach. Other QG approaches have their own locality modifications (noncommutative geometry, minimal length, causal set discreteness) but QG+F's version is distinctive.

**Alternative coverage:** Noncommutative geometry: [x_μ, x_ν] = iθ_μν gives a spacetime uncertainty principle. Minimal length scenarios (GUP): Δx ≥ l_P modifies the uncertainty principle. These are conceptually richer frameworks for understanding Planck-scale non-locality. QG+F's microcausality violation has no such interpretive framework.

**Explanatory opportunity:** MODERATE-HIGH. If microcausality violation could be connected to information-theoretic principles (e.g., it's the price of unitarity, or it reflects computational limits on spacetime), this would transform QG+F's oddest feature into its deepest insight. The connection to the information paradox (potential leakage channel) is suggestive but unquantified.

### Debt 7: One-Prediction Theory
**What QG+F cannot do:** Make experimentally testable predictions beyond the tensor-to-scalar ratio r ∈ [0.0004, 0.0045]. A comprehensive assessment of all 19 non-CMB signatures finds EVERY SINGLE ONE undetectable:
- Gravitational wave signatures: Planck-suppressed
- GQuEST tabletop detector: not relevant to QG+F
- BMV entanglement experiments: not discriminating (Marletto 2025: classical gravity may generate entanglement)
- Yukawa corrections: 25 orders of magnitude gap
- 21-cm/LSS: 10⁻¹¹⁰ suppressed
- Missing resonance (collider): 10¹⁵ beyond LHC
- LVK ringdown constraints: ℓ < 34-49 km (40 orders too weak)

The theory will not be meaningfully tested until LiteBIRD (~2036-2037) measures r. If r falls in the predicted range, it confirms Starobinsky-class inflation, not uniquely QG+F. If it doesn't, QG+F is in trouble but can retreat to the six-derivative extension.

**Severity:** CRITICAL — A theory that can't be tested isn't doing science.

**Specificity:** PARTIALLY QG+F-SPECIFIC. The 15-order gap between accessible scales and QG+F scales is shared by any Planck-scale theory. But other approaches have additional predictions: AS predicts modified BH properties; LQG predicts discrete area/volume spectra; causal sets predict stochastic Λ; Oppenheim predicts decoherence-diffusion trade-off. QG+F is uniquely impoverished in testable predictions.

**Alternative coverage:** Oppenheim's postquantum gravity: decoherence-diffusion bounds being tested NOW. LQG: area gap in principle observable via BH spectroscopy. Causal sets: swerves of massive particles (extremely small). AS: BH remnants, modified Hawking radiation. All weak, but QG+F is weakest.

**Explanatory opportunity:** HIGH. A theory that extends QG+F's predictive reach — that connects QG+F's parameters to accessible observables — would be enormously valuable. The fakeon mass M₂ is completely free; any mechanism constraining it would be a breakthrough.

### Debt 8: Non-Perturbative Completion
**What QG+F cannot do:** Define itself non-perturbatively. The theory is built on Feynman diagrams — perturbative expansion around classical backgrounds. By analogy with QCD, the perturbative theory (asymptotic freedom) should have a non-perturbative completion (confinement, bound states, mass gap). The QCD-gravity analogy (Holdom-Ren 2015/2024) identifies:
- AF ↔ AF (both have running couplings → 0 in UV)
- Ghost ↔ gluon (confined, never observed)
- Gravitational "hadrons" ↔ QCD hadrons

But 7 breakdown points are identified in the analogy. A mass gap in R² gravity was demonstrated via Dyson-Schwinger equations (arXiv:2501.16445, Jan 2025), but this is for pure R² gravity, not full QG+F.

**Severity:** CRITICAL — Without non-perturbative definition, the theory is incomplete at a fundamental level.

**Specificity:** PARTIALLY QG+F-SPECIFIC. All perturbative approaches face this (string perturbation theory → M-theory). But QG+F has a sharper version because the fakeon prescription is defined purely perturbatively — it's unclear what the fakeon "means" non-perturbatively.

**Alternative coverage:** LQG: inherently non-perturbative (background-independent quantization). CDT: non-perturbative (Monte Carlo simulations). AS: non-perturbative (functional RG). String theory: M-theory is the conjectured non-perturbative completion. QG+F has no candidate non-perturbative formulation.

**Explanatory opportunity:** VERY HIGH. The non-perturbative completion of QG+F is arguably the deepest open problem in the program. If ghost confinement is real, it would explain the fakeon, predict bound states, potentially resolve BH singularities, and connect to testable predictions (gravitational "hadrons"). This is where the QCD analogy promises the most.

### Debt 21: Sacrifice of S-Matrix Analyticity
**What QG+F cannot do:** Maintain analyticity of scattering amplitudes. A major 2025 paper (Anselmi et al., JHEP 05, 2025, 145; arXiv:2503.01841) explicitly catalogs the trade-offs of four amplitude prescriptions for theories with complex poles. Each prescription forfeits one or more tenets of standard QFT:
- (i) Textbook Wick rotation → **no optical theorem**
- (ii) Lee-Wick-Nakanishi → **no Lorentz invariance**
- (iii) Fakeon prescription → **no analyticity of the amplitude**
- (iv) Direct Minkowski → **no optical theorem AND no power-counting renormalizability**

The fakeon prescription REQUIRES giving up analyticity — the S-matrix is NOT analytic. The peak region of the fakeon propagator is "outside the convergence domain." This is not a minor technicality: analyticity is a foundational property of the S-matrix that enables dispersion relations, crossing symmetry applications, and the connection between Euclidean and Lorentzian physics.

Anselmi's position (arXiv:2601.06346, Jan 2026, "On Causality and Predictivity") is radical: he argues that causality itself should be abandoned as a fundamental principle, calling it "misleading" and "resting on metaphysical assumptions." The maximum achievable is "delayed prepostdictions." This is a philosophical sacrifice with no precedent in successful physical theories.

**Severity:** MAJOR — Gives up a foundational property of QFT.

**Specificity:** QG+F-SPECIFIC. No other QG approach requires abandoning S-matrix analyticity. String theory has excellent analytic S-matrix properties. LQG doesn't use S-matrices. This is unique to the fakeon.

**Alternative coverage:** String theory: the S-matrix is analytic and satisfies crossing symmetry beautifully. The bootstrap program relies on analyticity. The entire modern amplitudes program assumes analyticity. QG+F stands alone in renouncing it.

**Explanatory opportunity:** HIGH. The sacrifice of analyticity is profound — it means QG+F cannot use the powerful S-matrix bootstrap techniques. Understanding WHY analyticity must be sacrificed and what replaces it could lead to a new framework. If analyticity violation is the price of unitarity in higher-derivative gravity, understanding this trade-off more deeply could yield a novel principle.

### Debt 22: No Holographic Description
**What QG+F cannot do:** Connect to holography or AdS/CFT. The most powerful tool in quantum gravity research — the holographic duality — has no known formulation for QG+F. There is no "dual CFT" for quadratic gravity. The renormalized holographic entanglement entropy has been computed for quadratic curvature gravity (Fursaev et al., PRD 104, 2021), but this is in the context of AdS/CFT where quadratic curvature gravity appears as a bulk theory dual to a non-standard CFT. This is not the same as QG+F having a holographic description — the fakeon prescription specifically breaks the standard analytic structure needed for AdS/CFT to work.

The entanglement-gravity bootstrap (MVEH construction) derives QG+F from entanglement structure, but this operates at the level of entanglement entropy, not full holographic duality. The linearization barrier (Bueno et al.) means MVEH only gives linearized QG+F equations, not the full nonlinear theory.

**Severity:** MODERATE — Holography is a tool, not a requirement. But it's THE most productive tool in QG.

**Specificity:** QG+F-SPECIFIC (partially). LQG also lacks a holographic description. But string theory's holographic duality is its crown jewel. AS has been connected to holography via conformal field theory techniques. QG+F's analyticity violation (Debt 21) specifically obstructs the construction.

**Alternative coverage:** String theory/AdS/CFT: the gold standard for holographic description. AS: connected via conformal symmetry at the fixed point. QG+F: no connection.

**Explanatory opportunity:** MODERATE. Building a holographic description of QG+F would be valuable but is likely blocked by the analyticity issue. More promising: understanding WHY QG+F lacks holography — what this says about the theory's structure.

### Debt 23: No Explanation of Gravitational Thermodynamics
**What QG+F cannot do:** Explain WHY gravity has thermodynamic properties. Bekenstein-Hawking entropy (S = A/4G), Hawking temperature, and the laws of black hole thermodynamics are among the most profound results in theoretical physics. QG+F computes Wald entropy corrections (S = A/4G + O(l_P²/r_H²)) but provides ZERO insight into WHY area equals entropy. The theory treats BH thermodynamics as a computational result, not as a window into the nature of spacetime.

Jacobson's 1995 derivation of Einstein's equations FROM thermodynamics suggests gravity IS thermodynamics. The entanglement entropy program (Ryu-Takayanagi, Van Raamsdonk) suggests spacetime IS entanglement. QG+F participates in none of this — it merely computes corrections to formulas it doesn't explain.

**Severity:** MODERATE-MAJOR — BH thermodynamics is one of the few windows into quantum gravity.

**Specificity:** PARTIALLY SHARED. Most QG approaches compute BH entropy without fully explaining it. But string theory (microstate counting, AdS/CFT) and LQG (spin network counting) at least provide microscopic derivations. QG+F cannot even do this.

**Alternative coverage:** String theory: Strominger-Vafa microstate counting gives exact Bekenstein-Hawking entropy for extremal BHs. LQG: spin network state counting gives S = A/4G (with Barbero-Immirzi parameter fixing). Causal sets: S ∝ A from spacetime atom counting. QG+F: perturbative Wald correction only.

**Explanatory opportunity:** MODERATE-HIGH. A theory that explains WHY QG+F's structure leads to gravitational thermodynamics — connecting the fakeon/quadratic structure to information-theoretic properties — would be very valuable. The MVEH construction is a step in this direction but stops at the linearized level.

This is not a critique from outside the program — it's the program's architect acknowledging a deep conceptual tension. Fakeons reduce predictivity to "delayed prepostdictions."

This weakens the claim that QG+F is a unique, well-defined theory (see Debt 1).

4. **BMV entanglement test complications (Oct 2025):** Marletto et al. showed that classical gravity can also generate entanglement, undermining one of the few potential QG+F discriminating tests.

5. **Q-desics (March 2026, ScienceDaily):** New quantum geodesic equation shows particles in "quantum spacetime" may deviate from classical geodesics — potentially relevant for QG+F but developed outside the QG+F framework.

6. **Pre-geometric gravity (2025):** Emergent gravity from spontaneous symmetry breaking of larger gauge symmetry — a competing approach that explains spacetime emergence (something QG+F cannot do, Debt 13).

### Dark Corners the QG+F Community Avoids

1. **The non-perturbative problem is actively avoided.** Anselmi's group publishes prolifically on perturbative calculations but has published ZERO papers on non-perturbative QG+F. The Holdom-Ren QCD analogy is acknowledged but never developed by the core group.

This is effectively an admission that the perturbative framework is insufficient for the theory's own dynamics.

4. **Fakeon + unimodular combination unexplored.** Salvio proposed unimodular QG+F for the CC problem (2024), but the actual combination of the fakeon prescription with the unimodular constraint hasn't been worked out.
