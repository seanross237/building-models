# Meta-Learning: Explorations 007-010 (Devil's Advocate → Consolidation → Final)

## Exploration 007 (Devil's Advocate)
- Immediate devil's advocate after construction is the most important meta-lesson. CFT's fatal weakness (loop-level gap) was identified in 1 exploration, saving 2-3 that would have been wasted on repair.
- The severity rating system (FATAL/NEAR-FATAL/SERIOUS/MODERATE/MINOR) produces clear, actionable output.
- Asking the explorer to search for specific counterevidence (not just think of objections) grounds the attack in literature.

## Exploration 008 (Unified Assessment)
- Synthesis explorations work when they have clear deliverables (convergence statement, unique contributions, open problems).
- The 6 targeted literature searches for novelty verification were highly valuable — they confirmed the Wheeler bridge is genuinely unpublished.

## Exploration 009 (5-Tier Validation)
- The 5-tier + comparison table format from Strategy-001 continues to be the best assessment tool.
- Head-to-head comparison with QG+F forces honesty about the value of the work.

## Exploration 010 (Future Directions)
- A final survey exploration is valuable for handing off to the missionary with actionable recommendations.
- Asking for feasibility/novelty/prediction assessments per direction makes the recommendations concrete.

## Overall Strategy-002 Meta-Lessons
1. **Interpretive targets don't produce theories.** The gap-mine methodology correctly identified the fakeon interpretation as the most fertile gap, but "fertile for interpretation" ≠ "fertile for theory-building." Future strategies should distinguish these.
2. **The classical→quantum gap is structural.** All three concept sprints hit it. Don't send explorers down this path again without new mathematical tools.
3. **Parallel concept sprints are the best Phase 2 structure.** Three independent sprints with self-assessment scores made selection trivially easy.
4. **Explorer stalls on synthesis.** Strategizer should handle large-document synthesis directly.
5. **10 explorations is tight but sufficient** for the gap-mine → sprint → build → harden cycle with 2 explorations for cleanup/assessment.
