# MISSION COMPLETE — Quantum Gravity 2

## Mission
Develop a novel theory of quantum gravity that goes beyond simply reproducing known frameworks, prioritizing originality and explanatory clarity.

## Result
The mission produced the **Unified QG+F–AS Framework** — a structural conjecture that Quadratic Gravity with Fakeons (QG+F) and Asymptotic Safety (AS) are perturbative and non-perturbative descriptions of the same UV-complete theory, analogous to perturbative QCD and lattice QCD.

## What Was Achieved

### Primary Deliverable
The **complete theory document** (513 lines) at `strategies/strategy-004/explorations/exploration-007/REPORT.md`. It contains: the theory statement, evidence for and against, post-adversarial prediction catalog, three critical computations, comparison to alternatives, and open problems. Written to be readable by both non-physicists (core insight section) and domain experts (technical details with citations).

### Novel Contributions
1. **The Wheeler propagator bridge** (Strategy-002): The first observation that QG+F's fakeon propagator = the Wheeler (half-advanced + half-retarded) propagator at tree level, and that causal sets independently produce the Wheeler propagator via a completely different mechanism. This connection is unpublished and a domain expert would find it interesting.

2. **Systematic prosecution of the QG+F–AS conjecture** (Strategy-003): The first attempt to systematically test whether QG+F and AS are the same theory, testing 5 specific implications. Key novel findings:
   - **Analyticity dissolution:** AS already has non-standard analyticity (complex pole towers, obstructed Wick rotation), so the fakeon's analyticity sacrifice is not a barrier — it may solve AS's own Wick rotation problem
   - **Inflation reconciliation:** Both frameworks predict Starobinsky inflation with r ≈ 0.003; the pre-existing claim that r discriminates them was shown to be wrong
   - **Black hole regime compatibility:** Schwarzschild (QG+F) and modified BH (AS) describe complementary regimes, not competing predictions

3. **Three precisely specified critical computations** (Strategy-004): The exact calculations that would either confirm or falsify the unified framework:
   - C²-extended FRG graviton propagator (ghost dissolution test)
   - NGFP → inflationary scale RG flow for δ₃ (n_s tension test)
   - AF → NGFP trajectory existence (structural conjecture test)

4. **The δ₃ sign result** (Strategy-004): The NGFP predicts the R³ coupling with the correct sign (negative) for resolving the n_s tension, and the R³ direction is irrelevant at the NGFP (so it IS a prediction, not a free parameter). The magnitude requires the uncomputed RG flow, but the sign match is suggestive.

5. **23-debt catalog** of QG+F's explanatory gaps (Strategy-002): Comprehensive ranked list of what QG+F cannot explain, useful as a reference for future work.

### Methodological Evolution
The mission tested four distinct research methodologies:

| Strategy | Methodology | Assessment |
|----------|------------|------------|
| 001 | Diverge-Construct-Stress | Good divergence phase; premature convergence; multi-task explorations time out |
| 002 | Gap-Mine → Sprint → Build → Harden | Best of the first two; parallel concept sprints excellent; interpretive targets don't produce theories |
| 003 | Conjecture-Prosecution | **Best methodology overall.** Every exploration produces a verdict. Pre-loaded context maximizes efficiency. Keeps focus. |
| 004 | Targeted Prediction Extraction | Excellent for assessment but finds the fundamental limit: predictions require calculations text agents can't do |

**Golden rules discovered:**
- One task per exploration (non-negotiable; zero timeouts when followed)
- Devil's advocate immediately after first construction
- Pre-load ALL relevant context with specific paper names and numbers
- Each exploration gets a classification/verdict, not just a summary
- 200-400 lines per focused exploration; 400-600 for surveys
- "If this fails, explain WHY" in every exploration goal

## What Was NOT Achieved

1. **No genuinely discriminating predictions survive adversarial scrutiny.** Every prediction from the unified framework is either inherited from one standalone component, untestable at Planck energies, circular, or pending unperformed calculations. The null hypothesis (QG+F and AS are compatible but separate theories) explains all evidence equally well.

2. **The framework is a research program, not a predictive theory.** It identifies important computations and organizes existing knowledge, but does not make predictions that could be tested to confirm or deny the unification claim.

3. **Novelty is MARGINAL, not PASS.** The core conjecture (QG+F ≈ AS) has been speculated before (Holdom & Ren 2015-2016, Salvio & Strumia 2018). The prosecution evidence is novel but the central claim is not.

## Why the Mission Ends Here

The program has reached the **boundary of what can be achieved by reasoning about existing theories without performing original calculations.**

AI text agents excel at: literature surveys, conceptual reasoning, analogy identification, adversarial analysis, synthesis, and explanation. They cannot: compute RG flows, solve functional integral equations, run lattice simulations, or perform perturbative loop calculations.

Every promising direction identified across four strategies ultimately requires one of these computations. The three critical computations are precisely specified and ready for a human research group — but they cannot be executed within this system.

Further strategies would face diminishing returns. The framework has been built, tested, attacked, rebuilt, predicted from, and attacked again. What remains is computation.

## Recommendation for Future Work

The three critical computations are the clear next steps. They are ranked by feasibility and discriminating power:

1. **C²-extended FRG graviton propagator** — Most feasible. FRG groups (Knorr, Saueressig, Platania) have the tools. Ghost dissolution in the propagator would be strong evidence for unification.

2. **δ₃ from NGFP to inflation** — Highest impact. If the NGFP-determined R³ correction resolves the CMB n_s tension (testable by LiteBIRD ~2032-2036), this would be a genuine discriminating prediction. Requires ≥6-derivative FRG truncation.

3. **AF → NGFP trajectory** — Most fundamental. Proves or disproves the structural conjecture. Requires (R + R² + C² + E) FRG truncation with full trajectory computation.
