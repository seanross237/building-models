# Mission Validation Guide — Exact NS No-Near-Closed Tao Circuit

## Tier 1: Definition Precision

- "Near-closed Tao circuit" is defined concretely, not metaphorically.
- Active modes, desired couplings, and leakage thresholds are explicit.
- The definition is narrow enough that it could in principle be true or false.

## Tier 2: Exact-NS Fidelity

- The interaction law used is exact Navier-Stokes in the chosen representation.
- Leray projection and triadic geometry are handled explicitly.
- Any helical simplification is stated exactly and not confused with the full equation.

## Tier 3: Quantitative Content

- The mission produces either a lower bound, an impossibility statement, or a construction.
- "Too much entanglement" is not accepted without a quantitative witness.
- If the obstruction fails, the counterexample is explicit enough to invalidate the idea.

## Tier 4: Prior-Work Discipline

- Closed estimate-level routes are not reopened.
- The mission uses the prior mechanism reconstruction rather than redoing it.
- Any proposed host connection is clearly separate from De Giorgi / pressure / compactness dead routes.

## Tier 5: Outcome Sharpness

- The final result says one of:
  - concrete no-circuit theorem candidate
  - concrete near-circuit counterexample
  - idea too vague / not testable
- Every substantial claim is tagged `[VERIFIED]`, `[COMPUTED]`, `[CHECKED]`, or `[CONJECTURED]`.
- The next-step implication is unambiguous: pursue, kill, or reformulate.
