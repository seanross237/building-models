# Exploration 003 Summary: Positive Geometry Beyond N=4 SYM — Extension Map

## Goal
Map where the amplituhedron / positive geometry framework extends beyond N=4 SYM and where it breaks. Characterize what works, what breaks, and why, for 7+ theory classes.

## What Was Done
Systematic literature survey of 2019–2026 papers. Searched across N<4 SYM, pure YM, bi-adjoint scalars, gravity, QCD, cosmological polytopes, strings, and ABJM.

## Outcome: SUCCESS

Clear map of 7+ extensions produced with specific papers, mechanisms, and failure modes.

## Key Takeaway

**Two-tier structure**: positive geometry is a broad principle for *tree-level* amplitudes in color-ordered/planar theories, but the full *amplituhedron* (locality+unitarity derived from geometry, all-loop) is narrow — restricted to N=4 SYM and its 3D cousin ABJM.

The essential wall is **UV finiteness**: loop-level positive geometry requires no UV divergences, because UV poles are not logarithmic singularities on geometric boundaries. This excludes N<4 SYM, pure YM at loops, and QCD.

## Tier Structure

**Tier 1 — Full amplituhedron**: Planar N=4 SYM (all loops), ABJM (via symplectic reduction of N=4, arXiv:2306.00951). Requires Yangian symmetry + planarity + maximal SUSY.

**Tier 2 — Scattering forms / partial positive geometry**: Bi-adjoint ϕ³ (associahedron, arXiv:1711.09102), ϕ⁴ (Stokes polytopes, arXiv:1811.05904), multi-scalar (accordiohedra, arXiv:2104.04915), cosmological polytopes (arXiv:1709.02813), YM tree-level (scattering forms + corolla polynomial, arXiv:2405.10601), gravity tree (CHY pushforward, arXiv:2206.14196), open strings (associahedral grid, arXiv:2508.20161).

**Tier 3 — Structural insight only**: Non-planar N=4 (zero conditions, 2016), loop YM (corolla polynomial, messy, 2024), N<4 SYM (deformed measure, not canonical form, arXiv:1609.01923).

**Complete failures**: Color-full QCD, non-planar loop amplitudes, de Sitter gravity, non-perturbative physics.

## Case Where Geometric Approach Exceeds Standard Methods (Required)

1. **Color-kinematics duality explained** (arXiv:1711.09102): Geometric origin of BCJ duality — wedge products of kinematic factors satisfy same Jacobi relations as color. This *explains why* BCJ works, inaccessible from Lagrangian.
2. **Stokes polytopes fix ϕ⁴ weights from geometry** (arXiv:1811.05904): Relative weights of ϕ⁴ diagrams are determined by geometry, not from Feynman rules.
3. **ABJM all-loop from geometry** (arXiv:2306.00951): All-loop, all-multiplicity integrands from a single geometric object — unachievable from perturbative loop-by-loop methods.
4. **S-matrix as boundary of cosmological wavefunction** (arXiv:1709.02813): Geometric fact (codimension-1 boundary) that is opaque in standard QFT in curved space.
5. **Inverse KLT kernel geometric** (arXiv:2508.20161, 2025): Why gravity = YM² has a geometric home for the first time.

## Key Open Problems (2025 Status)

1. **Gravituhedron**: Trnka (arXiv:2012.15780, 2020) proposed G-invariants for NMHV gravity up to 8 points, but no actual geometric object constructed after 5 years. Major open problem.
2. **Non-planar amplituhedron**: Conjectured "zero conditions" hold (2016) but no geometry.
3. **Inflation/de Sitter**: Cosmological polytopes work for flat FRW; extension to dS with gravitons unknown.
4. **ϕ³ at loop level**: No positive geometry for UV-divergent scalar theories at loop level.

## Unexpected Findings

1. **ABJM amplituhedron is not "native"**: Despite ABJM having maximal SUSY in 3D, its amplituhedron must be imposed via reduction from N=4 SYM — only bipartite-graph geometries survive. This suggests Yangian geometry is more specific to N=4 SYM than to maximal SUSY in general.

2. **2025 is a highly active year**: Cosmohedra (JHEP 2025), associahedral grid (arXiv:2508.20161), tropical amplituhedron (2023), loop momentum amplituhedron (2022–2025) — the field is expanding rapidly into scalar/cosmological sectors even as the full gravity extension stalls.

3. **The gravituhedron program has stalled**: After Trnka's 2020 paper, no major follow-up has constructed the actual geometric object (as of 2025). This 5-year gap suggests the obstacle is fundamental.
