# Exploration 007 Summary: Experimental Signatures of Quadratic Gravity + Fakeon

## Goal
Calculate specific experimental signatures of quadratic gravity + fakeon for current/near-future experiments. We needed NUMBERS — quantitative predictions with detectability assessments.

## What Was Done
Investigated 6 experimental channels: (1) spacetime fluctuations (GQuEST/LIGO), (2) additional GW polarizations, (3) primordial GW spectrum beyond r, (4) modified binary inspiral waveforms, (5) gravity-induced entanglement, (6) microcausality violation signatures. For each, computed or estimated the predicted signal amplitude and compared against detector sensitivity.

## Outcome: SUCCEEDED — with a sobering conclusion

### The Theory Has Essentially ONE Testable Prediction

**Tensor-to-scalar ratio r ∈ [0.0004, 0.0035]**, testable by LiteBIRD (δr < 10⁻³, ~2031) and definitively by CMB-S4 (σ(r) ≤ 5×10⁻⁴, ~2033-2035). This is the theory's make-or-break test.

### All Other Channels Are Inaccessible

The massive modes (M₀ ~ 10¹³ GeV, M₂ ~ 10¹³ GeV or above) place all distinctive signatures astronomically beyond reach:
- **GW polarizations:** Extra scalar breathing mode exists but only above ~10²⁶ Hz (undetectable)
- **Binary waveform corrections:** Suppressed by exp(-10³²) (Yukawa at 10⁻²⁷ m Compton wavelength vs. 10⁵ m orbital separation)
- **Gravity entanglement:** Identical to GR at any macroscopic distance
- **Spacetime fluctuations:** Theory is a local QFT — no anomalous fluctuations (null at GQuEST is consistent)
- **Microcausality violation:** Δt ~ 10⁻³⁷ s, confined to microscopic scales, does not propagate

### An Underappreciated Signal: n_s Tension

The ACT DR6 measurement n_s = 0.974 ± 0.003 is already ~2.3σ above the theory's prediction n_s ≈ 0.967. If this persists, it could disfavor the theory before r is even measured.

## Key Takeaway

**The fakeon theory is testable by exactly one near-term experiment (CMB B-modes for r), and is already under mild tension from n_s measurements.** The theory's most distinctive signatures (microcausality violation, fakeon lineshape, modified entanglement at Planck scales) are all confined to energies ~10¹³ GeV. The predictivity paradox: the theory is highly constrained (only 2 free parameters), but those parameters take values that make almost all non-cosmological tests impossible. Among QG approaches, this is still one of the most testable — the bar is just very low.
