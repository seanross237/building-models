# Exploration 009: The Full d_s = 2 Landscape — What Else Is Possible?

## Mission Context

Our exploration 002 showed that d_s = 4 → 2 + Lorentz invariance + diffeo invariance + renormalizability uniquely selects quadratic gravity + fakeon. But what if we relax one constraint? This exploration maps the FULL landscape of d_s = 2 theories:

1. **Relax Lorentz invariance:** → Horava-Lifshitz gravity (z = 3). How does it compare? Does it pass the same validation tests?
2. **Relax renormalizability (keep ghost-free):** → IDG/nonlocal gravity gives d_s → 0, not 2. But are there non-polynomial ghost-free theories that give d_s = 2?
3. **Relax "exactly 2":** What if d_s → 2 is approximate? Does d_s = 2 ± 0.3 open the door to other theories?

## Your Goal

Map the landscape of theories that produce d_s ≈ 2 in the UV, comparing them to our main candidate (quadratic gravity + fakeon). This is a "gap-filling" exploration per the strategy.

**Write incrementally.**

### Specific Questions

1. **Horava-Lifshitz at z=3:** This is the other known Lorentz-VIOLATING theory with d_s = 2. Detailed comparison:
   - Does it pass our 7 validation tests? (Especially Lorentz invariance bounds!)
   - The GRB 221009A bounds: E_QG > 5.9 E_Pl for linear LIV. Does HL survive?
   - What is its status as of 2024-2026?
   - Is there a connection to quadratic gravity (e.g., does HL → quadratic gravity in some limit)?

2. **Non-polynomial propagators giving d_s = 2:** Our no-go theorem says entire functions give d_s → 0. But what about:
   - Propagators with branch cuts (non-analytic in p²)?
   - Propagators on non-commutative spacetime?
   - Propagators from causal set theory or CDT?
   - Can any of these give d_s = 2 while being ghost-free and Lorentz-invariant?

3. **Approximate d_s = 2:** If d_s → 2 ± 0.3 (allowing CDT's possible d_s ≈ 1.5 or LQG's d_s ≈ 2.5), what theories open up? Is there a continuous family parameterized by UV d_s?

4. **Novel construction:** Is there any mathematical structure we haven't considered that could produce d_s = 2 while avoiding ghosts? For example:
   - Theories on fractal spacetimes
   - Multi-fractional gravity
   - Theories with dynamical dimension
   - Theories where d_s = 2 emerges from topology change

## Success Criteria

- Assessment of the no-go theorem's loopholes (if any)
