# Exploration 005 Summary: Entanglement-First Quantum Gravity

## Goal
Investigate whether entanglement-area law + entanglement equilibrium (Jacobson 2015 + Ryu-Takayanagi) can serve as constructive axioms to determine a unique quantum gravity theory, independently from the spectral dimension direction.

## What Was Done
- Analyzed Jacobson's 1995 and 2015 derivations of Einstein equations from thermodynamics/entanglement
- Reviewed the Faulkner et al. (2013) and Swingle-Van Raamsdonk (2014) "gravity from entanglement" program
- Studied the Bueno-Min-Speranza-Visser (2017) extension to higher-derivative gravity
- Investigated the Dong (2013) holographic entanglement entropy for higher-derivative theories
- Connected to Sakharov induced gravity and Cooperman-Luty (2014) UV divergence renormalization
- Performed explicit calculation of Wald entropy for quadratic gravity and verified entanglement equilibrium reproduces quadratic gravity field equations
- Compared entanglement direction with spectral dimension direction from Exp. 002-003

## Outcome: SUCCEEDED — with a clear negative answer to the main question

### Entanglement equilibrium does NOT uniquely select a theory
The entanglement equilibrium framework is a **translation device**: given an entropy functional S[Σ], it produces the corresponding gravitational field equations. It maps S = A/(4G) → Einstein, and S = A/(4G) + Wald corrections → higher-derivative gravity. But it does NOT select which entropy functional is correct. It is a framework, not a selector.

### But it provides a powerful consistency check
The two approaches (spectral dimension and entanglement) are consistent and complementary:
- **Spectral dimension** is CONSTRUCTIVE: d_s → 2 forces quadratic gravity
- **Unitarity** is SELECTIVE: eliminates ghosts, forces fakeon prescription
- **Entanglement equilibrium** is CONFIRMATORY: given the Wald entropy for the selected action, it reproduces the correct field equations

### Novel observation: spectral dimension and entanglement entropy share UV origin
Both d_s → 2 and the subleading entanglement entropy corrections are determined by the same UV modification of the propagator (G(k) ~ 1/k⁴). The massive modes that produce d_s → 2 also produce logarithmic corrections to entanglement entropy. This is a genuine connection between the two programs.

## Key Takeaway
**The entanglement direction cannot independently derive quadratic gravity + fakeon.** It is complementary to, not a substitute for, the spectral dimension approach. The entanglement program's value lies in (a) confirming that the selected theory has consistent thermodynamic interpretation, (b) connecting the UV structure (spectral dimension, entanglement entropy corrections) to the gravitational action, and (c) identifying open problems (black hole entropy in fakeon theory, induced gravity coefficients). The spectral dimension constraint remains our strongest constructive axiom.
