# Mission Validation Guide — Exact NS Hidden-Precursor Firewall

## Tier 1: Definition Precision

- The delayed-transfer event is defined concretely.
- The precursor observable is a single explicit quantity, not a menu.
- "Hidden until threshold" is made measurable in the chosen setup.

## Tier 2: Exact-NS Fidelity

- The main test object remains faithful to exact NS structure.
- Any reduced model states explicitly which exact NS features it preserves and which it discards.
- The mission does not silently switch to packet/circuit objects after claiming to stay in physical space.

## Tier 3: Quantitative Content

- The mission produces either a lower bound, an observability statement, or an explicit counterexample.
- "There should be a warning signal" is not accepted without a named measurable witness.
- Any positive firewall must specify where and when the precursor is forced.

## Tier 4: Prior-Work Discipline

- Closed estimate-level pressure / De Giorgi / epsilon-regularity routes are not reopened in disguise.
- The mission uses the existing Tao mechanism reconstruction rather than redoing it from scratch.
- Any connection to known frameworks is made explicit rather than implied.

## Tier 5: Outcome Sharpness

- The final result ends in one of the mission's named outcomes.
- Every substantial claim is tagged `[VERIFIED]`, `[COMPUTED]`, `[CHECKED]`, or `[CONJECTURED]`.
- The next-step implication is unambiguous: pursue, kill, or reformulate.
