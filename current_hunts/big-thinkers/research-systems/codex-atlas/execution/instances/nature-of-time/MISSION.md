# Mission

What is time? Build the strongest, most compelling interpretation you can. You're free to draw on physics, philosophy, information theory, consciousness, mathematics, experience — whatever leads somewhere interesting. You're also free to take bold guesses and explore ideas that might seem out there, as long as you then stress-test them honestly.

The goal is not a literature survey. The goal is to arrive at an actual answer — or the closest thing to one — that holds up under rigorous logical scrutiny. It should be the kind of answer that a thoughtful person walks away from feeling like they genuinely understand something they didn't before.
