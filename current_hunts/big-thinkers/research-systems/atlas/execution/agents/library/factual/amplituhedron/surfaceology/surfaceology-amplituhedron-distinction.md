## Finding

**Critical clarification:** Surfaceology and the amplituhedron are *parallel* geometric programs, not nested. Surfaceology does NOT contain the amplituhedron as a special case, and vice versa. They share a philosophy (geometry encodes physics) and key figures (Arkani-Hamed), but address different theories and different kinematic spaces.

## The Two Frameworks Side by Side

| Property | Amplituhedron | Surfaceology |
|---|---|---|
| **Kinematic space** | Auxiliary twistor space (momentum twistors) | Kinematic space (Mandelstam variables X_{i,j}) |
| **Theory** | N=4 SYM (maximally supersymmetric) | Colored scalar, NLSM, YM, Yukawa, strings (non-SUSY) |
| **Loops** | Planar only (large-N limit) | All loop orders AND all genera (non-planar included) |
| **SUSY** | Maximal SUSY essential | No SUSY needed; or SUSY is obstacle |
| **Proof status** | Proved (Inventiones 2025, Even-Zohar et al.) | Core framework proved; extensions proved for each theory |
| **Shorthand** | Full supersymmetric power | "Poor man's amplituhedron" — broader class, simpler structure |

## What Surfaceology Unifies

The following frameworks are **special cases or limits** of surfaceology:

| Framework | Connection |
|---|---|
| **ABHY associahedron** (tree bi-adjoint Tr(φ³)) | Surfaceology on disk (g=0, L=0); triangulations of disk = Feynman diagrams |
| **Open string amplitudes** | u-variables define string amplitude via exp(−∑ α' X_C u_C); QFT = α'→0 limit |
| **Hidden zeros** (Tr(φ³), NLSM, gluons) | Big-mountain zeros manifest as geometric cancellations in the surface integrand |
| **NLSM amplitudes** | Tr(φ³) with δ-shift: X_{e,e} → X_{e,e}+δ, X_{o,o} → X_{o,o}−δ |
| **Yang-Mills loop integrand** | Scalar scaffolding: 2n-point scalar residue at scaffolding curves → n-point gluon |
| **Colored Yukawa (all-loop)** | Fermionic numerators → combinatorial determinants of the surface data |
| **Superstring amplitudes** | Gauge-invariant combinations of bosonic string amplitudes on surfaces |

## What Surfaceology Does NOT Cover

The amplituhedron is **not** a special case of surfaceology — N=4 SYM with full supersymmetry, in twistor space, is a structurally different beast. The question of whether a "supersymmetric surfaceology" exists that bridges the two programs is **open** (see surfaceology-open-problems.md).

## "Kinematic-Space Positive Geometry"

Surfaceology is the all-loop extension of positive geometry to kinematic space:
- **ABHY (2017):** canonical form of associahedron in kinematic space = tree-level amplitude
- **Surfaceology (2023+):** canonical form of moduli space of surfaces = all-loop amplitude

The amplituhedron, by contrast, operates in auxiliary twistor space — a conceptually separate arena.

## The Central Mysterious Open Question

The relationship between surfaceology (kinematic space) and the amplituhedron (twistor space) is not understood. Since both emerge from Arkani-Hamed's program and share the hidden-zero / positive-geometry philosophy, they may ultimately point to a deeper common structure. This is the most important open conceptual question in the positive geometry program.
