## The Core Verdict

**For N=4 SYM specifically, the amplituhedron is a computational and conceptual reformulation that produces identical predictions to Feynman diagrams.** No experiment could distinguish "locality is fundamental" from "locality emerges from positivity" within N=4 SYM, because both approaches compute the same amplitudes. Multiple experts confirm: "the observable results are finally the same."

Dismissing it as *merely* a reformulation, however, misses genuine contributions.

## Three Genuine Physical Contributions

**1. UV Finiteness as Selection Principle [ESTABLISHED]**
Positive geometry *requires* no UV poles (bounded region in projective space). This is not a tautology — it reveals WHY certain theories are UV-finite at all loops: it is geometrically enforced. The class of theories admitting a positive geometry formulation is strictly *smaller* than the class of unitary, local theories.

**2. EFT-Hedron Real-World Constraints [ESTABLISHED, PRACTICAL]**
The EFT-hedron gives concrete inequalities on real-world Wilson coefficients — constraints on photon-photon and graviton scattering, upper bound on new physics mass, amplitude growth restrictions. These apply to real theories and cannot be derived without the positivity framework. See `eft-hedron-positivity-constraints.md`.

**3. Hidden Zeros — Inter-Theory Structure [NEW, 2024–2025]**
Hidden zeros in ϕ³/pion/gluon amplitudes reveal unexpected unity across theories. At one loop (2025), they encode locality and unitarity for Tr(φ³) without assuming either. The geometric perspective discovers relations invisible to any single Lagrangian. See `hidden-zeros-inter-theory-structure.md`.

## What Remains Reformulation

- "Locality and unitarity are not fundamental" for N=4 SYM: logically true, physically empty for observable predictions
- The specific amplituhedron canonical form computation: same result as BCFW/Feynman sum
- The unitarity proof: confirms existing physics, does not predict new phenomena

## What Remains Speculative

- That emergent locality in the amplituhedron implies locality is non-fundamental in the real world (no evidence)
- That gravity will have an analogous "gravituhedron" (stalled 5+ years; obstacles appear fundamental)
- That Planck-scale physics reveals non-locality related to the amplituhedron (no mechanism)
- Holographic connection: amplituhedron lives in momentum-twistor space (NOT AdS bulk); no concrete holographic dual established
