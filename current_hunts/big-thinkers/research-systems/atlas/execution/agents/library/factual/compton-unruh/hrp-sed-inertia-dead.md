## Finding

The Haisch-Rueda-Puthoff (HRP) stochastic electrodynamics program, which proposed that inertia arises as an electromagnetic drag force from the quantum vacuum ZPF, is definitively refuted. Levin (2009) showed the relevant force component equals zero when treated relativistically. The program is dead.

## Mechanism (Historical)

HRP (Haisch, Rueda & Puthoff 1994, Phys. Rev. A 49, 678) proposed:
- Newton's F = ma can be derived from Maxwell's equations applied to the zero-point field (ZPF)
- When an object accelerates through the quantum vacuum, spectral distortion of the ZPF produces a Lorentz force opposing the acceleration
- Mass is not intrinsic but is an electromagnetic drag force from vacuum interactions
- Works within stochastic electrodynamics (SED) — treats vacuum as classical random electromagnetic field
- Does NOT invoke the Unruh effect directly (though the spectral distortion is mathematically related)
- Does NOT invoke Hubble-scale boundary conditions (unlike McCulloch's QI)

## Refutation: Levin (2009)

Levin, Phys. Rev. A 79, 012114 (2009) provided a definitive critique:

1. HRP's nonrelativistic implementation of the correlation function is **incorrect**
2. The assumption that large-time contributions to the force integral can be ignored is **wrong** — the force on an accelerated oscillator "remembers" the entire history of motion, including times when velocity was large
3. When treated relativistically, the relevant force component equals **zero**
4. Conclusion: "the interaction of the accelerated oscillator with ZPF radiation does not produce inertia"

## Current Status

Dead. The program has not produced a successful relativistic formulation since Levin (2009). Some experimental proposals continue in the SED community, but the theoretical foundation for vacuum-generated inertia via ZPF has been undermined.

## Key Papers

- Haisch, Rueda & Puthoff (1994) Phys. Rev. A 49, 678 — original proposal
- Rueda & Haisch (1998) Phys. Lett. A 240, 115 — extension
- Levin (2009) Phys. Rev. A 79, 012114 — definitive refutation
