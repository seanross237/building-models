- HIGH predictive potential: an answer could constrain M₂, predict new observables, or connect to information theory.
- Key leads: (a) information-theoretic origin (fakeon as price of consistent entanglement structure via MVEH), (b) computational interpretation (computational limits on reality), (c) causal interpretation (microcausality violation as fundamental), (d) analyticity-unitarity trade-off as new physical principle.

## DARK CORNERS THE COMMUNITY AVOIDS

Meta-observations about the QG+F research program's blind spots:

1. **Non-perturbative problem actively avoided.** Anselmi's group publishes prolifically on perturbative calculations but has published ZERO papers on non-perturbative QG+F. The QCD analogy (Holdom-Ren) is acknowledged but never developed by the core group.
2. **Testability problem acknowledged but not confronted.** All non-CMB tests are out of reach; the community focuses on r and hopes for the best.
3. **Classicization reveals deeper issues.** Anselmi's 2026 paper on classicized dynamics (JHEP 01, 2026, 104) acknowledges that "nonperturbative effects play crucial roles" and the classical limit introduces a "new type of uncertainty" — effectively admitting the perturbative framework is insufficient.
4. **Fakeon + unimodular combination unexplored.** Salvio proposed unimodular QG+F (2024) but the actual fakeon prescription + unimodular constraint interaction hasn't been worked out.

## COMPARATIVE ADVANTAGES OF ALTERNATIVE FRAMEWORKS

What other approaches explain that QG+F cannot:

- **String theory:** Holographic duality, BH microstate counting, analytic S-matrix, rich particle spectrum, UV completeness, gauge/gravity duality
- **LQG:** Background independence, discrete spacetime structure (spin networks), singularity resolution, BH entropy from first principles, non-perturbative by construction
- **Causal set theory:** Spacetime discreteness, CC prediction (pre-1998), swerves, problem of time resolution, Lorentzian from the start
- **Asymptotic safety:** Non-perturbative definition, BH modifications, spectral dimension computed
- **Oppenheim postquantum gravity:** Testable NOW (decoherence-diffusion bounds), falsifiable in principle, novel conceptual framework

## KEY INSIGHT

The most fertile gap is the **fakeon interpretation cluster (#1)**. This is 100% QG+F-specific, has the deepest room for novelty, and is the only cluster where a novel theory could both deepen understanding AND produce new predictions. The 2025-2026 literature shows this space is actively contested (IHO interpretation, analyticity sacrifice, causality abandonment).

Together with the **non-perturbative completion (#2)**, these two gaps point toward a novel theory that provides: (a) a physical/information-theoretic understanding of WHY the fakeon works, and (b) a non-perturbative picture of WHAT QG+F looks like at strong coupling.
