# The Meta-Interpretation: Time as Irreversible Determination

## Summary

A CI+CP meta-interpretation that synthesizes the Computational Interpretation and the Causal-Processual Interpretation into a single framework stronger than either parent. **Core claim: "Time is irreversible determination — the ongoing, physically constrained process by which the indeterminate becomes determinate, as encountered from within."** Originally scored 57.0/65; **revised to 52.5/65 after adversarial stress-test** (E006, five maximum-strength attacks). Still beats both parents (CI 47.5, CP 48.5) by +5.0 and +4.0 respectively. **Score is conditional on accepting real potentialities; without that commitment, the synthesis collapses to CI (~47.5).**

**Architectural insight (E006):** The synthesis is best understood as **"CI with an ontological upgrade"** rather than a transcendent meta-interpretation. CI provides the ground floor (established physics, zero metaphysical commitments). The real-potentialities commitment provides the upper floor (ontological depth, explanatory benefits, philosophical costs). The upgrade is optional.

The key insight: CI and CP were not competing answers to the same question but complementary answers to different questions about one underlying process. CI describes the *constraints* on determination (LBB engine, ML bound, computational irreducibility, Wolpert). CP describes the *ontology* of determination (actualization, real potentialities, intrinsic direction, perspectival "now"). "Determination" — the generation of the actual from the possible — subsumes both "computation" (CI's name) and "actualization" (CP's name).

## The Core Concept: Determination

**Determination** is a *bridging concept* — a proposed theoretical identification — rather than a discovered primitive (clarified by E006 Attack 1). The claim: the information-processing described by CI and the ontological becoming described by CP are the same process, and we call this process "determination." The evidence for this identification is: (a) the CI-CP structural isomorphism documented in the tournament (E004), (b) the specific claim that Landauer erasure is the thermodynamic signature of possibility-extinguishing, and (c) the unified explanatory power of the combined account. This identification is a substantive philosophical claim that could be wrong, but it is not merely a pun.

The word carries both senses deliberately:
- **Ontological:** becoming determinate (indeterminate → determinate, possible → factual)
- **Physical:** being determined (constrained by information-theoretic structure of a finite universe)

Time is not a background against which determination occurs. Time IS the ongoing occurrence of determination — the world determining itself step by step, subject to physical constraints, each determination inheriting prior results and generating conditions for future ones.

## Convergence Fork Resolution

The one genuine difference between CI and CP — the ontological status of quantum potentialities — is resolved by accepting CP's position: **potentialities are ontologically real** (following Heisenberg's potentia, Kastner-Kauffman 2017). This is a genuine metaphysical commitment that does specific work:
1. Grounds LBB irreversibility in something deeper than information destruction — extinguishing of real possibilities
2. Gives the "now" ontological weight — where real potentialities are being resolved
3. Provides intrinsic direction independent of the Past Hypothesis
4. Connects to Heisenberg's interpretation of QM

**Cost:** Incompatible with strictly epistemic QM interpretations (QBism) and in tension with Everettian interpretations.

## Three Tension Resolutions

### Tension 1: Computation vs. Actualization
**Resolution:** Two aspects of one process. "Computation" names the *constraints* (LBB, ML, CI, Wolpert). "Actualization" names the *ontology* (possibilities → one actuality). Asking which is "really" right is like asking whether EM is "really" electric or magnetic — frame-dependent, and the frame-dependence is part of the physics.

### Tension 2: Does the Arrow Need the Past Hypothesis?
**Resolution:** The *mechanism* is the LBB engine (CI's contribution — precise, established). The *direction* is intrinsic to determination: from indeterminate to determinate (CP's contribution). The Past Hypothesis remains an initial condition that must be assumed, not derived. Within the synthesis's framework, it has a specific characterization — the determination process began with a maximally open state — but whether this makes the PH less mysterious is a philosophical judgment, not a physical result (E006 Attack 3: the "natural initial condition" claim is substantially verbal). The synthesis's genuine directional contribution is the intrinsic possibility→actuality direction, not the PH reframing.

### Tension 3: What Is "Now"?
**Resolution:** CI's computational frontier IS CP's locus of active determination. The present is where determination is happening — ontologically real (following CP) and structurally characterized (following CI: LBB running, ML constraining, CI ensuring inaccessibility). Local and perspectival (no global "now"), compatible with special relativity via causal structure.

## Key Feature Constructions

### Irreversibility (5.0/5 — highest)
LBB engine (CI) IS the information-theoretic expression of ontological determination (CP). Information is "destroyed" because possibilities are genuinely extinguished. Landauer's kT ln 2 is the physical cost of extinguishing a real possibility. Precise mechanism grounded in clear ontology.

### Flow (4.0/5 — biggest improvement area)
Flow is what irreversible determination is like from the inside. Retention = inheritance of prior determinations (causal past prehended + input state). Primal impression = active determination (LBB running, possibilities being resolved). Protention = anticipatory openness (unresolved potentialities, computationally irreducible). Operates at Level 2 (moderate commitment) — no panexperientialism required. Hard problem of consciousness remains.

### Directionality (4.0/5 — revised down from 4.5)
The direction is intrinsic to determination (from open to settled) + the mechanism is the LBB engine. The Past Hypothesis remains assumed, not derived — the "natural initial condition" reframing is substantially verbal (E006 Attack 3). The genuine contribution: intrinsic direction (possibility→actuality) is a real improvement over CI's reliance on the PH.

## QRF-Process Tension (Unresolved — E006 Attack 5)

The synthesis currently claims simultaneously: (a) becoming is ontologically real (processual ontology) AND (b) the timeless Wheeler-DeWitt description is fundamental (QRF dissolution). These pull in opposite directions. If the timeless description is fundamental, "becoming" is perspectival and the potentialities commitment loses force. If becoming is ontological, the timeless description is merely a tool and the QRF dissolution loses philosophical weight.

**Recommended resolution:** Option A (process-primary) — becoming is fundamental; the WDW equation captures constraints on the determination process without being ontologically prior to it. This preserves the synthesis's distinctive contributions, which all depend on genuine becoming.

## QM Compatibility Matrix (E006 Attack 4)

- **Compatible:** Heisenberg/potentia, Kastner's transactional interpretation, objective collapse theories, some forms of relational QM
- **Incompatible:** Everettian many-worlds, pilot wave theory, strict instrumentalist Copenhagen
- **Unclear:** QBism (shares anti-realism about a single determinate reality but differs on ontological status of possibilities)

This significantly limits the synthesis's philosophical "market share." CI's great strength is that it requires zero metaphysical commitments beyond established physics.

## Honest Limits

1. **Hard problem of consciousness** — explains structure of temporal experience (retention-impression-protention) but not why this structure is accompanied by experience
2. **Ontological commitment to real potentialities** — genuine metaphysical claim not derivable from physics; someone rejecting it keeps CI's purely physical account. **Every improvement over CI traces to this single commitment (E006 Attack 4).** Incompatible with Everettian QM, pilot wave, strict Copenhagen.
3. **QRF-process tension** — the synthesis wants both genuine becoming AND timeless WDW as fundamental; these are in tension and a position must be taken (E006 Attack 5)
4. **"Determination" is a bridging concept, not a primitive** — proposes identification of CI's computation with CP's actualization; the identification is substantive but unproved (E006 Attack 1)
5. **Factorization problem** — presupposes decomposition into subsystems; in fundamental QG, this may itself be emergent
6. **No novel predictions** — explanatory value, not predictive; inherits CP's CST predictions but adds none
7. **Past Hypothesis not resolved** — the "natural initial condition" reframing is substantially verbal; the PH remains an assumed initial condition (E006 Attack 3)
8. **Prigogine dependency weakened but not eliminated** — LBB engine replaces Prigogine as primary mechanism, but ontological irreversibility claim is in same neighborhood

## The Final Statement

> **Time is irreversible determination — the ongoing, physically constrained process by which the indeterminate becomes determinate, as encountered from within.**

- "Irreversible" — LBB erasure + extinguished possibilities
- "Determination" — CI's "computation" and CP's "actualization" unified
- "Ongoing" — determination keeps happening; this IS flow
- "Physically constrained" — ML bound, Bekenstein bound, Landauer's principle
- "By which the indeterminate becomes determinate" — ontological heart; the "now" is where this happens; the arrow points toward increasing determination
- "As encountered from within" — QRF dissolution; becoming is not illusion but determination from inside
