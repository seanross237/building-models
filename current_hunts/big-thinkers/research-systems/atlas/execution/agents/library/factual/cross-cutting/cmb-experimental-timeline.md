## CMB-S4: CANCELLED (July 2025)

**Critical development:** In July 2025, the U.S. DOE and NSF jointly withdrew support for CMB-S4. The project is undergoing orderly shutdown.

- Original plan: σ(r) ≈ 0.0005, operations 2032-2033
- A revised half-cost Chile-only plan was proposed (June 2025) but rejected
- The CMB-S4 Science Collaboration (independent of the project) is developing alternative plans

**Impact:** CMB-S4 was expected to be the definitive experiment for QG+F vs. AS discrimination. Its cancellation shifts the timeline from "definitive by 2035" to "possible but less precise by 2036-2037" via SO + LiteBIRD.

## Near-Term: 2026-2028

### Simons Observatory (SO)
- 3 SATs began observations April-June 2024; LAT first light March 2025
- First r results expected ~2026-2027
- Target: σ(r) ≤ 0.003 (nominal 5-year survey with 3 SATs)
- 3 additional SATs being deployed by 2027 (total: 6 SATs)

### BICEP Array
- High-frequency receiver installed 2024-2025
- Target: σ(r) ≤ 0.003 with data through 2027
- Results expected ~2028

**What these test:** r > 0.01 detectable (strong AS signal); r ~ 0.003-0.005 marginal; r < 0.003 constrains AS.

## Medium-Term: 2028-2035

### Simons Observatory (Enhanced)
- 6 SATs + extended survey to mid-2030s
- Forecasted: σ(r) = 0.0012 (conservative, 10 years)
- Optimistic: σ(r) = 0.0007 (with 70% delensing)
- **First experiment that can distinguish QG+F from AS** if r ~ 0.005-0.01

### LiteBIRD (JAXA)
- Launch: JFY 2032 (early 2033)
- 3-year survey from Sun-Earth L2
- Target: σ(r) < 0.001 (including systematics)
- 15 frequency bands (34-448 GHz), 2.2 μK-arcmin at 0.5° resolution
- **First results: ~2036-2037**

## The "First Testable Prediction" Timeline

**First opportunity (2027-2028):** BICEP Array with σ(r) ~ 0.003
- Can detect r > 0.01 (strong AS signal)
- Cannot discriminate if r < 0.005

**Second opportunity (2030-2032):** SO enhanced (5+ years) with σ(r) ~ 0.001-0.002
- Can discriminate r = 0.003 (QG+F) from r = 0.01 (AS)
- **Earliest date for definitive QG+F vs. AS discrimination**

**Third opportunity (2036-2037):** LiteBIRD first results with σ(r) < 0.001
- Definitive measurement of r down to 0.001
- Can distinguish all QG+F variants from AS
- **Most reliable discrimination date**
