# Exploration 002 Summary: Deep Construction of the Causal-Processual Interpretation

## Goal
Build the Causal-Processual Interpretation in full: find the unifying principle, develop all six feature-explanations to full depth, and strengthen the weak points (flow, dilation, the "now").

## Outcome: Succeeded

The interpretation is now a unified, coherent framework organized around a single primitive: **actualization** — the irreversible creative transition from indeterminate possibility to determinate fact. The four traditions (CST, Whitehead, Prigogine, Smolin) are no longer separate modules but five perspectives on this single operation. Heisenberg's potentia (quantum measurement as potentiality→actuality) provides independent support from quantum foundations.

## Key Results

**Unifying principle found.** Actualization successfully unifies all components: CST element births, Whiteheadian concrescence, Prigoginian irreversible becoming, Smolin's temporal naturalism, and quantum measurement are all instances of the same primitive. The interpretation passes the coherence test.

**Scores improved.** Irreversibility: 4→5 (actualization gives a non-circular, non-statistical account). Flow: 3→4 (Husserlian retention-protention structure mapped onto Whiteheadian concrescence — the specious present IS concrescence from inside). Dilation: 3→4 (CST chain-length-as-proper-time provides physics grounding; dilation = varying density of actualization events). Total: 23→26/30.

**The "now" account is the strongest feature vs. CI.** Each actualization event IS a now while becoming. The present is not a slice but an active process. Past events are settled (fully actual); future events don't yet exist (merely potential). This ontological distinction — which CI lacks entirely — gives the causal-processual interpretation a genuine edge.

**Strongest advantages over CI:** (1) No Past Hypothesis needed for directionality — the arrow is intrinsic to actualization. (2) Genuine phenomenological account of flow with the concrescence-specious-present bridge. (3) Ontological "now." (4) Novel predictions (CST discreteness, cosmological natural selection).

**CI's advantages remain:** (1) Stronger physics grounding (ML, LBB, Wolpert vs. speculative CST + philosophical Whitehead). (2) Less metaphysically exposed. (3) More parsimonious.

## Key Takeaway

The causal-processual interpretation is now a genuine competitor to CI. The tournament should be genuinely contested — CI is safer and better-grounded in established physics, but CP is deeper on directionality, flow, and the "now." The question is whether depth or safety matters more for an interpretation of time. The Strategizer should proceed to Exploration 003 (steel-manning / adversarial attack) to stress-test the interpretation before the tournament.
