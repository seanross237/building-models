# Exploration 007: Surfaceology — The Unifying Framework

## Mission Context

Through 6 prior explorations, we've established:
1. The amplituhedron computes N=4 SYM amplitudes from geometry (verified computationally)
2. Positive geometry has a two-tier structure: full amplituhedron for N=4/ABJM only; partial geometry broadly applicable at tree level
3. Within N=4 SYM, the amplituhedron is a reformulation; beyond it, three genuine physical contributions exist
4. Hidden zeros (2024-2025) unify phi³/pion/gluon amplitudes and can fix 1-loop integrands from geometry
5. The EFT-hedron gives real-world constraints on Wilson coefficients

Exploration 005 identified "surfaceology" as the COMMON PARENT framework unifying the amplituhedron, hidden zeros, and potentially all positive geometry approaches. This exploration fills the last major gap in our understanding.
