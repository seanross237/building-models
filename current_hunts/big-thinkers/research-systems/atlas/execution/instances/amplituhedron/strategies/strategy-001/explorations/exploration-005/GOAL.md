# Exploration 005: Hidden Zeros — The Frontier of Positive Geometry Beyond N=4 SYM

## Mission Context

We're investigating whether the amplituhedron is just a calculator or reveals deeper physics. Prior explorations established: (1) within N=4 SYM, it's a reformulation, (2) the UV finiteness wall limits the full amplituhedron to N=4/ABJM, (3) BUT three genuine physical contributions exist beyond N=4 SYM.

The most exciting of these is **hidden zeros** — a discovery (2024-2025) that amplitudes in phi³, NLSM (pions), and Yang-Mills share a common structure of kinematic zeros, revealed geometrically from the ABHY associahedron. A March 2025 paper (arXiv:2503.03805) shows that at 1-loop in non-SUSY Tr(phi³), locality and unitarity EMERGE from imposing hidden zeros on a generic ansatz.

## Your Goal

**Deep dive into the hidden zeros program. Understand precisely what hidden zeros are, why they imply locality/unitarity, how far the program extends, and what the obstacles to further extension are. Assess whether this represents a genuinely new principle of QFT or is a reformulation of existing constraints.**

## Specific Questions

1. **What are hidden zeros?** At which specific kinematic configurations do amplitudes vanish? What is the geometric origin (ABHY associahedron boundary structure)? Give the simplest concrete example (e.g., n=5 bi-adjoint scalar).

2. **The 2024 discovery** (arXiv:2312.16282): How are phi³, pions, and gluon amplitudes related through hidden zeros? What is the "shift" that connects them? Is this a trivial kinematic relation or a deep structural fact?

3. **The March 2025 paper** (arXiv:2503.03805): How exactly does imposing hidden zeros at 1-loop force the integrand to be local and unitary? What is the starting ansatz? How constraining are the hidden zeros? Could you get the same result by imposing other physical conditions (unitarity cuts, for example)?

4. **Scope of extension:** Does this work for 2-loop? For higher multiplicity? For theories beyond Tr(phi³)? What are the concrete obstacles?

5. **Relationship to the amplituhedron:** Is the hidden zeros program a competitor to the amplituhedron, or a generalization? Can hidden zeros reproduce the same amplitudes the amplituhedron computes?

6. **Novel predictions:** Do hidden zeros predict anything NEW — relations between amplitudes, constraints on theories, or observable consequences not known from standard QFT?

7. **The "surfaceology" connection** (2024): How do surfaces/curves/tropical geometry relate to hidden zeros? Is this the same program or a separate development?

## Key Papers to Read

- arXiv:2312.16282 — "Hidden Zeros for Particle/String Amplitudes and the Unity of Colored Scalars, Pions and Gluons" (the original hidden zeros paper)
- arXiv:2503.03805 — "Emergence of Unitarity and Locality from Hidden Zeros at One-Loop Order" (March 2025)
- arXiv:1711.09102 — Arkani-Hamed, Bai, He, Yan "Scattering Forms and the Positive Geometry of Kinematics" (ABHY associahedron)
- Any 2024-2026 follow-up papers on hidden zeros or surfaceology

## Success Criteria

- **SUCCESS**: Clear explanation of hidden zeros mechanism with concrete examples. Honest assessment of scope and limitations. Understanding of how far the program can extend.
- **PARTIAL**: Good explanation but unclear on the 2025 paper's claim or limitations.
- **FAILURE**: Vague discussion without specific examples or honest limitations.
