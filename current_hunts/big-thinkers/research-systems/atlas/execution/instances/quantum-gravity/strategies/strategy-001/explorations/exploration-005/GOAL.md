# Exploration 005: Entanglement-First Quantum Gravity — Can Jacobson + Ryu-Takayanagi Determine a Unique Theory?

## Mission Context

Our main line of investigation (spectral dimension → quadratic gravity + fakeon) converged on an existing research program. We now pursue a SECOND independent direction from our constraint map.

Exploration 001 identified two "underexploited constructive axioms":
1. ✅ Spectral dimension d_s → 2 (explored in exp. 002-003 — led to quadratic gravity + fakeon)
2. ❓ **Jacobson's derivation + Ryu-Takayanagi** as dual axioms — THIS exploration

**Background:**
- **Jacobson (1995):** Einstein's field equations can be derived from thermodynamics of local Rindler horizons: δQ = TdS with S = A/(4G) and T = Unruh temperature → Einstein equations
- **Jacobson (2015):** Extended to derive Einstein equations from ENTANGLEMENT equilibrium: δS_entanglement = 0 for small causal diamonds → Einstein equations (with the UV-finite vacuum entanglement entropy replacing the Bekenstein-Hawking area term)
- **Ryu-Takayanagi (2006):** In holographic theories, entanglement entropy of a boundary region = area of minimal bulk surface: S_A = Area(γ_min)/(4G_N)

These results suggest: if we ASSUME (a) entanglement entropy obeys an area law, and (b) entanglement equilibrium determines field equations, what theory is forced?

## Your Goal

Investigate whether treating entanglement-area law + entanglement equilibrium as constructive axioms determines a unique (or nearly unique) quantum gravity theory, analogous to how d_s = 2 determined quadratic gravity.

**IMPORTANT: Write your report incrementally, section by section.**

### Specific Questions

1. **Jacobson's 2015 result:** What exactly does "entanglement equilibrium" assume, and what does it derive? What is the UV structure needed? Does it require a specific form of entanglement entropy (area law, or more general)?

2. **From entanglement to field equations:** If we assume S_ent = α · Area + corrections, what field equations follow? Are they uniquely Einstein's equations, or could higher-derivative theories also emerge?

3. **Higher-derivative gravity from entanglement:** Can the Jacobson program be extended to derive quadratic gravity (R² + C²) from modified entanglement entropy? The Wald entropy for higher-derivative theories has additional terms — if we impose Wald entropy = entanglement entropy, what action follows?

4. **Connection to our main result:** Can this direction independently arrive at quadratic gravity + fakeon? Or does it point to a different theory?

5. **The "gravity from entanglement" program (2015-2026):** What is the state of the art? Have Swingle, Van Raamsdonk, Faulkner, or others derived specific actions from entanglement principles? Any breakthroughs in 2024-2026?

6. **Novel theory potential:** If the entanglement axioms lead to a DIFFERENT theory from quadratic gravity, that's interesting too — it would mean different constraints select different theories, and we need to understand why.
