## A. Viable Conceptual Moves Extracted

### Move 1: Computational Cost as Physical Interpretation of the Fakeon
**The idea:** The Pedraza et al. (2023) result shows δC/δg = 0 gives full nonlinear Einstein equations. If gravity IS cost optimization, perhaps the fakeon reflects a computational constraint — certain modes of propagation are "computationally forbidden" because they would violate the optimization. The fakeon is a "dead branch" in the computation: it contributes to the cost accounting (loop integrals) but never manifests as a physical process (no asymptotic state).

**Connection to Gap #1 (Fakeon Interpretation):** DIRECT. This would give a physical story: the fakeon is the price of computational self-consistency. Nature doesn't propagate certain modes because doing so would violate the variational principle δC/δg = 0. The purely-virtual nature reflects that these modes are "virtual computations" — they contribute to the cost landscape but never realize as trajectories.

**Novelty assessment:** 7/10. The specific connection "fakeon = computationally forbidden mode" has not been proposed. Pedraza's result is established (in 2D). The gap is: can computational cost arguments constrain QUANTIZATION prescriptions, not just Lagrangians?

**Key obstacle:** The negative result — classical cost functions CANNOT distinguish Stelle gravity from QG+F. The cost function operates at the Lagrangian level; the fakeon operates at the quantization level. A "quantum cost function" would be needed. This is the deepest obstacle and would need to be the core novelty of any theory built on this move.

**Viability:** MODERATE-HIGH. The obstacle is clear but perhaps attackable. If a "quantum cost function" could be defined — one that constrains propagator structure, not just classical geometry — this would be genuinely novel.

### Move 2: Indivisibility as the Origin of the Fakeon Prescription
**The idea:** Barandes-Doukas show that quantum mechanics IS the mathematical description of "indivisible" stochastic processes — processes whose multi-time correlations cannot be decomposed into sequential steps. The fakeon prescription also enforces a kind of indivisibility: the massive spin-2 mode cannot be separated into real particle production events. Perhaps the fakeon is the gravitational manifestation of stochastic indivisibility — nature's computation is globally coherent (indivisible), and modes that would require decomposing into real intermediate states are forbidden.

**Connection to Gap #1 (Fakeon Interpretation):** DIRECT but speculative. The fakeon's defining property (purely virtual, no asymptotic state) maps onto indivisibility's defining property (no Markovian decomposition). Both say: "you can't break this process into separate real steps."

**Connection to Gap #2 (Non-Perturbative Completion):** If indivisibility is the origin, then QG+F's non-perturbative completion would be a fully indivisible stochastic gravitational process — providing the "gravitational confinement" picture.

**Novelty assessment:** 8/10. The connection "fakeon = gravitational indivisibility" is genuinely new. Nobody has proposed this.

**Key obstacle:** Barandes lifting is isomorphism, not derivation. Indivisibility may just be QM restated. If "indivisibility → fakeon" is just "QM → fakeon" with extra steps, it's not explanatory. The test: does the indivisibility framework predict WHICH modes must be fakeons (i.e., derive that the massive spin-2 is purely virtual while the massless spin-2 and massive spin-0 propagate)?

**Viability:** MODERATE. Conceptually appealing but risks being reformulation rather than explanation. Need to check: can indivisibility constraints, applied to a stochastic gravitational process, constrain propagator structure in a way that selects the fakeon prescription?

### Move 3: Irreducible Noise as Microcausality Violation
**The idea:** SCG's axiom 5 (irreducible noise σ > 0) states there is fundamental randomness at the deepest level. QG+F's microcausality violation means fields don't commute at spacelike separation below the Planck scale. If spacetime has irreducible stochastic fluctuations at σ ~ 1/M₂, this noise naturally breaks strict commutativity — events at "spacelike separation" can be connected through noise channels, and the commutator picks up noise-induced correlations.

**Connection to Gap #1 (Fakeon Interpretation):** INDIRECT. Microcausality violation is the fakeon's most distinctive signature. If noise explains microcausality violation, and microcausality violation requires the fakeon, then noise provides the physical substrate for the fakeon.

**Connection to Oppenheim:** The decoherence-diffusion trade-off has a similar structure — stochastic fluctuations of the gravitational field cause both decoherence and diffusion. The noise amplitude connects directly to measurable effects (decoherence rate, diffusion rate).

**Novelty assessment:** 5/10. Stochastic gravity (Hu-Verdaguer) has explored noise-induced effects for decades. The specific connection to QG+F's microcausality violation is somewhat novel, but the framework is well-trodden.

**Key obstacle:** QG+F's microcausality violation is mathematically precise (from the fakeon propagator), while stochastic noise effects are phenomenological. Mapping one onto the other requires showing they're the same mathematical structure, not just conceptually similar.

**Viability:** LOW-MODERATE. The conceptual connection is there but the technical bridge is unclear.

### Move 4: Finite Configuration Space as Non-Perturbative Regulator
**The idea:** SCG posits a finite configuration space Ω with N ~ e^{10^122} states. QG+F is perturbative — defined by Feynman diagrams around classical backgrounds. If QG+F is the continuum limit of a theory on a finite configuration space, then the finite space provides the non-perturbative definition the theory lacks. Ghost confinement would be a finite-N effect: on a finite space, the ghost mode is automatically bounded (can't propagate to infinity), and in the continuum limit this boundedness becomes the fakeon prescription.

**Connection to Gap #2 (Non-Perturbative Completion):** DIRECT. This is exactly what's needed — a non-perturbative framework whose continuum limit gives QG+F.

**Connection to Gap #1 (Fakeon Interpretation):** If ghost confinement is a finite-N effect, the fakeon gets a physical interpretation: it's the perturbative shadow of a mode that's confined to finite regions of configuration space.

**Novelty assessment:** 6/10. The idea of gravity on finite configuration spaces exists in CDT and causal set theory. The specific connection to QG+F's non-perturbative completion via ghost confinement is somewhat novel.

**Key obstacle:** SCG's continuum limit is unproven. Generic cost functions on finite spaces do NOT give manifolds (Gromov-Hausdorff). There is no 4D mechanism. This was a NEAR-FATAL problem for SCG and remains unsolved.

**Viability:** LOW. The continuum limit problem is too hard for a focused theory. This is a multi-decade research program, not a sprint target.

### Move 5: Causal Order as the Fakeon's Origin
**The idea:** SCG v2.0's key innovation was replacing symmetric cost with causal partial order + directed cost + volume measure. Via Malament's theorem, causal order determines conformal geometry. The fakeon prescription breaks time-reversal symmetry in the propagator (it uses neither Feynman's time-ordered nor the retarded/advanced prescriptions). Perhaps the fakeon IS the gravitational manifestation of causal order — it's the prescription that respects the partial order structure of spacetime.

**Connection to Gap #1 (Fakeon Interpretation):** DIRECT. If the fakeon arises from causal structure, it gets a physical interpretation: the fakeon is how the propagator encodes the fact that spacetime has a causal (rather than symmetric) structure. The analyticity sacrifice (fakeon → no analytic S-matrix) would correspond to the non-analyticity of causal structure (causal order is a discrete, non-smooth relation).

**Connection to Anselmi's causality essay:** Anselmi (Jan 2026) proposes ABANDONING causality as fundamental. This is the exact opposite — proposing causality as the ORIGIN of the fakeon. The two views may be complementary: the fakeon violates microcausality (Anselmi) BECAUSE it enforces macro-causality (causal order).

**Novelty assessment:** 7/10. The connection "causal order → fakeon" has not been proposed explicitly. Closest: Anselmi's own work on classicized dynamics where time ordering is modified, but he doesn't connect this to causal set theory.

**Key obstacle:** Causal partial orders are classical mathematical structures. The fakeon is a quantum prescription. The same classical-vs-quantum gap as Move 1 applies. How does a classical causal order constrain a quantum propagator?

**Viability:** MODERATE-HIGH. Conceptually strongest of all moves. The obstacle (classical → quantum bridge) is shared with other moves but may be more tractable here because causal order is a GLOBAL constraint on the theory, not a local one. The fakeon prescription is also global (it modifies the integration contour for all momenta). Global ↔ global matching may be easier than local ↔ local.

### Move 6: Complexity Plateau as Singularity Resolution
**The idea:** SCG proposed that spacetime complexity plateaus at a maximum value (related to the scrambling time), replacing the classical singularity with a "complexity wall." In QG+F, the fakeon selects Schwarzschild and goes silent about the singularity. If the Pedraza cost optimization is extended, the cost function naturally saturates at extreme curvatures, preventing the singularity from forming.

**Connection to Gap #4 (Black Hole Interior):** DIRECT. This would give QG+F a singularity resolution mechanism without requiring non-perturbative completion.

**Novelty assessment:** 4/10. Complexity saturation as singularity resolution has been discussed (Susskind, Brown-Susskind "second law of complexity"). Not very novel.

**Key obstacle:** Pedraza's result is 2D only. Extension to 4D is unproven. Also, this doesn't address the fakeon gap — it targets a different debt.

**Viability:** LOW for our purposes. Targets Gap #4, not the primary Gap #1.

## B. Technical Constraints Any Future Theory Must Respect

Hard negative results from strategy-001 that constrain the solution space:

1. **Classical cost functions CANNOT select QG+F.** The deepest obstruction. The Lagrangian L = R + αC² + βR² is IDENTICAL for Stelle gravity and QG+F. The fakeon is a quantization choice. Any theory built on classical optimization (cost functions, variational principles, geometric observables) will inevitably produce Stelle gravity, not QG+F. **Implication:** The fakeon's origin must be QUANTUM — it cannot emerge from any classical principle.

2. **Complexity = Anything (Belin et al. 2021).** There is an infinite class of equally viable complexity functionals satisfying all known constraints (linear growth, switchback, diffeomorphism invariance, positivity). Any construction based on "minimize complexity" must address this extreme degeneracy. **Implication:** If cost optimization is the path, you need a SELECTION PRINCIPLE for the cost function itself.

3. **Barandes lifting is isomorphism, not derivation.** The stochastic-quantum correspondence runs both ways. Born rule is definitional. Indivisibility may smuggle in QM. **Implication:** Any claim of "deriving QM from something deeper" must pass the Aaronson test: "What does it buy me that I didn't already have?"

4. **Causal structure is mandatory.** Positive-definite structures cannot produce Lorentzian geometry. This killed SCG v1.0. **Implication:** Any theory with emergent spacetime must build in causal order from the start.

5. **Pedraza is 2D only.** The Einstein tensor vanishes identically in 2D, so the 2D → 4D gap is not merely technical. **Implication:** Claims based on Pedraza should be treated as "suggestive" not "proven."

## C. Failure Patterns from Strategy-001

### Anti-Pattern 1: Over-Ambitious Synthesis
SCG combined Barandes + Pedraza + Oppenheim + causal sets + Nelson mechanics into one theory. Result: creative packaging, zero unique predictions, lost 16/21 against QG+F. **Lesson:** Don't synthesize 5 programs. Build ONE novel mechanism.

### Anti-Pattern 2: Claiming Inherited Predictions
SCG listed 6 "predictions" — every one was inherited from a component program. None was unique to SCG. **Lesson:** A novel theory must produce at least one prediction that is NOT derivable from any component program.

### Anti-Pattern 3: Classical → Quantum Leap
SCG and the cost function work tried to derive quantum properties (fakeon) from classical structures (cost functions, variational principles). This is structurally impossible. **Lesson:** Don't try to derive the fakeon from anything classical. The novel mechanism must be quantum from the start.

### Anti-Pattern 4: Late Devil's Advocate
SCG's fatal Lorentzian flaw was found in exploration 5, after 3 explorations building the theory. It could have been found in exploration 4 (right after construction). **Lesson:** Attack the theory immediately after the first construction.

### Anti-Pattern 5: Multi-Task Explorations
Exploration 006 tried to research + rewrite + fix multiple issues. It timed out. **Lesson:** One task per exploration, always.

## D. Design Brief for Phase 2 Concept Sprint

Based on the gap analysis (Exploration 001) and the viable moves extracted above, I recommend these 3 targets for Phase 2's concept sprints:

**Core concept:** The fakeon prescription is the propagator's encoding of spacetime's causal partial order. Analyticity sacrifice corresponds to the non-analyticity of causal structure. Microcausality violation at Planck scales reflects the transition from continuous to discrete causal structure.

**Novelty check needed:** Has anyone proposed causal order → fakeon? Search for Anselmi + causal structure, Sorkin + quadratic gravity, Dowker + fakeon.

#### Sprint 2: INDIVISIBILITY-BASED QUANTUM FAKEON (Move 2)
**Target gap:** #1 (Fakeon Interpretation) + #2 (Non-Perturbative Completion)
**Core concept:** The fakeon is the gravitational manifestation of stochastic indivisibility — modes that would require decomposing into real intermediate states are forbidden because the underlying stochastic process is indivisible. Ghost confinement = the non-perturbative expression of indivisibility.
**Why this sprint:** Highest novelty (8/10). Addresses both top gaps simultaneously. Novel specific claim: indivisibility constraints, applied to a gravitational stochastic process, select the fakeon prescription over Lee-Wick and Feynman.
**Novelty check needed:** Is there ANY work connecting indivisibility/non-Markovianity to ghost freedom or the fakeon?
**Risk:** May be reformulation (indivisibility ≈ QM ≈ unitarity → fakeon is just "unitarity selects the fakeon" restated). The Aaronson test must be passed.

#### Sprint 3: INFORMATION-THEORETIC FAKEON FROM ENTANGLEMENT STRUCTURE (New Move)
**Target gap:** #1 (Fakeon Interpretation) + #5 (Gravitational Thermodynamics)
**Core concept:** The fakeon prescription is REQUIRED for consistent entanglement structure across horizons. The MVEH construction (maximal vacuum entanglement → QG+F) already hints at this: modular flow unitarity demands the fakeon. Push this further: the fakeon is what prevents entanglement entropy from diverging in the UV. **Why this sprint:** Directly extends the most rigorous existing result (MVEH → QG+F via modular unitarity). Connects to the deepest physics (BH entropy, holography, information paradox). Could produce predictions (entanglement entropy corrections from the fakeon).
**Novelty check needed:** Has anyone pushed MVEH beyond the linearized level? Is there work connecting analyticity sacrifice to entanglement structure?
**Risk:** The MVEH construction only gives linearized results (Bueno et al. 2017). Extending to nonlinear may hit technical barriers.
