# Exploration 001 Goal - Draft The Candidate Definition Family

## Objective

Write a bounded family of exact candidate notions of `near-closed Tao circuit`
that inherit the Step-1 freezes and are sharp enough to be real test objects.

## Success Criteria

- Promote at most four candidate notions.
- Each candidate must live on a finite role-labeled helical packet class with
  mandatory conjugate completion.
- Each candidate must state one exact formula or finite checklist, one fixed
  finite window, one threshold language, and one declared sign / amplitude /
  phase sheet.
- At least one candidate must remain honestly friendly to a pro-near-circuit
  outcome if that can be done without importing Tao's averaged construction by
  hand.
- Reject any "candidate" that is only a mechanism label, only a pressure
  slogan, or only a threshold-tuned paraphrase.

## Failure Criteria

- Every candidate stays verbal or cosmetic.
- No candidate can be written without changing Step-1 packet semantics or
  canonicalization policy.
- The only pro-near-circuit-friendly candidate works only by hard-coding Tao's
  averaged coefficients.
