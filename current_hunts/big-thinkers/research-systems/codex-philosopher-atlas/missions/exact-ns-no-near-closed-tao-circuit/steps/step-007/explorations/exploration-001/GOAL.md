# Exploration 001 Goal - Freeze The Single Witness And Controlling Authority Sheet

## Objective

Freeze the single carried witness `F_SS(1/12)` on the canonical one-bridge
role-labeled helical packet ledger, and restate the controlling repaired
Step-6 authority sheets for `G_tmpl` and `G_leak` without reopening any packet
semantics, threshold choice, or discarded itinerary content.

The report must answer:

- what exact packet object on the frozen ledger is being called `F_SS(1/12)`;
- which normalization, sign / phase / role conventions, mandatory conjugate
  completion rule, and fixed window define that object;
- which local source paths are the authoritative repaired threshold sheets for
  `G_tmpl` and `G_leak`;
- where the earlier threshold drift appears, especially the `L_cross`
  mismatch; and
- what same-currency comparison rule later steps must inherit.

## Success Criteria

- One unambiguous witness freeze lands for `F_SS(1/12)`, not the family
  `F_SS(mu)`.
- One explicit authority memo lands for repaired `G_tmpl` and repaired
  `G_leak`.
- The earlier `L_cross` disagreement is logged as historical variance only,
  with one later source resolving it for this branch.
- The report states one honest same-currency rule for later closure and
  dynamics.

## Failure Criteria

- The witness can only be described by sliding back to `F_SS(mu)`.
- The threshold sheet still depends on choosing between conflicting records as
  a live branch decision.
- The authority memo needs a new bookkeeping currency, new packet semantics, or
  revived itinerary content.
