## Reusable Lesson

- If the repository lacks an explicit wavevector/helicity realization,
  an exact coefficient ledger,
  and a finite-closure convention on the same currency, that absence is itself
  a valid Step-1 endpoint.
  Do not patch the gap with family-shape heuristics or helper arithmetic.

## Caution

- Family-level screening dossiers can look concrete enough to tempt promotion,
  especially when they carry normalized budgets and friendly witnesses.
  Without same-currency derivation or citation paths, they remain screening
  artifacts rather than theorem authority.
