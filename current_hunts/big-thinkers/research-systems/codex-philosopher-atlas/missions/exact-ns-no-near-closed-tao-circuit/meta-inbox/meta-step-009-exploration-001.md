# Meta Note - Step 9 Exploration 001

## What Worked

- Separating canonical packet semantics from witness-local screening data and
  from Step-8 classwise burden kept the inherited ledger honest.
- Reusing the Step-7 witness freeze together with the Step-8 stop verdict made
  it possible to say exactly what Step 9 may inherit unchanged without quietly
  importing a new realization.

## Reusable Lesson

- When a branch inherits a sharp packet sheet but no explicit realization, make
  the open-freedom boundary explicit before any recursive closure or theorem
  language begins.
  Packet semantics,
  normalization,
  and burden classes can be frozen even when wavevectors, helicities, and
  closure convention are still open.

## Caution

- Symbolic sign-sheet names and classwise burden totals are not the same thing
  as one explicit wavevector/helicity realization.
  Treat them as packet-sheet or screening data only unless a same-currency
  derivation is actually on disk.
