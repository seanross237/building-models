# Meta Note - Step 5 Exploration 001

## What Worked

- Treating the final Step-4 result as the controlling dossier kept the repair
  pass from drifting back into disagreement about unfinished intermediate
  reports.
- Tightening only to the worst recorded friendly witness removed bookkeeping
  slack without changing the packet sheet or inventing new anti-family data.

## Reusable Lesson

- On a frozen exact ledger, the clean repair rule is:
  tighten each admissibility threshold to the largest recorded friendly value,
  and leave any already-saturated budget unchanged rather than pretending more
  tightening exists.

## Caution

- Do not fabricate a sharper hostile coefficient table just because the final
  branch verdict already says the hostile family fails.
  If the failure mode is already earned, use that earned failure mode and only
  tighten from the recorded margins/gaps.
