# Meta Note — Step 1 Exploration 001

## What Worked

- The inherited Atlas packet was already strong enough to freeze a
  mechanism-level feature ledger without returning to broad Tao-paper survey
  mode.
- Separating Tao's internal mechanism from the new branch's leakage-testing
  policy avoided conflating inherited facts with Step-1 scope-lock choices.

## Reusable Lesson

- When a branch wants a new exact object from an inherited mechanism memo,
  freeze the role ledger first:
  stage order,
  trigger logic,
  amplitude hierarchy,
  time-scale separation,
  and support semantics.
  Do not start from candidate formulas.

## Caution

- `admissible leakage` is not usually an inherited theorem fact.
  It is often a branch-level policy choice that must be stated explicitly
  instead of being smuggled in as if it came from the original mechanism.
