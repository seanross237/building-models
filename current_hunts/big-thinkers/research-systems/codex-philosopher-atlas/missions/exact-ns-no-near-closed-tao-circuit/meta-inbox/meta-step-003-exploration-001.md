# Meta Note - Step 3 Exploration 001

## What Worked

- Reusing the Step-1 packet-language freeze and the Step-2 candidate sheets was
  enough to turn the Step-3 robustness screen into one three-tier taxonomy
  instead of another abstract invariance debate.
- Keeping the true-symmetry tier minimal prevented the branch from demanding a
  luxury notion of coordinate-freeness that the mission never required.

## Reusable Lesson

- Once a branch has already frozen one canonical packet sheet, later robustness
  work should distinguish:
  true exact symmetries,
  dependence on that frozen canonicalization,
  and genuine model drift.
  Otherwise a branch can mistake `depends on the chosen honest ledger` for
  `mathematically nonexistent`.

## Caution

- Sign-sheet, threshold, spectator-partition, and time-window changes are not
  rescued by calling them canonicalization after the fact.
  If those items move after outcomes are inspected, the branch has changed the
  packet model.
