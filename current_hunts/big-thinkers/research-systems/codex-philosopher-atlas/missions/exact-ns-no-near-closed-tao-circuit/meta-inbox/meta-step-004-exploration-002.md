# Meta Note - Step 4 Exploration 002

## What Worked

- Separating the static Beltrami family from the live degenerate / tiny-trigger
  family kept the anti-circuit dossier honest.
  The first is a filter test; the second is the actual obstruction-facing
  evidence.
- The classwise leakage vector forced the anti-circuit read to name which
  spectators break the packet, not just that "entanglement happens."

## Reusable Lesson

- Static cancellation cases should be used to test admissibility filters, not
  to carry the main obstruction verdict.
  Live anti-families that keep the desired channels active but misordered are
  far more informative.

## Caution

- Near-degenerate geometry is only useful when the report also says what
  remains live and what breaks first.
  Otherwise it becomes another zero-activity excuse case rather than a real
  packet-level failure mechanism.
