# Step 2 Exploration 001 - Candidate Family Memo

## Promoted Candidate Menu

- `[INFERRED]` `Template-Defect Near-Closure`
  compares the exact role-projected NS vector field on one canonical
  role-labeled helical packet against a fixed five-channel Tao-like template on
  one finite activation window.
- `[INFERRED]` `Windowed Spectator-Leakage Budget`
  compares the integrated magnitude of all forced off-template channels against
  the integrated magnitude of the desired five channels, class by class.
- `[INFERRED]` `Delayed-Threshold Itinerary`
  asks for one exact finite ordered activation itinerary
  `clock -> trigger -> rotor -> next-stage transfer`
  with all spectator budgets predeclared.

## Why These Three And Not More

- `[VERIFIED]` The local Step-2 packet and the receptionist search log both
  pointed to the same bounded split:
  coefficient-rigidity / channel-isolation,
  leakage-based,
  and behavior-based notions.
- `[INFERRED]` A separate `projection-rigidity only` candidate was rejected
  because the local record treats exact Leray / pressure projection as the
  enforcement mechanism behind the promoted candidates, not as a distinct
  near-closure object.

## Core Reusable Lesson

- `[INFERRED]` A Step-2 candidate becomes real only when it names:
  one exact packet class,
  one finite window,
  one threshold language,
  and one canonical sign / amplitude / phase sheet.
  Without those, the candidate is still a slogan.
