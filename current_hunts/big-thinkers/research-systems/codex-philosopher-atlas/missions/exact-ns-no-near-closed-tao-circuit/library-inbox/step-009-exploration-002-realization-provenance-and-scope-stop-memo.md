## Findings Log

### 1. Promoted authority exists for the packet sheet and screening ledger, not for an explicit audited realization

- `[VERIFIED]` The local record does contain promoted authority for:
  the canonical one-bridge packet semantics,
  mandatory conjugate completion,
  normalization anchors,
  desired Tao-like core,
  classwise forced-extra categories,
  the carried witness identity `F_SS(1/12)`,
  and the repaired Step-6 screening sheets `G_tmpl` and `G_leak`.
- `[VERIFIED]` The same record also contains the Step-8 classwise closure-burden
  verdict and the explicit statement that no finite exact closed subsystem is
  yet frozen for `F_SS(1/12)`.
- `[INFERRED]` So the repository is **not** empty on Step-9 inputs.
  It already authorizes the background ledger and the scope guardrails.
  The gap is narrower:
  promoted same-currency realization authority for an exact audited object.

### 2. Draft helpers and runtime artifacts are not theorem authority

- `[VERIFIED]` Run-003 final control explicitly says that any realization data,
  coefficient sheet, or wavevector-level ledger used in the audit must come
  with one same-currency promoted derivation or citation path, and that draft
  logs, stale arithmetic, or hand-entered checkers are not enough.
- `[VERIFIED]` Step-2 exploration 002 likewise states that writing a fully
  explicit helical coefficient formula from memory would have been a **source
  guess**, not a repository-grounded finding.
- `[VERIFIED]` The carried helper artifacts,
  such as runtime logs and
  `step-004/explorations/exploration-003/code/pro_circuit_dossier_check.py`,
  may support orientation or arithmetic checking, but they are not promoted
  same-currency authority for theorem-facing realization data.
- `[INFERRED]` That distinction matters here because most of the tempting
  realization content on disk is family-shape or helper-level, not promoted
  derivation.

### 3. The explicit realization source basis is still missing on every decisive item

- `[VERIFIED]` The repository still does **not** pin one explicit wavevector
  family for the five roles.
  This is stated directly in the Step-2 and Step-8 records and repeated in the
  run-003 attack packet.
- `[VERIFIED]` The repository still does **not** pin one fixed helicity tuple /
  wavevector-level sign assignment for the carried audited object.
  The on-disk sign sheets are symbolic family labels and qualitative sign
  patterns, not one explicit wavevector/helicity realization.
- `[VERIFIED]` The repository still does **not** pin one exact coefficient
  ledger

  ```text
  C_{k,p,q}^{σ_k,σ_p,σ_q}
  ```

  for all desired and forced triads.
- `[VERIFIED]` The repository still does **not** freeze one finite
  packet-closure convention.
  Step 8 identifies this as the decisive local gap behind exact
  non-isolability.
- `[INFERRED]` The repository also lacks one explicit theorem-facing sheet of
  coefficient-zero or degeneracy subvarieties for a named audited realization.
  Degenerate / cancellation-sensitive regimes are discussed as risks and future
  audit duties, but no explicit exceptional-set ledger is promoted yet.
- `[INFERRED]` Therefore the Step-9 realization burden fails at the exact place
  the new chain was designed to test:
  the record has packet semantics and classwise burden,
  but not one same-currency exact realization protocol.

- `[VERIFIED]` `F_SS(mu)` and `F_SL(rho)` are real family labels on the Step-4

- `[VERIFIED]` But the Step-4 and Step-6 records use those families as
  screening or calibration objects on the normalized exact-packet ledger, not
  as theorem-grade wavevector/helicity realizations with promoted coefficient
  authority.
- `[VERIFIED]` Step-6 exploration 002 explicitly says that exact family-level
  formulas and same-currency transfer statements are still missing before any
  honest theorem or counterexample claim can be made.
- `[INFERRED]` So Step 9 cannot honestly promote:
  one audited family from `F_SS(mu)` or `F_SL(rho)`,
  nor one single audited realization from `F_SS(1/12)`,
  without importing exactly the missing source basis that the record still
