# Meta-Learning: Step 006, Exploration 002

- A vorticity-side reformulation can look more Navier-Stokes-specific than a
  velocity-side identity while still being Tao-insufficient inside a fixed LEI
  audit. Variable choice is not yet a discriminator.
- The useful diagnostic question for vorticity rewrites is:
  "Is this still acting on the frozen bad term, or has it quietly become a
  stretching / geometry branch?"
- When the only apparent rescue for a candidate is architecture drift, that
  should be recorded as evidence against continuation rather than as a near
  miss.
