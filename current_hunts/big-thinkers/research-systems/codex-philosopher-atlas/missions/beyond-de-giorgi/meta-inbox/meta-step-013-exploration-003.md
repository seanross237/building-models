# Meta Note - Step 013 Exploration 003

- Anti-repackaging classification should be defined by net effect on the full
  theorem-facing burden after all debt is restored, not by how novel the
  variable or notation looks beforehand.
- Prior-art audits are strongest when they force one nearest comparator, one
  exact shared structure, one exact mathematical delta, and one same-currency
  cash-out or transfer lemma.
- `ambiguous redistribution` is a real middle bucket: keep it only when the
  candidate points to one exact missing transfer step rather than vague future
  promise.
