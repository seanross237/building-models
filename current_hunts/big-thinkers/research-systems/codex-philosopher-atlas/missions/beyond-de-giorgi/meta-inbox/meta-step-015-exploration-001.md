# Meta Note - Step 015 Exploration 001

- On criterion-first geometry branches, freeze the endpoint family to the most
  concrete theorem-facing criterion already named on disk, not to a mechanism
  slogan like `full stretching` unless the repository has already frozen that
  slogan into a real theorem endpoint.
- Once the endpoint is a criterion family, force the gain currency to be
  `criterion input`; this prevents Step 1 from surviving on package-native
  stretching language that never cashes out at the endpoint.
- A useful negative outcome is still possible even when a candidate claim is
  writable: record whether the claim is only a proxy-level insertion line
  rather than an earned theorem statement.
