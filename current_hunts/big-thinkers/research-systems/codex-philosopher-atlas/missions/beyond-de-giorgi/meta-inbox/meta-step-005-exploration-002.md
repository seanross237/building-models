# Meta-Learning: Step 005, Exploration 002

- In rewrite audits, the main compatibility risk is often operator placement
  after localization, not the existence of a weak-solution class. That deserves
  to be asked directly.
- Freezing a cutoff protocol before touching rewrite formulas prevented the
  branch from quietly rewarding candidates that move the cutoff inside a
  projector or singular integral.
- A good standing question for future branches is:
  "Which exact identities remain exact after localization, and what commutator
  debt appears when they do not?"
