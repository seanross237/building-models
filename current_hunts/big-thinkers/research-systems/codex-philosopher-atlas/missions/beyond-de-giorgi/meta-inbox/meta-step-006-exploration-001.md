# Meta-Learning: Step 006, Exploration 001

- For Tao screens on exact rewrites, the decisive question is not whether the
  identity is genuinely Navier-Stokes-specific in isolation. It is whether the
  identity survives as a smaller coefficient on the frozen localized estimate.
- Pairing `divergence / stress` with `Lamb-vector / projected` in one audit was
  useful because both are tempting algebraic cleanups whose main risk is
  estimate-level washout after localization debt is restored.
- Future rewrite audits should ask directly:
  "After the cutoff is inserted, what exact coefficient becomes smaller, and
  what ledger entry proves it?"
