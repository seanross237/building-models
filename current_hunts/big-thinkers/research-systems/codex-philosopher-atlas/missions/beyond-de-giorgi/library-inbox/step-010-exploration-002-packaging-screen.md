### 4. Where The Candidate Collapses

- [VERIFIED] `representation identity only`:
  the exact pressure tensor formula remains live only as a narrow testable
  possibility; as long as it is just `partial_i partial_j p = R_i R_j(u_i u_j)`
  or another Calderon-Zygmund repackaging, it stays Tao-compatible and does not
  rescue the branch.
- [INFERRED] `local geometry only`:
  once the same line is expressed via eigenframe alignment, invariant
  combinations of `S`, or local alignment language, the surviving content is
  only local geometry. That does not directly control the remote-shell moments
  fixing the coefficient of `I_p^far`.
- [INFERRED] `reduced-model / restricted-Euler flavor`:
  once the line is expressed as a defect variable with remainder or any closure
  that acts only on a local or self-induced part, it hits the frozen kill rule
  against reduced models that leave the same full pairing untouched.
- [INFERRED] `conditional-regularity restatement`:
  if the line is recast as alignment or coherence sufficient for regularity, it
  becomes the same kind of criterion language the local record already treats
  as prior art rather than a new endpoint-attached gain.

### 5. Admission Decision

- [VERIFIED] No source in the local repository exhibits a packaging-independent
  estimate-level gain on the frozen full pairing `I_p^far`.
- [INFERRED] The only surviving family therefore does not clear Step 10. Its
  narrow remaining content is a testable question, not an admitted mechanism:
  whether exact pressure-tensor structure could force remote-shell
  harmonic-moment cancellation strongly enough to lower the same coefficient.
- [ADMISSION] `none survives`
- [INFERRED] First honest failure: `repackaging obstruction`.
  The family dies as soon as equivalent packagings are screened, because every
  documented wording collapses into known non-coercive representation,
  local-geometry, reduced-model, or criterion language before any smaller
  coefficient on the same full pairing appears.
- [VERDICT] `repackaging obstruction, branch should stop before tensor-package freeze`
