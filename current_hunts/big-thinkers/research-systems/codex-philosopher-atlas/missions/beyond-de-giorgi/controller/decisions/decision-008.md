## Mission-Control Verdict

`step-005` kept the current chain alive. It did the exact narrowing work that
the run-003 chain demanded before any Tao optimism was allowed:

- fixed the inherited architecture at `local-energy flux/localization`;
- fixed the audited bad term at
  `I_flux[φ] = ∬ (|u|^2 + 2p) u · ∇φ`;
- fixed the compatible weak-solution package at suitable weak solutions in the

  projection/CZ/Biot-Savart debt;
- fixed the gain currency as coefficient shrinkage in the same localized
  balance;
- and bounded the exact candidate family to three named rewrites with no
  hybrid escape hatch at this stage.

That is not a branch failure. It is exactly the kind of concretization the
chain required in order to become auditable.

## Why Replan Is Wrong

Replanning is for a materially weakened or closed branch. `step-005` did the
opposite: it removed ambiguity and made the next gate sharper. The branch has
not yet been tested on its decisive question, which is whether any of the
three frozen candidates has a real NS-versus-averaged discriminator with a
credible insertion point in the localized LEI estimate.

If that Tao screen kills every candidate, then a later evaluator can close or
replan the branch honestly. That has not happened yet.

## Why Terminate Is Wrong

The mission-level question is still open. No terminal positive mechanism has
been found, and no terminal negative result about the mission as a whole has
been earned. At most, the mission has now prepared one narrow reformulation
audit for its decisive screening step.

## Chain Assessment

The chain remains active.

The next logical move is still **Chain Step 2 only**: require each frozen
candidate to state exactly what NS-specific feature is supposed to matter, how
Tao-style averaging destroys or washes it out, and where that distinction
could alter the estimate on the already-fixed localized flux term rather than
living only at the identity level.

## Required Next Move

Proceed to `step-006` and run the Tao discriminator screen on the fixed
candidate family.

The next step should:

- test `divergence/stress`, `Lamb-vector/projected`, and
  `vorticity/Biot-Savart` one by one against an explicit NS-versus-averaged
  distinction;
- reject any candidate whose specialness disappears after localization or has
  no credible place to improve the estimate on `I_flux[φ]`;
- keep the claim bounded to the fixed LEI architecture and frozen protocol;
- and recommend branch invalidation immediately if no candidate survives this
  screen.

The correct controller decision is therefore to proceed, with a narrow Step-2
goal and a strong expectation that the branch may die honestly there.
