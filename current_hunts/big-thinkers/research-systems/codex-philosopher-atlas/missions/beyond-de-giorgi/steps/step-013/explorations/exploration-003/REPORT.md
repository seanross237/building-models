## Findings

### 1. Anti-repackaging classifier

- [VERIFIED] Anti-repackaging rules are strongest when written in the branch's
  own burden currency.
- [VERIFIED] Family labels should be collapsed before packaging claims are
  allowed to sound distinct.
- [INFERRED] The three-way Step-013 classifier should be frozen as follows:

| label | exact rule |
| --- | --- |
| `equivalent redistribution` | `[INFERRED]` after localization, pressure recovery, reconstruction, admissibility debt, and any claimed transfer, the candidate leaves the same full `I_flux[phi]` bundle with no positive-margin smaller coefficient; any apparent novelty is only variable choice, packaging, or redistribution among ledger columns |
| `ambiguous redistribution` | `[INFERRED]` the candidate names one exact ledger cell where derivative count, locality, cancellation, or integrability might improve, but it does not yet produce a same-currency cash-out or an explicit transfer lemma back to a smaller full-bundle coefficient |
| `potentially live mechanism change` | `[INFERRED]` after full debt, the candidate either directly lowers the effective coefficient on the full `I_flux[phi]` bundle by a positive margin or names a specific transfer lemma whose conclusion is that same positive-margin reduction on the frozen theorem ledger |

### 2. Minimum evidence to avoid immediate prior-art absorption

- [VERIFIED] The branch must name the closest known reformulation and the exact
  claimed mathematical delta.
- [INFERRED] Minimum evidence required for a later candidate packet:
  1. one exact nearest comparator on the local record;
  2. one sentence naming the shared mechanism or identity already present there;
  3. one exact mathematical delta, not a branding claim;
  4. one frozen ledger entry improved after all debt is charged;
  5. one same-currency cash-out on the full `I_flux[phi]` bundle, or one
     explicit transfer lemma leading there.
- [VERIFIED] If any of those items is missing, the candidate is absorbed by
  prior-art or held as merely ambiguous.

### 3. Frozen prior-art audit format

- [INFERRED] Later Step-2 candidate packets must include this exact audit
  format:

1. `candidate reformulation / unknown`
2. `nearest known comparator on the repository record`
3. `shared exact structure already present in that comparator`
4. `claimed mathematical delta`
5. `baseline burden entry being improved`
6. `debt ledger entries charged immediately`
7. `same-currency cash-out on full I_flux[phi] or explicit transfer lemma`
8. `anti-repackaging classification`
9. `prior-art absorption verdict`

### 4. Verdict on whether the scope lock is real

- [VERIFIED] Exploration 001 froze one primary endpoint family, one backup
  endpoint family, one solution class, and one localization regime.
- [VERIFIED] Exploration 002 froze one theorem-facing baseline burden and one
  admissible gain notion in the same burden currency.
- [INFERRED] The anti-repackaging classifier and prior-art audit format above
  are explicit enough to police later candidate packets mechanically.
- [INFERRED] Therefore the Step-013 scope lock is concrete enough to govern
  Step 2 candidate selection.

### 5. Step verdict

- [VERDICT] `scope lock passed, proceed to candidate slate and localized equation audit`
- [INFERRED] What is now frozen for Chain Step 2:
  primary endpoint family,
  backup endpoint family,
  solution class,
  localization regime,
  baseline burden,
  admissible gain notion,
  typed ledger currencies,
  same-currency comparability rule,
  transfer-lemma rule,
  anti-repackaging classifier,
  and the nearest-prior-art audit format.

## Dead Ends / Failed Attempts

- [VERIFIED] I found no support for a looser anti-repackaging rule stated only
  in stylistic or branding language.
- [VERIFIED] I found no support for letting prior-art comparison wait until
  after a candidate packet has already been promoted.

## Conclusion

- [INFERRED] Equivalent redistribution, ambiguous redistribution, and
  potentially live mechanism change can be separated mechanically on the frozen
  LEI burden ledger.
- [INFERRED] The prior-art audit is concrete enough to expose absorbed
  reformulations before candidate promotion.
- [VERDICT] `scope lock passed, proceed to candidate slate and localized equation audit`
