# Exploration 001 Goal

## Objective

Build the bounded Step-2 candidate slate for the active exact-rewrite audit.

Freeze:
- at most two active candidates;
- one reserve;
- one exact unknown or reformulated package for each active candidate.

## Required Output

Produce:
- `REPORT.md`
- `REPORT-SUMMARY.md`

The report must:
- choose at most two active exact reformulation families and one reserve;
- justify each active choice under the frozen LEI / CKN ledger from `step-013`;
- justify why the reserve was not promoted now;
- state why strain / pressure-Hessian or other older branch material is omitted
  unless it is shown to be repository-native exact-rewrite evidence for this
  branch;
- label each substantive claim `[VERIFIED]`, `[INFERRED]`, or `[PROPOSED]`.

## Success Criteria

- The slate is bounded, not a menu.
- Each active candidate has one exact package and one named route back to the
  frozen burden currency, even if that route later fails.
- The reserve is exact-rewrite-native rather than an imported geometry or
  pressure-branch surrogate.

## Failure Criteria

- More than two active families are kept alive.
- An active candidate is justified only by vague reformulation language.
- The report silently imports geometry or pressure-branch families without
  showing they belong to the exact-rewrite audit.
