## Objective

Freeze the endpoint-tied admission rules and the final Step-009 verdict.

The endpoint, cash-out line, progress currency, and threshold have already been
frozen by Explorations 001 and 002. Do not reopen them. This exploration should
write the exact rule for same-scale unconstrained exterior/harmonic freedom, the
exact Tao rule, and the final Step-009 proceed-or-stop classification tied to
the frozen `I_p^far` ledger.

## Required Questions

Answer all of the following from local repository materials:

1. What is the exact rule that same-scale unconstrained exterior or harmonic
   freedom counts as immediate failure for this branch, stated specifically for
   the frozen `I_p^far` endpoint rather than abstractly?
2. What is the exact Tao rule here:
   averaged-model sensitivity is mandatory but does not by itself establish
   theorem relevance?
3. Given the frozen endpoint and threshold, should Step 009 classify the branch
   as
   `endpoint frozen, proceed to prior-art and packaging screening`
   or
   `endpoint obstruction, branch should stop before tensor packaging`?
4. If the setup survives, what is already frozen and what later steps are still
   allowed to vary?
5. If the setup fails, where exactly does the kill condition fire?

## Working Hypotheses To Test, Not Assume

- The endpoint setup survives Step 1 because one theorem ledger, one cash-out
  line, one currency, and one threshold are now concrete enough.
- The same-scale failure rule should be stated as:
  if later tensor work leaves a same-scale unconstrained harmonic/exterior
  remainder in the full `I_p^far` pairing, the branch fails immediately because
  the frozen coefficient-shrinkage endpoint has not been met.
- The Tao rule should be stated as:
  a candidate mechanism must be destroyed by the relevant averaged-model
  comparison, but that sensitivity alone does not count unless it also yields
  the frozen coefficient reduction on the full pairing.

Confirm, refine, or reject those hypotheses from source-backed evidence.

## Success Criteria

- Produce two admission rules stated explicitly enough for later steps to use
  without reinterpretation.
- Produce one verdict only:
  `endpoint frozen, proceed to prior-art and packaging screening`
  or
  `endpoint obstruction, branch should stop before tensor packaging`.
- Label major claims `[VERIFIED]`, `[INFERRED]`, or `[PROPOSED]`.
- State exactly what later steps may still vary if the branch survives.

- The report restates the rules as abstract rhetoric rather than tying them to
  `I_p^far` and its frozen threshold.
- The report lets Tao sensitivity do analytical work that the endpoint ledger
  has not earned.
- The report reopens the endpoint, cash-out, or threshold that Explorations 001
  and 002 already froze.

If failure occurs, say so directly and recommend endpoint obstruction.
