# Exploration 001 Goal

## Objective

Map the real prior-art neighborhood for `step-010` and reduce the live
candidate-family menu to the smallest honest set inside the frozen `I_p^far`
endpoint ledger.

This is not a tensor-package choice and not an observable shortlist. The point
is to decide whether any Step-10 candidate remains genuinely distinct from
known alignment, eigenframe, restricted-Euler, pressure-Hessian modeling, or
conditional-regularity language once the branch is forced to speak in the
already frozen full-pairing cash-out currency.

## Required Questions

Answer all of the following from local repository materials:

1. What is the smallest serious candidate-family menu still live after
   `step-009` and the run-006 repair?
2. For each serious family, what is the nearest prior family already reflected
   in the repository record:
   strain-vorticity alignment,
   eigenframe geometry,
   restricted-Euler defects,
   pressure-Hessian modeling,
   conditional regularity criteria,
   or another clearly named family?
3. For each serious family, is the claimed leverage endpoint-attached to the
   frozen `I_p^far` ledger, or only descriptive / motivational?
4. Which candidate labels differ only by naming, rhetoric, or packaging and
   should therefore be collapsed into one family or rejected?
5. Does any family survive as more than a reformulation of known prior-art
   language before equivalent-packaging screening even begins?

## Working Hypotheses To Test, Not Assume

- The live menu is already very small:
  one endpoint-attached `pressure-Hessian / strain-defect` family, with
  `harmonic-moment cancellation` only as its narrow mechanism subquestion.
- `full stretching control` is not a family at all on this record; it is a
  bottleneck slogan unless tied to the frozen far-field pairing.
- Alignment, eigenframe, restricted-Euler, and conditional-regularity labels
  enter mainly as overlap comparators and failure modes, not as independent
  survivors inside this branch.
- Any family that cannot state estimate-level action on the frozen full pairing
  is descriptive only and should be rejected at Step 10.

Confirm, refine, or reject these hypotheses from source-backed evidence.

## Success Criteria

- Produce a compact overlap table with columns:
  `candidate family`, `closest prior family`, `endpoint-attached or descriptive`,
  `same family or genuinely distinct`, `survival or rejection note`.
- Label major claims `[VERIFIED]`, `[INFERRED]`, or `[PROPOSED]`.
- End with one reduced family menu only, or say plainly that the menu collapses
  fully into prior-art repackaging.
- Keep the conclusion narrow enough that Step 3 cannot silently resurrect dead
  families under new language.

## Failure Criteria

- The report mistakes a packaging variant for a distinct family.
- The report accepts Tao-sensitive or NS-specific rhetoric as if it were
  endpoint-level leverage.
- The report widens the family menu beyond what the local record actually
  supports.

If failure occurs, say so directly and recommend `repackaging obstruction`.
