# Chain 01 - SQG-Blueprint Commutator And Scalarization Route With Anti-Conjugation And LEI Endpoint Gate

## Central Premise

The clearest live mission lesson is still the SQG one: regularity did not come from beating the `beta = 4/3` Chebyshev ceiling, but from changing the drift-coupling mechanism. This chain is live only if one exact Navier-Stokes reformulation produces an estimate-level gain on one inherited LEI / CKN bottleneck that is not merely the old quadratic burden conjugated by projection, inversion, or a prettier variable.

## Why This Chain Is Meaningfully Different

This is the only chain whose core bet is that the right unknown or coupling package, not a better norm estimate in the old variables, changes the mechanism. It is not a spectral packet audit, not a material-trajectory route, not a geometry reboot, and not a compactness contradiction program.

## Ordered Steps

### Step 1 - Freeze the endpoint, baseline criterion, and anti-conjugation rule

- Depends on: none.
- Action: choose one inherited theorem-facing endpoint on the suitable-weak / LEI floor, normally local concentration exclusion inside the CKN/Lin contradiction package.
- Action: name one baseline burden that a reformulation would have to beat or genuinely sharpen.
- Action: define the anti-conjugation rule: a candidate dies immediately if its equation is only the original quadratic burden rewritten through projection, inverse operators, or a nonlocal change of variables with no estimate-level improvement.
- Expected output: an endpoint-and-baseline memo with one admissible notion of gain.
- Kill condition: if no single endpoint and burden currency can be frozen before algebra begins, stop and write a setup obstruction.

### Step 2 - Freeze one candidate unknown and derive the full exact equation

- Depends on: Step 1.
- Action: choose one candidate family only: scalarized active variable, extension variable, div-curl package, or another comparably specific unknown.
- Action: derive the exact evolution including pressure re-entry, vector reconstruction, localization debt, and any commutator remainder.
- Action: record which parts are genuinely exact-NS structure and which are generic Calderon-Zygmund packaging.
- Expected output: one exact equation sheet plus a remainder ledger.
- Kill condition: if the candidate family only displays a decorative commutator while the leading burden re-enters unchanged, stop and write an anti-repackaging obstruction.

### Step 3 - Run the estimate-level gain gate before broad Tao narration

- Depends on: Step 2.
- Action: ask whether the candidate yields one concrete gain on the frozen burden: derivative reduction, stronger locality, one-sided cancellation, or another explicitly benchmarked improvement.
- Action: test that gain before absolute values, generic inequalities, or reconstruction erase it.
- Action: keep at most one promoted candidate and one backup.
- Expected output: a short gain table naming the exact bottleneck term, gain currency, and minimum meaningful margin.
- Kill condition: if no estimate-level gain survives in the same burden currency as the frozen baseline, stop and write a non-coercive reformulation obstruction.

### Step 4 - Insert the surviving candidate into the inherited LEI / CKN ledger

- Depends on: Step 3.
- Action: write one explicit localization or contradiction line showing where the promoted gain enters the inherited endpoint argument.
- Action: charge cutoff commutators, pressure localization, vector reconstruction, and solution-class debt immediately.
- Action: reject candidates that improve a local surrogate while the full endpoint burden returns unchanged.
- Expected output: one endpoint-facing insertion memo.
- Kill condition: if the gain disappears once the inherited localization and pressure debt are restored, stop and write an endpoint-transfer obstruction.

### Step 5 - Demand one hard artifact

- Depends on: Step 4.
- Action: require one of: a prototype inequality, a localized toy model, or a failed lemma that pinpoints the obstruction sharply.
- Action: compare the artifact directly against the frozen baseline criterion.
- Expected output: an evidence packet strong enough for promotion or calibrated failure.
- Kill condition: if the route remains descriptive after this step, stop and write a documentary-success obstruction.

### Step 6 - Use Tao comparison only as a secondary discriminator and state the narrowest claim

- Depends on: Step 5.
- Action: ask whether the exact algebraic feature supporting the artifact would be destroyed by Tao-style averaging.
- Action: state the final result as either:
  - one proposition-scale conditional route with exact unknown, exact gain, endpoint, and first narrower missing lemma; or
  - one obstruction memo labeled by anti-conjugation collapse, non-coercivity, endpoint-transfer failure, or Tao-insensitive packaging.
- Expected output: a final-comparison-ready route or obstruction.
- Kill condition: if Tao sensitivity is the only thing that looks special, downgrade to obstruction immediately.
