## Selected Portfolio

The strongest three-chain portfolio is `chain-01`, `chain-02`, and `chain-03`.

- `chain-01` is the best high-upside inclusion. It stays closest to a genuinely pressure-adjacent Navier-Stokes mechanism while respecting the closed loopholes from the old far-field and exact-rewrite audits. It also fails usefully: even a negative result would sharply locate whether the surviving obstruction is lack of invariant observables, lack of full-stretching control, or lack of action on the live harmonic moments.
- `chain-02` is the cleanest Tao-sensitive algebra test. It is genuinely orthogonal to the tensor route because it works at exact Leray-projected triad geometry rather than physical-space rewrites or pressure packaging. If it fails, the failure modes are still valuable and well typed: summation collapse, bad-triad contamination, or localization collapse.
- `chain-03` earns the third slot because it is the most differentiated architecture from the first two and has strong bounded-negative value. It directly audits the Lorentz or weak-type lead named in mission context while imposing anti-recursion guardrails, so even failure would clarify whether there is any non-De Giorgi endpoint mechanism left.

## Why This Is The Strongest Portfolio

- The portfolio maximizes diversity across tensorial pressure structure, frequency-space symbol structure, and endpoint-functional architecture.
- It preserves at least one clear high-upside path in `chain-01`, while `chain-02` offers the sharpest exact-NS-versus-averaged-NS discriminator.
- All three selected chains are designed to yield informative obstruction memos on failure rather than diffuse negative outcomes.
- The set avoids redundancy better than any portfolio containing `chain-04`, because `chain-04` also tries to reach stretching control but does so through a heavier package-change burden.

## Why `chain-04` Was Not Selected

`chain-04` is the best excluded chain, but it is the weakest fit for a three-slot adversarial portfolio.

- It is less cleanly differentiated in target than `chain-03` because it still aims at stretching or criterion control, but with additional persistence, matching, and normalization debt.
- Its honest failure modes are informative, but they are more likely to terminate early at transport bookkeeping or tube-family persistence before reaching a decisive Tao-sensitive bottleneck test.
- Relative to `chain-03`, it contributes less portfolio breadth. Relative to `chain-01`, it is the less direct route to pressure/stretching relevance.
