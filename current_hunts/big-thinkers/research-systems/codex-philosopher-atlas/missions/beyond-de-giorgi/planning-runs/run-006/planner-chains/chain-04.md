# Chain 04 - Lagrangian Tube-Family Geometry Reboot

## Central Premise

The neutral Eulerian geometry package failed at the dynamic screen, but that does not prove geometry is dead under every packaging. A live geometry route now must openly change the package: choose a tube-adapted or Lagrangian tracking framework, define its normalization rules in advance, and test whether direction coherence or local alignment persists strongly enough to control the full stretching mechanism or feed a known geometric criterion.

## Verifiable Result Target

Either:

- one criterion-scale geometric program naming the tube family, tracking rules, observable, full-stretching target, and first missing lemma; or
- a bounded obstruction memo showing that even after an explicit package change, the route dies at persistence, normalization, or nonlocal-strain control.

## Why This Chain Is Meaningfully Different

This chain is geometric and transport-facing, but unlike the killed neutral geometry branch it treats the package change itself as part of the audited burden. It is not a tensor identity chain, not a triad-symbol chain, and not an endpoint weak-type chain.

## Ordered Steps

### Step 1 - Declare the package change openly

- Depends on: none.
- Action: choose one explicit adapted framework in advance:
  a tube-family tracking rule,
  a Lagrangian packet,
  or another comparably concrete transported-structure package.
- Action: freeze one target output in advance:
  full `S omega . omega` control,
  a Constantin-Fefferman type direction criterion,
  or another named geometric regularity criterion.
- Action: state anti-cheat guardrails:
  no post hoc tube fitting,
  no threshold retuning after seeing favorable geometry,
  and no hidden upgrade to stronger derivative regularity than the package carries.
- Expected output: a package-change memo naming one framework, one target criterion, and one normalization rule set.
- Kill condition: if the geometry route cannot be formulated without hidden relocalization or post hoc fitting, stop and write a setup obstruction.

### Step 2 - Define one primary observable on the transported family

- Depends on: Step 1.
- Action: choose one primary observable and at most one comparator, such as direction coherence along transported tube cores, local Beltrami deficit on the tracked family, or an anisotropy/coherence quantity attached to moving cross-sections.
- Action: define exactly how the observable is measured, matched across time, and protected against coordinate artifacts.
- Action: record the Tao discriminator and the intended leverage point on the frozen target criterion.
- Expected output: an observable memo with definitions, matching rules, Tao role, and intended leverage.
- Kill condition: if the observable is only meaningful after favorable coordinate choices or stronger regularity than the package supplies, terminate with an observable obstruction.

### Step 3 - Run a persistence and transport audit on the adapted package

- Depends on: Step 2.
- Action: test whether the observable has any credible persistence mechanism under advection, stretching, viscosity, tube splitting/merging, and loss of coherence across the chosen time window.
- Action: identify the first missing dynamic ingredient precisely:
  tube matching,
  direction propagation,
  cross-sectional control,
  or another named transport burden.
- Action: reject any route whose only believable rescue is the same hidden tube adaptation or threshold tuning that killed the neutral package.
- Expected output: a persistence ledger with explicit dynamic burdens and one honest rating.
- Kill condition: if no plausible persistence story survives the declared package, stop and write a transport obstruction.

### Step 4 - Charge the full nonlocal-strain and criterion debt

- Depends on: Step 3.
- Action: test whether favorable geometry on the tracked family controls the full stretching mechanism once exterior strain and inter-scale interactions are reintroduced.
- Action: if the target is a known geometric criterion, state exactly how the observable would enter that criterion and what remains missing.
- Action: classify failure as core-only control, criterion mismatch, nonlocal-strain freedom, or measure-insufficient signal.
- Expected output: a full-equation debt ledger saying whether the adapted geometry reaches the actual target criterion.
- Kill condition: if the route only improves a tube-core caricature while the decisive outside strain stays free, close the chain negatively.

### Step 5 - Apply the Tao screen and close with the narrowest earned claim

- Depends on: Step 4.
- Action: test whether the promoted geometric feature is genuinely tied to exact NS transport/stretching structure or whether an averaged model could reproduce it at the same operational level.
- Action: if one route survives, state a criterion-scale conditional program naming the package, observable, target criterion, gain, and first missing lemma.
- Action: if nothing survives, state the narrowest earned obstruction:
  no honest package,
  no persistence,
  no control of full stretching,
  or no Tao-sensitive geometric lever.
- Expected output: either a criterion-scale geometric reboot or a sharp package-change obstruction memo.
- Kill condition: if the final claim requires reverting to the dead neutral Eulerian package or to vague tube language, downgrade it to obstruction.
