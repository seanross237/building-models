# Chain 02 - Helical-Triad Differential Diagnosis

## Central Premise

Tao-style averaging may preserve energy, divergence-free structure, and standard harmonic-analysis estimates while still destroying the exact helical and triadic interaction geometry of the true NS Leray symbol. This chain asks whether a regularity-relevant discriminator lives at that symbol or triad level.

## Verifiable Result Target

Either:

- one triad-level or helical-selection mechanism that yields a concrete sign, coercivity, or forbidden-transfer statement unavailable in the averaged model; or
- a bounded negative result showing that the most natural helical and triadic candidates do not produce a usable regularity lever.

## Why This Chain Is Meaningfully Different

This is the only chain built around Fourier interaction structure. Its objects are helical coefficients, triad classes, and transfer rules, not localized cutoff estimates, tensor eigenframes, or coherent-vortex packages.

## Ordered Steps

### Step 1 - Fix one blowup-facing scenario and one triad-level target quantity

- Depends on: none.
- Action: choose one concrete adverse scenario, such as self-amplifying comparable-scale triads or a nonlocal triad family feeding a critical norm.
- Action: choose one fixed target quantity that would matter if constrained, for example critical-norm transfer, stretching-facing spectral production, or a helical energy/enstrophy imbalance.
- Action: define in advance what would count as a usable gain.
- Expected output: a scenario memo naming the triad family, target observable, and success criterion.
- Kill condition: if no target quantity can be named that would bear on regularity rather than only on descriptive spectral bookkeeping, stop immediately.

### Step 2 - Derive the exact NS-versus-averaged interaction difference

- Depends on: Step 1.
- Action: write the exact NS triad or helical coefficients for the chosen target and compare them to the averaged-model counterpart at the same level of description.
- Action: isolate what averaging preserves and what it destroys.
- Expected output: a symbol sheet showing the exact interaction coefficients, their symmetries, and the candidate Tao discriminator.
- Kill condition: if the averaged model preserves the candidate mechanism at the same effective strength, close the chain as Tao-blocked.

### Step 3 - Shortlist only a few candidate discriminators

- Depends on: Step 2.
- Action: test a bounded list of candidate mechanisms such as same-helicity depletion, signed triad imbalance, or orientation-specific cancellation.
- Action: reject any candidate that is only an identity-level curiosity with no path to a smaller target quantity.
- Expected output: a discriminator table stating for each candidate whether it changes the target observable, how it depends on exact NS structure, and what the first obstruction is.
- Kill condition: if every candidate is either averaged-model robust or too weak to affect the chosen target quantity, stop and write the negative memo.

### Step 4 - Test whether the discriminator survives localization or scenario reduction

- Depends on: Step 3.
- Action: check whether the surviving discriminator can be translated into a usable estimate, conditional criterion, or scenario exclusion rather than remaining a purely global spectral statement.
- Action: record any localization, phase-cancellation, or norm-conversion loss needed to make it operational.
- Expected output: a feasibility memo stating whether the triad mechanism survives the move from symbol level to a regularity-relevant mathematical statement.
- Kill condition: if the candidate only lives in frequency space and dies when converted into a regularity-facing statement, classify it as descriptive and stop.

### Step 5 - Close with a concrete route or a bounded obstruction

- Depends on: Step 4, or directly on Step 3 if the chain dies there.
- Action: package the result as either a conditional proposition or a narrow negative claim about helical-triad structure.
- Expected output: either a specific follow-on theorem target tied to one triad discriminator, or an obstruction memo explaining why exact helical interaction geometry still does not cash out as a regularity lever.
- Kill condition: if the final statement extends beyond the triad classes and target quantity actually audited, narrow it until it is fully earned.
