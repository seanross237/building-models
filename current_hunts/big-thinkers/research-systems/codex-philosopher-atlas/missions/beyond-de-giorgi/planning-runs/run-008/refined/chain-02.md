# Refined Chain 02 - Reformulation Audit With Front-Loaded Anti-Repackaging Gates

## Central Premise

Do not assume that an SQG-like commutator package exists for 3D Navier-Stokes.
Treat scalarization, projection, and extension variables as analytically
suspect until one concrete exact unknown shows all of the following:

- its exact equation is not merely the original NS nonlinearity conjugated by a
  singular integral, projection, or inverse operator;
- the hoped-for gain has one exact type:
  derivative,
  frequency,
  locality,
  truncation-compatibility,
  or sign/cancellation;
- the candidate gain depends on an NS-specific algebraic feature that Tao-style
  averaging destroys; and
- that gain survives pressure re-entry, vector reconstruction, and the chosen
  theorem endpoint.

If these conditions fail, the correct output is obstruction, not a positive
reformulation story.

## Verifiable Result Target

Either:

- one proposition-scale conditional route naming:
  one exact reformulated unknown,
  one exact endpoint,
  one baseline known criterion being sharpened,
  one exact gain type,
  one exact NS-specific feature used by the mechanism,
  one endpoint-facing inequality where the gain appears,
  and one genuinely narrower missing lemma; or
- one calibrated obstruction memo identifying the first honest failure:
  no admissible unknown,
  anti-conjugation failure,
  Tao-insensitivity,
  pressure/vector re-entry restores the full burden,
  localization destroys the gain,
  or the remaining lemma is equivalent to the original regularity problem.

## Why This Chain Is Still Worth Running

Even a negative result is useful here. A disciplined reformulation audit can
show whether every plausible SQG-style package collapses for the same structural
reason: vectorial reconstruction, pressure re-entry, or failure of endpoint
transfer. That is better than another round of attractive but non-theorem-facing
algebra.

## Admission Rule

Before any full derivation, freeze:

- one exact theorem endpoint;
- one exact baseline criterion or barrier to compare against; and
- one exact gain type.

No candidate unknown is admissible unless there is already a short argument that
its bad leading term is not just the original quadratic NS interaction written
through a nonlocal change of variables.

If this cannot be done concretely, stop and write a setup obstruction.

## Ordered Steps

### Step 1 - Freeze the endpoint, baseline, and gain type before selecting the unknown

- Depends on: none.
- Action: choose one exact theorem-facing endpoint:
  localized regularity,
  continuation criterion,
  or concentration exclusion.
- Action: choose one named baseline burden to beat or genuinely sharpen:
  a De Giorgi-type localization wall,
  a Serrin/BKM-type continuation package,
  or another explicit comparison target.
- Action: choose one exact gain currency and state why that gain would matter
  for the chosen endpoint.
- Expected output: a scope memo naming the endpoint, baseline criterion, and
  gain type.
- Kill condition: if these cannot be frozen precisely enough to benchmark later
  progress, stop and write a scope obstruction.

### Step 2 - Admit only one unknown that survives anti-conjugation and preliminary Tao screening

- Depends on: Step 1.
- Action: choose one candidate unknown only if there is a concrete argument that
  its leading bad term is not merely `u \otimes u`, pressure, or the LEI flux
  conjugated by projection, scalarization, or extension.
- Action: run a preliminary Tao screen before full derivation:
  name one exact NS-specific identity, symmetry, or tensor relation the route
  needs, and say why averaging would destroy the hoped-for gain rather than just
  alter notation.
- Action: reject candidates whose main appeal is that they display a formal
  commutator without reducing tensorial or differential complexity.
- Expected output: an admission memo naming one unknown and one concrete reason
  it is not obvious repackaging.
- Kill condition: if no candidate survives anti-conjugation or Tao admission,
  terminate immediately with obstruction.

### Step 3 - Derive the exact equation with pressure and vector reconstruction treated as primary debts

- Depends on: Step 2.
- Action: write the exact evolution for the chosen unknown and separate:
  the promoted commutator candidate,
  pressure re-entry,
  vector reconstruction debt,
  localization debt,
  and any genuinely quadratic leftovers.
- Action: state explicitly why the promoted commutator is supposed to be
  leading rather than decorative.
- Action: treat pressure or reconstruction as fatal by default unless they are
  quantitatively subordinated in the chosen endpoint.
- Expected output: a full-debt formula sheet with one promoted mechanism and one
  fatality audit.
- Kill condition: if the commutator gain is only formal or the full burden
  returns through pressure or reconstruction, stop and write a formulation
  obstruction.

### Step 4 - Re-run the Tao screen on the actual mechanism, not on presentation

- Depends on: Step 3.
- Action: test whether the candidate gain itself disappears under Tao-style
  averaging, not merely whether some displayed formula changes.
- Action: give one exact pass example and one exact fail example inside the
  chosen package.
- Action: reject any route whose surviving content can be explained by generic
  Calderon-Zygmund or singular-integral facts alone.
- Expected output: a Tao-comparator memo tied to the endpoint-relevant term.
- Kill condition: if the gain is Tao-insensitive or only presentation-sensitive,
  terminate with obstruction.

### Step 5 - Insert the mechanism into one benchmarked endpoint inequality immediately

- Depends on: Step 4.
- Action: write one explicit endpoint-facing inequality, implication, or
  contradiction line in which the gain acts on the chosen baseline burden.
- Action: specify:
  the norm or scale,
  the exact burden reduced,
  and why the line closes in the available regularity class.
- Action: include cutoff commutators, pressure localization, and transfer back
  to the original NS variables immediately rather than as later cleanup.
- Expected output: an insertion memo with one benchmarked gain over the frozen
  baseline.
- Kill condition: if the gain evaporates after localization, pressure, or
  transfer back to `u` and `p`, stop and write an endpoint-insertion
  obstruction.

### Step 6 - Audit whether the remaining gap is genuinely narrower than the original problem

- Depends on: Step 5.
- Action: isolate the first missing lemma.
- Action: test whether it is genuinely narrower than the original regularity
  problem rather than a disguised request to control the full NS nonlinearity in
  new variables.
- Action: check whether analogous lemmas already appear in prior SQG-inspired,
  projected, or microlocal reformulation attempts.
- Expected output: either a proposition-scale conditional route or a sharp
  obstruction memo.
- Kill condition: if the remaining gap reconstructs the original vectorial
  burden, downgrade immediately to obstruction.
