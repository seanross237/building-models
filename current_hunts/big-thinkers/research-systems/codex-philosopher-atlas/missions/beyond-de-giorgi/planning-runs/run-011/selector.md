## Why These Three Make The Strongest Portfolio

The selected set maximizes real diversity without wasting a slot on a downstream dependency. Chain 01 is the comparison-first filter that protects run budget and produces useful output even if everything dies. Chain 02 preserves the highest-upside direct mechanism path by forcing geometry to survive theorem-floor admissibility, same-currency accounting, and comparator pressure. Chain 04 adds the best non-geometric, non-transport structural lead still live: exact local harmonicity of the far-field pressure tail.

This portfolio also preserves useful failure modes. If Chain 02 fails, the run gets a criterion-scale geometric obstruction rather than more package theater. If Chain 04 fails, the run learns exactly whether local harmonicity is fake-local, too weak after recombination, or too Tao-compatible to matter. If Chain 01 fails to nominate survivors, it still closes dead families sharply and prevents redundant future reopening.

Chain 03 is the best reserve, but it is a weaker portfolio fit than Chain 04 for this top-three selection because its admission and endpoint-transfer debts are closer to the recently collapsed transport corridor. Chain 05 is explicitly conditional on upstream success and therefore should not consume a primary adversarial-testing slot in this run.
