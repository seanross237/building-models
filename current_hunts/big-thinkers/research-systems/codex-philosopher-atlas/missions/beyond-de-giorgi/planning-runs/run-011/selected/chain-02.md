# Chain 02 - Criterion-First Geometric Vorticity Mechanism Stress Test

## Central Premise

Geometry is still the clearest mission-facing candidate, but it is not allowed to survive on package elegance. This chain is live only if one explicit geometric package and one observable can be defined on the suitable-weak / LEI theorem floor, or on one explicitly bridged proxy class, and then deliver a theorem-facing gain beyond the closest Constantin-Fefferman-type prior art with all debts paid in the same endpoint currency.

## Why This Chain Is Meaningfully Different

This is the only geometry-first chain. It is not a Tao-map branch, not a transport observable branch, not a harmonic-tail branch, and not a compactness architecture. Its core bet is that exact NS vorticity geometry may still carry a criterion-scale lever that Tao averaging destroys.

## Ordered Steps

### Step 1 - Freeze one endpoint, one admissibility standard, and one comparator

- Depends on: none.
- Action: choose one theorem-facing endpoint only: a Constantin-Fefferman-type criterion by default, or one fixed full-stretching contradiction target.
- Action: state whether the observable lives directly on the theorem floor or on one named proxy class with explicit bridge burden.
- Action: name the closest prior-art comparator and the killed neutral-Eulerian package.
- Expected output: an endpoint-and-comparator memo.
- Kill condition: if no candidate proposition can be written before package selection, or the bridge burden is already theorem-scale, stop with an endpoint-free obstruction.

### Step 2 - Choose one package and one primary observable family

- Depends on: Step 1.
- Action: choose one package only: tube-following localization, high-vorticity tracking, or one fully specified alternative.
- Action: choose one primary observable family only: local Beltrami deficit, vorticity-direction coherence, Beltrami-plus-concentration, or another equally sharp candidate.
- Action: write the observable-to-endpoint map in the same endpoint currency.
- Expected output: a package-and-observable memo.
- Kill condition: if several observables are needed just to make the route sound plausible, stop with an under-specified geometry obstruction.

### Step 3 - Derive the theorem-facing local ledger before optimism accumulates

- Depends on: Step 2.
- Action: derive the localized evolution or insertion sheet that the chosen endpoint actually needs.
- Action: expose where `nabla xi` debt, boundary leakage, relocalization cost, pressure or cutoff debt, and nonlocal remainder enter.
- Action: separate exact geometric gains from terms that exist only off the theorem floor.
- Expected output: a localized same-currency ledger.
- Kill condition: if the gain exists only inside the package while the endpoint ledger stays unchanged, stop with a package-theater obstruction.

### Step 4 - Run the persistence and full-stretching audit

- Depends on: Step 3.
- Action: test whether the observable persists without repeated refitting of axes, tubes, or thresholds.
- Action: measure what share of the full stretching mechanism the observable actually controls, not just its favorable local fragment.
- Action: benchmark against the closest prior-art depletion or direction-regularity result.
- Expected output: a persistence ledger and a full-stretching audit note.
- Kill condition: if the branch controls only descriptive local depletion or collapses under persistence debt, stop with a full-stretching or persistence obstruction.

### Step 5 - Demand one hard artifact at criterion scale

- Depends on: Step 4.
- Action: produce one hard artifact: prototype estimate, representative insertion into a criterion-scale statement, or a hostile-scenario stress test.
- Action: treat a failed lemma as obstruction evidence unless it isolates a narrower live insertion point.
- Expected output: an evidence packet with comparator benchmarking.
- Kill condition: if the artifact does not outrun known direction-coherence or depletion language, stop with a prior-art absorption obstruction.

### Step 6 - Use Tao sensitivity only after theorem contact and then close narrowly

- Depends on: Step 5.
- Action: ask whether the artifact relies on geometric content plausibly erased by Tao averaging.
- Action: classify the route as `live geometry`, `hybrid geometry-transport`, `real but too weak`, or `bounded obstruction`.
- Action: state the first missing lemma only if the route survived the hard-artifact gate.
- Expected output: a comparison-ready final memo.
- Kill condition: if Tao sensitivity is the main reason the branch looks interesting, downgrade it to obstruction immediately.
