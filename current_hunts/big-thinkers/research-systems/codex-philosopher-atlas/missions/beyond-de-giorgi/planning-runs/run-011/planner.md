## Major Differences Between Chains

- Chain 01 is comparison-first and portfolio-shaping. Its natural output is a ranked property atlas or an obstruction map, not a mechanism proof.
- Chain 02 is geometry-first and criterion-scale. It focuses on vorticity direction, Beltrami deficit, and tube-aware observables under explicit package debt.
- Chain 03 is transport-first and deterministic. It studies deformation and cumulative stretching without stochastic machinery or geometric repackaging.
- Chain 04 is harmonic-structure-first and nonlocal. It is the only branch centered on local harmonic continuation of the far-field pressure rather than on generic pressure bounds.
- Chain 05 is architecture-first and downstream. It consumes an admitted firewall instead of searching for one from scratch.
