# Judgment for Chain 03

## Overall Assessment

The attacker identifies the real danger in this branch: it treats local harmonicity of the far-field pressure tail as if that almost by itself makes the pressure cheaper. That is not enough. The refined chain has to front-load admissibility, gauge choice, same-currency insertion, comparison against standard local pressure theory, and the Tao/prior-art hazard.

The attacker is somewhat too absolute in two places. The original chain was right to insist on one frozen endpoint and one observable, and exact-versus-debt separation is still useful bookkeeping if it is done honestly. The problem is not that those ideas are wrong; it is that they were not calibrated tightly enough.

## Major Critique Rulings

### 1. Step 1 understates decomposition, gauge, and comparator debt

**Verdict: Partially valid**

This criticism is right that the original Step 1 did not explicitly force the normalization and admissibility choices needed to make local pressure observables meaningful on the suitable-weak floor. Mean subtraction, pressure modulo time functions, and the exact split radius/cutoff are part of the branch, not background setup.

The attacker is also right that the comparator cannot be only the old failed global `H^1` route. The branch must also compare itself against the best ordinary local pressure decomposition technology.

What is overstated is the suggestion that freezing one decomposition and one endpoint is itself suspect. That focus is still correct; it just needs stricter admissibility and a stronger benchmark.

### 2. Step 2 permits fake locality and weak same-currency claims

**Verdict: Valid**

This is a central criticism. A local observable of `p_far` is not interesting unless it yields a theorem-line gain in the same burden currency, or via an explicit short conversion map, that standard local pressure theory does not already supply. Otherwise the branch is just the old exterior-source debt in local packaging.

The refined chain should therefore ban open-ended observable shopping, require an immediate source-to-observable cost analysis, and reject boundary-heavy observables unless the theorem-facing line genuinely needs them.

### 3. Step 3 overstates what harmonic tools can buy

**Verdict: Partially valid**

The attacker is right that Harnack is not a default tool here, since the pressure tail is sign-changing unless shifted and the shift may not respect the theorem-facing quantity. The attacker is also right that Caccioppoli, mean-value, and Poisson-representation estimates often propagate or re-express control rather than improve the actual coefficient that matters.

But the critique goes too far in treating exact-versus-debt separation as artificial in every case. That separation remains useful if the refined chain insists that split debt is intrinsic to the mechanism and that any claimed gain must survive translation into the frozen theorem currency.

### 4. Step 4 needs theorem-threshold, not cosmetic improvement

**Verdict: Valid**

The attacker is correct that insertion into a theorem-facing line is the real test. A pleasant local harmonic norm is irrelevant unless the exact LEI or criterion pairing can see it after the allowed gauge/mean subtraction. The attacker is also correct that "better than before" is too weak a standard; the net gain has to matter at the actual endpoint threshold.

The refined chain should therefore compare against both the old failed coefficient and the threshold required by the chosen endpoint.

### 5. Step 5 places the Tao filter too late

**Verdict: Valid**

This branch is exposed from the start to the possibility that it uses only generic elliptic interior structure of a tail generated outside the cylinder. If that is all it uses, the route is likely Tao-compatible from the outset. So the refined chain must front-load a prior-art / averaging note that names the candidate NS-specific feature before heavy estimate work begins.

The attacker is not entitled to declare the route dead on first principles, but the ordering criticism is correct.

### 6. Step 6 demands a hard artifact too late and with too-thin failure taxonomy

**Verdict: Valid**

For an estimate-driven pressure route, a prototype inequality or failed lemma should appear early, not only at the end. The attacker is also right that the failure taxonomy needed more categories, especially theorem-floor indefinability, gauge dependence, prior-art absorption, and real-but-too-weak gains.

The refined chain should force an early prototype inequality and keep the end-stage artifact requirement.

### 7. Structural claim: local harmonicity does not imply local cheapness

**Verdict: Valid**

This is the deepest substantive criticism. The far-field pressure tail is harmonic inside the local cylinder, but it is still driven by exterior quadratic data. Without a genuinely NS-specific cancellation or source-to-observable improvement, local harmonicity is only a syntactic localization of nonlocal debt.

The refined chain must treat "harmonic but not cheaper" as the default null hypothesis to beat.

### 8. Structural claim: the chain has too much survival slack

**Verdict: Valid**

The attacker is right that the open-ended phrases "another fully specified harmonic norm" and "another exact harmonic tool" leave too much room to rename the same failed idea. The refined chain should tighten the menus and require an early prototype inequality before the branch is allowed to continue.

### 9. Documentary issue: `selected/chain-03.md` is titled "Chain 04"

**Verdict: Valid**

This is not mathematically decisive, but it is a real bookkeeping problem in a run whose point is adversarial refinement. The refined file should be labeled consistently as Chain 03.

## What The Refined Chain Changes

- It front-loads admissibility, pressure gauge, mean-subtraction, and the exact theorem-facing insertion line.
- It upgrades the comparator: the branch must beat standard local pressure decomposition technology, not merely the old failed global `H^1` attempt.
- It requires one named candidate mechanism for why a local observable of `p_far` is genuinely cheaper, plus an early Tao/prior-art screen.
- It demands a prototype inequality by mid-chain and requires that split debt enter that prototype rather than being postponed as a later surcharge.
- It disallows cosmetic gains by testing against the actual endpoint threshold after near/far recombination.
- It tightens the failure taxonomy and removes the documentary mismatch.

## Probability Assessment

**Probability this refined chain yields a presentable result: 0.38**

That is below even odds because the branch still looks more likely to end in fake locality, prior-art absorption, Tao-compatible elliptic structure, or a real-but-too-weak gain after recombination debt is charged. It remains worth carrying forward because the refined version should produce a cleaner answer either way: a genuinely theorem-facing prototype inequality or a more decisive obstruction memo than the original chain could support.
