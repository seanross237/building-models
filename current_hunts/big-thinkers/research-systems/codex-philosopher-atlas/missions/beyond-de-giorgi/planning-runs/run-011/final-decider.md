Chain 02 has the strongest direct novelty ceiling, but it is too narrow to be the canonical next chain. It is best used as a harsh sub-protocol inside the winning audit, not as the whole mission.

- Its theorem-facing discipline is excellent and should be inherited.

- If geometry dies early, Chain 02 yields a useful bounded obstruction, but that is still a smaller mission outcome than a full exact-NS audit with comparable evidence gates.

The canonical winner therefore imports these Chain 02 strengths:

- exact theorem-facing target per surviving candidate;
- exact comparator theorem and literal claimed gain;
- explicit proxy/bridge debt that must be cheaper than the target theorem;
- early same-currency insertion artifact;
- joint persistence and prior-art absorption audit.

## Why Chain 03 Does Not Win

Chain 03 is differentiated and worth preserving as a candidate family inside the winner, but it should not be the top-level chain.

- Its presentable-result probability is materially lower.
- Its null hypothesis is harsher: local harmonicity is very likely to collapse into fake locality, prior-art absorption, or Tao-compatible elliptic structure.
- Its best use is as one audited candidate card inside the wider Tao-differential program, where it can still win honestly if it produces a theorem-facing artifact.

The canonical winner therefore imports these Chain 03 strengths:

- early anti-fake-locality screening;
- comparator pressure against standard local pressure technology, not only against the old failed global route;
- explicit recombination debt and theorem-threshold testing for harmonic-tail candidates.

The correct next move is not to pre-commit the mission to geometry or harmonic pressure. The correct move is to run the strongest exact-NS-wide audit that is still harsh enough to prevent rhetoric from surviving. Refined Chain 01, after tightening, does exactly that. It gives the mission the best useful floor, keeps the best surviving upside, and matches the current system's need for evidence-graded selection rather than premature mechanism commitment.
