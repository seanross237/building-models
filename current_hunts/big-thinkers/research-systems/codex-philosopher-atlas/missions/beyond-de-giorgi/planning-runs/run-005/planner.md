## Major Differences Between Chains

- Chain 01 is global and theorem-shaped. It starts from hypothetical blowup and tries to rule out a minimal counterexample.
- Chain 02 is spectral and algebraic. It works at the exact triad-symbol level and asks for a localized cancellation or depletion mechanism there.
- Chain 03 is tensorial and local-to-nonlocal. It targets `S omega . omega` and the pressure Hessian directly, with explicit nonlocal debt accounting.
- Chain 04 is geometric and region-specific. It focuses on coherent high-vorticity structure, local Beltrami deficit, and whether that structure can survive transport and localization costs.
