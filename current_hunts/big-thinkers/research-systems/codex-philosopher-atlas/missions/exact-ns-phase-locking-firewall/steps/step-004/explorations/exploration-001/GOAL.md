# Exploration 001 Goal - Canonical Third-Budget Seed Classification

## Objective

Classify the canonical connected three-triad repeated-wavevector cross-scale
seed families for the third budget under the frozen Step-1 equivalence rules
and the Step-2 / Step-3 lower-budget exclusions.

## Success Criteria

- State a canonical representative form for every genuinely new third-budget
  seed family.
- Separate true third-rung seeds from disguised first-budget and second-budget
  dead seeds, disconnected add-ons, and inert helical decorations.
- Make the equivalence reductions explicit enough that the step result can cite
  them directly.

## Failure Criteria

- The catalog still depends on hidden representative choices.
- The report cannot distinguish genuinely new cross-scale repeated-wavevector
  seeds from recycled rung-1 or rung-2 pictures.
