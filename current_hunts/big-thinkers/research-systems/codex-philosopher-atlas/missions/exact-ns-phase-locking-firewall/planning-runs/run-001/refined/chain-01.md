# Refined Chain 01 - Exact Closed-Support Delayed-Transfer Stress Test

## Central Premise

An honest exact finite-support counterexample is still worth chasing because a
single existential construction would seriously damage the firewall.

But this route must not assume in advance that the right mechanism is both
small and Tao-five-role-shaped. The disciplined version of the branch is:

1. search smallest-first over explicitly ordered exact support classes,
2. require recursive exact closure before trusting any reduced dynamics,
3. make delayed transfer quantitative relative to intrinsic clocks and
   spectator burden,
4. and treat any negative result as class-limited unless the obstruction
   survives the declared escalation ladder.

## Verifiable Result Target

Either:

- an exact invariant helical/Fourier subsystem with quantified delayed-transfer
  behavior, controlled spectator burden, and sufficient robustness to count as
  a firewall counterexample;
- a class-limited obstruction memo proving that no support in one explicitly
  declared search class or budget realizes those standards; or
- a bounded deferral memo identifying the exact failure point:
  recursive closure explosion,
  incompatibility of phase conditions,
  coefficient rigidity,
  excess spectator leakage,
  or lack of robust delayed transfer.

## Why This Chain Is Meaningfully Different

This branch keeps the original existential upside, but it no longer overclaims
what failure means and no longer forces Tao's causal template onto the exact NS
algebra before the algebra has earned it.

## Ordered Steps

### Step 1 - Fix The Exact Search Class, Ordering, And Success Metrics

Depends on: none.

Action: choose the exact objects of study. Default to finite Fourier/helical
mode supports. Use packet language only if an exact invariant packet
representation is written explicitly rather than heuristically.

Action: declare a smallest-first ordering and escalation ladder, for example by
mode count,
shell span,
recursive closure depth,
and parameter dimension. Do not use "smallest" informally.

Action: define the intrinsic success metrics up front:
what counts as delayed transfer relative to turnover and viscous times,
what spectator burden threshold is allowed during the window,
and what robustness standard is required
(exact invariant family,
normally hyperbolic manifold,
or an explicitly named alternative).

Expected output: one exact search-class memo and one quantitative metric sheet.

Kill condition: if no exact object class or intrinsic success metric can be
stated cleanly, stop and write a methodology-failure memo rather than a
counterexample search narrative.

### Step 2 - Enumerate Recursively Closed Exact Supports Inside The First Budget

Depends on: Step 1.

Action: starting from the first smallest support budget, recursively close each
seed family under conjugate completion, Leray projection effects, and all newly
forced triad interactions until the support is genuinely invariant or provably
spills over.

Action: treat spectator channels as part of this closure audit from the start.
Closure is recursive, not first-generation only.

Action: record the exact support ledger for every survivor and the precise
reason each failed seed died.

Expected output: one ledger of genuinely invariant finite supports inside the
current budget, or a proof that no such support exists at that budget.

Kill condition: if every seed either loses exactness or spills outside the
current budget, package only a budget-limited negative result and either move
to the next declared budget or stop.

### Step 3 - Derive Exact Dynamics And Solve Compatibility Conditions

Depends on: Step 2.

Action: for each genuinely invariant support, write the exact projected ODEs
with all coefficients and symmetries explicit.

Action: compute conserved quantities, gauge freedoms, and the algebraic
compatibility conditions for constant-phase or quantitatively slow relative
phase regimes. Any "slow drift" language must come with an explicit drift bound
relative to the transfer window.

Action: distinguish true invariant or robust near-invariant manifolds from
coordinate artifacts or one-orbit coincidences.

Action: track transfer directions and energy routing without requiring Tao's
five-role decomposition. If a mechanism appears later, the Tao roles may be
used only as a diagnostic summary.

Expected output: one exact dynamics ledger plus a short list of admissible
locking or near-locking regimes with explicit compatibility conditions.

Kill condition: if no exact support admits a compatible phase regime beyond
transient coincidence, stop and package that obstruction sharply.

### Step 4 - Test Delayed Transfer Against Intrinsic Clocks And Spectator Burden

Depends on: Step 3.

Action: for each admissible regime, compare the transfer delay to the support's
own turnover time and viscous decay time. Delayed transfer must be defined by
the metric sheet from Step 1, not by narrative convenience.

Action: enforce the spectator burden threshold during the same window. A
candidate fails if leakage or auxiliary channels overwhelm the mechanism before
the delayed transfer event lands.

Action: test robustness at the declared standard. Do not count a single finely
tuned favorable trajectory as success unless the standard explicitly allows it.

Action: allow merged-role or non-Tao-shaped mechanisms. The chain is testing
for exact delayed-transfer behavior, not for a predetermined role diagram.

Expected output: one best exact candidate regime or one named obstruction:
no robust locking,
no delayed window,
spectator overload,
or coefficient rigidity.

Kill condition: if the best survivor only produces short-lived favorable
phases without robust delayed transfer under the declared metrics, the chain
fails as a counterexample route at the current budget.

### Step 5 - Compare The Outcome To The Firewall With Bounded Claims

Depends on: Step 4.

Action: declare success only if an honest exact support and a robust
delayed-transfer window satisfy the metric sheet. That endpoint is existential
and does not require exhausting all other support classes.

Action: if no success appears, claim only what has actually been shown:
no counterexample inside the searched exact support classes and budgets,
plus the exact obstruction that caused failure.

Action: if the obstruction suggests a next move, name it precisely:
larger support classes,
different geometry classes,
or abandonment of Tao-style diagnostics.

Expected output: either a counterexample memo, a class-limited obstruction
memo, or a bounded escalation memo.

Kill condition: if the final writeup makes a firewall-level negative claim from
an unexhaustive search, or counts unquantified promising transients as
evidence, stop and report failure of interpretation.
