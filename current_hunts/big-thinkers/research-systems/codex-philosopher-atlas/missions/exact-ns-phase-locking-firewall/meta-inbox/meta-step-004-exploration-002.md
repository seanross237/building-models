## Reusable Lesson

- On recursively exact support audits, do not look only at the most thematic
  positive-positive couplings.
  Conjugate-complete mirror-cross pairs can kill the budget earlier and more

  guard, but it should not be promoted into a new canonical family unless it
  changes the abstract repeated-orbit pattern.
