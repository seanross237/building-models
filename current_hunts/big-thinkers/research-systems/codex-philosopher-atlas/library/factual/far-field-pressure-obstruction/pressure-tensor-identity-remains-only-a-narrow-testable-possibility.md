# Pressure-Tensor Identity Remains Only A Narrow Testable Possibility

Status: `VERIFIED` with `INFERRED` condition

The exact pressure relation

`partial_i partial_j p = R_i R_j(u_i u_j)`

is the only screened candidate that remains live at all, but only in a narrow
sense. It would have to force cancellations in the remote-shell moments that
generate the affine-or-higher harmonic part of `p_far` on `Q_k`.

As long as the identity stays only a representation formula or a
Calderon-Zygmund repackaging, it remains Tao-compatible and does not rescue the
branch. The surviving content is therefore a focused follow-up question, not a
green light.
