# Same-Target Hybrids Are Excluded At Step 1

Status: `INFERRED`

No same-target hybrid route should be admitted at Step 1 of the exact-rewrite
screen.

Pressure-tensor or geometry-side hybrids would blur the branch boundary before
the pure rewrite family has been audited on its own fixed architecture. The
Step-1 job is to test whether exact rewrites alone change the localized LEI
ledger, not to let adjacent branches silently enter through mixed packaging.
