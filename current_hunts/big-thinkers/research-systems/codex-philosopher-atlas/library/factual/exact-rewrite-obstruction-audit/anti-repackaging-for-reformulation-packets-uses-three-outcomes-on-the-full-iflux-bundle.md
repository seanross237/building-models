# Anti-Repackaging For Reformulation Packets Uses Three Outcomes On The Full I_flux Bundle

Status: `INFERRED` Step-013 classifier

For the Step-013 reformulation audit, anti-repackaging is not judged by how
novel a variable choice, notation sheet, or pre-localized identity looks.
Judge the candidate by its net effect on the full theorem-facing
`I_flux[phi]` bundle after localization, pressure recovery, reconstruction,
admissibility debt, and any claimed transfer are restored.

The frozen three-way classifier is:

- `equivalent redistribution`: after all debt is repaid, the candidate leaves
  the same full `I_flux[phi]` bundle with no positive-margin smaller
  coefficient, so the apparent gain is only packaging or redistribution among
  ledger columns;
- `ambiguous redistribution`: the candidate names one exact ledger cell where
  derivative count, locality, cancellation, or integrability might improve,
  but it does not yet produce a same-currency cash-out or an explicit transfer
  lemma back to a smaller full-bundle coefficient;
- `potentially live mechanism change`: after full debt, the candidate either
  directly lowers the effective coefficient on the full `I_flux[phi]` bundle
  by a positive margin or names a specific transfer lemma whose conclusion is
  that same positive-margin reduction on the frozen theorem ledger.

This makes the anti-repackaging screen mechanical on the inherited LEI ledger
instead of rhetorical.
