# Anti-Repackaging Collapse Occurs When No Local Or Pathwise Gain Survives Debt

Status: `VERIFIED` burden language with `INFERRED` enforcement rule

For the stochastic branch, reformulation does not count as progress if its
usable content is only the deterministic burden restated through expectation,
filtration, localization, or imported machinery.

The Step-012 record supports one mechanical kill rule. Classify the route as
`anti-repackaging collapse` if every usable consequence falls into any of
these buckets:

- expectation-only or conditional-expectation-only control, with no stated
  local or pathwise consequence on the named concentration or stretching
  burden;
- filtration, stopping-time, or localization bookkeeping whose apparent gain
  disappears once singular-regime debt is charged;
- claims that survive only after importing smoother flows, stronger
  filtrations, or regularity machinery whose substantive content is the
  original deterministic hard part.

This makes the anti-repackaging screen operational in the branch's own burden
currency instead of leaving it as a vague warning against elegant stochastic
language.
