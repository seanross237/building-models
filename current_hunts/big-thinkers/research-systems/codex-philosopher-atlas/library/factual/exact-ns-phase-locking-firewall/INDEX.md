# Exact-NS Phase-Locking Firewall

Reusable findings on the Step-1 intrinsic-object and metric freezes for exact
Navier-Stokes phase-locking branches, plus the first Step-2 seed, closure, and
enlargement screens on that frozen sheet, the Step-3 connected shared-mode
seed classification, and the later Step-3 closure / enlargement verdicts.

## Entries

- `intrinsic-phase-coherence-objects-must-pass-a-five-layer-helical-admissibility-screen.md`:
  intrinsic exact phase objects on this branch are screened modulo triad-orbit
  relabeling, conjugate completion, helical sign/phase conventions, gauge
  anchors, and amplitude normalization, with three statuses
  `intrinsic`,
  `canonically representable but non-intrinsic`,
  and
  `inadmissible`.
- `coefficient-corrected-triad-phase-orbit-ledger-is-the-best-current-intrinsic-object.md`:
  the surviving intrinsic object is the exact coefficient-corrected
  triad-phase orbit ledger, not a desired-channel score.
- `transfer-weighted-phase-coherence-scores-are-canonical-diagnostics-not-intrinsic-objects.md`:
  scalar coherence summaries are honest fixed-sheet diagnostics only when the
  orbit-level exact ledger is already in hand; the aggregation itself is not
  symmetry-forced.
- `desired-vs-spectator-phase-splits-are-definitionally-inadmissible.md`:
  any raw phase score that names desired and spectator routes by hand fails the
  branch's admissibility test immediately.
- `delayed-transfer-is-scored-by-exact-closed-support-inflow-rather-than-packet-itinerary-bookkeeping.md`:
  delayed transfer is measured by exact inflow into a predeclared high shell
  set on the closed support, not by replaying packet-itinerary language from
  earlier branches.
- `delayed-transfer-events-require-intrinsic-clocks-spectator-burden-and-non-single-trajectory-robustness.md`:
  any claimed delayed-transfer episode must be judged against frozen intrinsic
  turnover and viscous clocks, one same-window spectator-burden rule, and a
  robustness requirement stronger than a single tuned orbit.
- `step-2-is-well-posed-once-the-phase-ledger-search-class-and-metric-sheet-are-frozen.md`:
  Step 2 is ready only after the branch freezes the intrinsic phase ledger,
  recursively exact helical-support class, admissible enlargement policy, and
  the delayed-transfer metric sheet on one predeclared `A / B / R` partition.
- `first-budget-seeds-exclude-repeated-wavevector-and-collinear-triads-because-the-exact-projected-interaction-vanishes.md`:
  at the one-triad budget, repeated-wavevector and collinear relations are not
  dead survivors but non-seeds because the exact projected interaction already
  vanishes there.
- `one-triad-first-budget-seeds-reduce-to-three-canonical-helical-sign-sheet-classes-on-ordered-noncollinear-triads.md`:
  every honest first-budget seed reduces to one ordered noncollinear triad
  together with one of the three canonical sign-sheet classes
  `(+++)`,
  `(++-)`,
  `(+--)`
  allowed by the frozen equivalence sheet.
- `canonical-one-triad-seeds-spill-outside-the-first-budget-on-the-first-honest-closure-pass.md`:
  once the first-budget seed catalog is frozen to nondegenerate one-triad
  supports, mandatory conjugate completion and full-ledger closure already
  force new wavevectors such as `p - q`, `2p + q`, and `p + 2q`, so no
  canonical seed survives the one-triad budget.
- `distinct-off-orbit-pairs-not-self-pairs-drive-the-first-budget-spill.md`:
  the decisive first spill comes from mirror-cross off-orbit pairs on the
  conjugate-complete ledger, not from same-wavevector doubling, so the tidy
  six-mode picture fails for exact-closure reasons rather than for a fake
  self-pair obstruction.
- `admissible-enlargement-of-the-six-mode-mirror-complete-triad-ledger-confirms-it-is-only-an-over-pruned-pseudo-survivor.md`:
  once the tidy six-mode picture is treated as a pseudo-survivor, the smallest
  admissible enlargement on `p - q` reproduces the same spill and confirms
  that the picture was only an over-pruned artifact of stopping closure too
  early.
- `connected-two-triad-shared-mode-seeds-reduce-to-a-generic-five-orbit-family-and-a-four-orbit-parallelogram-family.md`:
  after the frozen quotient rules, every connected two-triad shared-mode
  cluster reduces to the canonical form
  `{ (a,b,a+b), (a,c,a+c) }`,
  and the honest second-budget catalog has exactly two genuine families:
  the generic five-orbit fan and the four-orbit mirror / parallelogram
  subfamily.
- `genuine-second-budget-shared-mode-seeds-must-add-new-connected-support-beyond-the-first-budget-pseudo-support.md`:
  a new second-budget seed must stay connected through the shared orbit and
  add at least one new wavevector orbit beyond the old six-wavevector
  pseudo-support, so duplicate redrawings, inert helical decorations,
  first-budget-contained pictures, and disconnected add-ons do not count as
  fresh second-rung seeds.
- `generic-five-orbit-shared-mode-seeds-spill-on-the-first-honest-closure-pass.md`:
  the generic shared-mode two-triad family `T_gen(a,b,c)` already spills on
  the first honest closure pass because cross-triad pairs force new targets
  such as `a+b+c`, `a+b-c`, or `a-b+c`.
- `mirror-parallelogram-shared-mode-seeds-spill-on-a-plus-or-minus-2b-on-the-first-honest-closure-pass.md`:
  the four-orbit mirror/parallelogram shared-mode family `T_par(a,b)` also
  spills immediately because the exact closure rule forces `a+2b` or `a-2b`.
- `chain-step-3-is-not-well-posed-on-the-second-budget-because-no-honest-two-triad-shared-mode-support-survives.md`:
  on the Step-3 two-triad shared-mode budget, every honest seed is already

  cross-triad forcing and the mirror/parallelogram subfamily through
  `a +/- 2b`, and the only tidy pseudo-survivors also fail admissible
  enlargement, so Chain Step 3 is not well posed on that budget.
- `admissible-enlargement-of-the-generic-five-orbit-shared-mode-fan-shows-it-is-only-an-over-pruned-second-budget-pseudo-survivor.md`:
  restoring a first forced orbit such as `a+b+c` to the generic five-orbit
  fan immediately forces targets like `a+2b+c` and `a+b+2c`, so the tidy
  picture is only an over-pruned second-budget artifact.
- `admissible-enlargement-of-the-four-orbit-mirror-parallelogram-cluster-shows-it-is-only-an-over-pruned-second-budget-pseudo-survivor.md`:
  restoring a first forced orbit such as `a+2b` to the mirror / parallelogram
  cluster immediately forces targets like `a+3b` and `2b`, so this tidy
  four-orbit picture is also only an over-pruned second-budget artifact.
