# Step-2 Admission Requires One Named Bad Term One One-Sided Gain And One Declared Burden Share On The Dyadic Ledger

Status: `PROPOSED` enforcement rule from `INFERRED` Step-011 synthesis

For the coupled energy-helicity chain, Step 2 should reject any candidate that
does not explicitly name all of the following on the same dyadic ledger:

- one dyadic coupled-flux observable or one forbidden transfer pattern;
- one stretching-production or enstrophy-growth burden;
- one claimed one-sided sign or exclusion gain on that burden;
- one declared quantitatively relevant burden share;
- one statement that still survives the Tao screen plus mixed-helicity,
  pressure, viscosity, and localization debt.

This rule is the mechanical version of the Step-011 report's admission
standard. It prevents attractive cascade language or thin sector anecdotes
from being promoted before they cash out against one named bad term on one
auditable ledger.
