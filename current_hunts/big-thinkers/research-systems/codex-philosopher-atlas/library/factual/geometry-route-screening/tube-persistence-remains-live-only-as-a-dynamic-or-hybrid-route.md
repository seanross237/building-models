# Tube Persistence Remains Live Only As A Dynamic Or Hybrid Route

Status: `VERIFIED` with `INFERRED` consequence

The geometry planning record repeatedly separates tube coherence or persistence
from both direction regularity and Beltrami deficit. The surviving value of
this family is not descriptive tube language or one-time coherent-structure
snapshots. The local corpus does not yet contain a standalone theorem-level
tube-persistence regularity criterion, so its only live content is dynamic:

- a transport or persistence mechanism for coherent structures across scales and
  time;
- tied to a fixed scenario and localization protocol;
- and relevant to the full stretching mechanism rather than to geometry alone,
  for example by shrinking an exterior or inter-scale stretching contribution
  or the localization-evolution cost.

Without that persistence ingredient, tube language is only descriptive and does
not clear the Tao screen.
