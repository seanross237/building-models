# Over-Specific Tube Variants Are Too Weakly Supported For Official Step-3 Use

Status: `INFERRED`

More specific scenario proposals such as `axial tube collapse` or
`filament reconnection-type concentration` are not supported strongly enough in
the current repository to serve as official Step-3 scenario classes.

On the local record, those variants appear only as illustrative examples in an
attack on underspecified scenario language. They are not backed by a matching
Step-2 survivor rationale, a dedicated screening memo, or a stronger tie to the
live hybrid than the broader `filament or tube concentration` class already
provides.

The consequence is a scoping rule for Step 3: scenario choice should stay at
the scenario-class level until localization and the stretching representation
are fixed. Promoting a thinner variant earlier would overfit the branch before
the repository has earned that refinement.
