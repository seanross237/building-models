# Local-Energy Flux Localization Is The Right Inherited Architecture For The Exact-Rewrite Audit

Status: `VERIFIED` source choice with `INFERRED` rationale

For Step 5 of the beyond-De Giorgi chain, the correct inherited architecture
is `local-energy flux/localization` rather than either De Giorgi truncation or
stretching localization.

The local CKN-style balance is the right fixed audit frame because it already
packages the branch in the exact form the chain wants to test: one cutoff, one
localized balance, and one visible place where localization, projection, and
Calderon-Zygmund costs are paid.

This makes the architecture fair to the bounded exact-rewrite shortlist. The
candidate rewrites all start from the same quadratic interaction before
localization, and the local-energy balance is the place where one can ask
whether the rewrite actually changes the operative cutoff cost instead of
merely moving algebra around.
