# Freeze Intrinsic Delayed-Transfer Metrics Before Support Searches

If a branch will later claim `delayed transfer`, make the step freeze the
metric sheet before support enumeration and trajectory screening begin.

That sheet should name one predeclared transfer partition, one exact inflow
observable on the closed support, one intrinsic clock, one spectator-burden
rule, and one robustness rule.
Otherwise ordinary fast transfer, spectator absorption, or one tuned orbit can
be mispackaged as evidence simply because the scoring language drifted with the
observed run.

The partition may be chosen only after the support is fixed, but once that
happens both the partition and the formulas must be frozen before any
trajectory is judged.
