# Anchor Reconstruction Goals To A Small Source List

When the task is to reconstruct an inherited argument, anchor the goal to a
short mandatory source list.

If no dedicated prior-art packet exists yet, say explicitly that the selected
mission or planning files are being used as a benchmark memo rather than as a
complete literature reconstruction. That keeps theorem-detail gaps honest
instead of letting the report imply coverage it does not have.

In Exploration 001 this prevented broad re-derivation of pressure background and
kept the report tied to the copied mission evidence that the branch actually
depends on.
