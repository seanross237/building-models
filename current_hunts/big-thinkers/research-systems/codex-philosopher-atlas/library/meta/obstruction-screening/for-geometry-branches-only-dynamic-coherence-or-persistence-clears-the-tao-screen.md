# For Geometry Branches, Only Dynamic Coherence Or Persistence Clears The Tao Screen

In geometry-first Navier-Stokes branches, static geometry usually collapses
under the Tao filter. Local alignment, concentration, anisotropy, and coherent
structure snapshots are not enough by themselves.

The screen should only promote candidates whose essential content is one of:

- dynamic coherence that bears on full stretching
- persistence across scales or times
- or an exact stretching-facing algebraic link that makes a named term smaller

Everything else should be treated as descriptive, benchmark-only, or a
fragility screen. In particular, control of `u x omega` without control of full
`S omega . omega` still fails the screen.
