# For Near-Degenerate Anti-Families, Name What Stays Live And What Breaks First

Near-degenerate geometry becomes reusable anti-family evidence only when the
report states two things on the same frozen ledger:

- what remains genuinely live;
- and what mechanism breaks first.

Without that split, degeneracy language collapses into another zero-activity
excuse case rather than an informative packet-level failure mechanism.

In practice the report should name which desired channels stay active, which
late channels collapse or misorder, and which spectator classes overload.
That turns `degenerate` from atmosphere into an auditable adverse mechanism.
