# When One Bad Term And One Gain Currency Are Frozen, A Tao Screen May End The Branch

Once a branch has fixed one live bad term and one admissible gain currency, a
Step-2 Tao screen does not need to manufacture a survivor list.

Its job can be narrower and sharper: decide whether any audited candidate
shrinks that same term on that same ledger. If none does, the right product is
a bounded obstruction memo and a clean branch-stop verdict.

This kind of kill is stronger than broad mission-level pessimism because it is
earned after the architecture, protocol, and comparison currency have already
been frozen.
