# Emergent Gravity

Approaches where gravity is not fundamental but emerges from deeper structures -- thermodynamics, entanglement, or condensed matter analogues. Includes Jacobson's thermodynamic derivation, Verlinde's entropic gravity, and the "gravity from entanglement" program.

## Specific Proposals

- [bianconi-entropic-action.md](bianconi-entropic-action.md) -- Bianconi (2025): gravitational action = quantum relative entropy S(ρ_metric || ρ_matter); Dirac-Kähler formalism; novel action principle but FAILS Tier 1 (not quantum, ghost concerns, phenomenological construction); inflationary predictions testable by CMB-S4; verdict: not viable as QG theory

- [thermodynamic-derivations/](thermodynamic-derivations/INDEX.md) -- Jacobson (1995, 2015), Padmanabhan, Sakharov: deriving Einstein's equations from thermodynamic and entanglement principles
- [verlinde-entropic-gravity/](verlinde-entropic-gravity/INDEX.md) -- Verlinde's 2010 entropic gravity and 2016 emergent dark matter program; observational tests and wide binary anomaly

- [criticisms-and-limitations/](criticisms-and-limitations/INDEX.md) -- Carlip's challenges, Kobakhidze's criticisms, shared UV completion failure mode, observational scorecard; promising at galaxy scales but incomplete for cosmology
