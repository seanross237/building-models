# Holographic Entanglement and Emergent Spacetime

## Ryu-Takayanagi (2006)

In AdS/CFT, entanglement entropy of boundary region A = area of minimal bulk surface homologous to A divided by 4G_N. Entanglement entropy IS geometric area.

## Van Raamsdonk (2010)

Reducing entanglement between CFT subsystems pinches off bulk spacetime. Zero entanglement = disconnected spacetime. Entanglement is the fabric of spacetime.

## ER = EPR (Maldacena & Susskind, 2013)

Einstein-Rosen bridges = Einstein-Podolsky-Rosen entanglement. Entangled black holes connected by non-traversable wormhole.

## Einstein Equations from Entanglement (Lashkari et al. 2013)

Linearized Einstein equations in AdS derived from first law of entanglement entropy: delta S_A = delta <H_A>. Fully quantum version of Jacobson's thermodynamic derivation.

## Connection to Verlinde 2016

Dark energy = long-range entanglement; baryonic matter displaces entanglement; elastic response = dark gravity. Area-to-volume entropy transition is entanglement-driven.

## Critical Limitation

All rigorous results are in anti-de Sitter space. Our universe has positive Lambda (de Sitter). Extension to dS is one of the most important open problems in theoretical physics.
