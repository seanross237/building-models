# Bi-Metric Emergent Gravity (BMEG)

A novel theoretical framework that exploits the bi-metric structure of asymptotic safety -- the distinction between background and fluctuation graviton sectors -- to construct a self-consistent theory where the UV background has spectral dimension d_s = 2 while the fluctuation graviton has its own independent anomalous dimension eta_h > 0.
