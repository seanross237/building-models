# Fakeon/Quadratic Gravity Research Program: Scope and Status

## Program Overview

Quadratic gravity with Anselmi-Piva fakeon quantization is a **well-developed, active research program** — not an obscure or niche idea. Damiano Anselmi (University of Pisa) and collaborators have published approximately 25+ papers on fakeons since 2017, and the theory has growing attention from multiple independent groups.

## Active Research Groups

- **Anselmi (Pisa):** Primary developer; 25+ papers covering fakeon prescription, unitarity proof, inflationary predictions, collider phenomenology, classicization
- **Salvio & Strumia:** "Agravity" program — quadratic gravity as a solution to the hierarchy problem (M₂ ~ 10¹¹ GeV)
- **Donoghue & Menezes:** Alternative ghost treatment via unstable particle interpretation
- **Holdom (Toronto):** Quadratic gravity phenomenology
- **Buoninfante (Radboud):** Connections to nonlocal gravity
- **Platania:** Connections to asymptotic safety
