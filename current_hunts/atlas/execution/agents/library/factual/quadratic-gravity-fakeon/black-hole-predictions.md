# Black Hole Predictions in QG+F

Comprehensive catalog of QG+F predictions for black holes: solutions, singularity status, entropy, Hawking radiation, quasi-normal modes, shadows, information paradox, and experimental prospects. **Bottom line: QG+F makes no testable BH predictions for astrophysical black holes.** All corrections are suppressed by (l_P/r_H)² ~ (M_P/M_BH)², ranging from 10⁻⁷⁶ (solar mass) to 10⁻⁹⁶ (SMBH).

## Nonlinear BH Solutions (Lü-Perkins-Pope-Stelle 2015)

**Key paper:** Lü, Perkins, Pope, Stelle (2015), PRL 114, 171601 [arXiv:1502.01028]

### Lichnerowicz-Type Theorem

All static BH solutions in quadratic gravity have vanishing Ricci scalar (R = 0). The R² term drops out entirely for static BHs; only the C² (Weyl²) sector modifies GR. The static BH problem reduces to Einstein-Weyl gravity.

### Three Solution Families

1. **Schwarzschild branch:** Standard GR solution with zero Yukawa charge (S₂⁻ = 0). Exists for all masses.
2. **Non-Schwarzschild "Yukawa attractive" branch:** Negative Yukawa charge (S₂⁻ < 0). Gravity stronger near horizon. Unbounded parameter space.
3. **Non-Schwarzschild "Yukawa repulsive" branch:** Positive Yukawa charge (S₂⁻ > 0). Exists only for narrow parameter ranges.

Branches intersect at a critical horizon radius r_H^cross ≈ 0.876/m₂. Using m₂ ≈ 1.42 M_P (from Benedetti et al. 2009 NGFP values, g₁*/g₃* = 2.02), this gives **r_H^cross ≈ 0.63 l_P** — sub-Planckian. The ghost-driven instability only triggers for BHs smaller than the Planck length, deep in the non-perturbative AS regime.

### Phase Diagram (Silveravalle & Zuccotti 2023, PRD 107, 064029)

Comprehensive mapping of all static, spherically symmetric solutions as functions of mass and Yukawa charge. Includes BHs, naked singularities, and wormholes. All solutions are "essentially identical at coordinate distances r ≳ 2M, with significant deviations appearing close to and below r = 2M only."

## Fakeon Prescription Selects Schwarzschild

**This is the central result for QG+F BH physics.** Under the fakeon prescription:

1. **The ghost is purely virtual** — it never forms classical field profiles. Therefore the Yukawa charge S₂⁻ must vanish for any solution generated by physical sources.
2. **Static BHs in QG+F are Schwarzschild.** Non-Schwarzschild branches are excluded.
3. **Quantum corrections via classicization** — the classicized field equations include nonlocal corrections from ghost averaging at O(1/m₂²), taking the form ⟨F⟩ = ma where ⟨F⟩ includes "a little bit of future" (Anselmi 2018, CQG 36, 065010). Inherently perturbative.
4. **Microcausality violation** at Δt ~ 1/m₂ ~ 10⁻⁴³ s. Does not propagate along light cones or via GWs.
5. **Non-perturbative uncertainty** — Anselmi acknowledges (JHEP 01 (2026) 104, arXiv:2510.05276) that classicized equations miss non-perturbative effects, introducing a "new type of uncertainty."

This naked singularity "renders the information paradox moot as it merges with the singularity issue." **Under the fakeon prescription, this would be suppressed** at the perturbative level; non-perturbative fakeon effects are an open question.

## Wald Entropy

The Wald entropy formula for the quadratic gravity action gives:

    S_Wald = A/(4G) + 32πα R_H + 64πβ (C_abcd ε^{ab} ε^{cd})_H

For the **Schwarzschild solution** (R = 0):

    S_BH = A/(4G) + O(l_P²/r_H²) × A/(4G)

## Hawking Radiation

QG+F modifications to Hawking radiation:

1. **Modified spectrum:** Massive scalars (mass m₀) provide additional emission channel when T_H > m₀. The fakeon is NOT emitted (cannot appear as asymptotic state).
2. **Temperature correction:** T_H = T_H^GR × [1 + O(l_P²/r_H²)] — negligible for astrophysical BHs.
3. **Ghost instability at late stages** (ghost-as-physical only): drives phase transition at late evaporation. Under fakeon, suppressed perturbatively.

**Prediction:** QG+F Hawking radiation is indistinguishable from GR for any macroscopic BH.

## Quasi-Normal Modes (QNMs)

**Key paper:** Tattersall & Silveravalle (2024), arXiv:2412.15037

The QNM spectrum in quadratic gravity contains:

1. **Standard GR modes:** Identical to Schwarzschild QNMs.
2. **NEW massive spin-2 modes:** Additional QNM families from the massive spin-2 DOF, with mass scale μ² = 1/(2α_W). "Both classes of modes can be excited in physical processes."

**Under the fakeon prescription:** Massive spin-2 modes are purely virtual — cannot carry energy to infinity as real particles. Appear as **virtual intermediate states** modifying ringdown waveform, suppressed by ~(l_P/r_H)². Not independent observable modes.

Massive spin-2 QNM frequencies ~ m₂ ~ M_P, corresponding to GW frequencies ~ 10⁴³ Hz — utterly beyond any detector.

## BH Shadows

**Key paper:** Held, Gold & Sen (2023), A&A 674, A190

Shadow size deviation from GR for M87*:

    ζ^{M87} = 2 exp(-6.5 × 10⁵⁰) S₂⁻

This is **exponentially suppressed** — literally e^{-10⁵⁰}. Since the fakeon selects Schwarzschild (S₂⁻ = 0), EHT prediction is identical to GR. Some non-Schwarzschild solutions (Type II naked singularities, ghost-as-physical only) are **observationally excluded** by EHT.
