# Unitarity and Ghost Freedom (Constraints A1 + A2)

## Unitarity (S-matrix Unitarity)

**Statement:** The S-matrix must be unitary: S†S = SS† = 1. Total probability of all outcomes of any scattering process must equal 1. For the graviton propagator, this requires positive spectral weight for all physical states (optical theorem).

**Mathematical form:**
- S†S = 1
- Im(M_forward) = (1/2) Σ_f |M_fi|² (optical theorem)
- Spectral function ρ(s) ≥ 0 for physical graviton modes

**Restrictiveness: MAXIMALLY RESTRICTIVE**

This eliminates:
- Higher-derivative gravity theories (R² + R_μν R^μν) generically introduce ghost states with negative spectral weight — the Ostrogradsky instability. However, recent work (2024) shows fictitious ghosts in truncations of an effective action may not indicate true unitarity violation when all operators are included.
- Naively quantized GR beyond one loop — perturbative non-renormalizability signals loss of predictivity, though not unitarity per se.
- Any theory with negative-norm physical states.

## Ghost Freedom (No Negative-Norm States)

**Statement:** The physical Hilbert space must have a positive-definite inner product. Ghost fields used in gauge fixing (Faddeev-Popov) are permitted but must decouple from the physical spectrum.

**Mathematical form:**
- ⟨ψ_phys|ψ_phys⟩ > 0 for all physical states
- BRST cohomology: physical states ∈ ker(Q_BRST) / im(Q_BRST)

Ghost freedom follows from unitarity + positive-definite Hilbert space. The key tension: higher-derivative terms that improve UV behavior generically introduce massive ghost modes.

**The classic tension:**
- **4-derivative gravity** (Stelle 1977): Renormalizable but contains a massive spin-2 ghost with m² ~ M_Pl²/α
- **Infinite-derivative gravity** (IDG): Uses entire function exp(-□/M²) in the propagator, potentially avoiding ghosts while maintaining UV improvement. Propagator G(p²) ~ exp(p²/M²)/p² has no additional poles.
- Recent work (2024): "Kinetic positivity constraints" — imposing positivity of total kinetic energy and performing Lorentz transformations in field space — may render ghost-ridden theories quantizable.

## Status Across Approaches

- **String theory:** Perturbatively unitary to all orders (no-ghost theorem, Goddard-Thorn). At high energies, S-matrix "cannot be a unitary operator in Fock space" due to black hole production — unitarity may require a more general framework.
- **LQG:** Fundamentally unitary at the full quantum level. Apparent non-unitarity from tracing over Planck-scale DOF (decoherence without dissipation, Perez 2021).
- **Asymptotic Safety:** Dynamical graviton spectral function is positive (Bonanno et al. 2022) — necessary condition. Full unitarity remains unproven. Higher-derivative terms generated by RG flow may produce fictitious ghosts (artifacts of truncation).

## Dependency

Ghost freedom (A2) follows from unitarity (A1) + positive-definite Hilbert space. Unitarity is genuinely independent — cannot be derived from other constraints.
