# Doubly Quantum Mechanics (2025)

## Core Idea

Promote the SU(2) rotation symmetry to the quantum group SUq(2) — the unique quantum deformation of SU(2). In this framework:
- Probability itself becomes a self-adjoint operator on a "geometry Hilbert space"
- Standard QM is recovered in the limit q → 1
- Deviations from standard QM appear at O(1-q)

## Key Features

- Published in *Quantum* (2025)
- The deformation parameter q could be quantum-gravitational in origin, consistent with q ≈ 1
- Provides a mathematically precise framework for "modified QM" from quantum geometry

## Status

Brief and novel; not yet connected to any concrete gravity → QM derivation. Represents an interesting mathematical possibility for how quantum-gravitational effects could modify the QM framework itself.
