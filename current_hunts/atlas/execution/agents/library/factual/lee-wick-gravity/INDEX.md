# Lee-Wick Quantum Gravity

Quantum gravity via meromorphic propagators with complex conjugate mass poles, originally proposed as an escape from the ghost/d_s=2 no-go theorem. The CLOP prescription has been **definitively shown to fail** (unitarity violation + Lorentz invariance breaking), with Modesto (the creator) co-authoring the 2025 paper concluding only the fakeon prescription is viable. The four-derivative version with fakeon prescription IS QG+F; the six-derivative super-renormalizable version with fakeon is a known extension. **Not viable as an independent program.**
