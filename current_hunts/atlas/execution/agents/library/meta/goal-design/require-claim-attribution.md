## Lesson

Ask the explorer to be explicit about which claims are sourced from papers versus the explorer's own reasoning. Without this instruction, reports blend literature findings and original analysis seamlessly, making it difficult to assess the reliability of individual claims.

## Evidence

- **riemann-hypothesis strategy-001 exploration 001** — The explorer's report was well-structured but "could have been more explicit about which claims are sourced from papers vs. the explorer's own reasoning."

## When to apply

Any exploration producing a report that mixes literature review with original analysis or synthesis. Particularly important for construction explorations and framework-building tasks where the boundary between established results and novel claims matters for downstream evaluation.
