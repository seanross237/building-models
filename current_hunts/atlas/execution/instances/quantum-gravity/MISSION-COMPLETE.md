### 2. The No-Go Theorem
Ghost-free Lorentz-invariant theories cannot produce d_s = 2. Proof relies on Hadamard's factorization theorem (entire-function propagator modifications grow exponentially, not polynomially) and the Kallen-Lehmann spectral representation (local QFT propagators fall no faster than 1/p^2). Consequence: all ghost-free nonlocal theories (infinite derivative gravity, etc.) give d_s -> 0, not d_s = 2. This forces a hard choice: accept ghost-like degrees of freedom (fakeon), break Lorentz invariance (Horava-Lifshitz), or abandon d_s = 2.

The minimal axiom set is: {Lorentz invariance, diffeomorphism invariance, locality, perturbative renormalizability, unitarity}. Relaxing any one axiom opens alternatives; keeping all five forces QG+F uniquely.
