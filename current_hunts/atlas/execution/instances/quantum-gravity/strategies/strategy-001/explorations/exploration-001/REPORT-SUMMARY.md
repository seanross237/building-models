## Key Takeaway
The **unitarity-renormalizability tension** is the single tightest bottleneck in quantum gravity. Every major approach defines itself by how it escapes this tension (string UV finiteness, asymptotic safety, LQG discreteness, IDG entire functions). For a novel constraint-driven construction, the most powerful strategy would be to start from the underexploited constraints — especially spectral dimension running to d_s ≈ 2 in the UV and the Jacobson thermodynamic derivation — and work backward to identify what mathematical structures they force.

## Leads Worth Pursuing
1. **Spectral dimension d_s → 2 as a constructive axiom** — instead of checking this after building a theory, use it as a starting point. What mathematical structures give exactly d_s = 2 in the UV while flowing to d_s = 4 in the IR?
2. **Jacobson + Ryu-Takayanagi as dual axioms** — demanding both entanglement-area scaling and thermodynamic derivation of field equations may uniquely constrain the microscopic theory
3. **The 2025 unified holographic inequality** — all Swampland bounds from a single geometric condition could serve as an organizing principle
4. **Lorentz invariance above E_Pl** — GRB 221009A bounds exceed E_Pl for linear LIV, which is a remarkably strong constraint that eliminates many discrete spacetime approaches unless they have a mechanism for exact Lorentz symmetry recovery
