**Lessons:**
1. For theory evaluation tasks, the Tier 1/2 validation framework is extremely efficient — it catches fatal flaws early

3. The entropic gravity program's fundamental weakness (no UV completion) applies to ALL variants (Jacobson, Verlinde, Padmanabhan, Bianconi) — this is a program-level finding, not paper-specific
4. A clear "this doesn't work" result is more valuable than 10 explorations of a dead end
5. Future GOAL.md files should say "write to REPORT.md after EVERY web search or significant finding, not just section by section"
