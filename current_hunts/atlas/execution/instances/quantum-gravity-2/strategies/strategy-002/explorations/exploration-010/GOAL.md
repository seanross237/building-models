# Exploration 010: Future Directions Survey — What Should the Next Strategy Target?

## Mission Context

Strategy-002 attempted to build a focused novel theory around QG+F's most fertile explanatory gap (the fakeon interpretation). After 9 explorations, the result is an interpretive bridge observation (Wheeler propagator = fakeon at tree level + causal set origin), not a full theory. The recommendation is to pivot.

This final exploration scouts the landscape for the NEXT strategy. What direction has the best chance of producing genuinely novel physics (not just interpretation) that advances beyond QG+F?

## Your Task

Survey 3-4 promising directions for a future strategy. For each, assess: feasibility, novelty potential, and whether it could produce predictions or computations beyond what QG+F already provides.

### Direction A: Non-Perturbative QG+F / Ghost Confinement
- What's the current state of lattice quantum gravity that could test the QCD-gravity analogy?
- Has anyone attempted to simulate QG+F non-perturbatively (lattice, CDT, or Monte Carlo)?

- Search: lattice quadratic gravity 2024 2025, CDT higher derivative gravity, ghost confinement gravity, non-perturbative fakeon

### Direction B: Parameter Determination / Observable Predictions
- Can QG+F's free parameters (M₂, M₀) be constrained by any principle (self-consistency, holographic bounds, entropy maximization)?
- Are there ANY new experimental avenues opening up beyond r from LiteBIRD?
- What about gravitational wave observations — can the ringdown spectrum of BH mergers ever constrain quadratic gravity parameters?
- Search: QG+F parameter determination 2025, gravitational wave higher derivative gravity 2025 2026, quadratic gravity observational constraints

### Direction C: Genuinely New Mathematics (not reinterpretation)
- Is there a mathematical framework beyond perturbative QFT that could host QG+F-like theories?
- What about functorial QFT, topological methods, or information-theoretic approaches?
- Could the "quantum cost function" (needed by the cost-function program) be defined rigorously?
- Search: non-perturbative higher derivative gravity 2025, functorial quantum gravity, information theoretic quantum gravity novel

### Direction D: The QG+F-AS Connection
- Can the question "are QG+F and AS the same theory?" be resolved computationally?
- What's the state of functional RG calculations that compare perturbative QG+F to AS?
- If they're different theories, what distinguishes them observationally?
- Search: asymptotic safety quadratic gravity connection 2025 2026, QG+F asymptotic safety equivalence
