# Meta-Learning: Exploration S4-008 (Mission Validation)

## What worked
- Giving the validation guide tiers and the mission text directly in the goal produced clean, structured scoring
- The explorer had read the theory document (E007) and the devil's advocate (E006) first, so the scoring was well-informed
- Asking for "one paragraph, no hedging" in the honest bottom line produced a sharp conclusion

## Lesson
Mission validation should always be a separate exploration from the theory document. The same agent that writes the theory will tend to defend it; a fresh agent evaluating against external criteria is more honest.
