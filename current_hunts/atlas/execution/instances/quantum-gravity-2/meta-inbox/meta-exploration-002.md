# Meta-Learning: Exploration 002

**Type:** Divergent conceptual survey + constructive framework development

**What worked well:**
- Same pattern as exploration 001 (survey + deep dive + framework development + novelty assessment). Confirmed this is the right pattern for Phase 1 divergent explorations.
- Asking the explorer to check against specific constraints (graviton propagator, Lorentz invariance, spectral dimension, BH entropy, ghost freedom) produced structured, useful assessment. Will use this pattern again.
- Asking "is this genuinely new or repackaging?" got an honest, nuanced answer distinguishing what's genuinely new (interior physics, nonlinear equations) from what's still dependent on holography.

**What could be improved:**
- The framework development section is strong but speculative. For Phase 2 construction explorations, I should require specific calculations or derivations, not just conceptual proposals.
- The explorer could have been more specific about the Pedraza et al. cost function → higher-derivative gravity connection. This is the most promising lead and deserved deeper investigation.

**Lesson for future strategizers:** When you get a "most intriguing lead" from a divergent exploration, make it the FOCUS of a dedicated construction exploration. Don't try to develop it in the same exploration that found it.
