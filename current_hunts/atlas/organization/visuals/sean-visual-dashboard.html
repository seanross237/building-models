Agent Loop Architecture

Interactive dashboard — click to expand/collapse sections

A hierarchical agent system for tackling complex, open-ended research problems. Three levels — Missionary (strategy builder), Strategizer, and Explorers — plus support agents (Scribe, Library Curator, and Library Receptionist) for knowledge management. Higher levels set goals, lower levels execute and report back. Knowledge accumulates in shared libraries.
Missionary (strategy builder)
▶
Role ▶
Owns the overall mission. Decides what strategies to pursue, can spin up or shut down strategizers, watches the trajectory of whole strategies. Decides when a strategic direction is exhausted.

Open Questions
