The Big Picture

What does the pipeline do?

Three automated phases every night. No human in the loop.

Midnight

Collect + Analyze + Present

Scrape YouTube (37 topics) + arXiv. Claude scores each item 1-10. Items scoring 9+ get HTML slide decks.

↓
+15 min

Detect Breakthroughs + Launch Missions

Breakthrough detector scans items. Score 8.5+ triggers auto-creation of a codex-atlas mission. Agents investigate overnight.

↓
6am

Morning Briefing + Notify

Checks for completed atlas missions. Reads reports. Generates briefing presentation. Commits, pushes, pings Telegram.

All three phases run via system cron on the Hetzner box. No Express server, no Supabase, no Docker.

What about the backlog?

With 1,269 items and a 10/night limit, the full backlog takes ~127 nights to clear. In practice, new items arrive daily but at a much lower rate than the initial bulk load. The system prioritizes the oldest unanalyzed items, so the backlog steadily shrinks. New items from tonight's collection get analyzed within a few days.
