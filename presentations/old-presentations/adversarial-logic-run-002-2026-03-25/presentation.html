Three-step argument: (1) RCSM predicts every feature of consciousness, (2) it explains why those features obtain, (3) positing a further "consciousness property" is explanatorily idle — Occam's Razor favors identification. Parallels water/H2O.

What Prose Did Well

Nuanced arguments
Hedging, qualification, extended reasoning — the Hard Problem treatment was more philosophically careful
More pointed attacks
The adversary could quote exact phrases, making attacks more textually grounded and specific

What Prose Did Badly

Hid weaknesses behind eloquence
The recursion criterion was left vague because "models itself-as-modeling" sounds precise in a flowing paragraph. Notation would have exposed the gap immediately.
Fewer flaws detected
12 attacks vs. 16, and only 3 must-fix vs. 8. The logical structure was less transparent, so circularity and hidden assumptions were harder to spot.
Key Finding: Prose Converges Toward Formalism
The architect's v2 naturally introduced quasi-formal criteria (R1, R2, R3; four-part model definition) even though the format was prose. The adversarial process pushes theories toward formalization regardless of starting format.

Takeaways

System Learnings

What this run tells us about how format affects the adversarial pipeline.

The Judge Found a Real Dependency
Attack 6 was conditional on Attack 4 — if the identity claim holds, the zombie objection dissolves. This kind of inter-attack reasoning is something the adversary can't do (it only sees the theory, not its own attacks as a whole). The judge adds structural analysis.
Prose Reduces Attack Surface Visibility
Fewer attacks found (12 vs. 16), fewer must-fix items (3 vs. 8). Prose lets the architect mask vagueness with rhetoric. This doesn't mean the theory was better — it means the adversary was less effective at finding flaws.
Adversarial Pressure Formalizes Regardless
Even starting in prose, the v2 theory introduced R1/R2/R3 sub-criteria and a four-part model definition. The system's demand for precision is format-independent. This suggests a hybrid (prose + formal definitions) may be optimal.

Implications for Pipeline Design

Structured Notation
More flaws detected, more must-fix items, harder to hide vagueness. Better for stress-testing.
Prose Format
Richer philosophical engagement, better for nuanced arguments, but easier to hide logical gaps.
