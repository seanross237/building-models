Proposal

From Data to Autonomous Responses

Three tiers based on response consistency and risk.

1
Auto-Send (no review needed)
Concerns where your response is the same every time. The KB has the approved response. Just send it.
Pricing question Thank you / gratitude Audio garbled (refund + remake) Can't afford (put $0) Is this a scam?
2
Auto-Draft + Quick Approve (Telegram tap)
Concerns with 2-3 response variants depending on situation. System picks the right one, you approve with a tap.
Can't find song (magic link, but check email match) Download question (attach vs explain vs link) Song won't play (internet vs bug vs link issue) Payment problems (varies by sub-issue) Sharing questions (varies by platform)
3
Human Required (needs Sean's judgment)
Unique situations, emotional complexity, or actions the system can't take (manual DB edits, refunds, regenerations).
Change lyrics (needs to interpret what they want) Wrong version (needs account lookup + manual swap) Slideshow bugs (case-by-case) Account paused (needs manual generation add)
