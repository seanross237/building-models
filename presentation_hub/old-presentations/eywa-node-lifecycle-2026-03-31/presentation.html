state.md Brain dump

Written for the agent's future self when it gets re-spawned for evaluation or synthesis. "Dark knowledge" — the stuff that doesn't go in formal outputs.
HUNCHES
The hardest part will be making each date feel like a cohesive experience rather than a list of SF activities. Emphasized narrative arc in the goals.
WHAT I REJECTED

Whether the foodie-artist date will deliver enough structural variety for a rich meta-analysis. Whether cited SF locations are accurate (training data, not live).

The Handoff Sequence

1. AGENT TERMINATES
The node agent wrote plan.md and state.md, then exited. It has no idea what happens next.
2. ORCHESTRATOR DETECTS plan.md
Next poll cycle finds plan.md exists. Reads importance: low. Skips review entirely — auto-approves.
3. CREATES FIRST CHILD
Parses Step 1 from plan.md. Extracts the Goal field verbatim. Creates children/step-01-adrenaline-junkie-date/ with goal.md and status.
